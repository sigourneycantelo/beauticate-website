## Beauty intelligence, not beauty noise.

Beauticate was founded in 2012 by journalist and beauty editor Sigourney Cantelo with one conviction: that Australian women deserved a beauty destination built on real expertise, not press releases.

Twelve years and thousands of articles later, that conviction hasn't changed. What has changed is the landscape — and our determination to stay ahead of it.

### What we believe

Beauty is not frivolous. The rituals we build around our skin, our bodies, and our sense of self are acts of care. We take them seriously.

**Trust is non-negotiable.** Every product recommendation on Beauticate is made because someone on our team has genuinely used it and found it worth your time and money. We decline far more than we accept.

**Expertise matters.** Our contributors are editors, scientists, dermatologists, nutritionists, and practitioners — people who have spent their careers understanding their field. We don't feature influencers who tried something for a week.

**Editorial independence is everything.** Advertising and affiliate relationships exist on this site, and we disclose them clearly. They never influence our recommendations.

### The Beauticate Collective

Beauticate is powered by a group of Australia's most trusted beauty and wellness voices — editors, experts, and practitioners who share our commitment to evidence over hype.

### The Vodcast

*Beautiful Inside by Beauticate* is our long-form interview series exploring what it means to live beautifully — conversations with founders, scientists, creatives, and thinkers who are changing how we understand health, beauty, and the way we live.

Available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, and YouTube.
