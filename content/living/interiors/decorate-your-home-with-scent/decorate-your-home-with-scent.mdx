## The Minimalist Home

![Howtodecorateyourhomewithscent](/living/interiors/decorate-your-home-with-scent/HowToDecorateYourHomeWithScent.jpeg)

Got a thing for pared-back, nothing-but-the-essentials interiors? Why not add some warmth to your beautifully curated home with a comforting fragrance, like this [Leather Candle](https://www.mecca.com.au/malin-goetz/leather-candle/I-031641.html) from cult New York perfumers, MALIN + GOETZ. It’ll help cosy up your space, without cluttering up your interiors in the slightest.

## The Scandi Home

![Howtodecorateyourhomewithscent](/living/interiors/decorate-your-home-with-scent/HowToDecorateYourHomeWithScent-1.jpeg)

Scandinavian interiors are all about *hygge *(a Danish word that pretty much means cosiness and warmth), a key ingredient of which is – you guessed it – candles. While the Scandis apparently tend to opt for those of the simple, unscented variety, we do love this fragranced beauty from Swedish candle maker Byredo; appropriately named [Woods](https://www.mecca.com.au/byredo/woods-candle/I-031085.html), it brings the Nordic countryside inside with its deep, forest-inspired scent.

## The French Provincial Home

![Howtodecorateyourhomewithscent](/living/interiors/decorate-your-home-with-scent/HowToDecorateYourHomeWithScent-2.jpeg)

The candle for you if you favour French antiques, natural fibres and elegant wood furniture in your home. With spring now upon us (thank goodness), bring your very own French country garden indoors with this heavenly bouquet of a candle. With notes of green vetiver and musky patchouli, Ambre by Parisian candle brand Diptyque is the ideal scent for beautifying your space *à la française*.

## The Hollywood Regency Home

![Howtodecorateyourhomewithscent](/living/interiors/decorate-your-home-with-scent/HowToDecorateYourHomeWithScent-3.jpeg)

For those who enjoy the opulent, luxurious aesthetic of Hollywood’s Golden Age, opt for a candle that is glamorous both in scent and looks. The intoxicating blend of mimosa and iris from Cire Trudon’s Les Belles Matières Esterel candle adds the perfect dose of silver screen headiness to a home – and the gold-gilt and rich blue jar certainly fit the bill, too.

## The Bohemian Home

![Howtodecorateyourhomewithscent](/living/interiors/decorate-your-home-with-scent/HowToDecorateYourHomeWithScent-4.jpeg)

The rich amber notes of Bastide’s Ambre Soir would work perfectly with the free-spirited, eclectic style of the boho home.
