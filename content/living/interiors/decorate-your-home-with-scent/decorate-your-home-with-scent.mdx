## THE MINIMALIST HOME

Got a thing for pared-back, nothing-but-the-essentials interiors? Why not add some warmth to your beautifully curated home with a comforting fragrance, like this [Leather Candle](https://www.mecca.com.au/malin-goetz/leather-candle/I-031641.html) from cult New York perfumers, MALIN + GOETZ. It’ll help cosy up your space, without cluttering up your interiors in the slightest.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="31106&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## THE SCANDI HOME

Scandinavian interiors are all about *hygge *(a Danish word that pretty much means cosiness and warmth), a key ingredient of which is – you guessed it – candles. While the Scandis apparently tend to opt for those of the simple, unscented variety, we do love this fragranced beauty from Swedish candle maker Byredo; appropriately named [Woods](https://www.mecca.com.au/byredo/woods-candle/I-031085.html), it brings the Nordic countryside inside with its deep, forest-inspired scent.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="31107&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## THE FRENCH PROVINCIAL HOME

The candle for you if you favour French antiques, natural fibres and elegant wood furniture in your home. With spring now upon us (thank goodness), bring your very own French country garden indoors with this heavenly bouquet of a candle. With notes of green vetiver and musky patchouli, Ambre by Parisian candle brand Diptyque is the ideal scent for beautifying your space *à la française*.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="31108&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## THE HOLLYWOOD REGENCY HOME

For those who enjoy the opulent, luxurious aesthetic of Hollywood’s Golden Age, opt for a candle that is glamorous both in scent and looks. The intoxicating blend of mimosa and iris from Cire Trudon’s Les Belles Matières Esterel candle adds the perfect dose of silver screen headiness to a home – and the gold-gilt and rich blue jar certainly fit the bill, too.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="31109&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## THE BOHEMIAN HOME

The rich amber notes of Bastide’s Ambre Soir would work perfectly with the free-spirited, eclectic style of the boho home. Inspired by the golden hour before sunset in Provence, just close your eyes and its spicy warmth will whisk you away to a lazy summer’s evening in the South of France.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="31110&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]*Story by Tess Schlink; photography by [Dan Hilburn](https://www.instagram.com/danhilburn/); styling by **Molly Gay*[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
