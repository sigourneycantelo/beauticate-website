“Sheers can completely transform a space. They add an almost whimsical, alive feeling,” says Chanice. “They diffuse light into a space depending on the time of day. The afternoon glow is my favourite; it diffuses a beautiful golden light, making the room glow like a lantern. Sheers also add softness and texture, creating a sense of movement and warmth.”

#### The Unique Blend of Oakbank Linen

One standout feature of Oakbank Linen is its blend of linen and polyester. “We find that a lot of linen blends have more polyester than linen, which can make them look and feel too plastic and overly transparent,” Chanice notes. “On the other hand, full linen curtains often don’t sit well on windows; they crease easily, can shrink over time, and don’t form that perfect s-fold. Oakbank Linen delivers the semi-sheer look I was after, combining the best of both worlds – great texture and a soft drape without compromising on quality.”

#### Tips for Choosing the Right Curtains

For those considering sheer curtains, Chanice offers some expert tips. “First, think about the purpose—do you need blockout, or are you just looking to add some softness and light diffusion? Then decide if you want to see everything through the curtains or just a hint. This will help determine whether you need sheers alone or a combination, like double curtains or sheers with a blockout layer.”

She also recommends considering the feel of the fabric and how it interacts with other elements in the room. “Let the elements in your space guide how heavy or light you want the curtains to be. Check the overall temperature of the space—are your walls cool or warm? Let that influence your colour choice, and then decide if you want the curtains to be lighter or darker than your walls. It’s all about personal taste.”
