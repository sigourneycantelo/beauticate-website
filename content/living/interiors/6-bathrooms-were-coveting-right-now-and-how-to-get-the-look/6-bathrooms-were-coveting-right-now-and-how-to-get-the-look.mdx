## 1. STACKED TO PERFECTION

Bathrooms act as a personal sanctuary where everyday we focus only on ourselves. And because the room should embody relaxation and encourage self-care, one of the most important qualities of a great bathroom is a neat and orderly space – after all, a tidy space leads to a tidy mind. Organising using something like these floating shelves from Temple & Webster ensures your beauty products will always be tidy and easily accessible for your daily routine.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="40388&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][vc_column_text]

## 2. MIRROR, MIRROR ON THE WALL

A large statement mirror can really open up and maximise a space. Whether you've got a cramped, inner-city bathroom, or are lucky enough to enjoy a more spacious one, it can be just the right solution to give your bathroom that luxurious aesthetic. Take a look at [West Elm’s beautiful vanity mirrors](http://www.westelm.com.au/modern-streamline-wall-mirror-d6073) to replicate that bathroom magic yourself.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="40397&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][vc_column_text]

Looking for a fun way to give your bathroom a classic yet chic twist? ## 4. VINTAGE FACE-LIFT

Is your bathroom stuck in the twentieth century? An easy way to give your bathroom a sophisticated rework is by incorporating bold, dark features. Inky linens and bathroom accessories, like these bathroom beauties from Country Road, are the perfect pieces to create a modern space while complementing your bathroom's old-school aesthetics. Add a relaxing candle into the mix and your bathroom will be a serene twenty-first century dream.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="40391&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][vc_column_text]

## 5. ROYALTY ON A BUDGET

Designing a beautiful and lavish bathroom can be a very expensive undertaking. But one way to make your bathroom feel fit for a queen is by incorporating a few choice gold features and accessories to achieve a luxurious finish. We love [West Elm’s collection of gold and stone accessories](http://www.westelm.com.au/modern-resin-stone-bath-accessories-d5998) that would give a uniform and dainty look to any bathroom – even if you’re on a budget. ## 6. MINIMALIST MAGIC

While minimalism is all about eschewing the unnecessary, Marie Kondo would totally approve of you bringing a little greenery and nature inside: a few potted plants bring a refreshing and serene energy into your space, instantly transforming your bathroom into an oasis. Head to your neighbourhood garden store and pick out a beautiful plant to spruce things up – it'll pair beautifully with this 2 piece set of sleek planters. [/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="40393&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][vc_column_text]
