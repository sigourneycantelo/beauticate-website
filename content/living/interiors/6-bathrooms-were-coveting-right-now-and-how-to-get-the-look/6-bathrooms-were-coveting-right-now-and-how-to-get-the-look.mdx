## 1. STACKED TO PERFECTION

![Screen shot 2019 08 21 at 1.51.22 pm](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/Screen-Shot-2019-08-21-at-1.51.22-PM-1.png)


Bathrooms act as a personal sanctuary where everyday we focus only on ourselves. And because the room should embody relaxation and encourage self-care, one of the most important qualities of a great bathroom is a neat and orderly space – after all, a tidy space leads to a tidy mind. Organising using something like these floating shelves from Temple & Webster ensures your beauty products will always be tidy and easily accessible for your daily routine.

## 2. MIRROR, MIRROR ON THE WALL

![54447318 2326611164028824 9101663636689207860 n](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/54447318_2326611164028824_9101663636689207860_n.jpg)


A large statement mirror can really open up and maximise a space. Whether you've got a cramped, inner-city bathroom, or are lucky enough to enjoy a more spacious one, it can be just the right solution to give your bathroom that luxurious aesthetic. Take a look at [West Elm’s beautiful vanity mirrors](http://www.westelm.com.au/modern-streamline-wall-mirror-d6073) to replicate that bathroom magic yourself.

## 3. S(TILING)

![54732071 779728719065438 4367917711966225597 n](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/54732071_779728719065438_4367917711966225597_n-1.jpg)


Looking for a fun way to give your bathroom a classic yet chic twist? Sticking on tile-printed wallpaper is an incredible way to level up your bathroom game if you're not lucky enough to be blessed with the real thing – there are actually wallpaper offerings that are humidity resistant, like [this one from Art3d](https://www.amazon.com.au/Art3d-Stick-Kitchen-Backsplash-Vinyl/dp/B07HRLYZD3/ref=pd_sim_201_2/357-5482412-0900002?_encoding=UTF8&pd_rd_i=B07HRLYZD3&pd_rd_r=2366cb5a-c07b-495b-89b0-ea89c7f17237&pd_rd_w=SYEqS&pd_rd_wg=moosX&pf_rd_p=f09e5598-fbdb-4712-af44-62e0022496fc&pf_rd_r=Q0VGSBZ3C37EB8TDVWW4&psc=1&refRID=Q0VGSBZ3C37EB8TDVWW4). Use a monotone colour to match the vanity counter in your space for a clean and sophisticated look. The best part? It's cheap and nonpermanent, so you can change the styles to your heart's content.

## 4. VINTAGE FACE-LIFT

![45282009 380984529309961 2696741814648691401 n](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/45282009_380984529309961_2696741814648691401_n-1.jpg)


Is your bathroom stuck in the twentieth century? An easy way to give your bathroom a sophisticated rework is by incorporating bold, dark features. Inky linens and bathroom accessories, like these bathroom beauties from Country Road, are the perfect pieces to create a modern space while complementing your bathroom's old-school aesthetics. Add a relaxing candle into the mix and your bathroom will be a serene twenty-first century dream.

## 5. ROYALTY ON A BUDGET

![43109888 622783014790879 3605568098269730949 n](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/43109888_622783014790879_3605568098269730949_n.jpg)


Designing a beautiful and lavish bathroom can be a very expensive undertaking. But one way to make your bathroom feel fit for a queen is by incorporating a few choice gold features and accessories to achieve a luxurious finish. We love [West Elm’s collection of gold and stone accessories](http://www.westelm.com.au/modern-resin-stone-bath-accessories-d5998) that would give a uniform and dainty look to any bathroom – even if you’re on a budget.

## 6. MINIMALIST MAGIC

![27891308 686748098382004 984656278066298880 n](/living/interiors/6-bathrooms-were-coveting-right-now-and-how-to-get-the-look/27891308_686748098382004_984656278066298880_n.jpg)


While minimalism is all about eschewing the unnecessary, Marie Kondo would totally approve of you bringing a little greenery and nature inside: a few potted plants bring a refreshing and serene energy into your space, instantly transforming your bathroom into an oasis. Head to your neighbourhood garden store and pick out a beautiful plant to spruce things up – it'll pair beautifully with this 2 piece set of sleek planters.
