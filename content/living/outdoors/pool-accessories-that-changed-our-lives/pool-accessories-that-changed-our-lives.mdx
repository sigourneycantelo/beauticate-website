## The Robotic Pool Cleaner That Actually Does What It Says

				
				
				
				
									

***(A proper road test of the Aiper Scuba X1)***

I’ve tested enough “smart” home tech to be deeply sceptical. So when the **[Aiper Scuba X1](https://aiper.store/au/products/aiper-scuba-x1-cordless-robotic-pool-cleaner)** arrived, I had one question: will this actually save us time, or just add another thing to manage?

I can say this now with confidence: it has completely changed how we live with our pool.

The **[Scuba X1](https://aiper.store/au/products/aiper-scuba-x1-cordless-robotic-pool-cleaner)** is cordless, powerful, and genuinely intelligent. It doesn’t just skim the obvious leaves – it cleans everything you don’t see too. Fine sand. Algae. Dust. Hair. Insects. All the micro-debris that standard filters quietly ignore.

What surprised me most wasn’t the suction (which is enormous), but the **[MicroMesh™ ultra-fine filter](https://aiper.store/au/series/accessories)**. It captures the tiny stuff that’s usually responsible for cloudy water and that “is it clean or not?” feeling.

**Translation: the pool looks consistently, confidently clean, without constant chemical intervention or manual scrubbing.**

If you’re currently weighing up functional upgrades versus aesthetic ones, this sits firmly in the “why didn’t we do this sooner?” category – much like many of the luxury pool design decisions we now consider non-negotiable.

## The Bit No One Talks About: The Waterline

				
				
				
				
									

If you have a pool, you know this truth: the waterline is the worst part to clean.

No matter how much you scrub, the grime comes back. It’s awkward. It’s messy. And it usually involves harsh chemicals and getting soaked.

**[The Scuba X1’s](https://aiper.store/au/products/aiper-scuba-x1-cordless-robotic-pool-cleaner)** horizontal waterline cleaning **[(Waveline™ 2.0)](https://aiper.store/au/products/aiper-scuba-x1-cordless-robotic-pool-cleaner) **is where it earns its keep. It actively targets that exact zone, removing algae, oils and buildup without you having to touch it.

For Damo, this was the moment. No more scrubbing. No more complaints. Jorge officially retired.

## The Pool Towel Hack I’m Slightly Too Proud Of

				
				
				
				
									

I went on a journey trying to find the perfect pool towels. I had my heart set on Baina – elevated, editorial, beautiful – but the colours weren’t quite right for our grey-green scheme.

Then, in a plot twist I didn’t see coming, I found the perfect forest green checkered towels on Temu. They’re thick, genuinely absorbent, and somehow look amazing rolled up.

We bought eight and keep them rolled in a basket by the pool, ready for spontaneous swims. No scrambling. No apologising. No one drip-drying on your good cushions.

Rule of thumb: if friends can jump in the pool at a moment’s notice and you’re prepared, you’re doing summer right – a philosophy we also apply to **[effortless hosting](https://www.beauticate.com/living/lifestyle/lazy-girls-guide-summer-entertaining/)** more broadly.
