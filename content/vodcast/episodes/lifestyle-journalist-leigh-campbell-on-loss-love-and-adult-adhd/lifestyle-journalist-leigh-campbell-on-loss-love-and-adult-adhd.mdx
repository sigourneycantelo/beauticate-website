So excited to be back with Season Two of Beautiful Inside by Beauticate!

Our first interview is with a dear friend of mine, Leigh Campbell.

Join us in this deeply personal and heartfelt episode of *Beautiful Inside* as we reconnect with Leigh Campbell, an incredible talent in the beauty and lifestyle media world and someone I’ve had the pleasure of knowing for many years. We were invited into her stunning home, which she shares with her husband Rich and their adorable son Alexander. The setting for our chat couldn’t have been more cosy-sitting together on her bed, we reminisced about our magazine days and delved into some deeper moments that have shaped her journey.

In this episode, Leigh opens up about her childhood and her family's adventurous spirit, providing a window into the experiences that have made her who she is today. We explore her career trajectory in the media industry and how it's been influenced by her recent adult ADHD diagnosis, a revelation that has offered her new insights into her personal and professional life.

Leigh also shares her profound experiences with motherhood and the complexities of managing grief, offering heartfelt reflections on how these moments have shaped her. Our conversation touches on the challenges and joys of balancing a demanding career with family life, and Leigh’s approach to self-care amidst it all. Her authenticity and openness make this discussion both inspiring and deeply relatable.

But that's not all-this conversation was so rich that we couldn't fit it all into one episode! Stay tuned for Part Two next week, where we continue this candid and enlightening discussion.

Whether you're navigating similar challenges or simply looking for an uplifting and genuine conversation, this episode is packed with wisdom, laughter, and the warmth of shared experience. Don’t miss this deep dive into Leigh's world.
