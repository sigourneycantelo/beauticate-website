For our first episode of our Season Three Sacred Spaces Series, sponsored by King Living, we interviewed the brilliant Emma Lane, the creative force behind The Range Estates and the farm in Byron Bay. Emma graciously invited me into her exquisite home, Copperstone, nestled in the picturesque countryside of Bangalow, New South Wales. Copperstone is more than just a home; it’s a sanctuary that celebrates slowness, intentional design, and harmony with nature. Set on 48 acres of pristine landscape, the estate is a seamless blend of European heritage with an authentically Australian touch. Our conversation took place in Emma's beautifully curated bedroom, surrounded by her treasured objects and her loyal dog, Ranger, relaxing between us. In this episode, Emma shares her inspiring journey from her boarding school days to a successful career in advertising, and how she came to create her extraordinary spaces rooted in sustainability and mindful living.
