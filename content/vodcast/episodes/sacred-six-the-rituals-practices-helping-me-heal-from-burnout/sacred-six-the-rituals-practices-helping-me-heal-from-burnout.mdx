At the end of 2023, I hit a wall. Anxiety and burnout forced me to slow down and rebuild, and you can hear more about that in earlier episodes. Out of that experience came Beautiful Inside, a space for honest conversations about mental health, healing, and redefining beauty as something that starts within. In this solo episode I am sharing my current Sacred Six, the six rituals and mindset shifts helping me heal, reset and find balance.

## 1. Creating a Sacred Space

For me, healing starts with environment. I have curated a corner of my home that holds everything I reach for when I want to slow down. It includes my [Infraredi red light](/wellness/biohacking/red-light-therapy-infraredi-review) (here is my [discount code](https://infraredi.com/?sca_ref=6635739.8kg23hwHHr)), my [Dyson Supersonic R](/beauty-style/beauty-tips/dyson-supersonic-r-hair-dryer-review), my Five Minute Journal, and my [Pulsetto device](https://pulsetto-gb.myshopify.com/?sca_ref=7074273.5OdIohWvEy2&utm_source=uppromote&utm_medium=15-affiliate-commission-inf&utm_campaign=sigourney-cantelo) for calming the nervous system.

There are fragrances by [The Ayu](/sigourneys-edit/edit/if-you-like-unique-scents-take-a-whiff-of-these) and [Shemana](/beauty-style/skin-care/think-twice-about-using-a-towel-to-dry-your-face), candles, incense, crystals, prayer beads, and a photo of my grandmother. I also keep tools like my De La Heart lymphatic tool and my Lonvitalite dermaroller close by. The space itself, with a Viola marble benchtop from Allambie Marble Crafts and VJ panels, feels beautiful and intentional. It reminds me daily of the power of ritual and environment in healing.

## 2. Energy Healing with Kellie

I recently completed an eight-week Spiral with [Soul Healing with Kellie](https://www.soulhealingwithkellie.com/). Her own story of trauma and transformation inspired me to dive in, and the process combined emotional clearing, EFT and inner child work.

What it gave me was more openness and less procrastination, the courage to take action, cleared blocks around goal-setting and asking for what I want, and the freedom to dream bigger without fear. It taught me one vital lesson: get out of your own way.

## 3. Spinal Energetics with Dr Sarah Jane

[Dr Sarah Jane](/vodcast/episodes/dr-sarah-jane-on-trauma-transformation-and-trusting-the-body) created [Spinal Energetics](https://spinalenergetics.com/), blending chiropractic, psychology and energy work. In my session she read my pain with uncanny accuracy, and at one point my arm felt like it was on fire. Though my neck pain is ongoing, I saw clearly how physical and emotional healing mirror one another.

![Dr Sarah Jane working with Sigourney during a Spinal Energetics session](/content/vodcast/episodes/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout-1.jpg "A Spinal Energetics session with Dr Sarah Jane.")

## 4. Breath & Sound Healing with Tanya Alijani

Through her Soulchemy method, [Tanya Alijani](/vodcast/episodes/tanya-ali-jalani-on-awakening-mental-health-and-the-human-side-of-healing) combines breathwork and sound frequencies that shift the body into deep relaxation. My intention was to release parenting anxiety, and I left feeling lighter, calmer and transformed.

![Sound healing with crystal bowls in a tropical garden setting](/content/vodcast/episodes/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout-2.jpg "Breath and sound healing.")

## 5. Yin Yoga at Modern Movement

Yin has become my Sunday reset. At [Modern Movement](https://modernmovement.com.au/), teachers Tim and Rose create a spiritual, still space that balances out the intensity of my other practices.

![The calm studio space at Modern Movement](/content/vodcast/episodes/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout-3.jpg "Sunday reset at Modern Movement.")

## 6. Mindset Shifts with Mel Robbins

[Mel Robbins'](https://www.melrobbins.com/) Let Them philosophy and Five Second Rule gave me structure and motivation. They helped me clarify my fitness goals, weights, running and swimming, and reminded me that movement is medicine for body, mind and soul.
