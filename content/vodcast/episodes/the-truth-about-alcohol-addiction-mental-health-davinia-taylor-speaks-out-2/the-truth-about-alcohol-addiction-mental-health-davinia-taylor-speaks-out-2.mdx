In this episode of *Beautiful Inside by Beauticate*, I sat down with the brilliant Davinia Taylor - bestselling author, actress, and biohacking expert - when she was in Sydney visiting from her home in the UK. We dive deep into her incredible health journey, from overcoming addiction to discovering the power of gut health, hormones, and biohacking for longevity. We also cover:

- The unexpected link between gut health and mental wellbeing

- Davinia's go-to biohacks for glowing skin and boundless energy

- Why women must rethink the way they approach diet and exercise

- The simple daily habits she swears by for staying sharp, strong, and happy

This is one of my favourite interviews yet - raw, real, and packed with game-changing insights.
