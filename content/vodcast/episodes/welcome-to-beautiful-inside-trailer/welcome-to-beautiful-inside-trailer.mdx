Beautiful People, I am beyond excited, and quietly freaking out, to share something deeply personal and close to my heart. Beautiful Inside by Beauticate is finally here, and it marks a new chapter for us. This is not just another podcast. It is a journey into what truly makes us beautiful, inside and out.

## Why I Made This Show

Last year, life threw me some challenges that left me feeling like I could not keep up. After years of pushing through, I made the terrifying decision to check myself into a treatment centre to deal with anxiety and some hard things I went through as a kid. It was not easy, but it changed me. That experience became the driving force behind Beautiful Inside, a space where I want to destigmatise mental health and help all of us embrace our vulnerabilities with grace.

## What To Expect

I will be sharing candid stories, my own and those of incredible guests like [Trinny Woodall](/vodcast/episodes/trinny-woodall-on-purpose-pressure-and-picking-yourself-back-up), [Pip Edwards](/vodcast/episodes/pip-edwards-from-perfectionism-to-self-compassion) and [Poppy King](/vodcast/episodes/lipstick-queen-poppy-king-on-the-psychological-impact-of-cosmetics), who have faced their own battles and come out stronger. Together we will cover their mental health journeys, their self-care and daily routines, and the link between inner peace and outer radiance. I will also be talking to experts in aesthetics, longevity, wellness and mindset to uncover their secrets to looking and feeling our best.

What sets Beautiful Inside apart is the way we bring these stories to life. As a video podcast, we film in the spaces that define our guests' lives, from their pantries to their walk-in wardrobes, surrounded by their personal stories and treasures. Look out for our Sacred Six, the products and practices our guests swear by for inner and outer wellbeing.

## Come Join Me

This is a new era for Beauticate, where we keep celebrating resilience, authenticity and the courage to live boldly. Thank you for being part of this community. I hope Beautiful Inside will inspire, empower and uplift you, just as it has me.

So grab your favourite beverage and join me on this beautiful journey inward. Do not forget to subscribe and leave a review on Apple Podcasts, it means the world to me.

With gratitude and excitement,

Sigourney xx
