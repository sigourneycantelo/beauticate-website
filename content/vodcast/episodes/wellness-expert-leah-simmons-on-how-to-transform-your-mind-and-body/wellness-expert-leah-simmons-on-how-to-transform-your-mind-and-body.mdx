Welcome back to Beautiful Inside. Today I am so excited to be joined by the incredible [Leah Simmons](https://www.instagram.com/iamleahsimmons/), a powerhouse in the wellness world and the founder of [KAAIAA](https://kaaiaa.com/), a revolutionary workout that blends breathwork, bodywork, meditation and mindset. There is so much more to Leah than fitness, and this is one of those conversations that stays with you long after it ends.

## From London's Music Scene to Wellness

Before becoming a wellness pioneer, Leah was living it up in London's vibrant music scene, working for a record label and soaking up every experience. Her path has not always been smooth. In our chat she opens up about her struggles with authenticity, how social media once became her crutch, and the bold steps she took to turn it all around.

We dive deep into her story of resilience and self-discovery, and how she now uses challenges as fuel for growth rather than reasons to hold back.

## The Magic of KAAIAA

I first got a taste of Leah's magic during lockdown, when she hosted free [KAAIAA](https://kaaiaa.com/) classes on Zoom. Her infectious energy and those feel-good 80s and 90s dance vibes were just the lift we all needed.

What makes KAAIAA different is that it is not only about the body. It weaves together breathwork, bodywork, meditation and mindset, so you leave a class feeling stronger and clearer, not just worked out. It is movement designed to transform how you feel from the inside.

## Living More Authentically

This episode is packed with inspiration for anyone wanting to live more authentically and embrace holistic wellbeing. Leah is honest about the pressure of performing online and about choosing a more genuine way of showing up, both in her work and in her life.

If you are ready for a conversation about resilience, self-discovery and finding your way back to yourself, settle in and let's dive in.
