[Jane Wurwand](https://www.instagram.com/janewurwand/) is not just the founder of [Dermalogica](https://www.dermalogica.com.au), she is a force of nature. A visionary in the beauty industry, she built a billion-dollar global skincare empire while championing financial independence for women. Behind the success is a deeply personal story of resilience, reinvention and sheer determination.

In this episode of *Beautiful Inside by Beauticate*, I sat down with Jane at Dermalogica's headquarters in LA to hear her extraordinary journey firsthand. She opened up about losing her father at just two years old, growing up in Scotland with a single mother who instilled in her an unshakable drive for independence, and the challenges she faced as a young woman in an industry dominated by men.

## From Scotland to a Billion-Dollar Brand

Jane shares how she escaped a toxic marriage, started over in South Africa, and later took a bold leap to California, arriving with little more than a plan, unstoppable determination and a vision that would change the beauty world forever.

She founded Dermalogica and [The International Dermal Institute](https://dermalinstitute.com/) from the ground up, and grew it into the number one professional skincare brand in the world. Her story is proof that the things you think are your biggest setbacks can become the tools that build everything.

## How Adversity Became Fuel

Throughout our conversation Jane explains how adversity shaped her work ethic and why she has never seen hardship as something to hide from. She talks candidly about reframing trauma and setbacks, and turning them into stepping stones rather than roadblocks.

It is a theme she explores in her book, [Skin in the Game](https://www.amazon.com/Skin-Game-Everything-Already-Inside/dp/1400224306), a transparent account of how the traits and experiences you might consider your weaknesses can become your greatest strengths.

## Why Financial Empowerment Matters

Financial empowerment for women sits at the very heart of Dermalogica. Jane explains that her personal experience of navigating life without financial security made it central to everything she built, from the brand's culture to its education programmes.

For Jane, helping women earn, save and stand on their own two feet is not a side project. It is the reason the business exists.

## Intuition, Magical Thinking and Daily Rituals

We also dive into the power of intuition, the role of magical thinking in success, and the daily rituals that keep Jane grounded while leading a global brand. She shares how visualisation has guided some of her biggest decisions, and why she trusts her instincts even when the path is not yet clear.

This is a conversation about far more than skincare. It is about reinvention, resilience and the courage to bet on yourself. If you have ever felt stuck, faced personal or professional setbacks, or wondered how to build something truly meaningful, this episode is packed with insight and inspiration.
