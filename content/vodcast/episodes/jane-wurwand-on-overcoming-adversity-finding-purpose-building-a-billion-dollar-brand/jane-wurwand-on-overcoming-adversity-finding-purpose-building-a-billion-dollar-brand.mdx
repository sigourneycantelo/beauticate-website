Jane Wurwand is not just the founder of Dermalogica—she’s a force of nature. A visionary in the beauty industry, she built a billion-dollar global skincare empire while championing financial independence for women. But behind her success is a deeply personal story of resilience, reinvention, and sheer determination.

In this episode of *Beautiful Inside by Beauticate*, I sat down with Jane at Dermalogica’s headquarters in LA to hear her extraordinary journey firsthand. She opened up about losing her father at just two years old, growing up in Scotland with a single mother who instilled in her an unshakable drive for independence, and the challenges she faced as a young woman navigating an industry dominated by men.

Jane shares how she escaped a toxic marriage, started over in South Africa, and later took a bold leap to California—arriving with nothing but a plan, unstoppable determination, and a vision that would change the beauty world forever. She explains how adversity shaped her work ethic, why financial empowerment is at the heart of Dermalogica, and the mindset shifts that helped her turn setbacks into stepping stones.

We also dive into the power of intuition, the role of **magical thinking** in success, and the daily rituals that keep Jane grounded while leading a global brand. Her story is one of reinvention, courage, and unwavering belief in the power of purpose-driven business.

If you’ve ever felt stuck, faced personal or professional setbacks, or wondered how to truly build something meaningful, this episode is packed with insight and inspiration.

**What You’ll Learn in This Episode:**

	•	How childhood adversity shaped Jane’s drive and independence

	•	The bold decisions that led to the creation of Dermalogica

	•	How to reframe trauma and setbacks into fuel for success

	•	The power of visualization and intuition in business and life

	•	Jane’s daily rituals for balance, resilience, and creative thinking

This is a conversation about more than just skincare—it’s about **reinvention, resilience, and the courage to bet on yourself.**
