Welcome back to Beautiful Inside. Today I am chatting with the fabulous Jodie Matthews, founder of [ashaya](https://www.instagram.com/ashaya_rituals/). This episode is extra special as it ties into Menopause Awareness Month, something I have personally been navigating as I enter perimenopause.

## On Entering Perimenopause

I have had physical symptoms, from anxiety to gastrointestinal issues, that I did not even realise were related to peri. That is part of what makes this life stage so tricky. The signs do not always announce themselves as hormonal, so they can go unrecognised for a long time.

I caught up with Jodie recently and was really inspired by how she has approached this new phase in her life. I wanted to get a different perspective on dealing with peri and menopause, and she was the perfect person to ask.

![Jodie Matthews, founder of ashaya](/content/vodcast/episodes/talking-perimenopause-with-ashaya-founder-jodie-matthews/talking-perimenopause-with-ashaya-founder-jodie-matthews-1.jpg "Jodie Matthews, founder of ashaya.")

## Why Jodie Built ashaya

I have known Jodie for years, from when she was heading up MAC Cosmetics, and she has always been someone who leads with passion and purpose. In this episode she opens up about the struggles she faced, and how those personal experiences drove her to start [ashaya](https://ashaya.com.au/), a brand that is all about supporting women through every stage of life.

From the mental health side to the physical transformations, we dive deep into how we can all navigate these changes with knowledge, compassion, and a bit of humour. If you would like to go deeper on this, Jodie returned for a second conversation in [ashaya founder Jodie Matthews on perimenopause and Ayurveda](/vodcast/episodes/ashaya-founder-jodie-matthews-on-perimenopause-and-ayurveda).
