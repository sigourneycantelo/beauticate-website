Welcome to my interview with the remarkable Trinny Woodall. Trinny's life has been devoted to style and beauty: first as the co-host of the hit show *What Not To Wear* and now as the founder of Trinny London.

At 60, Trinny is a social media force, sharing daily outfits, workouts, and beauty tips. Her loyal Trinny Tribe adores her, and she champions women over 40, giving them the attention they deserve.

Despite her success, Trinny's journey wasn't easy. In her twenties, she struggled with addiction, but after two stints in rehab and years of daily NA meetings, she has been sober ever since. She also tragically lost her ex-husband and the father of her child, Lyla, a few years ago to suicide.

In this episode, Trinny shares candid stories, from her early makeovers and entrepreneurial ventures to dealing with acne and the emotional toll of 16 rounds of IVF before having her daughter, Lyla. Her honesty and energy are inspiring, and her reflections offer valuable lessons in resilience and self-care.
