I am the kind of person who will try almost anything once in the name of feeling better. Some of it sticks, most of it does not. This is a solo episode, just me, talking through the handful of practices and products that have earned a permanent place in my life. I call them my Sacred Six. None of them are quick fixes. They are the small, daily things doing the quiet work, on my mind, my skin and my nervous system. Here is what is actually working.

## 1. Hypnotherapy for the Mind

Everything starts in the head for me. When my thoughts are racing, nothing else lands. Hypnotherapy was the practice I was most sceptical about and it has become the one I lean on most. It is not the swinging watch you imagine. It is a deep, guided relaxation that lets you speak to the part of your brain that holds the old stories, the anxiety, the patterns you cannot seem to think your way out of. I come out of a session feeling like someone has wiped the windscreen clean.

## 4. Diet Shifts That Stuck

I have made every dietary mistake going, the crash diets, the cleanses, the rules so strict I lasted a fortnight. What works now is far less dramatic. More protein, more plants, less of the processed stuff I used to reach for without thinking. No perfection, just better defaults. The shift was less about losing anything and more about giving my body the raw materials to actually function. My energy is steadier and my skin tells the truth about what I eat.

## 6. Longevity, the Thread That Ties It Together

The sixth one is less a product and more a frame of mind. I have stopped chasing how I look for the season ahead and started thinking in decades. How do I want to move, think and feel at seventy. Every other practice on this list answers to that question now. Longevity is not about living forever. It is about staying well enough to enjoy the life you have built. That is the version of beauty I am interested in, the kind that starts on the inside.
