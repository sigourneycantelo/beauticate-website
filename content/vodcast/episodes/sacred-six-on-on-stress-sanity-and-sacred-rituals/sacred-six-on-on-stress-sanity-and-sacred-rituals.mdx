I am writing this with a knot in my shoulder and a to-do list that refuses to end. Over the past year I have been quietly building a whole new chapter of Beauticate. It has been exciting, creative and deeply fulfilling. It has also, if I am honest, been the most stressful stretch of my life.

So in this solo Sacred Six episode I am not theorising. I am handing over the six rituals, tools and practices that have actually kept me grounded, regulated my nervous system and protected my wellbeing through long workdays, chronic pain, anxiety and the kind of overwhelm that creeps up before you notice it.

If you have been feeling stretched too thin, sleeping badly or simply trying to hold everything together, this one is for you. Small rituals, I have learned, do the heavy lifting.

## 2. Walks, Friendship and Community

When my head is loudest, the answer is rarely to be alone with it. A walk with a friend does what no app can. We move, we talk, we offload, and somewhere around the second kilometre everything feels more manageable. The research on community and mental health is clear, but you feel it long before you read it. We started a [Beauticate Walk and Talk WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/GdEee0VDgPKDh2ld7KVr9f?s=cl&p=i&ilr=2) for exactly this, so come and walk with us.

## 3. Breathwork That Shifts Everything

I came to breathwork sceptical and left a convert. A single 9D session with [Brooke Stephenson at Luxe Wellness Collective](https://www.luxewellness.com.au/) moved something I had been carrying for months. Your breath is the fastest, cheapest, most portable tool you own for changing your state, and most of us are barely using it.
