In this deeply honest episode of Beautiful Inside by Beauticate, Sigourney sits down with scientist, beauty entrepreneur and founder of Synergie Skin, Terri Vinson Jones , to talk about her personal and professional transformations—from battling bulimia and self-doubt to finding purpose, power, and peace. Terri opens up about the pain of eating disorders , navigating divorce , and overcoming imposter syndrome as a woman in science and business. She also shares her struggles with anxiety, OCD, and self-sabotage , and how therapy, self-awareness, and deep spiritual work helped her grow. This is a must-watch for anyone who’s ever felt not enough—or wanted to find their way back to self-worth and authentic success. Sigourney and Terri explore: How shame around food and perfectionism led to years of silence Navigating post-divorce anxiety and depression while running a business Her suspected ADHD and the emotional toll of overthinking Letting go of imposter syndrome in male-dominated science spaces Why therapy, vulnerability, and self-compassion changed everything Terri’s story is a heartfelt reminder that beauty isn’t skin deep—and success doesn’t mean perfection.
