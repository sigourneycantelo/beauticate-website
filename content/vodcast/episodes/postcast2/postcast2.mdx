*"I grew up in Argentina, and had a very fun childhood that revolved around nature, sports and, most of all, spending time with family.*

I believe my early years shaped my life in a tremendous way and helped me develop into the person I am today. That is especially true when it comes to the importance I place on wellness, staying active, and my loved ones.*Leaving my family behind in Argentina and travelling the world was a very challenging time for me.*

That eventually meant moving to the US to work on my dreams, which was scary as I knew absolutely no one. However, it was ultimately an amazing experience I would never take for granted.

Knowing I had my family’s support made an enormous difference in my outlook. I think some key factors in how we move through challenges are the passage of time, gratitude for the good and the bad, and having patience and perseverance.*I never had a specific career goal, but thought my work would ultimately involve entrepreneurship.*

I think landing in the world of wellness was very much shaped by all my travels, and learning from different people and different cultures.

I feel so glad that my job allows for me to learn, create and innovate. The worst part about what is otherwise a wonderful career and life is that I am still based so far away from my beloved family.*I start every morning with my maté, the traditional South American herbal drink, and have breakfast with my baby Rio before he goes to school. *

Then I do my skincare routine, take my Enlighten from Kapowder and then off to the gym I go!*My skin regime varies a bit depending on the amount of time I have.*

I usually start with [Tatcha The Rice Polish](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/tatcha/the-rice-polish-gentle-I-049784/?gad_source=1&gclid=CjwKCAiA_tuuBhAUEiwAvxkgTg3tkb7sFZl05jj6Xpztb6JGUMrrCP4dNr8qVdHiXYo-tkpwab0zPRoCBsgQAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds), then apply a hydrating mask, Vital Hydra Solution, from Dr. Jart. I like to keep these in the fridge for a cooling effect. Once that’s removed, I spray my face with [Mario Badescu Facial Spray With Aloe, Herbs & Rosewater](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/mario-badescu/facial-spray-with-aloe-herbs-rosewater-V-004680/), and pop on some Auteur Definitive Eye Cream, which is one of my ultimate favourite products.

I love when my face glows and looks very glazed, so I like to use the [C.E.O. Glow Vitamin C + Turmeric Oil from Sunday Riley](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/sunday-riley/ceo-glow-vitamin-c-turmeric-oil-V-037324/). Then I move on to my Definitive Restoration Cream from Auteur, which is another great product of theirs. It locks in all the hydration. When it comes to face creams, what is key for me is 'the thicker the better'!

Last but most important is SPF – I like a tinted one, and alternate between [Heliocare 360 Color Gel Oil-Free SPF50+](https://www.caretobeauty.com/au/heliocare-360-color-gel-oil-free-spf50/) and [ISDIN Fotoprotector Fusion Water Magic SPF50](https://www.caretobeauty.com/au/isdin-fotoprotector-fusion-water-spf50-50ml/?gad_source=1&gclid=CjwKCAiA_tuuBhAUEiwAvxkgTnio-dD3tmbW8oL0M7Z5wzF1vVIOBMneihXm3Vu7N96nU53VwsXFqxoCUMEQAvD_BwE).*My mom has a very simple approach to beauty. She was never into makeup products or skincare routines. I think I have showed her all the secrets she has, haha!*

For my everyday face, I like to keep it easy and start with a tinted SPF. I then put on a little bit of Benefit's Hoola Matte Bronzer, curl my lashes, brush up my brows and use a little bit of the Benefit Benetint Lip & Cheek Tint as a blush. Then I finish off with my Dior Addict Lip Glow Oil.

If I am going out at night, I will take things up a notch. I love to use my [Chanel Healthy Glow Foundation Hydration and Longwear](https://bit.ly/3USB3JU), as it’s light and feels so smooth and beautiful on the skin. I add in my [NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/nars/radiant-creamy-concealer-cafe-au-lait-I-039937/?gad_source=1&gclid=CjwKCAiA_tuuBhAUEiwAvxkgTlFjRW5Vat5u8RR9rDPbNRQ2Xr93qGkAU9QA-qTHvabJEjypt0x2ShoCvPgQAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds) to cover any little blemishes. I find the the [Hourglass Ambient Lighting Palette](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/hourglass/ambient-lighting-palette-I-017068/?gad_source=1&gclid=CjwKCAiA_tuuBhAUEiwAvxkgTr5WtJsXsRD9vRWS4Y_KcnmQgAp80vaqQKqkYQkTIPdMwD9W3ToY4xoCqi8QAvD_BwE&gclsrc=aw.ds) so useful, as it comes into play for my contouring and even as eyeshadow.

Estée Lauder Sumptuous Extreme Mascara is my holy grail for lashes. For the lips, the lip liner I use the most right now is the Make Up For Ever Artist Color Pencil. I’m currently obsessed with Givenchy Rose Perfecto Liquid Lip Balm – it’s so hydrating, plus it doesn’t feel sticky which is great.*Usually when hairdressers see my hair they assume it’s thin, but once they start working with it they realise that there's a lot of it and it's actually very thick and full. *

I do lots of hair masks, mainly from [Briogeo](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/briogeo/?page=1&filters=form%3AMask) and Amika, as well as the MOROCCANOIL Restorative Hair Mask. I also love my Moroccanoil Brumes Du Maroc Hair & Body Mist – I actually never leave home without it.*I think when you feel good inside you feel the best outside.*

I love to cook; nothing brings me more joy. Not many people know this about me, but I could spend *hours* in the supermarket – seriously, a very excessive amount! It’s pretty much my favourite place to be, haha.

I eat a lot, minimum five meals a day. I very much enjoy a good meal, whether it’s fish, chicken, a delicious steak with veggies… I’m getting hungry just talking about it. And of course I love to use my favourite products from Kapowder, which are Enlighten and our probiotic/biotin Saviour.

I also enjoy going for long walks, doing Pilates, and just staying active overall.

We are (hopefully!) all going to grow older, and I want to enjoy the process."*Story by Zoe Briggs. Imagery provided by Valentina Ferrer.*

## Frequently Asked Questions

![Valentina 0](/vodcast/episodes/postcast2/Valentina-0-1.jpg)


**Q: Who is Valentina Ferrer?**
A: Valentina Ferrer is an Argentinian model, former Miss Argentina, and wellness entrepreneur who moved to the US to pursue her career. She is the mother of son Rio, a wellness product advocate, and passionate about cooking, staying active with Pilates and long walks, and eating a diet of at least five meals a day.

**Q: What is Valentina Ferrer's skincare routine?**
A: Valentina starts with Tatcha The Rice Polish, then applies Dr. Jart Vital Hydra Solution mask kept in the fridge for a cooling effect. She follows with Mario Badescu Facial Spray, Auteur Definitive Eye Cream, Sunday Riley C.E.O. Glow Vitamin C and Turmeric Oil, Auteur Definitive Restoration Cream, and finishes with a tinted SPF, alternating between Heliocare 360 and ISDIN Fotoprotector.

**Q: What makeup does Valentina Ferrer wear every day?**
A: For an everyday look, Valentina starts with a tinted SPF, adds Benefit Hoola Matte Bronzer, curls her lashes, brushes her brows, uses Benefit Benetint as a blush, and finishes with Dior Addict Lip Glow Oil. For nights out she adds Chanel Healthy Glow Foundation, NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer, Hourglass Ambient Lighting Palette, and Estee Lauder Sumptuous Extreme Mascara.
