There is a particular kind of glow that comes from someone who genuinely loves how they live, and Valentina Ferrer has it in spades. The Argentinian model and former Miss Argentina swapped her childhood of nature, sport and big family lunches for a new life in the US, and somewhere along the way she became a wellness entrepreneur and co-founder of the supplement brand Kapowder too. In this episode of Beautiful Inside she lets us into her mornings, her glazed skin, her easy everyday makeup and the simple philosophy that ties it all together: when you feel good inside, you feel the best outside.

## From Argentina To Building A Brand

"I grew up in Argentina, and had a very fun childhood that revolved around nature, sports and, most of all, spending time with family," Valentina tells us. "I believe my early years shaped my life in a tremendous way and helped me develop into the person I am today. That is especially true when it comes to the importance I place on wellness, staying active, and my loved ones."

Leaving all of that behind was the hard part. "Moving to the US to work on my dreams was scary, as I knew absolutely no one. However, it was ultimately an amazing experience I would never take for granted." Knowing she had her family's support back home made an enormous difference, and she credits the passage of time, gratitude for the good and the bad, and plain patience and perseverance with carrying her through.

She never had a fixed career goal, but always sensed her path would involve entrepreneurship. "Landing in the world of wellness was very much shaped by all my travels, and learning from different people and different cultures. I feel so glad that my job allows me to learn, create and innovate." The only downside of an otherwise wonderful life, she says, is still being based so far from her beloved family.

## How She Starts The Day

"I start every morning with my mate, the traditional South American herbal drink, and have breakfast with my baby Rio before he goes to school." Then comes skincare, her Enlighten from Kapowder, and off to the gym she goes.

## Her Glazed Skincare Routine

Valentina's regime shifts depending on how much time she has, but the goal is always the same: glow. "I love when my face looks very glazed."

She usually begins with [Tatcha The Rice Polish](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/tatcha/the-rice-polish-gentle-I-049784/), then applies a hydrating Vital Hydra Solution mask from Dr. Jart. "I like to keep these in the fridge for a cooling effect." Once that comes off she spritzes [Mario Badescu Facial Spray With Aloe, Herbs and Rosewater](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/mario-badescu/facial-spray-with-aloe-herbs-rosewater-V-004680/), then pats on Auteur Definitive Eye Cream, one of her ultimate favourites.

For that glaze she reaches for [Sunday Riley C.E.O. Glow Vitamin C and Turmeric Oil](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/sunday-riley/ceo-glow-vitamin-c-turmeric-oil-V-037324/), then locks everything in with Auteur Definitive Restoration Cream. "When it comes to face creams, what is key for me is the thicker the better." Last but most important is SPF. She likes a tinted one and alternates between [Heliocare 360 Color Gel Oil-Free SPF50+](https://www.caretobeauty.com/au/heliocare-360-color-gel-oil-free-spf50/) and [ISDIN Fotoprotector Fusion Water Magic SPF50](https://www.caretobeauty.com/au/isdin-fotoprotector-fusion-water-spf50-50ml/).

## Everyday Makeup, Night-Out Makeup

Valentina keeps her daytime face simple, a habit she traces back to her mum. "My mom has a very simple approach to beauty. I think I have showed her all the secrets she has, haha."

By day she starts with a tinted SPF, adds a little Benefit Hoola Matte Bronzer, curls her lashes, brushes up her brows, dabs on Benefit Benetint as a blush and finishes with Dior Addict Lip Glow Oil. For nights out she takes it up a notch with [Chanel Healthy Glow Foundation Hydration and Longwear](https://bit.ly/3USB3JU), [NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/nars/radiant-creamy-concealer-cafe-au-lait-I-039937/) and the [Hourglass Ambient Lighting Palette](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/hourglass/ambient-lighting-palette-I-017068/), which doubles as contour and eyeshadow. Estee Lauder Sumptuous Extreme Mascara is her holy grail for lashes, and right now she is reaching for the Make Up For Ever Artist Color Pencil and the Givenchy Rose Perfecto Liquid Lip Balm, which she loves for being hydrating without the stickiness.

As for her hair, which is far thicker than it looks, she does plenty of masks from [Briogeo](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/briogeo/?page=1&filters=form%3AMask) and Amika, plus the MOROCCANOIL Restorative Hair Mask. She never leaves home without her Moroccanoil Brumes Du Maroc Hair and Body Mist.

## Feeling Good Inside

"I think when you feel good inside you feel the best outside," Valentina says, and food is a huge part of that. "I love to cook, nothing brings me more joy. Not many people know this about me, but I could spend hours in the supermarket. It is pretty much my favourite place to be, haha."

She eats a lot, a minimum of five meals a day, whether it is fish, chicken or a good steak with veggies, and rounds it out with her favourite Kapowder products, Enlighten and the probiotic and biotin Saviour. Add in long walks, Pilates and staying active overall, and you have her recipe for wellbeing. "We are, hopefully, all going to grow older, and I want to enjoy the process."
