In this “Sacred Six” episode of Beautiful Inside by Beauticate, I’m coming to you from the stunning Somatheeram Ayurvedic Resort in Kerala, India, to share six transformative experiences and takeaways from my time here. From ancient wellness practices to the slower, traditional pace of life, India has offered me a new perspective on mindfulness, balance, and beauty-inside and out.

I experienced the power of Ayurveda, explored breathtaking temples, savoured incredible food, shopped for silks and jewellery, and embraced a sense of calm and introspection through yoga and mindfulness. These moments have reminded me of the importance of disconnecting, slowing down, and connecting with the sacred-both within ourselves and in our surroundings.

Join me as I share simple yet profound ways to bring these practices into your own life, wherever you are.Thanks to our season sponsors, King Living, for helping us create spaces that nourish the soul.
