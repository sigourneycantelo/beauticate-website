In this episode of the podcast, we chat with the incredibly funny and unfiltered Amy Gerard. From her viral parenting videos to her successful podcasts and book, Amy has built a career on sharing her life’s raw, relatable, and often hilarious moments.

Amy opens up about:

- Her rise to fame through social media and her transition to hosting two hit podcasts, *Beyond the Chaos* and *Beyond the Likes*.

- The deeply personal journey of buying and renovating the home she grew up in-a house that tragically burned down in the 1994 bushfires.

- How she turned her family’s past into a stunning, nature-inspired space filled with love and memories.

- The highs and lows of parenting, marriage, and managing anxiety, along with the humor that helps her navigate it all.

- Her work as a weekend radio host for Kiss FM and the inspiration behind her laugh-out-loud memoir, *Strap Yourself In*.

This episode is equal parts entertaining and inspiring, packed with stories that will make you laugh, cry, and marvel at Amy’s resilience and wit.
