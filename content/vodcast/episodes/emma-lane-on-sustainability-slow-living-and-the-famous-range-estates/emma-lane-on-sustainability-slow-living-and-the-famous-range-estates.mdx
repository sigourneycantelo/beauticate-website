Join me for an enchanting episode featuring the brilliant Emma Lane, the creative force behind Range Estates and the farm in Byron Bay. In this special interview, Emma graciously invited me into her exquisite home, Copperstone, nestled in the picturesque countryside of Bangalore, New South Wales. From the moment I drove up the long driveway, it felt like I was transported to another world—almost like the serene hills of Tuscany.

Copperstone is more than just a home; it’s a sanctuary that celebrates slowness, intentional design, and harmony with nature. Set on 48 acres of pristine landscape, the estate is a seamless blend of European heritage with an authentically Australian touch. Our conversation took place in Emma's beautifully curated bedroom, surrounded by her treasured objects and her loyal dog, Ranger, relaxing nearby. The scene was complete with perfect weather, the laughter of her children, and the tranquility of Copperstone embracing us.

In this episode, Emma shares her inspiring journey from her boarding school days to a successful career in advertising, and how she came to create her extraordinary spaces rooted in sustainability and mindful living. If you’re looking for a dose of inspiration drawn from the power of family, nature, and the beauty of thoughtful design, this conversation is for you.
