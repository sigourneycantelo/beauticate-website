She's sold millions of books, shared the stage with Oprah, and helped people around the world break cycles of shame, anxiety, and fear. But Gabby Bernstein's story is far more than just manifesting dream lives. It's about choosing again and again to live with purpose.

In this episode of *Beautiful Inside*, Gabby reveals her four-step method for rewiring your beliefs, the real reason manifesting isn't about doing more, and how shame and protection mechanisms block our ability to truly receive. She also shares the turning point of getting sober at 25, and how choosing inner peace became the foundation of everything that followed.

We talk recovery, resilience, and the rituals that keep her grounded - from twice-daily non-sleep deep rest meditation to tuning in with divine direction. This conversation is a must-listen for anyone seeking more alignment, self-compassion and spiritual clarity.

**What You'll Learn in This Episode:**

- The truth about manifesting - and why it's not about doing more

- Gabby's four-step method for rewiring limiting beliefs

- How shame blocks your intuition and power

- Her personal story of recovery, transformation and healing

- Simple ways to access your intuition and co-create your life

- The exact routine she uses to stay aligned and attract with ease
