In this raw and redemptive episode, **Olivia Arezzolo** - sleep expert, author, and wellness advocate - opens up about her extraordinary journey from despair to deep healing.

From a lonely childhood and years of depression, bullying, and eating disorders that saw her hospitalised, Olivia's story is one of survival, awakening, and self-love. She shares the pivotal moment she decided *"I deserve more than this"* and the practices, therapies, and spiritual experiences that helped her rebuild her life - from intensive group therapy and meditation to a life-changing Ayahuasca journey in Costa Rica.

Now a respected voice in holistic health, Olivia talks candidly with **Sigourney** about turning pain into purpose, the science of sleep, and her daily rituals for staying grounded, productive, and present. Expect a deeply human conversation about recovery, routine, and the radical act of choosing yourself - every single day.

**In this episode:**

- How early trauma and family dynamics shaped Olivia's fierce independence

- Her teenage battle with depression, bulimia, and anorexia - and what finally helped her recover

- The therapeutic breakthroughs that rewired her relationship with herself

- The Ayahuasca experience that transformed her outlook on love, fear, and purpose

- Her science-backed sleep and morning rituals (including why she swears by a *three-hour morning routine*)

- How to build true wellness from the inside out
