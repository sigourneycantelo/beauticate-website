### “I wasn’t allowed to wear makeup when I was younger.

Maybe that’s why I’m so obsessed with it now. When I went to school – I don’t know if my parents even know this – I used to leave the house in the morning with no makeup on and sneak to my best friend’s house and we’d put eyeliner on. I’d put it right on the inner corner and that’s all I’d wear. Then I’d quickly wash it off before they would get home. Now, on the weekends I try to wear nothing, or just mascara – it’s good to give your face a break. After a long day working when I’ve got a full face of makeup, the first thing I want to do is wash my face, lather moisturiser on and just hang out.

KSENIJA, WHO WASN'T ALLOWED MAKEUP WHEN SHE WAS YOUNGER, LOVES A BARE FACE NOW

A MAKEUP LOVER, KSENIJA LOVES A GOOD YOUTUBE TUTORIAL TO UP HER SKILLS

### I still watch YouTube tutorials on how to do makeup…

…because I love it, I love seeing how people do different things. If I could do a winged eyeliner like Chloe Morello I think I’d be set! My friends would come over for sleepovers and I’d go, ‘Can I do your makeup?’ I’d do the worst job. We have photos of it and it was shocking. I’ve always really loved playing [with makeup] and when I started modelling I was always so interested in what the makeup artist was doing to me.

### I always buy two colours of foundation…

…because I change colour so much; I’ve either got a spray tan or I’m pale, so I mix to make sure I have the right colour. It’s good practise – you’re not going to be the same colour all year. For proper foundation, Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk is the best foundation. I don’t want to admit how many bottles I’ve been through. And Rae Morris Radiance Brush is the best brush in the world. When I’m on camera, the It Cosmetics Bye Bye Under Eye is a really nice concealer. It covers everything and is very thick, so I only put a little bit as a triangle under my eye and pat it in.

GIORGIO ARMANI LUMINOUS SILK FOUNDATION, MAC STROBE CREAM AND RAE MORRIS BRUSHES FILL HER MAKEUP KIT

KSENIJA LOVES THE CLAIRSONIC FOR SLOUGHING OFF THE DAYS FACE

### I’m very simple when it comes to skincare.

I have this amazing beautician in Double Bay that does my facials, [Isabelle Almeida](http://isabel-almeida.com.au/), she’s ah-mazing. She’s old school – she used to do Miranda Kerr’s facials. She recommended a cleanser, Jan Marini, and I use it everyday. I used to have all these under-the-skin pimples and this cleared it up – it’s amazing. I also use the [GlamGlow Mask](http://bit.ly/1qip5bd) and the Embroylisse Lait Crème Concentrate is the nicest moisturiser. Then twice a week I use a Clarisonic.

### I don’t like it when [my hair] looks too perfect.

I wash my hair with a Kevin Murphy Plumping Shampoo. When it’s still wet, I put this O&M Rootalicious Mousse or a Kevin Murphy one through it and let it dry naturally overnight to get a bit of texture. When I tong it, I always spray it with [De Lorenzo Sandstorm](http://bit.ly/1TuMly1) or [L’Oreal Elnett](http://bit.ly/25FA66u) and then I’ll tong and brush it out – it leaves a bit of grit.

KSENIJA FAVOURS BEAUTIFUL FRAGRANCE

### I really like smells that aren’t super feminine.

I wear Tom Ford Black Orchid. I’ve had it for a while now. I like that it’s a little bit androgynous. I love candles, as you can probably tell around my house – there's like ten! I love a nice candle – I love the way someone walks in and goes, ‘It smells good [in here]’. I love the Ecoya Violet, Fig and Cassis.

…and with my mum in the kitchen cooking. I love cooking something for someone and they go, ‘That’s delicious!’ I love making people feel good, I love entertaining. We have our friends over all the time. For New Years Eve this year we didn’t go out we just had dinner here. I made whole baked snapper and salad and potatoes and a cheeseboard. ![Ksenija21](/content/interviews/actors-presenters/ksenija-lukich-tv-host/Ksenija21-1.jpg)


VINTAGE CAMPBELLS FILL THE KITCHEN, WHERE KSENIJA LOVES PUTTING TOGETHER AMAZING MEALS

BREAD, PASTA AND CARBS ARE ALL ON THE MENU

### I love bread and I really love gluten.

When we’re home, my fiancé and I will cook. It’s pretty simple; I do roast chooks, lots of meat, lots of fish and lots of salad. I eat everything. I have no qualms with food, nothing makes me feel ill. Some people feel unwell when they eat gluten, I personally feel fabulous! It’s delicious! Obviously I don’t eat pasta every night, but occasionally I love a Fratelli pasta or I like to cook pasta. I’m Serbian, I think it influences my appetite! It has a lot to do with the way you’re brought up, and for me…

### I never used to like exercise…

…but my fiancé has always exercised a lot and in the last two years, I have started exercising four to five times a week and I love it. I feel so much better. I train with Transform Health; he’s a personal trainer called Jordan Ponder. I train down at the park or at his gym with a lot of circuit and body weight training and he’s incredible. He’s transformed me – I’m so much stronger and fitter. He trains a lot of models (Nic Trunfio, [Jess Gomes](https://www.beauticate.com/who/jessica-gomes-model?rq=Jessica%20Gomes) and VS model Bridget Malcolm) so he gets the lean thing. Then I do yoga twice a week at [BodyMindLife](http://www.bodymindlife.com/). I also do circuit training with Dan – we go to the park or beach or go for a run along Bondi.

KSENIJA LOVES TO KEEP FIT, TRAINING AT TRANSFORM HEALTH

A MIX OF SEXY KNEE HIGH BOOTS AND KITSCH DISNEY LAND ESPADRILLES MAKE UP KSENIJA'S ECLECTIC WARDROBE

### I’m really into Australian brands.

I’m wearing a lot of Camilla and Marc. I think I like to be a little bit chic with tailored pants, a high heel and a cropped top but I think overall my style is relatively pretty, with some colour and a little bit sexy. I bought knee-high suede boots in LA – they make me feel like a superhero. But, it’s not all sexy. I’m most obsessed with a pair of Mara and Mine espadrilles from Disneyland, Florida my friend (the designer) sent me.”
