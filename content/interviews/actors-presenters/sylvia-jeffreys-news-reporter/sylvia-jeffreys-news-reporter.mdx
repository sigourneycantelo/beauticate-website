### "My favourite Prada cross-body bag was just stolen…

…on my European trip when our hire car was taken. It was a gift from Pete. All of my cards were inside, but I was more devastated about the bag though, obviously. My Celine sunglasses and Tom Ford Jasmine Rouge Perfume were taken as well. It’s so hard to find that Prada [bag] online so I might have to find a different one. Turns out I wasn’t covered for the possessions in the vehicle because of some fine print loophole so I’m pretty devastated.

### I give myself 20 minutes to get ready in the morning.

I shower and change, that’s it. I spend 20 minutes at work reading the papers. It’s like cramming for an exam every morning. I flick through the Daily Tele, The Australian and SMH, and then I go online look at news.com and just flick around to get a feel for what they’re leading with. We look at Buzzfeed, Twitter and things like that as were also covering Internet trends.

### I’m up at 3:15am and in the office at 4am.

I set two alarms two minutes apart and I always hit snooze. I never put my phone on silent so work knows to call me if I’m not in by a certain time. It’s only happened once when I broke my phone and set my alarm on my laptop, but I left the screen closed! I woke up at 4 and was ready in record time. I go to bed at 8:30pm at the latest. Pete normally comes to bed not long after me; sometimes he’ll watch a little Netflix.

### My brother told me to wear makeup…

Makeup was something I discovered very late in life. When I was working behind a bar at 21, my brother said ‘Sylvia it’s time to start wearing a little makeup’. I looked like I had just come off the netball court. I couldn’t be bothered, but now I have access to everything in the Channel 9 makeup room, I’ve developed an interest and can appreciate a good product.

### I love my Parlux hairdryer.

I use that and Evo every morning before blow-drying on the set. It protects it and styles as well. Morgan in the studio does my cut and colour, but he also works at [Valonz ](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/valonz-paddington). He does my hair five days a week at the moment. After washing my hair I use Elemis Body Cream . For the weekend I use Charlotte Tilbury Light Wonder Foundation  over my Rationale moisturiser

### I over-plucked my eyebrows when I was 15…

…  and I had this spot that never wanted to fill. Now I go to [Kristen Fisher](http://www.kristinfisher.com.au/) for my brows. I’m also about to start a few blueberry peels at [The Clinic](https://www.theclinic.net.au/)  in Bondi Junction to start my pre-wedding regime. I have tried a few other treatments but I’m terrible with appointments.

![Bsjf12 1](/content/interviews/actors-presenters/sylvia-jeffreys-news-reporter/BSJF12-1-1.jpg)


### Stacey McPherson comes to my house to do my tan.

She uses JBronze and worked closely on the development of it with Jen.  I used to be brown all the time growing up but now I’m scared of the sun.

### My exercise motto is: Do as much as possible.

I try and go as soon as I’m finished the show because that’s when my energy levels are highest. I do a lot of barre pilates now at [Barre Body](https://www.beauticate.com/who/emma-seibold-fitness-entrepreneur). I also do Power Living Yoga in Bondi or Bondi Junction. I love running; I just did the City to Surf. I used to swim competitively when I was young but not so much anymore, I do still own five pairs of Camilla swimmers though!

### My diet is pretty regimented…

… from Monday to Friday, anyway. Weekends I let loose and have a bit of pasta. I’m not strictly anything ‘free’, although I’m not great with dairy, so I keep it minimal. I try to steer clear of bread but it’s hard. Sugar is something I’ve reduced in the last year. Once I get on a roll with sugar, my addiction gets stronger and stronger so I really have to pull it right back. It makes such a huge difference to my energy levels.

![Bsjf2](/content/interviews/actors-presenters/sylvia-jeffreys-news-reporter/BSJF2-1.jpg)


### A QLD company called Good Mix supplies our on set breakfast because they fed us at a shoot and we all got hooked!

I have porridge with soaked seeds, they're so healthy. Instead of trying to get something sweet, I’ll have a spoon of peanut or almond butter or a piece of cheese because it feels satisfying but I don’t feel the need to keep having more. If I’m really craving something sweet, I’ll have medjool dates at lunch. I also snack on a little bit of fruit as well.

### I have [Ecoya](http://www.ecoya.com/) candles in every corner of my house.

Pete may love them more than I do. They add so much warmth to every room and it’s such a simple and effective decorating tool without any effort. I love the [French Pear](http://bit.ly/2cQ9KNj) . I love interiors; I’m working on our place right now. We have a super king bed from AH Beard . It’s perfect for two people who don’t get a lot of sleep – it’s seven army rolls to get to Pete! Sheridan  does linen for super kings so when they’re on sale I pounce.

![Bsjf4b](/content/interviews/actors-presenters/sylvia-jeffreys-news-reporter/BSJF4b-1.jpg)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is Sylvia Jeffreys' morning routine as a Today Show host?**
A: She is up at 3:15am and at the office by 4am. She gives herself just 20 minutes to shower and change at home, then spends time at work reading newspapers and online news to prepare for the show.

**Q: What beauty and hair products does Sylvia use regularly?**
A: She uses a Parlux hairdryer with Evo before blowdrying on set, Charlotte Tilbury Light Wonder Foundation over Rationale moisturiser on weekends, and has her cut and colour done by Morgan, who also works at Valonz.

**Q: How does Sylvia approach her diet during the week?**
A: Her weekday diet is regimented. She reduces sugar, minimises dairy and avoids bread where possible. She has porridge with soaked seeds from Good Mix on set and snacks on almond butter or medjool dates rather than reaching for something sweet.

**Q: What exercise does Sylvia do?**
A: She trains straight after the show when her energy is highest. She does barre pilates at Barre Body, Power Living Yoga in Bondi and loves running. She completed the City to Surf and swam competitively as a child.
