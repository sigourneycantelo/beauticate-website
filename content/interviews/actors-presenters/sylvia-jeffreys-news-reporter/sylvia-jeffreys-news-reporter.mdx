### I give myself 20 minutes to get ready in the morning.

I shower and change, that’s it. I spend 20 minutes at work reading the papers. It’s like cramming for an exam every morning. I flick through the Daily Tele, The Australian and SMH, and then I go online look at news.com and just flick around to get a feel for what they’re leading with. We look at Buzzfeed, Twitter and things like that as were also covering Internet trends.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I’m up at 3:15am and in the office at 4am.

I set two alarms two minutes apart and I always hit snooze. I never put my phone on silent so work knows to call me if I’m not in by a certain time. It’s only happened once when I broke my phone and set my alarm on my laptop, but I left the screen closed! I woke up at 4 and was ready in record time. I go to bed at 8:30pm at the latest. Pete normally comes to bed not long after me; sometimes he’ll watch a little Netflix.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19343&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="CHARLOTTE TILBURY IS SYLVIA'S GO-TO WEEKEND FOUNDATION, WHILE THE HEADPHONES MUFFLE PETE'S NETFLIX WHILE SHE SLEEPS." font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19345&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="SYLVIA WASN'T NATURALLY INCLINED TO MAKEUP WHEN SHE WAS YOUNGER" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My brother told me to wear makeup…

Makeup was something I discovered very late in life. When I was working behind a bar at 21, my brother said ‘Sylvia it’s time to start wearing a little makeup’. I looked like I had just come off the netball court. I couldn’t be bothered, but now I have access to everything in the Channel 9 makeup room, I’ve developed an interest and can appreciate a good product.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I over-plucked my eyebrows when I was 15…

…  and I had this spot that never wanted to fill. Now I go to [Kristen Fisher](http://www.kristinfisher.com.au/) for my brows. I’m also about to start a few blueberry peels at [The Clinic](https://www.theclinic.net.au/)  in Bondi Junction to start my pre-wedding regime. ### My exercise motto is: Do as much as possible.

I try and go as soon as I’m finished the show because that’s when my energy levels are highest. I do a lot of barre pilates now at [Barre Body](https://www.beauticate.com/who/emma-seibold-fitness-entrepreneur). I also do Power Living Yoga in Bondi or Bondi Junction. I love running; I just did the City to Surf. I used to swim competitively when I was young but not so much anymore, I do still own five pairs of Camilla swimmers though![/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My diet is pretty regimented…

… from Monday to Friday, anyway. Weekends I let loose and have a bit of pasta. I’m not strictly anything ‘free’, although I’m not great with dairy, so I keep it minimal. I try to steer clear of bread but it’s hard. Sugar is something I’ve reduced in the last year. Once I get on a roll with sugar, my addiction gets stronger and stronger so I really have to pull it right back. It makes such a huge difference to my energy levels.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19341&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="WEEKENDS SYLVIA INDULGES A LITTLE DIET-WISE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19342&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="SYLVIA KEEPS ENERGISED FROM A 3AM WAKEUP WITH PORRIDGE AND SEEDS" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### A QLD company called Good Mix supplies our on set breakfast because they fed us at a shoot and we all got hooked!

I have porridge with soaked seeds, they're so healthy. Instead of trying to get something sweet, I’ll have a spoon of peanut or almond butter or a piece of cheese because it feels satisfying but I don’t feel the need to keep having more. If I’m really craving something sweet, I’ll have medjool dates at lunch. I also snack on a little bit of fruit as well.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]
