### "Right now, being part of Neighbours is honestly such an incredible time for me.

I love everything about this job, from the cast crew and production to my character herself. Playing Elly Conway has its constant challenges and I love the complexity of her nature/character & the story lines they give me.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32804&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32805&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### Recently I asked an elderly friend how their day was and they replied ‘Good day, bad day, it's all the same right?’  I LOVE that outlook on life.

How we see situations and how we react to them; how we make the bad days good and how we keep a positive outlook always, no matter whether it's a good day or a bad one. Knowing your face and what parts you should emphasise. Then educating yourself on how to use the right products in the right places. It’s all perspective.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### Being a working mum has made my beauty routine rather fast…

…I've gone through stages in the past where I loved using every different product under the sun. But now time is such a priority, I have my key products I use religiously and they're amazing. I think as you get older you also invest in skincare and make sure you’re using the right products.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32806&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32807&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### I'd love to say less is more but I LOVE make up.

I love trying different looks pending what I'm wearing. I also love lashes but getting them right is often an art in itself. For make up I start with a Becca Backlight Priming Filter, it's heaven. Then, I use MAC Face and Body or Armani Foundation followed by MAC concealer. I love the famous [Laguna Bronzer](http://www.mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder/V-000778.html) by NARS and I live by my fave mascara in the world – Diorshow in waterproof black (it makes my lashes look almost fake). I lightly fill in my brows, too. Finally my fave lip colour is a MAC Patentpolish Lip Pencil in *It's Really Me*, then my Mecca Cosmetica lip gloss in a pinky colour. That's it for my daily look, but if I'm heading out I'll add a cheek or a lip colour, or occasionally a smoky eye. I'm obsessed with Nars, Mac, Armani, Charlotte Tilbury, Anastasia Beverly Hills, Hour Glass & a good old trusty paw paw.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32808&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32809&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### My life fragrance is [Dominique Ropion Portrait of a Lady by Frederic Malle](http://www.mecca.com.au/editions-de-parfums-by-frederic-malle/portrait-of-a-lady-edp/V-010761.html).

I've always been a scent person – constantly changing and mixing it up however with this one I feel like I've found THE scent I'll wear forever.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### I love music. Over the years it's helped me through tough times & it really inspires me.

I also love reading, I’m currently reading a book called *The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck*. And I need my mags, like Vogue, Harper’s BAZAAR and Rush. They're all forms of creativity and escape from learning lines or life's hectic schedule. If I had it my way I'd walk for an hour a day but usually I get in 45 minutes to an hour walk a week and interval training occasionally, I also do a series of floor exercises. It's all very simple stuff.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32810&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32811&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

### I've recently cut back on sugar and cakes.

I've grown up eating them and I love to bake so it's been a hard transition however a healthier one. I think, for me, it's about small snacks frequently and making sure I get all the right food groups into my day. Then I also crave sugar less. I'm pretty healthy with food. I eat well, I eat a lot and I mix it up so I can indulge if I want. I'm also obsessed with Lola Berry at the moment so Aleeia and I pick a different recipe most weekends and have some fun in the kitchen.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]
