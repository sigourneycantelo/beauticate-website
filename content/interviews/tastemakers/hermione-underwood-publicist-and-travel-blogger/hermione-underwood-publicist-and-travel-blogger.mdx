### “I feel very fortunate to be able to ‘work’ doing what I truly love.

Hermione Olivia was conceived as an outlet to share my travel experiences. It has evolved to include stylish contributors I admire, as well insights into my personal life and style in my new home of London. I never thought it would be possible to call this a profession and fortunately, my two passions are now intertwined in two different outlets. They have allowed me to better understand a part of the digital media industry, from the other side of the table.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20069&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="HERMIONE RECENTLY RELOCATED TO LONDON AND IS TRAVELING REGULARLY FOR HER WEBSITE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20070&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="HERMIONE USES HER TRAVELS TO STOCK UP ON CULT BEAUTY BRANDS LIKE BY REDO, CIRE TRUDON CANDLES AND LA MER" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My personal style has evolved living in London.

I’ve always had a fairly androgynous look and I must admit, I wear a lot of black. Comfort is crucial for me. I adore Parisian style – French Vogue Editor, Emmanuelle Alt is my go to reference. There is much more structure to my look as I mature, but when I am travelling I like to have more fun with it.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### [Lara](https://www.beauticate.com/who/lara-bingle) (Bingle) taught me most about beauty…

I swear she has an encyclopaedic knowledge. The tanning products from her range, [The Base](http://www.thebase.me/), are saving me through this long European winter. I also have the most amazing mum, who taught me that beauty comes from within and confidence is key.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20071&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="HERMIONE DID PUBLICITY FOR THE LAUNCH OF AUSTRALIAN BRAND CR FORMULATIONS" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20072&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="HERMIONE SWEARS BY BY REDO TRAVEL SPRAY TO FIT IN HER CHANEL BOY BAG" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### Simplicity is key for me with beauty.

I am very low maintenance, as I like to be out the door in two minutes flat. My day-to-day my look is very clean and simple: tinted moisturiser, mascara and lip balm. I buy a La Mer lip balm at every duty free counter I pass. It is my daily velvety, heavenly indulgence and my absolute favourite product.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My day starts with…

the smell of Aesop Rose Petal, Cardamom and Black petal body wash in the shower. I religiously use CR Formulationsproducts, they have turned my skin around. I worked with Cheryl, the founder, last year and she taught me so much about skincare and health. I use the Rice Milk Cleansertwice a day. I also recently had an experience with [GENEU](https://www.instagram.com/geneubeaute/), a must do when in London. They create personalised skincare built on your DNA. I now use the serums morning and night.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20073&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="BYREDO LOVELESS CANDLES ARE AMONG HERMIONE'S FAVOURITE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20074&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="THE PUBLICIST BELIEVES YOU SHOULD FIND YOUR SIGNATURE SCENT AND WEAR IT FOR THE REST OF YOUR LIFE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I love the ritual of wearing perfume…

And it holds great value and important memories for me. “Find ‘your’ perfume before you turn thirty. Wear it for the next thirty years” is a quote from the book, How to be Parisian, that resonates with me. Mine is Frederic Malle’s Portrait of a Lady and I will wear it for the rest of my life. It’s very unique and often a conversation starter. I buy it from the beautiful flagship store in Paris.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### At night I make more of an effort with my make up…

Wearing foundation and either a smokey eye or a bold lip. I swear by Tom Ford lipstickand I probably have 20 MAC eyeliner pencils. I use white eyeliner or the insides of my eyes to make them pop. The [Kevin Aucoin Ash Blonde](http://mecca.com.au/kevyn-aucoin/precision-brow-pencil/V-002417.html) eyebrow pencil is genius and easy to use. I use bronzer as eye shadow, it’s the perfect colour for green eyes. I always have perfume close by and the [Byredo travel size Gypsy Water](http://mecca.com.au/byredo-parfums/gypsy-water-edp/V-008253.html) fits into even the smallest clutch. For a special occasion I’ll do a lip or an eye but never both.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20075&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="THE BARE ESSENTIALS: TOM FORD, CHANEL, LA MER…" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20076&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="COFFEE TABLE BOOKS, PERFUMES AND CANDLES ADORN THE SHELVES" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I religiously have a bath at night…

With epsom salts if I’ve been training. I burn a candle every evening in the bathroom, usually Byredo Loveless, Diptique Baies or anything by [Cire Trudon](http://www.ciretrudon.com/). I also collect candles when travelling. If in Paris, I will always buy a candle at Hotel Costes.

Massages are a true luxury for me. If I have a special occasion I will get a spray tan. In Sydney, the best spray tans are at [The Parlour Room](http://www.theparlourroom.com.au/). In London, I use the beauty concierge app [RUUBY](https://www.ruuby.com/) for last minute appointments.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### London gyms are very luxurious and private….

I do spin and Reformer pilates at my gym ([Body Works West](http://www.bodyworkswest.co.uk/)) in Notting Hill. It’s the first time I have enjoyed going to the gym. When I ‘m home in Sydney I train outdoors and try to swim as often as possible at Balmoral Beach. When travelling, I make sure to walk a lot.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20077&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="HERMIONE LOVES TOM FORD NUDE LIPSTICK WITH A WINGED EYE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="20078&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="A DIET OF CLEANSING LEMON WATER AND GREEN JUICES KEEN HERMIONE FEELING FRESH WHEN TRAVELING" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:left" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I have hot water and lemon with ginger…

…first thing in the morning and last thing at night, as well as a fresh green vegetable juice early in the morning when I rise early to catch the end of the Australian business day. Generally, I try to get a lot of rest, drink a lot of water and find a good balance of healthy food with occasional indulgences. I really listen to my body. Cutting out sugar recently was one of the best decisions I’ve made for my health."[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]*Interview by Sigourney Cantelo; Story by Ally McManus; Photography by Sabana Amode; Shot at Hermione’s home, recently developed by Lautner Group, in Central London. *[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
