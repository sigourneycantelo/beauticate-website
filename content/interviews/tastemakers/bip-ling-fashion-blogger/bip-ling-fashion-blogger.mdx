### I love to experiment with makeup

and play around with colours but I think looking natural is beautiful. I rarely wear foundation- just concealer. When makeup artist, Lisa Eldridge did my makeup she taught me that I don’t need foundation so I gave it up and haven’t used it since. I’m lucky to come from an Indian family with strong female role models – my mother and grandmother always encouraged me to focus on the important things – to be happy, be gracious, to smile…and have fun![/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I used to be a tomboy…

who wore shorts and shirts all the time. Now I’m the complete opposite. It was after I took a foundation course at Central St Martin’s University of Arts that I started blogging about my friends and the outfits I wore. I had no idea what I wanted to do, I wanted to do everything!  I always knew I would end up in a creative field because I love art and design. My blog started off as a sort of scrapbook and a way for me to express myself but I never intentionally set out for it to become what it has turned into. Now everyday is different and I spend my time going from modelling, DJing, blogging, vlogging and creating content for my [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/bipling). ### I like to look after my skin

My mother always taught me to tone the skin before cleansing because it keeps skin pimple free. I always use Kiehl’s Midnight Recovery Concentrate before I sleep and I apply it whenever I fly because it’s really hydrating. During the day I use their Rose Arctica Youth Regenerating Cream. I’ve been obsessed with Kiehl’s since I started using it because my skin has been so much better since. All the products really work, without being oily or greasy.  When I wash my face, I always rinse with cold water. In fact I am obsessed with cold rinsing in the shower and I even use it in the bath. It makes my skin feel tight and my hair glossy –it also makes my hair get less knotty during the day.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### When it comes to my hair…

I’m quite low maintenance. I visit Paul Edmonds in London regularly for a hair treatment and blow dry. At home I use Kiehl’s shampoo and conditioner and rub Moroccan oil through it everyday except when I go in the sun….oil in the sun can be so damaging to hair but used daily it makes it feel like silk.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21045&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="BIP WEARS DROP WAIST SKATER DRESS BY ASOS AND SILVER TOE PUMPS BY PRETTY BALLERINA" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21057&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="BIP PREFERS TO DO HER OWN MAKEUP WHEN GOING TO EVENTS" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I feel at my most beautiful when…

I’m fulfilled and that can depend on what I’m working on. A little spray tan from celebrity tanner, Jules Von Heptonstall at St Tropez helps too. I pick up tips from makeup artists here and there but actually I prefer doing my own makeup – I know my face really well and what suits it. I used to have professional makeup done for events but now I mostly do it myself. I like my makeup to look a little undone, less precious. I always think it looks best when it’s been on all night and my skin is a little shiny. When I go out I don’t really take much makeup with me or touch it up. In my bag you’ll find just a Mac concealer and a lip-gloss. I LOVE Dior lip-gloss.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I use really quick makeup techniques

Lots of YSL black liquid eyeliner is key and sometimes I’ll use Dior mascara on my lashes and then apply it on my eyebrows too! I use my fingers to apply everything from lipstick, concealer and even eye shadow. Dolce and Gabbana blusher is my favourite for cheeks and I like a bit of CK One Colour Skin Illuminator across the cheekbones for a glow. Fragrance is also really important. I wear Dior Ambre Nuit. It reminds me of my friend I used to live with in New York, Aussie model Bambi – she wears it too. I like my big Indian eyes (they run in the family!) I’ve been playing around a lot with the CK one Colour Powder Eye Shadows at the moment, using all sorts of intense, bright colours. ### I’m really happy when…

I’m at home with my family and my dog on a Sunday. I love to cook with my mother and make fresh juices from cucumber, celery, mint, lemon and ginger. Eating healthily is important to me and I feel my best when I’m eating well. My favourite treats are Bounce balls. I like to run in the park and do regular yoga.
