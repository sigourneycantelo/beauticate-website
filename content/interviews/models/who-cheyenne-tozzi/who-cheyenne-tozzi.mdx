### "I just fell into modeling.

Mum was modeling and Alex Perry would call her when I was eight and be like, “I need Cheyenne for a shoot” and my mum’s like, “Yeah, whatever… put the kids in.” Then it progressed from there. It’s a really nice job to have. I love that I can be a chameleon and just float around the world, depending on whatever mood I’m in and I’ve got incredible friends all over the world, so I’m extremely lucky. I’ve worked extremely hard to get where I am so I feel like at this point now, it’s just fun.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21292&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="CHEYENNE WAS BORN INTO THE MODELING WORLD" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21293&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="CLEAN TEETH ARE A SIMPLE BEAUTY STAPLE FOR THE MODEL" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I’m extremely low maintenance…

My aunts, my mum, they were all models and they all look incredible. We weren’t nuts on product after product because we’re just very relaxed. In Australia I think you’re really lucky to have that outdoor kind of thing, so you jump in the ocean if your skin’s bad [and use] aloe vera. I love moisturizing with coconut oil and salt water’s extremely good. I think keeping yourself dewy is nice. When I’m traveling I keep it really minimal. I’ll take face wipes on the plane; I like Milk by Lindy Klim with the exfoliant on the back. I drink fifty tons of water and green teas the whole flight. Another thing, you need to brush your teeth fifty times a day, I love Macleans.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### Jergens BB Body Perfecting Skin Cream is really good…

I actually came across Jergens when I was in New York and it was just a huge brand I was like, “sweet, they’re actually really good”. The ultra healing is my favorite; I like the smell of that one and it’s really good because I have to fly.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21294&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="CHEYENNE'S BEAUTY ESSENTIALS" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21303&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="TOM FORD, DIOR MASCARA AND CHANEL LES BEIGES BRONZER ARE AMONG CHEYENNE'S FAVOURITES" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### With makeup I’m really natural…

If I’m having a shocker, mascara is a definite. I like Diorshow, that’s a good one. I love my Tom Ford; the nude lip is really nice. I like Chanel, the Les Beiges bronzer. I do cleanse and tone. I like SK-II. But I work every day and they take [my makeup] off me, then  I just wash my face with water and jump into bed. A good moisturizer’s really good; I have so many moisturizers. I use a Dior one that I really like. I just started using Nip + Fab lavender body wash, there’s sort of crazy stuff in it but it’s really good.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I go to the [Last Resort Day Spa](http://thelastresort.com.au/) in Bondi…

Saimaa, who runs it, she’s incredible and she puts me on these little pills and potions that I take now that just keep you calm, they just clean you out really. I had (a colonic) yesterday as well. I go a few times a week. They’re not for everybody. I have a green juice in the morning with my powders, then I prefer a sashimi salad, I like the one from Jackie’s Cafe.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21308&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="CHEYENNES SWEARS BY COCONUT OIL FOR SOFT SUMMER SKIN" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21309&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="TO STAY CAMERA-READY CHEYENNE COMBINES STRICT TRAINING WITH A SENSIBLE DIET." font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

Christian beats the shit out of me. He’ll come to my house. ### It’s always so nice to come home…

It’s the people that make the house. All the houses in the family are quite eclectic, everyone’s lived overseas and everyone’s traveled so it’s very worldly. There are so many bits and pieces from everywhere around the world.
