### *Beauty starts with great skin…*

And beautiful skin is far more important than beautiful makeup. A healthy glow is key. Hydration is the most important thing for beauty and diet. Drink lots of water to stay fresh and hydrated, take vitamins, maintain a healthy diet, have a good skincare regime, and get enough sleep. Never go to bed with makeup on! It’s the worst thing you can do. Change it up from day to day, so your skin doesn’t get resistant. Use different creams since each one supplies your skin with something different, my mother taught me this.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### *I like to mix my moisturiser…*

With a few drops of MAC Foundation. [YSL’s Touche Éclat](http://www.myer.com.au/shop/mystore/yves-saint-laurent-touche-eclat) is a miracle, it conceals everything. For some colour on my cheeks, I like [Nars Douceur blush](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/Nars/Blush?utm_campaign=bazaarvoice&utm_medium=SearchVoice&utm_source=RatingsAndReviews&utm_content=Default). [RMS Living Luminizer](https://www.mecca.com.au/rms-beauty/living-luminizer/I-020192.html) is a great highlighter that I apply to my inner eyelids, cheekbones and upper lip. [Burberry Bronzer](http://shop.nordstrom.com/s/burberry-beauty-warm-glow-natural-bronzer/3118741) gives me the right sun kissed glow, I dust it over my nose to narrow my face. For my eyes, I use MAC Eye Kohl in Smoulder, which is perfect for an easy smudged eye.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="22458&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]

### *I am very self-aware and comfortable in my skin…*

Which wasn't always the case when I was younger. It's empowering to know yourself, you're in control and have a clear perspective. It all comes with some sort of maturity. I think modelling is a great industry if you approach it the right way. It opens so many doors. I've also travelled all over the world and I've absorbed knowledge and the beauty of different cultures which gives me a worldly outlook and awareness that I feel is very important. I've been lucky enough to work with the most incredible people in the industry. I've learned so much from them.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### *I feel the most beautiful and sexy when…*

I'm in a great dress by one of my favourite designers. Another thing that makes me feel sexy is a great unique concept for a shoot. I'm thrilled to work with Ferragamo. The brand has always represented iconic classic luxury: strong and beautiful, Italian heritage, the best quality and effortless classics. I'm grateful to be a part of it. Mario Sorrenti has an iconic way of making a woman look both strong and delicate. We shot by the sea, in the most beautiful location, Sorrento. The place has iconic scenery of Italy, there is a natural Italian elegance, which is very inspirational, also Mario is Italian so it was lovely.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="22457&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]

### *Perfume to me is a classic part of my everyday routine*

I am loyal to a fragrance that arouses my senses. When you find a scent you love, you always go back to it. Signorina Eleganza (ph 1300095224 for details) would be perfect for a romantic evening out or for a party…with a beautiful dress. I'd wear it with something luxurious, sensual and feminine but modern. A beautiful silk dress in a lighter colour or a white shirt with a pair of cool jeans. I identify with the Signorina Eleganza woman. She is feminine, sensual and elegant. I like the grapefruit paired with floral notes and white leather. It's unique and a very sensual, indulgent scent.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### *Yoga and exercise are big for me…*

They make me feel energized and fresh. I do yoga regularly and every time I finish a session, I'm in an amazing mood. I love going on long runs, it helps me unwind and get any stress out. I hit the gym twice a week as well and scuba diving is one of my favourite hobbies. Enough sleep is also pretty important. If I’m up late working on a shoot, I’ll try to squeeze a nap in the next day to even out. I’ve been eating organic and local as much as I can and I feel great. I love RAW coconut water, which is super hydrating. I am vegetarian and superfoods like avocado, goji berries, blueberries, beets are both good for your body and skin.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="22456&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]
