### “I launched the label in 2007 as I saw a gap in the market for quality Australian merino knitwear.

I studied a Bachelor of Design at RMIT in Melbourne before I started working at Country Road as a textile and graphic designer. The opportunity gave me my introduction to fashion and that was it! I love graphic design, and fashion/running my own label allows me to turn graphic design into something beautiful, tactile and three dimensional.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19952&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="GEORGINA HEADS FASHION LABEL CABLE MELBOURNE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19948&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="SHE HAS A SLEW OF TRAVEL BEAUTY ESSENTIALS SHE STASHES IN HER HANDBAG" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My beauty routine is very nighttime based.

I just have more time and my skin responds better. PM is my revive opportunity! At night – I use the [Garner Micellar Water](http://bit.ly/29YzRQ7) to remove make up. And then I can’t go past [James Vivian](http://jamesviviandt.com/), they sell amazing things!. I use the SkinMedica Lytera Brightening Complex, Results Rx A Solution Vitamin A and Triple AHA Serum. I also am obsessed with Aspect Super Moisturising Complex (S.M.C). In the morning I just moisturise and make up, very straight forward. I always carry Kora [Rosehip oil](http://www.koraorganics.com/us/luxurious-rosehip-oil.html), Aesop Deodorant and organic hand cream during travel so I can feel human and 'fresh' even on the longest of flights.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### My favourite product is the [Stila 12 Colour Eyeshadow Palette](http://mecca.com.au/stila/eyes-are-the-window-shadow-palette/V-018937.html)…

I add a smoky eye for PM and it’s the best. As for the day I’m definitely a minimalist. I always start with [Stila Illuminating Foundation](http://mecca.com.au/stila/illuminating-foundation-60-watt/I-002839.html?gclid=Cj0KEQjwt-G8BRDktsvwpPTn1PkBEiQA-MRsBR_O732WKOxSpteRQVnLE2cKcROTFMHznAKQFP4Ifc8aAsjO8P8HAQ), it’s light and the perfect base for my skin. [Nars Laguna Bronzer](http://mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder/V-000778.html#q=nars%2Blaguna%2Bbronzer&start=1) is next for an all over glow and then I add Nars Shimmer Stick to my cheeks. I also always have my Chanel lipstick compact handy. I prefer it over one lipstick given I often have to move from day to night- adding a fresh lip colour is my go too. I can't be without Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Lipbalm as well. A spritz of [Serge Lutens – L’Eau de Paille](http://mecca.com.au/serge-lutens/leau-de-paille/V-023912.html?cgpath=brands-serge#start=1) and I’m ready to go.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19954&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="GEORGINA FAVOURS STILA, CHANEL, NARS AND SERGE LUTENS IN HER MAKEUP KIT" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="19953&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="AN AVID FAN OF A GOOD BLOW DRY, GEORGINA IS OBSESSED WITH THE NEW DYSON TO KEEP HER CURLS IN CHECK- BUT FOR WHEN ALL ELSE FAILS, A LITTLE HEADWEAR CAN'T HURT" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I knew how to blow dry my hair from age 13…

I had outrageous curls that were very difficult to manage. My mum taught me to never leave the house with wet hair and how to tame the mane! I am loving the new [Dyson Supersonic](http://www.dyson.com.au/haircare/supersonic.aspx)right now- it's amazing. It really has been a great life lesson that I will definitely pass down to Heidi.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I have a quarterly treatment from [James Vivian Dermal Therapies](http://jamesviviandt.com/)…

to ensure my skin retains its glow and can combat the typical Melbourne four seasons in one day! The treatment consists of a triple exfoliation to deep cleanse; Cutera’s Laser Genesis to non-invasively stimulate collagen, refine pores and help heal breakouts; a light chemical peeling treatment to brighten and hydrate; and a custom mask to further address any remaining concerns. It’s divine and sounds epic because it is! And would you believe it only takes an hour.

### My walk in wardrobe is my happy place.

I especially designed it to have no doors as I love to be able to see everything. It’s an organised mess! My style is understated, I like classic with an edge. My favourite designers are [Celine](https://www.celine.com/en) for simplicity, [Helmut Lang](http://www.helmutlang.com/) for the technical design elements and Max Mara for classics. I recently indulged in the Celine Small Cabas Phantom as a work tote update and it literally holds everything.
