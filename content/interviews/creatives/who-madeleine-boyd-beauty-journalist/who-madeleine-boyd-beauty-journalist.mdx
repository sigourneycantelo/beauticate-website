### I feel really lucky that I get to try beauty products

…and call it work.  I love that there is always something new and exciting to discover and I believe makeup is something you can really have fun with.  When I was four I loved playing with lipstick. I’ll never forget that one time I used Mum’s favourite shade of pink to ‘decorate’ my chest of drawers.  Not much has changed, though I do keep my lipsticks away from furniture. Despite being a beauty journalist I don’t actually spend much time doing my makeup in the morning. I prefer to keep it quite natural.  My go-to foundations are [ByTerry Sheer Expert Foundation](http://mecca.com.au/by-terry/sheer-expert-fluid-foundation/V-013526.html) and Chantecaille Future Skin – both are quite dewy and sheer coverage. I use ByTerry’s Touché Veloutee concealer – it’s really brightening, which is ideal for me as I can get quite dark under eye circles especially after a late night. I love blush and usually wear either Nars Deep Throat or Gilda, then dust on a bit of [Nars Laguna Bronzer](http://mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder-laguna/I-000778.html).[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21146&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="MADELEINE BELIEVES MAKEUP SHOULD BE FUN" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21167&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="SHE WEARS SCANLAN THEODORE TOP & SKIRT, MAWI CUFF AND GIANVITO ROSSI HEELS" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I’ve always done my own eyebrows

I’m such a perfectionist about them!  I fill them in with an [Hourglass Brow Pencil](http://mecca.com.au/hourglass/arch-brow-sculpting-pencil/V-015196.html#q=arch&start=1) in soft brunette.  I don’t feel ‘done’ unless I’ve penciled my brows. I also swear by [Chantecaille Faux Cils Longest Lash Mascara](http://mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder-laguna/I-000778.html). While it’s not cheap, I have a weakness for a lash serum mascara that gives long, voluminous lashes. Liquid eyeliner is probably one of the first beauty products I ever mastered. I’ve tried so many different black liquid eyeliners but I always go back to Rimmel Glam Eyes.  It’s around $14 from the supermarket and the best thing going round. Eye shadow-wise, I’m a fan ofStila’s Kitten Eye Shadow (for day) and for nighttime or if I have an event, I look to Chanel or Bobbi Brown’s smoky eye shadow quads in browns and taupes.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I can’t live without [Eve Lom’s cleanser](http://mecca.com.au/chantecaille/flower-harmonizing-cream/I-008123.html#start=1)

Which I use every night.  It removes makeup, cleanses, gently exfoliates and has quite the cult following. It’s thick like a balm and you apply it to dry skin, then wipe it off with a damp muslin cloth. I love the ritual of it. I have really dry skin and this doesn’t strip, yet my skin always feels squeaky clean after I use it.  After that I apply Ultraceuticals Red-Action Serum (it’s one of those products that you can actually see working).  I follow this with [Verso Super Eye Serum](http://mecca.com.au/verso-skincare/super-eye-serum/I-017818.html#start=1).  Every few days I’ll use Dr Dennis Gross peel pads to exfoliate – they’re magic! I’ve got so many of my friends onto them.  I also do an Aesop clay maskonce a week.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21169&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="ON HIGH ROTATION; A LOT OF EVE, A LITTLE SUNDAY AND A DAB OF CHANTECAILLE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21171&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="MADELEINE HAS REPLACED THE SOLARIUM WITH SUNSCREEN" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I probably shouldn’t be admitting this…

But when I was younger I used the solarium all the time. My attitude to tanning is so different now; I wear sunscreen everyday, even in winter. Every few months I have sessions of Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) for my pigmentation at [Clear Complexions](https://www.clearcomplexions.com.au/) in Balmain, the nurses there are really lovely. In the morning I cleanse with Sunday Riley Crème Cleaner (it’s super gentle) then apply Chantecaille Vital Essence hydrating serum – it’s amazing especially if I’ve had one too many champagnes the night before. I moisturize with [Chantecaille’s flower harmonizing cream](http://mecca.com.au/chantecaille/flower-harmonizing-cream/I-008123.html#start=1).[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I’m obsessed with fragrance

And have a penchant for florals. I discovered Jo Malone perfumes when I was living in London about six years ago. Her Red Rosesand Wild Bluebell are two of my classics.  I also love fragrances by Serge Lutens ([Nuit De Cellophane](http://mecca.com.au/serge-lutens/nuit-de-cellophane/I-006639.html)) and Byredo (Inflorescence and Bal D’Afrique especially). Tom Ford’s Black Orchid is great for a night out.  I remember Mum used to wear Elizabeth Arden Red Door when we were growing up – it always reminds me of her.  My first ever fragrance was Ralph Lauren Romance – I loved it. ### I’m so hopeless at styling my own hair

Makeup I can do, but hair is another story! Having colour treated hair means it gets stressed out pretty easily and often needs a lot TLC. Shu Uemura’s Ultimate Remedy Treatment is my once-a-week savior for strong, silky locks. I have really fine hair so I can't go past my Living Proof Root Lifting Spray. I love my Mason Pearson hairbrush and Tangle Teezer too. I’ve been getting my cut with Paloma and my colour done with Michael at Oscar Oscar in Paddingtonsince I moved to Sydney.  When I first moved up here from Melbourne I had no idea where to go.  I asked the girls in the [Vogue] fashion office and they all said, “Go see Paloma”.  So I did and haven’t looked back. I get BioSculpture on my nails at The Nail Lounge. I’ve tried Shellac in the past but I find it starts chipping off my nails after only a few days and leaves them quite brittle. For me BioScuplture is so much better.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528872736472{padding-bottom: 30px !important;}"][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

### I definitely struggled a lot with my body image

When I was younger, like most teenage girls I suppose.   I’m so much more comfortable with my body now in my late twenties. I do find I have to work harder to stay in shape though. I try to exercise frequently – whether that’s running the Bondi to Bronte or doing a reformer Pilates session.  I’ve been seeing Kirsten King at [Fluid Form Pilates](http://www.fluidformpilates.com.au/) for about three months now, she’s amazing.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="21178&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="OPULENT FRENCH DELIGHTS; CHANEL, DIPTYQUE AND HERMES" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]*Photography by Jake Terrey; Hair by Penny Baldas for Oscar Oscar Salon; Make up by Desiree Wise; BioSculpture Manicure by The Nail Lounge; Shot on location at Watsons Bay Boutique Hotel.*[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
