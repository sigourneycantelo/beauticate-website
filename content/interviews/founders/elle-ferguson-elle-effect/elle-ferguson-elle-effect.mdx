### I truly believe it was my destiny to create my own stand alone brand.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-1.jpg)


Fashion is in my blood. My mum Kim Ferguson had her own label when my sister and I were growing up, so she was always sewing or making patterns. I remember when I was in primary school I used to do all my school projects by cutting relevant bits out of *Vogue*, and weekends were spent at the Powerhouse Museum looking at exhibitions on fashion – it was just the way we did things in our family. My sister also has her own label now, [Babyanything](https://babyanything.com.au).

I studied Interior Design after school, and then after four hard years of hand drafting and all kind of wild assignments I was drawn back to fashion through my first job, which was head of Visual Merchandising for [One Teaspoon](https://www.oneteaspoon.com). Sometimes the universe just brings you back to where you belong.

### I love that no two days are the same in my job, and the fact that it allows me to create my own story, and do things my way.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-2.jpg)


I’ve worked really hard to be where I am and to be able to make the choices I am making. Most of all I love that I believed enough in myself to ‘give me a go’. It’s very exciting to have high standards for myself and the brand and strive to live up to them.

### When it comes to my personal style, I’m pretty eclectic. I dress differently every day. I can be super feminine one day or monochrome black the next.

![Elle portrait 6](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-6-1.jpg)


My absolute favourite designer would be [Babyanything](https://babyanything.com.au) (of course). I can’t leave the house without my sister's jewellery – it completes every outfit. Other than that I adore Isabel Marant, Bassike, Loewe, Zara, Spell and of course Revolve, who I am lucky enough to work with. In terms of some favourite pieces, I’m recently back from  New York, where it was snowing, so I bought a [divine Loewe cashmere coat](https://www.instagram.com/p/Bttn5HVhzfA/) before I left. Also, I just had a crazy shopping session at Zara with my sister, where I got some great pieces to update my wardrobe for under $300! I’m all about mixing high and low.

### I can see my boyfriend saying that my approach to beauty is just, “I LOVE PRODUCTS”, haha!

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-3.jpg)


Which I do, of course, but when it comes to my actual beauty must haves, there honestly aren’t that many. I just love trying everything out there and then narrowing it down to the best of the best.

My beauty routine starts with the Elle Effect Tanning Mousse applied once a week to my entire body and to my face every three days. My whole look is based around that perfect golden glow. Next up would be my GHD curling tong. I use this first thing in the morning and always curl away from my face, then brush my curls out. I always wash my face with warm water, followed by Olay Regenerist Micro Sculpting Cream which I let absorb into my skin before I start my make up.

### So, here we go, my full makeup routine…

![Elle portrait 4](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-4-1.jpg)


I use ModelCo Face Base as my base – I’ve been using this product for over five years and love it, and it stops me from breaking out. I then mix [Stila Stay All Day Balm](http://bit.ly/2UR9Ysd) with [Chantecaille Illuminator](https://www.mecca.com.au/chantecaille/liquid-lumiere/V-018711.html) as my primer. I blend this into my skin using BECCA's The One Perfecting Brush. For foundation I mix Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk Foundation in shade 6.5 with the BECCA highlighter in Moonstone. Again I use the BECCA brush as I find it helps to blend it all the way down your neck. Next up I use MAC Studio Finish Concealer in NC45 for any blemishes I need to cover, I just use my finger to apply as I believe the product blends better into the skin with the warmth of your finger tip.

I set this with MAC Mineralize Skinfinish in Medium Dark in medium dark, using a big fluffy brush. From here I add [NARS Laguna](https://www.mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder-laguna/I-000778.html?gclid=EAIaIQobChMI6eiG7PzY4AIVQYuPCh1itgU3EAQYASABEgLWYfD_BwE) to my cheekbones and temples (I have been using this bronzer since hair and makeup artist Max May suggested it to me many many moons ago). Once I’m even more golden thanks to that, I use Charlotte Tilbury Magic Away in shade 3 under my eyes and blend it with my fingers, then set it with Charlotte's loose powder. I do my brows with MAC Great Brows in Tapered and fill these in quite full as I do *love* a bold brow. I set my brows with Benefit Gimme Brows in Shade 01 which lasts all day and night.

### A trick that I have done since high school is to use a nude eyeliner on the water line, as it really opens your eyes up.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-5.jpg)


The best one I’ve found is the nude one by MAC. I mix [By Rosie Jane Cheek & Lip Gloss in Rose](https://www.mecca.com.au/by-rosie-jane/essentials-cheek-lip-gloss-rose/I-024908.html?gclid=EAIaIQobChMIovvZwYHZ4AIVzYyPCh0CngD2EAQYASABEgLR1_D_BwE) with [NARS blush in Gina](https://www.mecca.com.au/nars/blush/V-000376.html) and apply them to the apples of my cheeks. I love to use cream with powder. For mascara I’m loving [Kevyn Aucoin](https://www.mecca.com.au/kevyn-aucoin/the-volume-mascara/I-002418.html?gclid=EAIaIQobChMI1dWhjYLZ4AIVkQsrCh2_Ug8uEAQYASABEgLWffD_BwE) and have been using it for about six months. It's tubular so it doesn’t smudge, which is the best thing ever. From here I set my makeup wth a couple of sprays of MAC Fix+, which I find allows my skin to breathe but sets my face for the day. For lips, I have, like, sixteen different versions of the same colour, but some favourites are NARS' Melody *[Ed. note: this was limited edition and is no longer available]*, NARS Velvet Lip Pencil in Dolce Vita, and Sedona Sands by Burts Bees. Then at the end of the day I take everything off with good old Neutrogena wipes, which I swear by.

### For a night out, I love to add a lash.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-7.jpg)


My favourite way to do this is to use [Ardell individual lashes](http://bit.ly/2UBHSRu) and really pile them on, the more the merrier! Other than the lashes, even if it's an evening look I still like to keep my makeup quite natural but have a bit more fun with it. I may add a bold lip in a red or a plum colour, and I love adding some bling to my hair.

### A trick with my hair is that I always spray it with dry shampoo before I go to sleep, and then I brush it out in the morning. I find this adds volume and grit to my hair for when I tong it.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-8.jpg)


I wash my hair maybe three times a week (depending on if I’m shooting or not). I’m currently addicted to [TRESemmé Pro Collection Violet Blonde Shampoo](http://bit.ly/2UO7sCI) and Conditioner to ensure my blonde doesn’t go brassy. I find all the pollutants in the air sometimes means my blonde doesn’t look as bright as it could, and this is so great for fixing that. I also love every now and again not washing my hair for a week and letting the natural oils repair any damage. I’ve done this since I was a kid.

### My ideal beauty look is natural with a golden glow and great hair.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-9.jpg)


I feel my best with a fresh coat of Elle Effect Tanning Mousse. Nothing beats that. Believe me, I’ve used enough bad fake tan in my time to know! If you haven’t found your own look yet, I say experiment. What I love about beauty right now is that everyone is giving it a go.

I’ve got great memories of some early products I used, like grape-flavoured Lip Smackers, remember those? "Cause you go ape over grape!" Oh, it was love at first sight. And then some not-so-great memories, like using body glitter on my face, having stick straight bleached hair… but why not, really. It makes us feel good, so why not have fun with it.

### I have two top beauty tips. Number one, always brush your curls out, and two, if you’re ever working with a makeup artist, ask them a million questions.

![Elle portrait 10](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-10-1.jpg)


I was recently shopping in [Mecca](https://www.mecca.com.au) and happened to have one of Australia’s leading makeup artists, Chantelle Baker, with me. I dragged her around the store and made her show me her must have beauty buys. It was a real experience!

### I go to [Venustus](https://venustus.com.au) in Paddington weekly for a body scrub and a massage. There’s nowhere in the world like Venustus – it's magic.

![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-11.jpg)


I see [Kristen Fisher Eyebrows](https://www.beauticate.com/who/creatives/kristen-fisher-eyebrow-stylist) for a tint and shape every six months. I have my hair coloured at [Paloma](https://www.beauticate.com/who/creatives/paloma-rose-garcia) and cut by Remington at [Edwards & Co](https://edwardsandco.com.au), and I go to [Tan Temple](https://tantemple.com.au) with my own mousse and get them to put it through their spray gun.

### I eat super clean and I exercise regularly.



![Elle portrait](/interviews/founders/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/Elle-Portrait-12.jpg)


I love The Beauty Chef and add their glow powder to my breakfast smoothies. If I need to shed for a shoot, I turn to Eat Fit Food.

In terms of exercise, I swim, do Pilates and train with my boyfriend (Joel Patfull at JPPT). Kirsten at [Fluidform Pilates](http://fluidformpilates.com.au) trains me every week and has changed my body. Then Joel trains me three times a week with his technique. ## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is Elle Effect and who founded it?**
A: Elle Effect is a self-tanning brand founded by Elle Ferguson, an Australian influencer with a background in fashion and visual merchandising. Elle developed the brand around her signature golden-glow aesthetic, with the Elle Effect Tanning Mousse as the hero product she applies weekly to her body and every three days to her face.

**Q: What is Elle Ferguson's full makeup routine?**
A: Elle's routine starts with ModelCo Face Base, followed by a Stila Stay All Day Balm and Chantecaille Illuminator primer blend. She then mixes Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk Foundation with BECCA Moonstone highlighter, sets with MAC Mineralize Skinfinish, and adds NARS Laguna bronzer. Brows are filled with MAC Great Brows and set with Benefit Gimme Brow. She uses a nude MAC eyeliner on the waterline, cream and powder blush, Kevyn Aucoin mascara, and sets everything with MAC Fix+.

**Q: What are Elle Ferguson's top beauty tips?**
A: Elle's two top tips are always to brush out your curls after tonging for a more natural finish, and to ask your makeup artist questions while they work on you. She also swears by spraying dry shampoo on her hair before bed and brushing it out in the morning for added volume and grit.

**Q: How does Elle Ferguson keep her blonde hair healthy?**
A: Elle washes her hair about three times a week and is currently using TRESemme Pro Collection Violet Blonde Shampoo and Conditioner to prevent brassiness. She also periodically goes a full week without washing, letting natural oils repair damage. She occasionally lets her hair go unwashed for a week to allow the natural oils to repair damage.
