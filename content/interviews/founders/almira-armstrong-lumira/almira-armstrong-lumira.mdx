### "I’ve had a love affair with fragrance for as long as I can remember. One of my earliest memories is of playing with Mum’s bottles of perfume.

After university, a career in marketing kept me in creative circles and offered the valuable opportunity to live and work overseas, but becoming a mum prompted me to rethink the path ahead. I eventually decided to pursue my dream of creating products, which allows me to indulge my passions for scent, design and travel, and so in September 2013 [Lumira](https://www.atelierlumira.com) was born.

LUMIRA TUSCAN FIG CANDLE

### I would say my style is casual chic, as I never know what my day will involve.

Most days I am quite hands on with the business, so I'm usually in my favourite Frame jeans with a white tee, and then a black blazer that can take me into meetings when needed.

I really enjoy the flexibility my work gives me, but my favourite part of my work is the interaction with clients and customers. Bringing a personal touch is important, both to me as a person and to the brand. Getting to share the story of each fragrance inspires my whole creative process.

### I am a beauty minimalist, though I do adore products and enjoy taking care of my skin. I always aim for minimal coverage with a glow.

I rely on Dermalogica Vitamin C serum and Prisma Protect Moisturiser for the day. I also really like that it includes SPF 20, as I'm one of those people that religiously wears sunscreen, even indoors. I finish off with Charlotte Tilbury Airbrush Flawless Finish and Filmstar Bronze and Glow. I keep it pretty simple on the makeup front, but find it hard to leave the house without mascara! I usually opt for the YSL mascara, for both the quality and the packaging.

DERMALOGICA BIOLUMIN-C SERUM

DERMALOGICA PRISMA PROTECT

### I have a few favourite products for my night time routine.

I cleanse using Charlotte Tilbury’s Goddess Cleansing Ritual. It's a two-step process that makes your skin feel really clean. I then use the [Royal Fern Anti-Aging Eye Cream](https://www.peonymelbourne.com.au/product/phytoactive-anti-aging-eye-cream/) and either Dermalogica Sound Sleep Cocoon or Charlotte Tilbury Magic Night Cream.

### I think it's important to keep to a regime for your skin and to try to make the time to relax.

Good skin for me starts from the inside out, so hydration is absolutely key. When I was growing up, my mum always said, “if there is one thing you do everyday, make sure it’s moisturising”. I don’t think I have missed a day since first hearing that. I’m glad I followed that advice!

I regularly visit [Jocelyn Petroni](https://www.beauticate.com/who/creatives/who-jocelyn-petroni-beauty-therapist/) for a facial or a quick Omnilux session. I like visiting [The Langham Spa](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/to-be-indulged/the-day-spa-the-langham-sydney/) for a little indulgence. I also like going for long walks or doing a workout at [F45 Training](https://f45training.com.au). When I travel, having a massage when I arrive is great to relax after a long haul flight.

### When it comes to my hair, I have recently converted to Christophe Robin products.

I use the Hydrating Shampoo, Volumizing Conditioner and the Shade Variation Care for blonde hair. I enjoy how gentle and natural their products are.

### I do believe in regular exercise, whether it be at the gym or walking outdoors, to take care of myself.

I go to [F45](https://f45training.com.au) for a high intensity workout or [Bodylove Pilates](https://www.bodylove-pilates.com) for a low impact session. I also use a body moisturiser every day to keep my skin feeling soft.

LUMIRA PERSIAN ROSE PERFUME OIL

### Some of my favourite things to eat are lots of fruit and vegetables so you will always see salads on our table.

We will then add a protein to the meal and keep them quite healthy. A book I recommend is [Simple by Ottolenghi](https://www.amazon.com.au/Ottolenghi-SIMPLE-Yotam/dp/1785031163), as it has lots of fresh foods with a Mediterranean twist. I am also a big fan of The Beauty Chef, and their Collagen elixir is a part of my daily routine.

LUMIRA SIROCCO CANDLE
