### "My dad has always told me to trust my gut instinct…

… in business and my personal life. My vision [for Mode Sportif] was a destination for off-duty dressing. I wanted to build a brand known for combining luxury fashion with elevated sportswear – the go-to for finding the perfect dress to style with designer sneakers, and a chic and functional workout look, all under the one roof. Having opened the doors to our first retail space in Paddington [Sydney] in July last year, we are focusing on an omni-channel growth model and have many exciting projects on the horizon. Watch this space!

### I was a dancer from the age of five…

… and regularly performed in eisteddfods, so I had a very early introduction to make-up. Stage make up on a pre-teen certainly is a sight to see! I used to insist on doing it myself- think: intense bronzed glitter eyes and dark red lips, eek!

### My mother has incredible skin.

Naturally, I grill her on her secrets hoping they might work for me too! Her advice has always been wear SPF 30+ on your face and get monthly facials from the age of 25. I have memories of being at the beach with friends, and receiving a call from my mum checking I had my 30+ on my face. I would cringe at the time, but I know I’ll thank her one day.

### I love bright, clean and fresh skin.

I keep things fairly simple when it comes to my beauty regime. I focus on cleansing, exfoliating and moisturising and get regular facials to keep my skin in check. I use Lauren Napier make up removing wipes (they’re also amazing for travel as they come in individual packets and never dry out) and The [Eve Lom cleanser](http://mecca.com.au/eve-lom/cleanser/V-007964.html) has changed my life. I use it twice a day to clean and hydrate my skin. Once a week, I use the [GlamGlow Youthmud Mask](http://mecca.com.au/glamglow/glam-glow-youthmud-mask-50ml/I-015263.html). This usually takes place on a Sunday before bed to exfoliate and brighten my skin before the week ahead. I love [Radical Skincare Age Defying Pads](http://mecca.com.au/radical-skincare/age-defying-pads-60-pack/I-018503.html?gclid=CLL14OCIvtICFQybvAodTJkF-g) to gently exfoliate without drying out the skin. Finally, I apply Apot.Care Rich Texture Cream. I tend to have dry skin so tailor my products to focus on bright and hydrated skin.

### I discovered Tom Ford Shimmering Body Oil in Hong Kong.

It’s ideal for highlighting your décolletage for an evening out. I also love the Tom Ford packaging. [Makeup artist] Liz Kelsh told me I needed the Chanel Soleil Tan de Chanel Bronzing Make Up Base in my life, and I’ve never looked back. [Chantecaille](https://www.chantecaille.com) Liquid Illuminatorprovides this dewy glow that lasts the entire day. It’s perfect for all the high points on the face, I love to layer over a tinted moisturiser for a natural look. The marbled packaging from [Hourglass](http://mecca.com.au/hourglass/ambient-lighting-edit-surreal-light/I-025331.html?gclid=CPj52NeKvtICFVNwvAodVqEAsA) is seriously beautiful. I use the translucent rectangle as a powder, and the tonal squares to contour and highlight.

### I change my hair regularly. I’m talking colour changes from light brown, to black, to blonde in the space of a year…

…so I do a weekly Olaplex treatment on a Sunday. It’s critical to keep my hair health. Coming from a girl with extremely frizzy naturally dried hair, the [Dyson Hairdryer](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/dyson-supersonic-hair-dryer-review-is-it-worth-699#elW6Kcl6IU9VA8gb.97=) and its smoothing nozzle has honestly changed my life. [I started using a] large round brush after watching some tips from YouTube and my hair stylist, I have the at-home blow dry down pat. I use Bumble & Bumble Thickening Hair Spray – it’s everything for a voluminous blow dry. I have quite flat hair, so I spray this on my roots prior to blow drying my hair. It works like a charm! For a slick pulled back look nothing beats the Evo Gangsta Grip Bonding Resin.

### I definitely watch what I eat.

I need to be careful, so I always try and eat clean, whole foods. I definitely have a strict diet during the week, and try and stick to proteins, fruits and vegetables. For breakfast I love poached eggs with avocado, for lunch I will have some sushi, sashimi or a salad and for dinner I love salmon with salad. I am definitely not perfect all the time, but I try and find the best balance.

### I love working out.

I definitely mix it up to keep things interesting, with a combination of boxing, pilates, hot yoga and walking. I also love trying new workouts, and when travelling nothing beats [SOULCYCLE](https://www.instagram.com/soulcycle/).

### I have a relaxed day-to-day style…

… filled with a mix of prints and colours. I love creating a look with my outfits. I am not the type of person that has a uniform, I could be wearing a very minimal and monochrome look one day, and then a bright bold print the next. Fashion is very much about expression and I love playing around with it."Story by Rikki Hodge-Smith; Interview by [Sigourney Cantelo](https://www.instagram.com/sigourney.cantelo/); Photography by [Alice Mahran](https://www.instagram.com/alicemahran/?hl=en)
