### From the moment you step into the Scandinavian designed hair studio, you feel instantly at ease and comforted by the wooden walls and surrounding greenery. One of my favourite elements to the salon's design is that there are designated sections for colouring and blow-drying. Mirrors are only located at the blow-dry bar, therefore you do not have to feel uncomfortable by staring at yourself looking like Medusa with a head of foils.

The team at Follow Hair Studio are experienced and friendly, and always keep your hair on trend while respecting your requests. The team also have regular training sessions where they learn the latest techniques and hair trends, so you can count on them for your babylights and ecaille baylayage. Follow Hair Studio uses quality Kevin Murphy products that contain no parabens or sulphates, so you can guarantee your fresh hair colour will not fade in one wash. The salon now uses Olaplex so not only will you leave with a fresh new 'do, you will also leave with hair stronger than what you entered the salon with. Follow Hair Studio is located at Budds Beach on the Gold Coast, directly above Bumbles Café.

Review by Gracie Clough
