Occupying a beautiful corner position on Booth and View Street, **[Rêve Skin + Beauty Annandale ](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/)**announces itself quietly but confidently. My eye was instantly drawn to the warm burgundy exterior – rich, elegant and inviting. As I stepped through the front door, adorned with an oversized bow for the festive season, I felt an immediate sense of ease. Not rushed. Not clinical. Just calm.

Founder **[Meghann Nikola](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/about)** and her team greeted me warmly at the door, and before I had even begun my tour, a loyal client followed closely behind me, happily sharing how devoted she is to Meghann and how regularly she books her appointments. It felt like the ultimate endorsement – the kind of loyalty that can’t be manufactured.

### Thoughtful Design, Inside and Out

As I was guided through the space, **[it became clear that every detail at Rêve has been thoughtfully considered](https://www.instagram.com/reve.skin.beauty?utm_source=ig_web_button_share_sheet&igsh=ZDNlZDc0MzIxNw==)**. Soft lighting that flatters rather than interrogates. Beautiful cabinetry that feels as practical as it is elegant. Even thoughtful touches like in-built charging cables during treatments – because yes, we’ve all been there.

Meghann and I spoke briefly about her background in science and pharmaceutical marketing, which so clearly underpins everything she has created here. **[Rêve](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/)** isn’t simply about indulgence – it’s about evidence-based treatments delivered with warmth, honesty and connection.

A high-end experience, without the intimidation that sometimes accompanies clinical spaces.

### Treatments That Prioritise Skin Health

I was then whisked away for my very first **[lash lift](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/lash-lift)** with Samantha. I’ll admit I was slightly nervous, but her calm confidence immediately put me at ease. The results were genuinely impressive, and it was clear I was in expert hands.

My lash lift and tint flowed seamlessly into a **[Hydrafacial](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/skin/hydrafacial-sydney)**, with a thoughtful handover between therapists to explain aftercare – a small detail, but one that speaks volumes about the level of care here.

During the Hydrafacial, I chatted with Faireen about skin and her extensive experience in the beauty industry, spanning more than 15 years. As the **[treatment](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/service-menu)** progressed, I could feel the gentle warmth on my skin as the glycolic and salicylic acids worked their magic.

The treatment was finished with a decadent layer of **[Synergie Skincare ](https://www.beauticate.com/vodcast-by-beauticate/i-thought-i-wasnt-enough-terri-vinson-jones-on-body-image-anxiety-and-self-worth/)**and a generous application of **[UberZinc](https://www.beauticate.com/sigourneys-edit/winter-beauty-rituals/)**, leaving my skin calm, nourished and visibly refreshed.

### A Retreat Built on Trust

From there, I headed downstairs to meet Sara for my **[manicure](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/nail-treatments-sydney)**. Her work was beautifully precise, leaving me with soft pink nails that felt polished yet effortless – exactly my style.

What struck me most throughout my time at **[Rêve](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/)** was the sense of trust that naturally develops when you’re being cared for by therapists who truly know their craft.

There’s a particular touch that conveys confidence and expertise – something refined only through years in the industry. Every team member I met at **[Rêve](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/about)** seemed to possess it.

### A Moment of Calm in the Middle of the Week

Founder Meghann Nikola’s mission is simple but powerful: to build confidence and connection through **[tailored treatments](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/service-menu)** and compassionate care. She wants every client to feel seen, supported and celebrated – and it’s something you feel in every interaction.

Spending time in a space where multiple treatments can be seamlessly delivered by highly skilled professionals – surrounded by such beautiful interiors – left me feeling genuinely uplifted, especially on what had otherwise been an ordinary weekday.

My time at **[Rêve Skin + Beauty in Annandale](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/)** felt like pressing pause on the rhythm of daily life. Meghann and her team champion skin health in a way that feels thoughtful and empowering – creating a welcoming space for women to experience both luxury and results under one beautifully designed roof.

If you find yourself in Annandale and in need of a little pause from the pace of everyday life, **[Rêve Skin + Beauty](https://www.reveskinandbeauty.com.au/service-menu)** offers exactly that –  thoughtful treatments, beautiful surroundings and a team who truly care.
