Brooke's philosophy is all about marrying serious skin science with a nurturing, intuitive touch — and that's exactly what I experienced during my visit. I was lucky enough to be booked in with co-owner Rachael, whose magic hands gave me one of the most memorable facials I've had in a long time.

## The Facial: Synergie Skindulgence in Expert Hands

The moment I lay down on the treatment bed, I knew I was in expert hands. Rachael started with a thorough skin analysis using a wood's lamp, then moved into the clinic's Signature Synergie Skindulgence Facial. It's a tailored treatment that pairs Synergie Skin's high-performance cosmeceuticals with a deeply skilled massage technique.

(As it happened, I'd just interviewed Synergie founder Terri Vinson the day before for the podcast, so it felt serendipitous to experience the products in a treatment setting so soon after. And yes, they absolutely delivered.)

The facial began with a double cleanse and a Synergie lactic acid peel to gently exfoliate, followed by sonophoresis infusion with the brand's B serum and Hylavive for deep, targeted hydration. But the real hero here? The massage.

![Clinic interior at Me Skin and Body](/content/destinations/clinics/me-skin-and-body-south-yarra/clinic-interior.jpg)

## Why Massage Makes All the Difference

I've always believed that the difference between a good facial and a great one lies in the hands. You can have all the tools, lasers and serums in the world, but if the massage is lacklustre, it just doesn't hit the same. Rachael's hands? Pure alchemy. She strummed my facial muscles, released the tension I didn't know I was holding, and worked the product deep into my skin with a precision and intention that you simply can't fake.

## What Is Buccal Massage — And Why You Need It

Then came the pièce de résistance: buccal massage. If you haven't tried it, it's where the therapist uses gloved hands inside your mouth to massage your jaw and cheeks. I know — it sounds a bit nuts. But it's unbelievably effective for releasing tension and lifting the face. When Rachael removed her gloves, my jawline looked noticeably more defined and the puffiness I'd been carrying had vanished.

![Facial treatment at Me Skin and Body](/content/destinations/clinics/me-skin-and-body-south-yarra/facial-treatment.jpg)

## Post-Facial Glow and Acupressure Bliss

She followed with a long, luxurious session of acupressure facial massage, and by the time I floated out, my skin was noticeably brighter and plumper — and I felt lighter, more grounded, and more than a little bit glowy from the inside out.

I was so impressed with how much clinical know-how Rachael brought to the treatment, but also how nurturing the whole experience was. That's the magic of Me Skin & Body: they're able to deliver real, skin-changing results and hold space for your nervous system to rest and reset.
