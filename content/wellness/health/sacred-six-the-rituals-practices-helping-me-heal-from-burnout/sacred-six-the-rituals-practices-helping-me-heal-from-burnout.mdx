## 2. Energy Healing with Kellie

I recently completed an 8-week *Spiral* with Kellie from [**Soul Healing with Kellie**](https://www.soulhealingwithkellie.com/). Her story of trauma and transformation inspired me to dive in. The process blended emotional clearing, EFT, and inner child work.

**What it gave me:**

-

More openness, less procrastination

-

Courage to take action

-

Cleared blocks around goal-setting

-

Freedom to dream bigger, without fear

One lesson stood out: *get out of your own way*.

## 3. Spinal Energetics with Dr Sarah Jane

Dr Sarah Jane created [*Spinal Energetics™*](https://spinalenergetics.com/), a therapy combining chiropractic, psychology, and energy work. In my session, she “read” my pain with uncanny accuracy. At one point, my arm felt like it was on fire.

Though my neck pain continues, I came away understanding how deeply physical and emotional healing mirror one another.

## 4. Breath & Sound Healing with Tanya Alijani

Through her [*Soulchemy*](https://www.tanyaalijani.com/) method, Tanya combines breathwork and sound frequencies to shift the body into deep relaxation.

I set an intention to release parenting anxiety, and left her session feeling lighter, calmer, and transformed.

## 5. Yin Yoga at Modern Movement

Yin yoga has become my Sunday reset. At [**Modern Movement**](https://modernmovement.com.au/), teachers Tim and Rose create a deeply spiritual, still space that balances the intensity of my other practices. It’s slow, grounding, and exactly what I need to recharge.

[![Mel Robbins speaking on her podcast about the Let Them philosophy and mindset shifts](/content/wellness/health/sacred-six-the-rituals-practices-helping-me-heal-from-burnout/website-images-25-re77mor7drqd08oxuv1e2knydvgmhz3iu4eegu9m34.png)](https://www.melrobbins.com/)

## 6. Mindset Shifts with Mel Robbins

Sometimes the simplest tools make the biggest difference. [Mel Robbins](https://www.melrobbins.com/)’ *Let Them* philosophy and *5 Second Rule* gave me structure and motivation.

They helped me clarify my fitness goals - weights, running, swimming - and reminded me that *movement is medicine* for body, mind, and soul.
