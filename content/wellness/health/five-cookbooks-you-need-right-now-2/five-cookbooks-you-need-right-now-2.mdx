### Lola Berry *Beauty Food*

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Turn your kitchen into a beauty lab. Lola Berry’s latest book is full of amazing recipes that nourish your body, inside and out. Not only are we drooling over her breakfast recipes like the three berry [collagen-boosting porridge](https://thecarousel.com/food/lola-berrys-three-berry-collagen-boosting-porridge/), we are also excited to try her DIY face masks and hair oils. With simple ingredients and instructions, this book teaches even the most kitchen-averse chefs how to cook for beauty.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32056&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" css_animation="fadeIn" link="http://lolaberry.com/lolas-store/"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Teresa Cutter *Purely Delicious*

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32057&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" css_animation="fadeIn" link="https://thehealthychef.com/blogs/recipes/tagged/purely-delicious/"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

[THE HEALTHY CHEF](https://www.thehealthychef.com/tag/purely-delicious/)

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]If you want simple, healthy food that the whole family will love, this book is your new best friend. Teresa Cutter shows us how easy it is to make our own food, without spending tons of time in the kitchen. From busy mums, to dinner party hostesses, this book can be a life-saver. Check out this [green pea and spinach risotto](https://www.thehealthychef.com/2013/07/green-pea-spinach-risotto/), it is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Carla Oates *The Beauty Chef*

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]The Beauty Chef might be the most beautiful cookbook we have ever seen. Carla Oates focuses this cookbook on improving gut heath and ensures that each recipe is nourishing, delicious, gluten-free, and dairy-free. With this book you can look forward to decadent meals that improve your digestion and natural vibrance. Some of our favorites include the Sweet Potato Wedges with Almond and Chili Dukkah, and the Beetroot and Chocolate Brownies (better than regular brownies- promise!)[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32058&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" css_animation="fadeIn" link="https://thebeautychef.com/products/the-beauty-chef-cook-book"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Ellie Bullen *Elsa's Wholesome Life*

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32059&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" css_animation="fadeIn" link="https://www.booktopia.com.au/elsa-s-wholesome-life-ellie-bullen/prod9781925481433.html"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

[ELSA'S WHOLESOME LIFE](https://www.booktopia.com.au/elsa-s-wholesome-life-ellie-bullen/prod9781925481433.html)

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Although this cookbook might leave you yearning for the warmer days of summer, Elsa’s recipes, bursting with colour and flavour, are sure to bring a little bit of sunshine back into your kitchen. All of the meals are all plant-based, which perfect for any vegans and meat-eater alike. The bright colour scheme and gorgeous images make this book almost as fun to flip through as it is to eat from. Elsa makes some of the most gorgeous and delicious smoothie bowls on the planet, like this island dream bowl.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Nigella Lawson *How to be a Domestic Goddess*

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]This book holds the recipe to the dessert of your dreams, we guarantee it. If you want to make everyone on your street jealous with the smells wafting out of your kitchen, this book is for you. Nigella Lawson shows us how to make a gorgeous autumnal birthday cake, fantastic blueberry muffins, and the [classic American pancake](https://www.nigella.com/recipes/american-breakfast-pancakes), without fuss. We dare you to not devour a whole stack in once sitting.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32060&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" css_animation="fadeIn" link="https://www.booktopia.com.au/how-to-be-a-domestic-goddess-nigella-lawson/prod9780701189143.html"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
