Pregnancy is quite an incredible thing. Not only are you supporting your own body, you’re housing a tiny, growing human in your ever-expanding tummy. While it’s a remarkable experience, it also comes with a few less-than-desirable side effects. From reflux to skin tags and the scientifically proven (promise!) ‘baby brain’, I decided to shed some honest light on the less glamorous side of being pregnant. Whether you’re a mother, expecting, or a one-day parent, I hope you’ll enjoy the video and perhaps give yourself (and your mum) a little extra credit today.
