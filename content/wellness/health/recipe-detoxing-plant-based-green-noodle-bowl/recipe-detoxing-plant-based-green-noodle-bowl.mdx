### Vegetarian or not, there’s no denying the benefits of incorporating plant-based meals into your recipe repertoire. It can lessen digestive strain and help you to move away from processed foods, plus there’s all that green goodness creating the coveted ‘beauty from the inside’ glow. So, partnering with [Jurlique  ](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/a-journey-into-natural-skincare-sigourneys-edit?rq=Jurlique)to celebrate Wellness Month this September, [Jacqueline Alwill](https://www.beauticate.com/who/jacqueline-alwill-nutritionist?rq=Jacqueline%20Alwill%20) of The Brown Paper Bag shared with us her gluten and dairy-free recipe for a green noodle bowl packed with disease-fighting phytochemicals (and it tastes amazing).

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="34816&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## INGREDIENTS:

Serves Two.

- 2 kale leaves, torn from stems

- 1 bunch broccolini, trimmed

- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed

- 90g soba noodles

- 1 silverbeet leaf, trimmed on stalks and roughly torn

- 1 zucchini, spiralised

- 1 cucumber, shaved into ribbons

- 2 tablespoons sauerkraut

- 1 tablespoonpepitas

- 2 teaspoons sesame seeds

- 1 tablespoon micro coriander

- 1 tablespoon micro garnet

- 1/2 avocado, sliced

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## DRESSING:

- 1 tablespoon miso

- 2 tablespoons olive oil

- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

- 1 teaspoon honey

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## METHOD:

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

Bring a medium saucepan of water to the boil and blanch kale, broccolini and asparagus for 2 minutes. Remove the vegetables and place aside. Add soda noodles to the water and cook according to packet instructions. Meanwhile, prepare salad dressing by whisking ingredients together. Assemble the green bowl with vegetables and soba noodles around the bottom before topping with sauerkraut, pepitas, sesame seeds, avocado and micro herbs. Add dressing and enjoy.
