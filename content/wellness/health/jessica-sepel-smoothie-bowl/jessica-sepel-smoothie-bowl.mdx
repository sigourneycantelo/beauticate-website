## INGREDIENTS:

![Youneedtotryjessicasepelshealthychocolateicecreambowl](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/jessica-sepel-smoothie-bowl/YouNeedToTryJessicaSepelsHealthyChocolateIceCreamBowl.png)

*(Serves 1)*

- 1 cup ice cubes

- 1 cup almond milk or milk of choice

- 1 serving (25 g) of chocolate protein powder

- 1 tbsp smooth or crunchy peanut butter

- 1 tbsp chia seeds

- 1 tsp cinnamon

- ½ frozen banana

- ¼ tsp xanthan gum, optional

- [JSHealth Signature Granola](https://jessicasepel.com/homemade-granola-2/)

- Handful fresh berries

- Cacao nibs

## METHOD:

Place all of the smoothie ingredients into a high-powered blender and blitz until smooth. Pour into a bowl or mason jar and serve with granola, fresh berries and cacao nibs, if using.

Note: For protein powders, Jessica uses and recommends [Nuzest Clean Lean Protein](https://www.nuzest.com.au/clean-lean-protein/) (for a vegan pea protein) and Myprotein Impact Whey Protein (stevia sweetened).Story by Jessica Sepel and Daniella Giancarli
