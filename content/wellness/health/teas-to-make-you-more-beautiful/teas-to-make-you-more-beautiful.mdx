IT'S ALWAYS TIME FOR TEA WITH THE BEAUTIFUL WEDGWOOD TEA GARDEN COLLECTION

## THE SKIN DEFENCE TEA

The Wedgwood Tea Garden Green Tea & Mint Three Piece Set is the perfect partner for green tea. The most potent of the green tea family is the Japanese varietal Matcha. Often made into a dissolvable stone-ground powder rather than in a bag or in leaves – Matcha Maiden Matcha Green Tea Powder is helps take care of the weather-ravaged skin, and is also believed to help prevent illness and boost memory and concentration.

## THE HAIR REFRESHING TEA

The Wedgwood Tea Garden Lemon & Ginger Set is a bright yet elegant print that will benefit from an equally mellow yellow brew. We like Soothe Tisane from Australian skincare range Synthesis. The nourishing mix of herbs including honeybush, chamomile, ginger root and lemon balm are pumped full of both immune boosting antioxidants and amino acids. ## THE AGE-DEFYING TEA

![](/content/wellness/health/teas-to-make-you-more-beautiful/2.jpeg)


The striking Blackberry set pairs beautifully with a berry blend like Ovvio Organics Ruby Heart Berry Organic Tea by Paddington-based naturopath and herbalist Anthia Koullouros. This vibrant blend of juniper berry, hibiscus flower, and rosehip is like a super-injection of antioxidants providing skin with a radiant boost and preventing aging. These ingredients also assist with relieving cystitis, stomach pain, circulatory issues and inflammation.
