### Ingredients

60g cacao butter, finely chopped

1 tablespoon cold-pressed raw organic coconut or macadamia oil

2 tablespoons raw nut butter

1 tablespoon pure maple syrup, raw dark agave or sweetener of your preference

¼ – 1/3 cup organic raw cacao powder

1 cup frozen organic mixed berries

Melt cacao butter and macadamia oil in a glass mixing bowl over boiling water. Remove from heat adding everything except berries and combine until beautifully smooth. Scatter berries into the bottom of some pretty moulds or a lined glass dish and pour warm chocolate over them tilting slightly to semi-coat each one. The icy fruit will set it in less than a minute! Refrigerate. Serve from the freezer or fridge.

5 minutes to prepare. 1 minute to set. Makes chocolate for 4–6.

TIPS: you can find raw cacao butter at health food stores or online, along with quality raw nut butter and raw cacao powder. Everything else you can find from the supermarket.
