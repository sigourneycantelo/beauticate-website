## [RACHAEL RUDDICK](http://rachaelruddick.com/), ACCESSORIES & JEWELLERY DESIGNER

On swapping champagne for mineral water…

“I find that I am drinking less now than ever before as I need a clear head at night in order to work with our clients in Australia and Asia on different time zones. My advice?  If at an event try to alternate each alcoholic drink with sparkling mineral water”.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26196&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## TANJA G, MODEL AND BLOGGER (MY EMPIRICAL LIFE)

On hangover cures…

“In my early twenties I used to love going out and I still don't mind a great glass of wine or champagne (or three!) I keep my body balanced by making sure I combat the acidity that alcohol causes in the body by sipping alkaline cold pressed vegetable juices the next day. A spoonful of aluminium free baking soda after a night out is my go-to hangover cure”

On finding the perfect running shoe…

“I love running but I always had really bad knee problems until I discovered Nike Free’s – for some reason I never get knee pain now. It is hard to get your hands on all black ones though as they are in high demand!”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26197&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## [MARGARET ZHANG](https://www.beauticate.com/whos?tag=Margaret%20Zhang), BLOGGER (SHINE BY THREE)

On making exercise fun…

“Incidental exercise has always been the best way for me to relax and stay in high spirits – frequent ballet classes and walking between meetings, for instance. You don't realise that you're working hard, which is somehow more rewarding than killing yourself at the gym.”

## [REBECCA VALLANCE](http://rebeccavallance.com/), FASHION DESIGNER

On holding the canapés…

“I often have a quick healthy snack before I go to parties so that I don't feel the need to eat all the canapés. I often have a quick salmon salad or maybe some tamari almonds. I also try and keep my diet as green and organic as possible with lots of leafy salads and green juices.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26199&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## GAIL ELLIOTT, CO FOUNDER & CREATIVE DIRECTOR, [LITTLE JOE WOMAN BY GAIL ELLIOTT](http://littlejoeny.com/)

On early morning exercise…

“I love feeling energised from a fast paced walk along the Bondi to Bronte coast before heading to my office in the morning. It allows me to clear my mind and prepare for the day ahead. I also do Pilates once or twice a week. "I love to run the soft sand at Bondi so I try and get down there 3-4 times a week. It's a great way to de-stress and clear my mind while keeping me active."
