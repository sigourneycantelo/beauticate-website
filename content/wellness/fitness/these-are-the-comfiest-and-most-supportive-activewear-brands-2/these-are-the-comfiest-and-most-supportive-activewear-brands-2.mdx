## Accessories

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]I have weird shaped ears and earphones frequently fall out of them, making running quite stressful. So when[ Huawei launched their new sunglass earphone collaboration with Gentle Monster](https://amzn.to/3HNm4b1), it was very exciting. The suits-practically-everyone black Wayfarer-style sunglasses have a unique acoustic “privacy design” meaning the semi-open speakers on each side of the lens temple, sends the sound into your own ears with the sound barely audible to others even when they’re sitting next to you. They’re the perfect thing for runners who don’t love wearing ear phones as you protect your eyes from the sun at the same time! Another running essential? A phone holder for walking or running. I love the Nimble Athletic Run Belt which can hold your phone and keys  in a comfy snug belt. And finally, if we’re going to actually do sporting activities in our activewear, a fitness watch is a huge help to track your progress. I love the [Fitbit Luxe](https://www.fitbit.com/global/us/products/trackers/luxe) because, with its slim line design and option of a gold link band, it doesn’t look at all like a tracker. It measures my daily activity, sleep and syncs with a variety of other health apps to keep everything in one place.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="57007&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" onclick="custom_link" img_link_target="_blank" link="https://amzn.to/3HNm4b1&#8243;][vc_column_text]
