### If there’s one person who knows how to pack a sports bag in style, it’s exercise aficionado and founder of chic sportswear brand [The Upside](http://theupsidesport.com), Jodhi Meares. Our recent interview with her inspired us to put together a Beauticate edit of the ultimate essentials for three of our favourite workouts. So whether it’s yoga, paddle boarding or running that you’re into, it’s time to get packing.

## What to pack for Bikram Yoga

Soothing feminine tones of pink and purple add a refreshing vibe to an intense bikram practice. Add to that some wipes to take off the day, a stylish [deodorant](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/MalinGoetz/EucalyptusDeodorant/), a refreshing facial mist and Orchard St’s delectable Cashew Chai smoothie to nourish post-vinyasa. With Ayurvedic chai spices, dates and a pinch of sea salt, this alkalising, nutrient and protein rich nut and coconut-based smoothie promotes a calming, restful sleep.

- [Malin & Goetz eucalyptus deodorant](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/MalinGoetz/EucalyptusDeodorant/) from Mecca Cosmetica

- Orchard Street Cashew Chai smoothie

- Evian water spray

- Lululemon Yoga mat

## What to pack for Paddle Boarding

Channel your inner gidget with neoprene accents, a faux glow and an action-girl bikini.

- Lululemon pure focus glass water bottle

- [Bumble and Bumble surf spray](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/Bumbleandbumble/SurfSpray/) from Mecca Cosmetica

- [Missoni ‘lara’ beach towel](http://www.spenceandlyda.com.au/lara-beach-towel-p-728.html)

- Lisa Marie Fernandez ‘Genevieve’ bikini

- Lancôme flash bronzer for face

- The Upside neoprene bag
