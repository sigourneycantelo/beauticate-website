### The marketplace for hair products is ever-changing. It can be hard to keep up with the latest formulas and tools, there's innovation at every turn – [products we didn't know we so desperately needed](http://beeco.green/best-organic-shampoo/). Seemingly innocuous trips to the haircare aisle can suddenly be overwhelming. In short, we're spoiled for choice so much that it can all end by walking away empty-handed. So, Beauticate is here to knock down that wall of unfamiliarity that is preventing you from trying new products and tools. These innovative products promise to revitalise your hair, and we simply couldn't let you miss out on them…

 Image: Instagram @oliviaculpo

## Best for: when you and your tresses are under stress

### This Works Stress Check Hair Elixir

This Works Stress Check Hair Elixir repairs damaged hair, and gives off a fragrance targeted to calm the nervous system. With floral inclusion of neroli, patchouli, lavender, vetivert and chamomile, these olfactory additions were created specifically to reduce feelings of stress and anxiety throughout your day. The formula also adds hydrating Mongogo and Tamanu oils to keep flyaways and frizz at bay, nourishing and conditioning the hair as you go about your busy day.

 

## Best for: improving damaged hair on the go

### Bhave Fresh Ends Split Ends Repair

When it comes to combatting split ends, often quick fixes don't get as great a result as time-consuming hair masks do. So, when Bhave's Fresh Ends Split Ends Repair crossed our desks, we were most impressed by the efficacy and its promise to be busy-friendly. The leave-in formula (that works on dry and wet hair) continues to do its work all day long, restoring health and moisture to your damaged ends.

 

## Best for: obtaining perfect beachy waves

### Bumble and Bumble Surf Foam Blow Dry Spray

Combining the old with new(ish) methods of getting those texture just-went-for-a-quick-dip-in-the-ocean waves we want – so, mousse and sea salt spray – the Surf Foam Blow Dry Spray is the product of the best of both. The soft hold of a mousse, with the natural finish of a salt spray, this product is designed to work with a blow dry (as opposed to air drying). For a more refined look, go for a brushed through blow dry; or for a barely-tried finish, scrunch through ends and use a diffuser to dry. It is also infused with coconut water and UVA/UVB filters to protect against the drying effects of the sun.

## Best for: creating volume anytime, anywhere

### [Davines Volume Creator Brush](http://www.ry.com.au/davines-your-hair-assistant-volume-creator-brush-and-powder-9g.html)

[](http://www.ry.com.au/davines-your-hair-assistant-volume-creator-brush-and-powder-9g.html)

Although many products claim to be purse-friendly in size, little is considered when talking application. Powders, sprays and oils are great in your bathroom, but either inconvenient, or inconsiderate to those around you. The Davines Volume Creator Brush volumises without offending the senses, simply dab the small kabuki-like brush to your roots. This brush applies styling powder seamlessly throughout your hair for an instant textured look.
