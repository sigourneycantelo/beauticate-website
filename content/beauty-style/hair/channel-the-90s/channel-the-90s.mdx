After all, the moodiest of eras did reinvent the burgundy lip- prompting Pinot pouts the world over. It taught us to brush (or not brush) our hair with vigilance and hike up the hemlines, all while rocking a pair of knee-highs like a total Betty. Whether you were totally buggin’ or totally Cher, fresh faced or effortlessly grunge à la Chloe Sevigny; the renaissance of 90’s chic means you get to do it all over again. Here’s how…

### 1. The Flip

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27568&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

ALICIA SILVERSTONE AS CHER HOROWITZ

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Besides having one of the most sophisticated and envied computerized wardrobes to date, Cher Horowitz had a way normal life. While always on the perpetual pursuit of happiness, Cher taught us how to avoid winding up a total Monet. In case you totally paused, Cher hair is making a comeback.

**Get the look:** For shine, brush hair fifty times a night with a Mason Pearson Hairbrush. To style: simply blow dry, part to the side and flip. And flip again[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27570&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 2. Pixie-Grunge Hair

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27576&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

WINONA RYDER

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Before the shoplifting epidsode there was Winona circa ‘Girl Interrupted’, who sported one of the best pixie cuts of the 1990’s. These days, the cut is worn by countless celebs, but it was the raven-haired ingenue who made this short, shabby-chic do’ a salon staple in the 90’s. Her petite features and coal-lined eyes made it all the more adorable.

**Get the look:** Wash and let hair air dry naturally. Then define pieces with a matte texture paste.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27578&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 3. Fresh Skin

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27582&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

JENNIFER LOVE-HEWITT

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Pre-BB and CC, there were the teen queens who showed the beauty world just how fresh youthful skin can be. Sporting little to no make up at all, a little blemish control (blessed genetics) and a great cleanse – both inside and out really will pay off. Eternal note to self, never fall asleep with a full-face of make up.

**Get the look:** A drop of rosehip oil under the eyes and on the cheekbones at night ought to do the trick.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27583&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 4. Moody Eyes

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27584&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

NEVE CAMPBELL

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]The ultimate big screen scream queens showed us just how far a little eyeliner can go. Keeping your base minimal or working with a deep and slightly dangerous lip, a strong eye pencil and lashings of mascara is wildly on trend.

**Get the look:** Line the eyes with a soft kohl pencil and smudge with your finger. ### 5. Dark Lips and Eyes

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27590&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

SARAH MICHELLE GELLAR

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Even with the worst of intentions we couldn’t help but love Sarah Michelle Gellar’s endlessly moody palette of the decade. From maroon to chestnut to mahogany, Gellar could work a stain like nobody’s business. We’ve also learnt if executed correctly, dark eyes and lips can happily exist without committing a beauty faux pas.

**Get the look:** Colour in lips with a deep, inky shade.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27592&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 6. Natural Hair

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27594&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

KERRI RUSSELL

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]The small screen’s favourite college girl had a love life that could make your head explode and a head of hair that looked as though it already had. ‘Felicity’ had a lioness mane all to herself and like Carrie Bradshaw that followed, she proved naturally wild and beautiful hair doesn’t need to be tampered with. Great curl control shampoos have taken over in recent years with more curly girls sporting it untamed.

**Get the look:** Look for a curl-cosseting shampoo and conditioner.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27597&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 7. Girl gone Grunge

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27599&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

CHLOE SEVIGNY

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]When the doe-eyed Sevigny starred in ‘Kids’ our jaws dropped. Decades later and Chloe is still pushing boundaries, this time in the fashion stakes. Another pixie perfected, Chloe was many a 90’s girl’s ‘girl-crush’ striking the perfect cord between ‘I’m in love with Kurt Cobain’ and ‘grunge girl next-door.’

**Get the look:** Skip the eyeshadow and use bronzer to rim your eyes.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27602&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
