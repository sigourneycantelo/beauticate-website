### "Growing up, I watched my mum juggle three kids, full time work and a life so I never saw her spend hours in the bathroom…

She didn’t have time! She would duck in, have a few go-to products (I can still remember the smell of her apricot/almond night cream that she used to put on) and that would be it. Now I’m the same when I'm not at work!

### Between working on The Project, looking after two kids and keeping on top of everything at home, my routine is really simple.

![Screenshot2016 08 29at6.39.25pm](/content/beauty-style/hair/newly-brunette-carrie-bickmore-spills-her-beauty-routine/ScreenShot2016-08-29at6.39.25pm.png)


I put on Garnier BB Cream in the morning before school drop off with mascara and lip balm- that's it. I wear heavy make up on the show so I can't wait to come home, wipe off my make up with [Micellar Water](http://bit.ly/2bQ2ZI2), put on a couple of serums and some moisturiser.

### As far as my hair is concerned, I need low maintenance, and that’s why the colour is so important.

While I get it done every day on the project, on weekends I like to go for a run, duck to a pilates class and have a long list of errands. I need to be able to chuck it up in a pony and get on with things. When I have a good colour, it makes it so much easier.

![Screenshot2016 08 30at12.34.23pm](/content/beauty-style/hair/newly-brunette-carrie-bickmore-spills-her-beauty-routine/ScreenShot2016-08-30at12.34.23pm.png)


CARRIE PRE HAIR-COLOUR TRANSFORMATION

A NEW HUE IS THE PERFECT TRANS-SEASONAL PICK-UP

### It's been really nice to have a change of hair colour.

The last time I tried to go brown I was 19 and it was a disaster, it came out red and didn’t suit me. I still remember the look on my friend’s faces! I think it scared me off ever trying it again, but thankfully home hair colour has come a long way!  But I am finding the older I get, the more confident I am getting to take risks. It's only hair, it will grow back, and it’ll go back to its natural colour, just give it a crack I say!

### I love my blonde but…

… with the change of seasons I wanted to try being a brunette. That's why I love Nutrisse because it makes my hair so soft and shiny that even when I don't style it, it looks done. I used [Garnier Nutrisse in shade 6](http://bit.ly/2bt2DKv), Acorn.

A POST COLOUR BLOW-DRY IS A MUST

### My style is simple, I wear pretty much jeans and a tee or activewear…

I do actually exercise in it, not just coffee! I’m conscious of spending more time looking out to the world than in a mirror, it was something my mum taught me. I really want to pass it on to my daughter in this social media heavy world. Of course I like to look nice, eat well, exercise, work hard and have fun, but I like to feel like I am contributing to the world and making a difference, that’s what’s important".
