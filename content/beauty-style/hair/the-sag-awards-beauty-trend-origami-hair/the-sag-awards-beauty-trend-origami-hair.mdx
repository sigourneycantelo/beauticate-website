### While the crowd pleasers are always commended, this year the SAG awards upped the anti in the beauty stakes with a notable nod to the orient. From sleek yet understated origami buns, blunt fan-like ponytails, sharp fringes and cherry blossom lips; these stars weren’t afraid to push the envelope or embrace the fold. Behold, our favorite Japanese-inspired beauty moments.

### 1. Felicity Jones' origami bun

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26959&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

Wearing an impossibly delicate shade of baby-shower blush, Felicity gave her look a little edge with a sharp fan chignon. As crisp as the folds in her tailored Balenciaga, nothing says ‘fierce’ quite like the fan bun.

Be sure to invest in a heavy-duty heat spray to avoid breakage after straightening, like the cult classic L’Oreal Elnett Satin Heat Sprayto keep your look on point all night long.
