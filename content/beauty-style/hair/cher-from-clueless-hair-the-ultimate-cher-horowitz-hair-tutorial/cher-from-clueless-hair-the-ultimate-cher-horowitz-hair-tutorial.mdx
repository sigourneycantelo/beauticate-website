## Hydrate, Hydrate, Hydrate

In order to get Cher-levels of shine (particularly impressive given than blonde hair is notoriously tricky to make glossy), hair needs to be in tip-top condition. If your strands are damaged and dull, rehabilitate them with a nourishing hair mask: we're currently loving Virtue's Restorative Treatment Mask, infused with alpha keratin to help repair less-than-lustrous locks.

## Great heights

Cher from Clueless's hair is gorgeously voluminous and bouncy, without descending into overdone and backcombed pageant queen territory. ## Get brushing

No Cher Horowitz hair tutorial would be complete without some serious brushing – forget the textured, tousled look, Cher's hair is famously silky and knot-free. The [Mason Pearson brush](https://www.beauticate.com/product/popular-bristle-and-nylon-brush/) is iconic for delivering shine and effortlessly detangling strands, while also redistributing oils from scalp to ends to nourish dry locks.

## Blowdry bliss

One of our favourite things about Cher from Clueless's hair? It's a frizz-free, silky and soft style, which literally never dates (hence our love affair with the 'do a quarter of a century later). The quickest way to get Cher's hair texture is by blowdrying strands smooth with a round brush, to nix flyaways and build volume at the root. Opt for a tool like Dyson's Supersonic Hair Dryer, for gorgeous results sans damage.

## Style it out

Cher's style isn't straight, nor is it your run-of-the-mill bouncy blowdry. It sits somewhere comfortably between the two: smooth and soft with a little natural movement throughout the ends. Run the GHD Glide through your locks to eliminate frizz and kinks, and turn the brush under at the tips for that subtle Cher from Clueless hair curl.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do you get Cher from Clueless hair at home?**
A: Start with a volumising shampoo like John Frieda Luxurious Volume Core Restore to build the foundation, then rehabilitate dry strands with a keratin-infused mask like Virtue's Restorative Treatment Mask. Blowdry with a round brush using the Dyson Supersonic for volume and frizz control, then run the GHD Glide through to eliminate kinks and curl the ends under slightly for that signature Cher movement.

**Q: What brush does Cher Horowitz use in Clueless?**
A: The Mason Pearson brush is the iconic tool for achieving Cher's silky, knot-free hair. It delivers shine, effortlessly detangles strands, and redistributes oils from scalp to ends to nourish dry locks - all key elements of the famously glossy Clueless look.

**Q: How do you get shiny, frizz-free blonde hair like Cher from Clueless?**
A: Hair needs to be in tip-top condition first, as blonde hair is notoriously tricky to make glossy when damaged. Use a nourishing mask to rehabilitate dull strands, blowdry smooth with a round brush to eliminate flyaways, then finish by spritzing Davines Silkening Oil Mist through styled hair and brushing out for amplified shine without greasiness or lost volume.

**Q: What products do you need for Cher Horowitz hair?**
A: You need a nourishing hair mask (Virtue Restorative Treatment Mask), a volumising shampoo (John Frieda Luxurious Volume Core Restore), a Mason Pearson brush, a Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer with a round brush attachment, a GHD Glide, and a lightweight finishing oil like Davines Silkening Oil Mist.

**Q: Is Cher Horowitz's hair straight or curly?**
A: Neither - it sits between the two. It's blowdried smooth and silky with a round brush for volume, then the ends are turned under slightly with a GHD Glide for a subtle, bouncy curl. The result is glossy and frizz-free with a little natural movement, not poker straight and not a full blowout curl.
