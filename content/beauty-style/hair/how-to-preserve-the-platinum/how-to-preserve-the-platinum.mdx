### On that note, behold the best buys a soon-to-be platinum plunger ought to armour up with. From restorative masques to colour-tempering shampoo, we’re talking purple, silver and bound to preserve your platinum ‘do. So make like Kim K and try something new.

AGYNESS DEYN

Model Agyness Deyn sparked a platinum pixie revival when she quickly became model of the minute in early 2000’s. This short, low maintenance cut counteracts perfectly with the high maintenance colour.

AGYNESS DEYN

To keep your hue ultimately ashy and your platinum on point, try Pureology’s Perfect 4 Platinum Miracle Filler Treatment. The leave-in filler will aid to protect your fragile strands and help to boost volume.

[PUREOLOGY’S PERFECT 4 PLATINUM MIRACLE FILLER TREATMENT](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/how-to-preserve-the-platinum?rq=How%20To%20Preserve%20Your%20Platinum#)

MICHELLE WILLIAMS

Michelle Williams perfects the retro wave and back comb while nailing the hue of the minute.

MICHELLE WILLIAMS

A shade reviving balm like Shu Uemura Color Lustre in Cool Blonde will neutralize any brassy yellow tones and bring out the silver nuances.

![1.agynessdeyn](/content/beauty-style/hair/how-to-preserve-the-platinum/1.AgynessDeyn-1.jpg)


SHU UEMURA COLOR LUSTRE IN COOL BLONDE

MARILYN MONROE

Arguably the world’s most recognizable blonde bombshell Miss Monroe went from honeyed auburn to piercingly platinum, before there even was such a thing.

MARILYN MONROE

For those with full-bodied hair, try a keratin-based shampoo that’ll keep the frantic frizz at bay. Keratin Complex Blondshell Shampoo**:**boasts equal parts keratin and fade-fighting chamomile properties to simultaneously bring you back to life.

KERATIN COMPLEX BLONDSHELL SHAMPOO

JAMIE KING

Jamie King proves even the boldest of colours can be softened with the right approach to styling. Be warned, however, pulled back platinum styles require optimal root control.

![1.pureologyplatniummiraclefiller](/content/beauty-style/hair/how-to-preserve-the-platinum/1.PureologyPlatniumMiracleFiller.jpeg)


Bumble & Bumble’s Prêt-à-Powder will help to mask any telltale signs of regrowth and also give you that “I woke up like this” bedroom hair. Need we say more?

BUMBLE & BUMBLE’S PRÊT-À-POWDER

EMILY WEISS

Emily Weiss of ‘[Into The Gloss](http://intothegloss.com/)’ does gritty-root-meets-polished-platinum like a pro. The tousled and undone approach to such a shade is a sure fire winner.

Weiss credits Christophe Robin’s Shade Variation Care Nutritive Mask in Baby Blond as her shade saver, quite fittingly dubbing this crème de la crème “the Rolls Royce of Purple Shampoos”.
