### “I will never be the girl with the perfect hair who can wear white and not spill on it.” – Carrie Bradshaw.

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27551&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

CHARLOTTE ROCKS THE ALICE BAND

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 1. There is a headband for every occasion, upper East Side and beyond.

Let it be known, Charlotte York was working the headband long before Blair Waldorf could crawl, so credit where credit’s due Ms. York. Sporting a Chanel belt as a headband and proving polished doesn’t necessarily mean ‘preppy’ or ‘prim.’[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27553&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

CARRIE DID OMBRE BEFORE IT WAS A THING

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 2. When in doubt, grow it out.

One could argue that Carrie and her wild roots pioneered the now viral trend, which has since seen salon-goers catch the balayage (or Balliage depending who you ask) bug the world over. From Saint-Germain to Fifth Avenue.  Balayage according to Bradshaw, imperfectly perfect.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27555&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

SAMANTHA KNEW THE IMPORTANCE OF HAIR IN THE GAME OF SEDUCTION

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 3. Men may come and go but your mane is forever – so avoid getting stuck in a style rut.

While man-friends were a dime a dozen, one thing continues to rein true nothing prompts change quite like a fresh haircut, colour or style. Out with the old, in with the new.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27556&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

CARRIE DOES TWENTIES BETTER THAN A FLAPPER

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 4. A retro do will never cease to sizzle.

When faced with an abundance of pre-first date decisions, revert back and consider opting for a sleek pin curl and bold red lip. Striking, versatile and bound to tie in with the majority of your wardrobe.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27558&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

A TOP KNOT KEEPS ALL THE FOCUS ON YOUR FASHION

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 5. Top Knots and high-fashion go together like peanut butter and jam.

From your standard post-work out knot to a sleeker statement up-do, Carrie rocked them all with perfect precision. The top-knot carried Carrie through her on-again off-again relationship woes, many a shopping spree and even made a cameo at the Big wedding that never was. We won’t forget you, knot.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27560&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

MIRANDA SHOWED MANY SHADES OF RED

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 6. A statement colour is a sure fire way to stand out, regardless of the oh-so-chic company you keep.

Miranda may have not been as daring on the wardrobe front, but her hair evolution is something to be admired. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different colour notes if you do decided to go red. The perfect colour won’t happen over night.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27562&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

CHARLOTTE CONSIDERS DOING A VODKA RINSE

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 7. If your hair is shiny and polished, everything else in your life will eventually fall into place. Promise.

Charlotte may not have had very attainable upper East Side Princess priorities, but one thing she undoubtedly did achieve was a shiny head of hair – similar to that of the Duchess of Cambridge. A balanced diet rich in fish oils and super-foods like lentils and spinach, will keep your tresses shiny from the inside out. And the occasional cosmo won't hurt, either.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]*Words by Ashley Ropati*.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row]
