[vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]We spoke to Aleks Abadia, Head Stylist and Founder of Esstudio Galleria, who revealed the secrets to sophisticated strands that stand a chance against wet, windy days.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## The Topknot

A fast fixture in the hairstyling world, the topknot is a simple, no-fuss option for the girl on the run.

“Worn dirty, this look is great for work and casual day wear. Add dry shampoo and away you go,” says Aleks.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="48828&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @lena.simonne

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Braided Bun

Stepping your technique up a notch, the braided bun is a chic choice for those wanting a more composed look.

“Try a loose, low braid and simply twist it into a bun, securing with bobby pins,” says Aleks.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="48830&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @kellymccormickhair

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Side Braided Pony Tail

Following on the theme of braids, this style is a great way to add some texture to your tresses.

“Great for long hair, this style adds some interest to a basic pony tail,” says Aleks.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="48826&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @jourdandunn

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## The Low Bun

Sleek and sophisticated, this style will take you from the office to drinks seamlessly.

“This is a very stylish and very Spanish tango dancer look," says Aleks. "Sleek and smooth will always work well on an evening spent out and about!”[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="48834&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @rosiehw

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Twisted Sisters

This imperial style is quite literally an elegant twist on the classic low bun.

“Twist away from the face and secure with U pins," explains Aleks. "Create interest with a twisted knot on the ends, before securing with bobby pins.”[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="48832&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]
