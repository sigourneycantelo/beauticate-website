## BEST FOR: DARKER HAIR

### Klorane Gentle Dry Shampoo with Oat Milk$14.99

KLORANE GENTLE DRY SHAMPOO WITH OAT MILK$14.99

“Hearing that dry shampoo can leave tell-tale signs of use with white marks, especially on darker hair (mine is medium brown), I proceed with caution. This brand is a known classic, so I’m also quietly optimistic. Formulated with oat milk, this dry shampoo immediately removed my roots of visible oil – I’d deliberately extended my usual wash day to test the product. My hair appears to be freshly washed but with natural additional volume. My hair isn’t left with any obvious signs that I’ve called on the magic shampoo-in-a-can, and if you have darker hair this classic is now also available with a tinted formula for brown to dark hair.”

MOROCCANOIL DRY SHAMPOO, $39.95

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which dry shampoo is best for darker hair?**
A: Klorane Gentle Dry Shampoo with Oat Milk is recommended for medium to dark hair. It removes oily roots without leaving white marks, and a tinted formula for brown to dark hair is also available.

**Q: Which dry shampoo offers sun protection?**
A: Moroccanoil Dry Shampoo has built-in UV protection. It is available in both light and dark tone versions and refreshes hair without a crunchy or starchy feeling.

**Q: What makes Bumble and Bumble Pret-a-Powder different from a standard dry shampoo?**
A: It is a multi-tasking powder that absorbs oil while maintaining shine. Unlike many dry shampoos, it does not dull strands and works well on lengths to add volume to waves and textured styles.

**Q: Which dry shampoo is the most affordable option tested?**
A: Pantene Volume Booster Dry Shampoo at $9.99 was the most budget-friendly. It delivered serious volume and held well through the day for fine, limp hair.
