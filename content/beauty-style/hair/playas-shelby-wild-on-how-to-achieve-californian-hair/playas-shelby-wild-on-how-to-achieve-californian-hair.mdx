## West Coast Vibes

While there are plenty of products on the market geared towards glossy, polished locks, Shelby was keen to create a line for women wanting a more natural, undone look. “When I began the journey of making these products, I wanted them to be infused with the laid-back California spirit I love so much,” she shares. “I wanted them to be effortless and healthy, but I also wanted them to actually work.” The women that exemplify this spirit in Shelby’s eyes? “Sabina Socol, [Quincy Davis](https://www.instagram.com/quincydavis/), [Anna Feller](https://www.instagram.com/annafeller/), and Yan Yan Chan.”[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="38784&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @sabinasocol

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Note not one of these girls is from California – so there is hope for us all. In fact, Shelby sees parallels between how Californian and Australian women like to wear their hair. “Both women want an effortless style that lasts as long as it can,” she explains. “[Dry shampoo](https://www.mecca.com.au/playa/pure-dry-shampoo/I-032325.html?cgpath=brands-cv) and (our) Soft Volume Powder are essential for Californian and Australian women.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Air Control

The best part about Californian hair? It involves a lot of air drying (fantastic news for the hairdryer averse amongst us). “I love any trick that allows me to air-dry my hair,” says Shelby. Her tricks for perfect hair, sans heated tools? “I will twist my hair into a bun and spray our New Day Hair Mist all over, let my hair dry then take my bun out for a natural soft wave.”[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="38786&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

Image: Instagram @playa

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Another trick she loves is to twist sections of her hair away from her face, then spritz with her [Endless Summer Spray](https://www.mecca.com.au/playa/endless-summer-spray/I-032326.html?cgpath=brands-cv) and air dry to finish. And the trends Shelby anticipates being big this year besides air drying? “Scalp health and clean products,” she predicts. ## Californian Must-Haves

What does a true California girl have in her beauty kit? “Our [Every Day Shampoo](https://www.mecca.com.au/playa/every-day-shampoo/I-032323.html?cgpath=brands-cv) and [Supernatural Conditioner](https://www.mecca.com.au/playa/supernatural-conditioner/I-032324.html?cgpath=brands-cv) (because good hair starts in the shower) and [Kosas’ ultra-hydrating lip balm](https://kosas.com/products/kosasport-lipfuel),” shares Shelby. Shelby suggests you finish your Cali-girl look with [RMS’ Un-Coverup in 22](https://www.mecca.com.au/rms-beauty/un-cover-up/V-020196.html), [Fat and The Moon’s lip and cheek stain](https://www.fatandthemoon.com/products/lip-cheek-stain-5-oz), and [Hourglass’ Caution Mascara](https://www.mecca.com.au/hourglass/caution-extreme-lash-mascara/V-033537.html?cgpath=makeup-eyes-mascara).[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="38788&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]
