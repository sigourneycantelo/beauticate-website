After sitting pretty in the early 2000s’ with a safe blonde layered mid-length ‘do (the mane uniform of all teen pop princesses back then), Hilary Duff has since become something of a hair chameleon. From pastels to green, long to short, she’s unafraid to try new things in the tress category. She’s done it again, [and couldn’t be more on trend](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/the-one-hairstyle-everyone-wants-right-now).

Posting on her Instagram at the weekend, Duff revealed her latest look: a layered ‘shag’ with bangs.
