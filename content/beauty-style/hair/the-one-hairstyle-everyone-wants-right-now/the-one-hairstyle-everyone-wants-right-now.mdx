## WHAT IS IT AND WILL IT SUIT ME?

Straight from the seventies, the shag is all about rough, imperfect texture (think [Stevie Knicks](https://www.beauticate.com/who/stevie-knicks-musician-and-icon)). The beauty of this look is that it works with varying lengths- from medium to short (if you’re game). Curlier hair should keep it longer (think to your shoulders) to avoid an afro, whereas straighter types can afford to loose the length.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="8656&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="KARLIE KLOSS HAS KEPT A LITTLE OF HER LENGTH" font_container="tag:p|font_size:10px|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="8657&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="ANJA RUBIK ROCKS ASYMMETRICAL LAYERS" font_container="tag:p|font_size:10px|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="8658&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_custom_heading text="TAYLOR SWIFT WITH SIDE-SWEPT BANGS AND SHOULDER-GRAZING LENGTH" font_container="tag:p|font_size:10px|text_align:center" use_theme_fonts="yes" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528112419904{padding-top: 30px !important;}"][vc_column][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

## WHAT TO ASK YOUR HAIR STYLIST FOR:

Ask your hairdresser for textured layers with messy ends, and if you’re taking a leaf out of Karlie or Taylor’s book, ask for piecey bangs as well as the face framing layers. If you’re hair is quite thick, ask your stylist to take out a little bit of the bulk.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528112419904{padding-top: 30px !important;}"][vc_column][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]

## HOW TO STYLE:

As far as styling, less is certainly more (music to our ears). If your tresses are on the finer side or lack texture, work through a texturizing spray like Shu Uemera Texture Wave Dry Workable Spray. And if you really want to roughen things up, we love R&Co Badlands Dry Shampoo Paste. Warm it between your fingers, before working through roots and mid lengths. If you want to add movement, use a heat styling tool to add some imperfect bends. The new copper GHD Platinum will do the job perfectly.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row css=".vc_custom_1528112419904{padding-top: 30px !important;}"][vc_column][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeInUp"]
