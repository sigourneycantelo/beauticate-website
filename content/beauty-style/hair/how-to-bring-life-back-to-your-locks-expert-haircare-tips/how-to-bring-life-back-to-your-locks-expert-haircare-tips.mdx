**1. Hydrate with Deep Conditioning Treatments**

Lack of moisture is one of the biggest culprits behind dry, brittle hair. A high-quality deep-conditioning treatment is essential for restoring hydration, softness, and strength. Look for masks infused with nourishing ingredients like avocado oil, argan oil, and shea butter-these are rich in fatty acids that help repair damage and lock in moisture.

**4. Embrace Spring Hair Trends: Light Waves and Pastel Hues**

*How to Get Soft Waves:*

Use a curling wand on low heat to create loose curls, then brush them out for soft, natural waves.

Finish with a texturizing spray for volume and hold.**
