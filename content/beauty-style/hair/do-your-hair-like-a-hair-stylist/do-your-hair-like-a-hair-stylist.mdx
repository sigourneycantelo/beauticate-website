### As co-owner of [Oscar Oscar salon](http://oscaroscar.com.au/) in Sydney’s Paddington and artist for Redken, Paloma Rose Garcia needs to be a walking billboard for great hair. Clients and beauty editors constantly look to her for guidance on the latest trends and techniques, so it’s lucky she has an enviable mane of her own to work with. Even so, she insists that it’s very low maintenance, with two small children, she needs hair that doesn’t take a lot of time. Here, in her lovely bedroom in Sydney’s Surry Hills, she shows us how easy it can be if you work with your natural texture.

Video by [Jake Terrey](http://jaketerrey.com) and [Maneula Leigh](http://www.manuelaleigh.com/); Hair by Paloma Rose Garcia from [Oscar Oscar Paddington](http://oscaroscar.com.au/); Makeup by Regina Gao.
