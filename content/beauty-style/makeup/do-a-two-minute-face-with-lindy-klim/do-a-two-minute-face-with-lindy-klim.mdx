### Mum, model and founder of skincare brand Milk & Co, Lindy Klim is a master at time management. Not only does she have three beautiful children under the age of eight, she writes a fashion and lifestyle blog, DJs and travels the world with husband, former Olympic Swimmer Michael Klim. Lucky she’s got her beauty routine down to a super speedy art. Here she shows reveals her time-saving tricks.

*Makeup by Becca Gilmartin; Hair by [Brad Ngata](http://www.bradngata.com.au); Lindy wears Ellery; Sigourney wears Scanlan Theodore and Saba; Filmed at [The Establishment Hotel](http://merivale.com.au/accommodation/establishmenthotel/).*
