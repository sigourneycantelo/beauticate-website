  - question: "How do you wear glitter eyeshadow without looking too young or party-ready?"
    answer: "The key to grown-up glitter is in the application technique and product choice.     answer: "Yes, when applied strategically. Pairing glitter with matte, neutral shades in browns, blacks, or taupes creates a polished, editorial look suitable for the workplace."
