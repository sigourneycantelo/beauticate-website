### This week we're out to lunch at the vibrant Panama House in Bondi. Sigourney enlightens us with one of her beloved beauty tips for on-the-go looks: Multi-function Products/ Palettes. Great for saving room in your handbag while also saving you time, we absolutely love the concealer pen that doubles as an eye shadow primer and the brow palette that disguises itself as a great eyeliner. Finish this look up with a burst of coral, and you’re ready for a casual lunch date or a night out on the town.
