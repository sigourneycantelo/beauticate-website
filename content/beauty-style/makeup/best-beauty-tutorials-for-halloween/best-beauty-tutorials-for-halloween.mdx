### We’ve collated our favourite frightening (but not nauseatingly scary) Halloween beauty tutorials so you can trick or cheat your way through the ghoulish, cobweb-laden parties. So fire up the cauldron and have your broomstick at the ready; double, double toil and trouble…

#### 1. CHLOE MORELLO’S EASY SEDUCTIVE VAMPIRE WITH BLOODY MOUTH

Thanks to the tween Twilight revolution, vampires have made an unexpected resurgence in the sexy department. Stick your fangs into Chloe’s surprisingly simple blood-sucking temptress, a sure-fire way to be both hot and hellish amongst the ghouls this Halloween; oozing blood obligatory.

## 2. STEPHANIEMAII'S PLASTIC SURGERY BARBIE

This is a hilarious take on the plastic fantastic swell which is consuming the world currently. Channel, like, a Kardashian (or Jenner) and, like, amp up the tawdry and tasteless vibes with a glamorous yet surgically enhanced guise. In the er, wise words of Mean Girls' Miss Regina George, “Halloween is the one night of the year when girls can dress like a total slut and no other girls can say anything about it”.

#### 3. MADEYEWLOOK’S ‘THE COUNTESS’ FROM AMERICAN HORROR STORY

Referencing a scary, silver-screen siren is but a must on Halloween, and who better than Lady Gaga’s Countess Elizabeth in the upcoming season of American Horror Story. In true Gaga fashion, she is set to make a thrilling and twisted debut in the series, with hype burgeoning. Add a touch of avant-garde couture to this makeup and scare your way through Halloween like Pop’s First Lady.

Cast a spell upon an unwitting princess this Halloween, and channel Angelina Jolie’s evil Queen Fairy Maleficent. Bewitchingly beautiful, Maleficent’s maquillage is attainable and yet commanding. A spooky, stylised and sexy take on Disney’s original 'Mistress of All Evil' in Sleeping Beauty; remember "the evil queens are the princesses that were never saved".

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which Halloween looks are featured in this guide?**
A: The guide covers five looks - a seductive vampire by Chloe Morello, a Plastic Surgery Barbie by StephanieMaii, The Countess from American Horror Story by MadeYewLook, Maleficent by Miranda Hedman and Black Lady Sailor Moon by Michelle Phan.

**Q: What product creates the bloody effect in the vampire look?**
A: Ben Nye Blood Effects Gel is recommended for the Chloe Morello vampire tutorial.

**Q: Which look is inspired by Lady Gaga?**
A: The Countess look, based on Lady Gaga's character in American Horror Story, is styled using Becca Shimmering Skin Perfector in Moonstone and Charlotte Tilbury's The Rock Chick palette.

**Q: What eye product is recommended for the Maleficent look?**
A: Eylure Exaggerate Lashes and the Urban Decay Naked3 Eyeshadow Palette are key products for achieving Maleficent's commanding look.
