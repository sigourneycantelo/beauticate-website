When it comes to the perfect mascara, we’re constantly on the hunt for a formula that’s not too heavy but not too light, and definitely with no clumps –  along with a superb brush for defined, lengthened and full-looking feathery lashes. So when we heard Teen Vogue had discovered the best-selling global mascara, we couldn’t wait to try out the product in question.

Out of the world’s 195 countries, this is the mascara that sells the most…..

This is [Too Faced Better Than Sex Mascara](http://mecca.com.au/too-faced/better-than-sex-mascara/V-016521.html), and it isn’t your standard run-of-the-mill product. The innovative brush was developed with the curves of an iconic blonde bombshell in mind. (Read: Marilyn Monroe).

So what makes it so good? According to Too Faced, a damn lot.

"The Better Than Sex Mascara hourglass-shaped brush was designed after discovering the bust-waist-hip ratio of an iconic silver screen blonde bombshell held the secret to the perfect brush silhouette that separates, coats and curls each lash to voluptuous perfection.

One coat of the carbon black, collagen-fuelled mascara formula and lashes are full, defined and stretched to unbelievable lengths. Two coats and lashes are even more luscious, curled and dramatic. Three coats and achieve the most intense black, oversized, multidimensional lashes possible!"

Nice. If you want to try it out for yourself, the mascara will only set you back [$37](http://mecca.com.au/too-faced/better-than-sex-mascara/V-016521.html) – affordable, and nifty. #winning
