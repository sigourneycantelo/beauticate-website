There are so many great things about the holiday season, but our personal favourite is the festive spirit of beauty brands – gilded special editions, pampering gift packs and one-off collections. Today, the latter has come in the form of Kylie Cosmetics’ Kylie Lip Kit Vault, released today to kickstart the brand’s online 12 Days of Christmas.

The kit contains 12 full-sized lip shades – Maliboo, Posie K, Kristen, 22, Mary Jo, Kourt K, Exposed, Dolce K, Candy K, Koko K, Ginger, True Brown – and retails for about $AU160 (a single lipstick is approx. $AU23). Steal!

Sounds like the perfect pressie to put under the tree, but get in pronto – the kit is selling fast.
