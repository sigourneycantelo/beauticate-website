### We’ve all seen pose-perfect pics on Instagram… So why then, when we hit the switch screen, do we look like a selfie cat? You don't need to have a glam squad and a personal photographer handy to pull of a great selfie though. According to YouTube beauty vlogger Jaclyn Hill, it's all about one simple beauty trick…  Highlighting. Ready, set, selfie..

## START WITH HYDRATED SKIN…

Apply a hydrating body moisturiser to your shoulders, décolletage and neck area. Once absorbed, apply a highlighter of your choice – shimmer lotion, dusting powder, creme highlighter – to the same areas.

## FOR FULL BODY SHOTS…

Highlight the centre of your legs to make them appear longer.  This well-placed highlighting gives your body a glow and contour that's works to create shape with the depth of a phone camera. Layering multiple shades of champagne, gold, and peach toned highlighters create a dimensional and sun-kissed look.

![](/content/beauty-style/makeup/the-one-trick-that-will-give-you-a-perfect-selfie-every-time/iphones-1.jpg)


It may be hard to believe that your newsfeed filled with beautiful photos aren’t all credited to decorative filters and ideal lighting, and it's totally attainable in a very easy way. Just be sure not to apply too close to your clothing to avoid any product transferring surfaces.
