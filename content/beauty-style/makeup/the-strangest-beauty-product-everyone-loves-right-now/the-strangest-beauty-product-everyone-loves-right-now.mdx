The latest craze to hit the beauty world is the [SiliSponge](https://www.mollycosmeticsshop.com/products/silisponge?variant=31245784332). It looks convincingly like a breast implant, but the silicone disc is actually the handiwork of Molly Cosmetics.

With six restocks selling out to date, the silicone-filled ‘sponge’ works like a beauty blender, buffing in makeup to filter-like perfection. The hook that has everyone lining up for pre-order is that, unlike its traditional counterpart, it doesn’t absorb any of your foundation and is a cinch to clean. If patience is your forte, the current waitlist for the product is delivery by mid Jan.

It will only set you back around 15 Australian dollars, and if it delivers on the ‘use half the amount of foundation’ promise, it might just be worth the wait.
