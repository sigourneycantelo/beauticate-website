  - question: "What's the best way to measure where your eyebrows should start and end?"
    answer: "To find your brow's starting point, hold a makeup brush vertically from the outside of your nostril straight up to your inner eye corner. For the endpoint, hold a brush from the outer corner of your eye outward at a 45-degree angle. These measurements work for most face shapes and help create a balanced, flattering arch."
  - question: "How do you fill in eyebrows without them looking fake?"
    answer: "Use short, upward strokes with an angled brush rather than one continuous line. This mimics natural hair growth and creates a softer, more dimensional look. Fill the brows using short strokes going up and outwards, which keeps them looking feathered and realistic rather than harsh or block-coloured."
  - question: "Can you use hairspray to set eyebrow makeup?"
    answer: "Yes, hairspray is an excellent budget-friendly way to set eyebrow makeup and keep hairs in place throughout the day. ### Desiree Wise is an excellent makeup artist and one of our favourite girls to work with. Here she tames model Emily Green's incredible brows into submission with nothing more than two eyebrow brushes, some powder and some hairspray.

**Step 1.**Brush your hairs down so you can see the natural line of the brow.

![Img](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/img.gif)

**Step 2.** Measure the end point of your brow by using another makeup brush and holding it from the outside of the eye.

![img 1](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/img-1.gif)

**Step 3.**Measure the beginning of the brow by drawing an imaginary line from the outside nostril straight up to the inner corner of the eye.

![img 2](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/img-2.gif)

**Step 4.**fill in the top line of the brow with a short angled brush.

![emily brows 4](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-4.gif)

**Step 5.** Brush the hairs up.

![emily brows 5](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-5.gif)

**Step 6.** Fill in the brows using short strokes up and outwards.

![emily brows 6](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-6.gif)

**Step 7.** Spray your eyebrow brush with hairspray.

![emily brows 7](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-7.gif)

**Step 8.** Brush them up and outwards.

![emily brows 8](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-8.gif)

And there you have it – tamed brows in minutes.

![emily brows 9](/content/beauty-style/makeup/how-to-fill-in-your-brows-with-desiree-wise/emily-brows-9.gif)
