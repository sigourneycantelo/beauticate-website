  - question: "How do you apply mineral eyeshadow for a smokey eye?"
    answer: "Start by applying a light neutral shade across your lid as a base, then layer darker charcoal or bronze tones in the crease using a fluffy blending brush. Build the colour gradually, working from the inner corner outward, and blend thoroughly to create a seamless gradient. Finish by intensifying the outer corner for extra depth and dimension."
  - question: "Is mineral makeup good for party makeup?"
    answer: "Yes, mineral makeup is excellent for party looks because it's long-wearing, blendable, and creates a naturally polished finish that photographs beautifully. Mineral formulations are also gentle on the skin, making them ideal if you're wearing makeup for extended periods. They won't feel heavy or caky, even when you're dancing and moving around."
  - question: "What's the best way to blend mineral eyeshadow?"
    answer: "Use a soft, fluffy blending brush and circular motions to seamlessly merge colours together, working in the crease and outer lid areas. Mineral eyeshadows blend beautifully when you use light pressure and build colour gradually rather than applying too much product at once. A damp brush can also help blend mineral shadows more effectively if needed."
  - question: "How do you make a smokey eye last all night?"
    answer: "Apply an eyeshadow primer before your mineral makeup to create a long-lasting base that prevents creasing and fading. Set your eyeshadow with a setting spray once you've finished, and consider using a waterproof eye pencil in your crease for added definition that won't budge. These steps ensure your party look stays flawless from evening through to the early hours."
