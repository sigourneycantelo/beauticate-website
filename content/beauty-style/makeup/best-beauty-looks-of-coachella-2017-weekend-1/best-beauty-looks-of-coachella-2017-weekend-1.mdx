It’s that time of year again. Mid-April, when all the festival lovers of the world, along with a ton of celebrities, gather in the dusty Colorado desert to celebrate music, fashion and, of course, good times. Coachella looks and trends never fail to disappoint, and this year is no exception. Long days in the desert call for pretty, low-maintenance looks, which we’re all for. Let’s take a look at our favourite beauty looks from this past Coachella weekend.

## Victoria Justice's Pink Lip

[A post shared by Victoria Justice (@victoriajustice)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BTAO-W4lTQh/) on Apr 17, 2017 at 3:43pm PDT

EN-US

 

Victoria Justice, looking stunning as ever, sported a bright pink lip as part of her Coachella look this past weekend, adding some color to her black outfit. We love a nice bright pink lip because it goes well with minimal makeup. Victoria wore her gold lip with a fresh face and some light eye makeup. A simple outfit can be played up with a pretty pink lip, so next time you’re looking to spice up a plain look.

## Elle Ferguson's Full Waves

[A post shared by Elle Ferguson (@elle_ferguson)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BS_4s05A7E_/) on Apr 17, 2017 at 12:28pm PDT

EN-US

 

Are we surprised that style guru Elle Ferguson looked absolutely flawless at Coachella? Definitely not. Her full, effortless waves were the perfect, low-maintenance look. Elle nailed the trendy, festival look but pairing her beautiful blonde waves with a black hat, also giving her some sun protection, so extra points for for choosing a skin conscious accessory.

## Talita Correa's Boho Braid

[A post shared by Talita Correa (@talita_c)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BTAKcDchkYO/) on Apr 17, 2017 at 3:03pm PDT

EN-US

 

Bring out your nimble fingers because braids are so in. Talita Correa had a simple loose braid that made the coolest model look even cooler. Braids are so versatile and come in handy when your hair just won’t cooperate. Whether its two French braids, a loose side braid, or a fishtail braid, these looks will have you looking festival-girl cool any day.
