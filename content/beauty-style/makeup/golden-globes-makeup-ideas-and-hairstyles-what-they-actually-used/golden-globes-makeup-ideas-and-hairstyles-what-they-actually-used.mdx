### In amongst the snapping and pap-ing, we got the scoop from the celebrity make up artists who painted the faces of Hollywood’s most famed dames, and shared the products they actually used. Pens at the ready; you’ll want to take notes. Cue the applause.

## 1. REESE WITHERSPOON – GOLDEN GIRL

### America’s petite sweetheart opted for a smouldering, metallic-laden maquillage; with her make up artist Molly R. Stern creating a look that “shined brilliantly like the prize she is.” Shine she did; thanks to an array of products by Laura Mercier.

## 2. KATE BECKINSDALE – COMPLEXION PERFECTION

### Ms. Beckinsdale’s Globe glamour was impeccable; the classic English belle channelled an earthy, gently bronzed look. She screamed sultry siren, all the while without looking ostentatious, owing to an entirely Chantecaille makeover.

## 3. KEIRA KNIGHTLEY – BARELY BLUSHING

### The English Rose was true to her elegant and pretty style; a flushed complexion, bold brows and tea rose stained lips keynote to her Globe look. Her flawless beauty was care of Chanel, *naturellement*.

## 4. DAKOTA JOHNSON – SMOKE AND MIRRO

### The up and coming actress’ beauty was most definitely ‘it’ girl worthy; understated and earthy, not to distract from her sparkly and swanky gown. Like any cool cat, her smoky-eyed Globe guise came courtesy of Chanel.
