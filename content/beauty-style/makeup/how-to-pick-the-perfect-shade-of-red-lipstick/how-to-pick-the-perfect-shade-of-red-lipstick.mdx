One easy way to determine your undertone is by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If the veins look blue, you're likely a 'cool'. If the veins appear greenish, you're likely a 'warm'. Keeping this big red rule of thumb in mind next time you’re on the pout peruse will keep you on the right track.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 1.    Marilyn Monroe

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23183&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="MARILYN MONROE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Features: Fairer skin tone, blonde, dark brows.

Miss Monroe was a beauty icon in more ways than one and it’s not hard to see why. With her all-consuming curves and buttery skin, the big guns in beauty clamored to get Monroe in their corner. Her signature pout transfixed the world and was supposedly mastered mixing five very different hues of red.

A go-to when time poor was Guerlain’s Rouge Diabolique. Although discontinued, Guerlain has duped this devilishly delicious shade with Rouge Automatique Liu.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23184&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="GUERLAIN ROUGE AUTOMATIQUE" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 2.    Elizabeth Taylor

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23185&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="ELIZABETH TAYLOR" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Features: Dark brows, blue/grey eyes and porcelain skin.

Liz was a true advocate for the power of the pout, famously quoted “pour yourself a drink, put on some lipstick and get over it.” With raven hair and dark, bold brows, experts say a little drama on the lips adds the perfect counter balance. To emulate the iconic Elizabeth Taylor, opt for something equally as punchy, with cool undertones in a cherry or burgundy red, lining the lips with [Nars Satin Lip Pencil ](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/Nars/SatinLipPencilCollection/)in Hyde Park.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23186&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="NARS SATIN LIP PENCIL" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 5.    Grace Kelly

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23191&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="GRACE KELLY" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Features: Ash blonde hair, fair to light skin and blue eyes.

Princess Grace had refined red down to a mastered art, often seen sporting a raspberry red from the tennis court to the luxury yacht, reaffirming the perfect shade of matte red can be a faithful beauty signature, whatever the occasion. Dark, deep lip colors with gold undertones brighten up fairer hair and warm skin. Try [Stilla Color Balm Lipstick](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/Stila/ColourBalmLipstick/) in Ruby – balmy and nourishing enough for all daywear.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23192&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="STILA COLOR BALM LIPSTICK" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 6.    Lucile Ball

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="23193&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_custom_heading text="LUCILE BALL" font_container="tag:h3|text_align:center" css_animation="fadeIn" el_class="img-title"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Features: Fair skin and fire engine red hair.

Lacquer up and gloss your heart out! Lucile Ball and her ‘bigger the better’ beauty mantra left many an admirer lusting over her toffee apple glossy lips. With her signature red hair, whiplash brows and ruby red nails, the I love Lucy star was a spokesperson for Max Factor, later updating her lip look and switching to subtle corals to avoid her signature red lip bleeding. ### 7.   Rita Hayworth

Without a doubt one of the most preferred pouts, Hayworth was famous for heavily lining her lips prior to slicking on her signature shade. If you’re fairer, opt for a hue that compliments your skin tone and sets off your auburn tresses, the right shade will light up your look and emphasize whatever statement you choose to make.
