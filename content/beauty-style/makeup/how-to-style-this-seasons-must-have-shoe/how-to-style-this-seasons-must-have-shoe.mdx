## WORK

While you may need to tone it down a notch to maintain a professional appearance in the office, workwear doesn’t need to be boring – it’s all in the details. The key to pulling off the Sabines with your work attire? Focus on subtle textures, minimalist prints and elevated classics. Style your slingbacks with the H&M Ribbed Top tucked into these ASOS Design High Waist Tapered Pants – both are key staples to any working woman’s wardrobe. Then add the Prince of Wales check (a.k.a. the minimalist’s print du jour) [Atmos & Here Loriel Longline Blazer](http://bit.ly/2OWGG4w) over the top and finish with a spritz of a light, fresh fragrance like Clean Reserve Rain [Reserve Blend] Eau de Parfum to match your minimalist aesthetic.

## DATE NIGHT

With spring in bloom, fun and flirtatious frocks are ready to come out and play, and so too are your bow-adorned Sabine slingbacks – the perfect match for a spring fling. Ruffles and romantic, floaty fabrics and textures will be the key to creating an alluring look. Match it with this [Shona Joy Ruffle Mini Dress](http://bit.ly/2vPUyGP), crafted to accentuate your silhouette with its wrap style and blouson sleeves – the ideal partner for your Sabines. The soft velvet chevron Gucci GG Marmont Shoulder Bag will fit all the essentials for the evening’s amorous adventure, including your answer to oh-so-kissable lips – the Elf Cosmetics Lip Plumping Gloss. Add a captivating finishing touch with these dainty Sarah & Sebastian Infinity Chain Earrings and you'll be date night ready.

## FRIDAY NIGHT DRINKS

The working week has drawn to a close, bottles of bubbly are being popped and excitement is in the air as the weekend begins to weave its way into existence for the next 48 hours or so. Whether you’re celebrating with your work wives or catching up with some of your gal pals, your Friday night calls for an elegant and chic number. Enter the Self-Portrait Camo Lace Frill Jumpsuit – a piece that will trick the eye into believing you’re wearing separates. This faux duo, with its inviting neckline, cold shoulder and high-waisted “pant”, not only create a sleek silhouette but also adds sophistication when paired with your Sabine slingbacks. Working with this evening's colour palette of navy and black, accessorise with a bold box clutch, such as the Edie Parker Lara Clutch, and some subtle silver earrings (our pick? The Holly Ryan Hepworth Earrings). Create some magic with a shimmering eye using Eye of Horus’s Eyeshadow Palette in Sekhmet Smokey to complement this luxe look.

## WEEKEND BRUNCH

Sipping on an almond latte, pondering the idea of a Bloody Mary or Bellini to go with your Instagrammable smashed avo on sourdough toast –  the one thing you can be sure about is your perfectly compiled weekend look that has seen you receive compliments from friends and strangers alike. As wafts of delicious and delectable meals come your way, your vibrant red floral Athena Procopiou Farrah Dress and Sabine slingbacks are the ideal combination for your (quite literally) picture-perfect brunch look. Though it wouldn’t be quite perfect without the help of Instagram’s favourite bag, the Cult Gaia Ark Bamboo Clutch – the perfect choice for carrying only the essentials. The final details to this It-girl number? A layered necklace like the [Topshop Circle & T-Bar Necklace](http://bit.ly/2MZw9VC) to match the Farrah Dress’s V-neckline, and a ruby red mani courtesy of Butter London’s Pillar Box Red Nail Polish for maximum Insta-impact.
