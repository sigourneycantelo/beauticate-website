## WORK

While you may need to tone it down a notch to maintain a professional appearance in the office, workwear doesn’t need to be boring – it’s all in the details. The key to pulling off the Sabines with your work attire? The working week has drawn to a close, bottles of bubbly are being popped and excitement is in the air as the weekend begins to weave its way into existence for the next 48 hours or so. Whether you’re celebrating with your work wives or catching up with some of your gal pals, your Friday night calls for an elegant and chic number.
