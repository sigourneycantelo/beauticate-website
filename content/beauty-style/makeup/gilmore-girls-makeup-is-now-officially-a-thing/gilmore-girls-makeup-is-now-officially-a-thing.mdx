Get your Pop-Tarts and bottomless pit of coffee brewing: The Gilmore Girls are back on our screens (the revival series airs Friday on Netflix), and we can't wait. But, what has us buzzing more than a Gilmore caffeine-fuelled word exchange is the release of beauty brand Brija Cosmetics' 17-piece eye-shadow collection, inspired by our favourite characters. What a time to be alive.

There’s ‘Know It, Don’t Blow It’, a bright lavender for Lane; ‘It All Comes Out In Moron’, a dark mulberry for Lorelai; ‘Hit By A Deer’, a baby pink for Rory; and ‘Notes In The Margins’, a rich dark brown for Jess – and, of course, the 'Vicious Trollop' (a nod to Lorelai’s overbearing, well-intending mother, Emily Gilmore). Each shadow shade is available in matte, and comes with a Gilmore Girls themed hand-painted cap.

Oh, and did we mention they’re 100% vegan, cruelty free, dye free and made with organic oils? Because, yep. If you needed another reason to stock up ASAP, the brand are also offering a limited edition 'Luke's Diner' inspired lip balm free with any purchase of the entire collection. Be sure to add to cart, pronto.
