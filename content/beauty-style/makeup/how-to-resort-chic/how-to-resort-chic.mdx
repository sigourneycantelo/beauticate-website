MARILYN MUNROE

### Marilyn Munroe

Make like Marilyn with a classic-cut Eres one-piecein crisp white that will have you looking every bit the fifties bombshell.  For textured beach waves, look no further than Original Mineral’s Sea Salt Hair Spray.  Lastly, don’t forget to apply a bright red lip colour for that pop of retro glamour.

ERES SWIMSUIT

LANCOME L'ABSOLU ROUGE LIPSTICK IN ORANGE SACREE

ORIGINAL MINERAL SURF BOMB SEA SALT SPRAY

JACKIE KENNEDY

### Jackie Kennedy Onassis

If there was ever anyone who knew how to dress for sunset cocktails on a super yacht, it was Jackie O.  Take a style cue from the former First Lady with a modest, summery Diane von Furstenberg shift dress and bold pink lip à la M.A.C. Cosmetics ‘show orchid’.   

MARY GREENWELL SOLID PERFUME

GIAMBATTISTA VALLI FLORAL PRINT DRESS

M.A.C LIPSTICK IN SHOW ORCHID

GRACE KELLY

### Grace Kelly

Oh Princess Grace, could she be any more chic?!  We think not.  To channel her subtle, natural-looking holiday glow try applying a layer of Hampton Sun Sunless Tanning Gel, enriched with aloe vera, walnut oil and vitamin E.  A tinted moisturiser with an SPF is a must-have for any beachside beauty regime and ideal for when going straight from the beach to afternoon spritzers.

AUDREY HEPBURN

### Audrey Hepburn

Ms. Hepburn understood the importance of protecting her porcelain skin from the sun’s harsh rays and she knew how to do it in style. A chic wide-brim hat by Melissa Odabash will help to protect your face while you’re enjoying the sunshine.  For thick, voluminous lashes like Audrey’s that won’t give you panda eyes even in forty-degree heat, M.A.C. Cosmetics waterproof Zoom Lash Mascara is the answer.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which 1950s beauty icons inspired the resort chic looks in this article?**
A: The edit draws from Marilyn Monroe, Jackie Kennedy Onassis, Grace Kelly and Audrey Hepburn - each interpreted through a modern beach and resort lens.

**Q: What lip colour is suggested for a Marilyn Monroe-inspired beach look?**
A: A bright red lip is recommended to capture that classic retro glamour, paired with a white one-piece swimsuit and textured beach waves.

**Q: How can you achieve a natural-looking holiday glow like Grace Kelly?**
A: The article suggests Hampton Sun Sunless Tanning Gel, enriched with aloe vera, walnut oil and vitamin E. A tinted moisturiser with SPF is also recommended for going straight from the beach to afternoon drinks.

**Q: What mascara is recommended for a beach day when you don't want panda eyes?**
A: M.A.C Cosmetics Zoom Lash Waterproof Mascara is recommended for thick, voluminous lashes that won't run in the heat.
