[**Saint Laurent Le 5 à 7 Hobo Bag**](https://bit.ly/446M9Ol)

Understated yet undeniably elegant, the [Le 5 à 7 Hobo Bag by Saint Laurent](https://bit.ly/446M9Ol) exudes Parisian sophistication. Its sleek silhouette and soft leather finish make it perfect for both day and night outings. The gold-tone YSL hardware adds a touch of glamour to its minimalist charm.

[**Dior Micro Saddle Bag**](https://bit.ly/4kgu077)**Proof that good things really do come in small packages - this pre-loved [Dior Fuchsia Goatskin Micro Saddle Bag](https://bit.ly/4kgu077) delivers a bold pop of colour in the house’s most iconic silhouette. A collectible that’s as chic slung crossbody as it is styled on your vanity.

[**Prada Re-Edition 2005 Nylon Mini Bag**](https://bit.ly/4n8ivkF)

Infusing new life into an enduring classic, the [Mini Prada Re‑Edition Shoulder Bag](https://bit.ly/4n8ivkF) delivers luxurious sophistication in a compact form. Crafted in Prada’s signature nylon with polished hardware and sleek lines, this petite powerhouse is both practical and effortlessly chic.

[Coach Pillow Tabby Shoulder Bag 18**](https://bit.ly/3HKPds5)

Soft, squishy, and undeniably adorable, the [Pillow Tabby*](https://bit.ly/3HKPds5) combines luxury and comfort in a compact size. The pillowy leather finish and refined detailing make it a standout option.

**Balenciaga Le Cagole XS Shoulder Bag**

Edgy and embellished, Balenciaga’s Le Cagole XS* is the perfect bag for those who want to make a bold statement. With its punk-inspired hardware and slouchy silhouette, it’s a rebellious take on luxury.**:**
