## WATERLILY SPACEUTICALS RESVERATROL FX

This treatment masque promises to redefine the texture of your skin in a matter of weeks. The formula contains active resveratrol, whipped into a chocolate fondant-like consistency. You’ll notice right away how super-soft your skin feels (which thanks to the added cacao and vitamins, works for most skin types), and how instantly it absorbs into skin for an addictive glow. At $119, the price point is a little exxy, but it's hard working formula makes it a sound investment if dry skin is your main beauty problem.

## [SUNDAY RILEY BIONIC ANTI-AGEING CREAM](http://mecca.com.au/sunday-riley/bionic-anti-ageing-cream/I-015826.html)

Formulated with the highest concentration of key ingredients, including the hero resveratrol, this night treatment works to revitalise and battle blemishes while you sleep. You can expect calm, soothed and plumper skin, plus that elusive glow we're all constantly lusting after. Ensure your skin is cleansed and dried before use.

## BITE BEAUTY HIGH PIGMENT PENCIL

If you’re anything like us, perfectly hydrated lips are the top of your beauty wishlist. This is where Bite Beauty comes into play, showing that our spot-light ingredient isn't just for skincare. Each lip product contains the same amount of resveratrol as what is found in five glasses of wine – without the hangover. This lip pencil delivers a modern matte look, as well as fighting free radicals with long-term anti-ageing benefits. A triple threat? You bet.

## CAUDALIE RESVERATROL FIRMING SERUM

If you’ve been on the hunt for a hardworking serum that won’t dry your skin, you need look no further. Give your complexion an instant radiance boost with this vitamin-charged serum that will help firm and lift the skin. The resveratrol is combined with micro hyaluronic acid that both work seamlessly in creating a more youthful appearance. Double the fun. We like to apply this under our daily makeup.
