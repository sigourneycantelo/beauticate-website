## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is resveratrol and what does it do for skin?**
A: Resveratrol is an antioxidant ingredient found in red wine that fights free radicals, helps firm and lift skin, and offers anti-ageing benefits. It is used in both skincare and lip products to combat premature ageing and improve skin texture.

**Q: What are the best resveratrol skincare products?**
A: This article highlights Waterlily Spaceuticals Resveratrol FX masque, Sunday Riley Bionic Anti-Ageing Cream, Caudalie Resveratrol Firming Serum, Bite Beauty High Pigment Pencil, and Vine Vera Travel Hand Cream as standout resveratrol-infused products.

**Q: Can resveratrol be used in lip products?**
A: Yes. Bite Beauty's High Pigment Pencil contains the same amount of resveratrol as found in five glasses of wine. It delivers a modern matte finish while fighting free radicals and providing long-term anti-ageing benefits for the lips.

**Q: How does the Caudalie Resveratrol Firming Serum work?**
A: The serum combines resveratrol with micro hyaluronic acid to boost radiance, firm the skin and create a more youthful appearance. It can be applied under daily makeup for an instant glow without drying the skin.
