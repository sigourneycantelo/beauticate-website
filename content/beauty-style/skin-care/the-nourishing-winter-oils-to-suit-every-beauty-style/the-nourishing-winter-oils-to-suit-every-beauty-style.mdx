## The Clean Beauty Buffs

Natural beauty lovers, rejoice – there are a plethora of gorgeous and green facial oils out there for you to lap up. Votary's Super Seed Facial Oil is an established favourite in the category, and understandably so – its super nutritious formula, packed with delicious plant oils, is a real treat.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="38276&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]For the greasy skin gals amongst us who are frightened by facial oils, a lovely pick is Jurlique's Skin Balancing Face Oil which manages to be hydrating without swerving into heavy, pore-clogging territory.

As their names suggest, [Grown Alchemist's Antioxidant+ Facial Oil](https://www.grownalchemist.com/au/antioxidant-facial-oil-borago-rosehip-buckthorn.html) and Endota Spa's Multi Vitamin Treatment Oil are as nutrient-rich as your morning smoothie, and just as lush – plus, both are certified organic.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="38291&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## The Skincare Connoisseur

Utterly obsessed with the world of cutting-edge skincare, and possess a serum collection that could rival a cosmetic scientist's? (No judgment from us, we relate entirely). Then these are for you:

Dermalogica's Phyto Replenish Oil manages to be majorly hydrating while also sinking in beautifully and swiftly, making it perfect for under foundation – think of it as a facial serum/oil hybrid.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38278&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38289&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]For brightened and hydrated skin, look no further than Murad's Multi Vitamin Infusion Oil – featuring vitamins A through F, it ticks all your skin requirements in one hit.

And no list of facial oils would be complete without a mention of one of Clarins' iconic offerings. Their Blue Orchid Oil is arguably the most well known of the range, and is a boon to dehydrated skins (read: most likely all of us in the winter). Plus, we promise you'll quickly become addicted to its ultra relaxing scent.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38285&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38279&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## The Indie Enthusiast

If you're always seeking out the latest and greatest on the niche skincare front, let us give you a head start. Achingly cool RECREATION Beauty have created multi-purpose magic with their Glow Queen Glow Oil – frankly, the name says it all about how you'll feel post-application.

The offerings from cult Sydney beauty institution [Vaia Beauty](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/beauty-maintenance/vaia-beauty-darlinghurst/) are reliably gorgeous – their [Sol Serum](https://vaiabeauty.com.au/products/sol-serum) and Luna Oil (for mornings and evenings respectively) both help plump and rejuvenate exhausted complexions.

Chic Aussie brand Theseeke's Rose Elixir is a delight – it can be used in a myriad of pampering ways, and yes, those are real rose buds scattered through the bottle.

The Ayu have [a selection of beautiful body oils](https://www.theayu.com.au/collections/vedic-body-oils), each geared to serve a particular Ayurvedic dosha (which are Kapha, Pitta and Vata, for the uninitiated). Ayurvedic benefits aside, these are gorgeously nourishing, and smell divine, too.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="38281&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

## The Luxe Lover

For those who enjoy the finer things in life, skincare included. The Renewal Oil, La Mer's divine facial oil offering, is an appropriate place to start – infused with their trademark Miracle Broth, it's a treat for the skin and the senses alike.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38282&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="38292&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Chanel's L'Huile Orient and L'Huile Rose body massage oils were crafted for the Ritz Spa Paris, and are suitably luxurious. A nourishing treat for dry winter limbs, they'll make your post-shower routine worthy of even the chicest Parisian hotels.

If you like to imbue your skincare with a little Ayurvedic magic, [Subtle Energies' Facial Blend](https://www.beauticate.com/product/facial-blend/) is utterly beautiful – full of Indian rose and adaptogens, it's little wonder that it was awarded a spot in our Top 100 list.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="38296&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]
