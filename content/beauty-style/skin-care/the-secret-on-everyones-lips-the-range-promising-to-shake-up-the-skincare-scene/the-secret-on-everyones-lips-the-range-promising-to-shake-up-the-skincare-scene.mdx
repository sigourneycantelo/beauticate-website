## Why all the hype?

What’s got us excited is the highly personalised nature of the range: the products are custom-made for each patient, to address their particular skin issues, from pigmentation, to acne and ageing. Ingredients like tretinoin and hydroquinone, which usually require a doctor’s script, are combined with medical grade active ingredients, making for a combination unlike anything currently available on the Australian market.

And the result are pretty impressive, as their astonishing before and after photos and breathless customer reviews can attest to. ## What’s in the products?

The Secret's products are based around the duo’s patented base, to which they add medical grade ingredients to suit each customer’s needs. The current line-up consists of 11 products, all of which are free of parabens, sulphates, and fragrance, with more additions to the line on the horizon.[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="47894&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

## Where can you get it?

Due to the personalised nature of the range, The Secret's products are available only after consultation to ensure the ideal product is prescribed. You do an online questionnaire, which is then assessed by medical professionals to select the best products for you and spot red flags. The team then get in touch, and once you order, your customised product is freshly compounded just for you.

Alongside [their online store](https://thesecretskincare.com.au/), they very recently launched at [Sydney’s All Saints Clinic](https://allsaintsclinic.com.au/), a go-to for the city’s social set (it’s regularly listed as a favourite in our WHO profiles).[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]The clinic’s founder, Dr Joseph Hkeik, shared: “I've only been using The Secret for a few weeks but the results I am seeing are simply amazing.
