### Fitness blogger and model Bianca Cheah has the most ridiculously gorgeous glowy skin. Apparently it hasn't always been so. As she told us in her Beauticate [interview](https://www.beauticate.com/whos/bianca-cheah), in her early twenties, Bianca struggled with acne and was tortured with the scars that followed. She discovered rosehip oil (known for its scar-fading properties) and applied it every night using this massage technique that was inspired by the facials she used to give as a Shiseido beauty therapist. She shows us how to do it…

**Step 1 –** Place three drops of rose hip oil into your hands and rub your palms together. Bianca likes Sukin Certified Organic Rosehip Oil.

![Bianca 1](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/glow-giving-shiatsu-massage/bianca-1-6.gif)

**Step 2 –** Smooth over your face from the nose, up between the eyebrows and out around the sides of the face. Use a steady amount of pressure, easing the tension in your brow and around your temples.

![Bianca 2](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/glow-giving-shiatsu-massage/bianca-2-1.gif)

**STEP 3 –** Using your middle and ring fingers massage in small circular motions across your forehead and temples. Keep the rest of your face relaxed.

![Bianca](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/glow-giving-shiatsu-massage/bianca-3.gif)

**STEP 4 –** Continue the massage with stroking motions down the sides of your jaw to your chin and up the other side of your jaw, using your pointer and middle fingers.

![Bianca](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/glow-giving-shiatsu-massage/bianca-4.gif)

**STEP 5 –** Wipe upwards along your neck up to your chin using all four fingers.

![Bianca](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/glow-giving-shiatsu-massage/bianca-5.gif)

Now, let the rosehip oil penetrate, put your feet up and wait for the compliments about your complexion to roll in!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What rosehip oil does Bianca Cheah use for this massage?**
A: Sukin Certified Organic Rosehip Oil, which she places three drops of into her palms before starting.

**Q: What are the benefits of doing a shiatsu facial massage with rosehip oil?**
A: The massage activates lymphatic drainage, works the facial muscles to plump the skin and, when done with rosehip oil consistently, can help fade scars and improve overall skin radiance.

**Q: How long does the whole routine take?**
A: The massage itself takes only a few minutes. After the five steps are complete, you leave the rosehip oil to penetrate while you relax.

**Q: Where did Bianca learn this technique?**
A: She learnt it during her time as a Shiseido beauty therapist, where shiatsu-inspired facial massage was part of the treatment she gave to clients.
