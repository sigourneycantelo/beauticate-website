*If you're going to take recommendations for men's preening products, let it be from [Darren Palmer and husband, Olivier Duvillard](https://www.beauticate.com/who/darren-palmer-interiors-designer-and-olivier-duvillard). The former, an interiors genius with a natural eye for quality, and the latter, a skincare maven with almost 20 years in the beauty industry (notably, one time CEO of Ultraceuticals), they're one good looking pair to be reckoned with. [Recently, we went to their incredible home to talk love, life and legal legitimacy](https://www.beauticate.com/news/video-how-to-voteyes-with-darren-palmer-and-husband-olivier-duvillard).
