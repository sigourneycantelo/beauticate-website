### The organic coconut oil concealer

MIRANDA IS A FAN OF ORGANIC BEAUTY

With arguably the most coveted of complexions and shiniest of hair, Miranda’s beauty repertoire is an organic force to be reckoned with. In our [exclusive shoot and interview](https://www.beauticate.com/who/miranda-kerr-model) with the supermodel, she revealed to Beauticate her love of organic beauty brand RMS. Her pick is the [UN-cover Up](http://mecca.com.au/rms-beauty/un-cover-up/V-020196.html).

RMS UN-COVER UP WILL BRIGHTEN EVEN THE DARKEST OF CIRCLES

### The face-chiseling bronzer

ANJA HAS SPENT A WEALTH OF TIME TRYING DIFFERENT BEAUTY BRANDS

[Anja’s](https://www.beauticate.com/who/anja-rubik-model) creamy Polish complexion gets a sun-kissed flush from her favourite bronzer, Burberry Warm Glow Bronzer. She says she dusts it down the centre of her face to elongate it and make it look thinner.

PERFECT TO ADD A LITTLE WARMTH TO YOUR COMPLEXION OR MIMIC A HOLIDAY GLOW

### The statement-making lipstick

WE LOVE THIS PHOTO FROM VOGUE OF COCO IN HER NURSERY

As we discovered in our interview with her, [Coco](https://www.beauticate.com/who/coco-rocha-model) is a new wave of supermodel – a self-starter and incredibly digitally savvy. She particularly impressed us when she announced she does her own makeup for events. Favouring bright lips and dramatic eyes to offset her fine features and glossy hair, Coco swears by MAC lipsticks in oranges, pinks and plums.

![Holding1](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/seven-of-the-best-model-approved-beauty-buys/Holding1_result-1.jpg)


COCO LOVES MAC FOR THEIR BRIGHT COLOURS AND INTENSE PIGMENTS

### The Victoria’s Secret Angel Hair Tong

ROSIE IS NO STRANGER TO TIME WELL SPENT IN THE MAKEUP CHAIR

As the face of ModelCo Cosmetics, [Rosie](https://www.beauticate.com/who/rosie-huntington-whiteley-model) is a modern goddess and (blessedly) an open book when it comes to divulging her beauty secrets. So, the key to those cascading waves? The Sarah Potempa Beach Waver Pro. Rosie positions the hair tool among her "Favourite things in the world". This unique curling wand will give you customised curls worthy of a Victoria's Secret Angel ready to hit the runway. She also swears by ModelCo Tan Airbrush In A Can for that golden glow.

THE SARAH POTEMPA BEACH WAVER PRO

### The portable eye and lip balm

JESS LOVES A DUO PRODUCT THAT SAVES TIME AND HANDBAG SPACE

Savvy New York-based model [Jess Hart](https://www.beauticate.com/who/jess-hart-model-entrepreneur) claims that on her off-duty days she’s fairly low maintenance. But that doesn’t mean going without a good eye and lip product. She swears by La Prairie’s glamorous Pret A Porter Eye & Lip Portable Perfector – with a light reflecting eye balm on one side and a pout protector on the other, it kills two birds with one beautiful stone.

LA PRAIRIE EYE AND LIP DUO IS THE ULTIMATE MULTI-TASKER

### The cult skin elixir

ROBYN KNOWS THE BENEFITS OF A LUXE SKINCARE REGIME

Like many celebrities before her ([Ms. Blanchett](https://www.beauticate.com/who/cate-blanchett-actress-and-director) we’re talking to you), [Robyn Lawley](https://www.beauticate.com/who/robyn-lawley-model) is a fan of Japanese skincare brand SK-II. She credits the brand’s Facial Treatment Essence as the secret behind her crystalline complexion. She also loves their treatment masks, but – despite being tempted – draws the line at wearing them on long haul flights.

![Miranda](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/seven-of-the-best-model-approved-beauty-buys/Miranda_result-1.jpg)


SK II FACIAL ESSENCE

### The (all-natural) secret cellulite treatment

IT WOULD SEEM THAT NOT EVEN MODELS ARE IMMUNE TO SOFT SPOTS

Another refreshingly low-maintenance Manhattan-based Aussie model is [Elyse Taylor](https://www.beauticate.com/who/elyse-taylor-model). The new mum has found the perfect organic antidote to stretch marks and cellulite – Weleda Birch Cellulite Oil. As she says, “Everyone has a bit of a cellulite.” Even models, it seems.

MASSAGE ON WELEDA BIRCH CELLULITE OIL  TO WORK ON DIMPLED SKIN
