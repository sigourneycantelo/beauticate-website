Ageing isn't something that scares me. It's part of life and I embrace the wisdom and adventures that we gain from each year that passes. However if there is something that I can do to slow down or reinvigorate the problem areas, I'm in. Enter: Douglas Pereira, Skin Therapist at his Clinical Skin Therapy medispa in Park Street, Sydney. I meet with him at the clinic, which has this feeling of being in New York on the Upper East Side. It overlooks Sydney's own Hyde Park and sits perfectly above the trees so that while I get my treatment, I feel like I'm  floating.Pereira gets to work on my problem areas with a Neocollagenesis Remodeling Therapy. First a cleanse, then red light therapy then the heavy duty, but pain free, treatment Mechanical Facial Drainage. It includes a full face lifting and firming device, finished off with a vitamin infusion, mask and massage. It's a direct massage to my face, neck and back of neck but with a vibrating machine with pulsating currents. It works its way through renewing muscle fibres, improving muscle tone and skin elasticity.Normally a treatment with this many benefits comes with plenty of pain, (no pain, no gain right?). But in fact, this treatment is so relaxing, I find myself drifting in and out of sleep. What astounds me the most is the results; dramatic and instant. My neck and chin instantly look tighter and higher, my eyebrows perk up and my skin looks full and plump. And all with no injections, lasers or pain. It's incredible.One week later and I am still getting compliments, and what I love the most about this is that I don't need to necessarily be getting this done every month. One to three treatments is enough. Pereira recognises that we are all time poor, so if we are going to invest in a skin treatment, it not only needs to work quickly and be long lasting, but not have a high maintenance requirement. Which is music to my ears and why Douglas Pereira's Skin Clinic will remain one a reliable "go to" for investment advice, in my skin*Story by Canna Campbell; Holding Photography by Daniel Gurton; In-copy Images via Instagram @dpclinicalskintherapy*

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is Neocollagenesis Remodeling Therapy facial?**
A: It is a non-invasive facial treatment performed by skin therapist Douglas Pereira at his Sydney clinic. The treatment includes a cleanse, red light therapy and Mechanical Facial Drainage - a full face lifting and firming device using pulsating currents and vibration - finished with a vitamin infusion, mask and massage.

**Q: Does the lifting facial treatment hurt?**
A: According to writer Canna Campbell who trialled it, the treatment is so relaxing she drifted in and out of sleep. The article describes it as pain-free with no injections, lasers or scalpels involved, and most of the work done through a vibrating machine with pulsating currents.

**Q: How long do the results of the facial last?**
A: The article notes that results were dramatic and instant, with a tighter neck and chin, perked brows and plumper skin visible immediately. Canna Campbell was still receiving compliments a week later, and the treatment does not need to be repeated monthly - one to three sessions is described as enough.
