The serum is no less impressive, packed with age-defying ingredients like retinol for renewal, caffeine for reducing puffiness, niacinamide for brightening, and peptides for firming the skin. Together, these components target fine lines, under-eye darkness, and puffiness with remarkable precision.

## Here’s how I use them…

I apply the patches to my under-eye area and marionette lines but you can also pop them on the “11’s” between your eyebrows, or anywhere on the face that needs plumping or brightening. The application is straightforward: after cleansing, I peel off the strips, place them on my targeted areas, and press down for 5-10 seconds to activate the micro-cones. These tiny cones dissolve into the skin, releasing the concentrated serum where it's most needed.

You should wear the patches for a minimum of two hours, though best results are achieved overnight, which is what I do. The beauty of this treatment lies in its simplicity-there’s no mess, no fuss, just serious skincare while I sleep.
