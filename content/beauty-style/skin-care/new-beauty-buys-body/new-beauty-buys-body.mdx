### Maine Beach Lord Howe Island Organic Body Polish

MAINE BEACH LORD HOWE ISLAND ORGANIC BODY POLISH $13.45

Hold onto that sweet scent of summer for a little longer with this coconut and lime body scrub. For under $15, it’s practically a steal. Beauty editor’s tip:  For intense exfoliation, try applying to dry skin and massaging in with an exfoliating glove before showering.

### [The Beauty Chef Antioxidant Inner Beauty Boost](http://www.thebeautychef.com/shop/antioxidant)

[THE BEAUTY CHEF ANTIOXIDANT INNER BEAUTY BOOST](http://www.thebeautychef.com/shop/antioxidant) $39.95

Nourish your body from the inside out with The Beauty Chef’s Inner Beauty Boost; an organic, enzyme-packed, probiotic drink.  As the name suggests, it’s bursting with antioxidants that help to repair and rejuvenate and firm skin, fight free radicals and boost your skin’s overall radiance. It tastes yummy too.

### Nuxe I love Huile Prodigieuse Body Oil

NUXE I LOVE HUILE PRODIGIEUSE BODY OIL

Sooth dry, stressed out skin with Nuxe’s super nourishing I love Huile Prodigieuse** **Body Oil.  It’s a dry oil that absorbs instantly, which means it won’t leave your skin with that icky, sticky residue that some other body oils can.  It’s limited edition, so be sure to snap it up now before it sells out.

### Tom Ford Jasmine Rouge Body Moisturiser

TOM FORD JASMINE ROUGE BODY MOISTURISER $100

Oh so rich and creamy, Tom Ford’s new Jasmine Rouge Body Moisturiser is the ultimate indulgence.  The seductive spiced floral scent makes for the perfect ‘date night’ body prep ritual.

### Aesop Resolute Hydrating Body Balm

AESOP RESOLUTE HYDRATING BODY BALM $37 / 120ML

They call it a balm, but the texture of Aesop’s Resolute Hydrating Body Balm** **is really more like a body cream. Not that we’re complaining, in fact we can’t get enough it. With a blend of essential oils that includes coriander seeds, black peppercorns and patchouli, it smells divine too.

### Yes to Coconut Head-To-Toe Restoring Balm

YES TO COCONUT HEAD-TO-TOE RESTORING BALM $14.95

Here’s a product that is a true multi-tasker.  You can use it all over, but it’s especially great for those dry patches like knees, elbows and feet, as well as your cuticles and even the dry ends of your hair.

### [Le Labo Santal 33 Shower Gel](http://mecca.com.au/le-labo/santal-33-showergel/I-020226.html)

[LE LABO SANTAL 33 SHOWER GEL](http://mecca.com.au/le-labo/santal-33-showergel/I-020226.html)$68

Direct from New York comes Le Labo’s new body collection we’ve been dying to get our hands on.  The leathery scent of Santal 33 is a favourite and the enigmatic olfactory mix of cardamom, iris, violet makes showering a dream. Beauty Editor’s tip: Want the scent to last even longer? Slather on Le Labo’s Santal 33 Body Lotion after your shower.

### 10G Light Hydrating Body Oil

10G LIGHT HYDRATING BODY OIL $34.95 / 100ML

Get your glow on with 10G’s Light Hydrating Body Oil. Made from grape seed oil, coconut, hemp seed and olive, it promises radiant, healthy looking skin minus the mineral oil and chemical nasties of commercial moisturisers.
