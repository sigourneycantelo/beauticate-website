## **1. A (SKIN) DRINK IN PARIS**

A moisturiser’s success tends to be in the serum you pair it with – need we remind everyone that it’s the serum that makes your moisturiser work harder to hydrate your skin. This month we opted to try Sampar The Impossible C-Rum is on its way to becoming a cult favourite. Originating in Paris and all made in France, this milky white Vitamin C serum is not classical in any sense of the word. Acting as a skin guard, the C-Rum activates a 5 in 1 action by boosting skin firmness, fighting wrinkles and dark spots, all whilst aiding hydration and radiance. After two weeks of morning and night application,  skin is super hydrated – even in the harsh and drying winter elements. Even dark spots on the face seem to be fade. Simply put four drops into the palm of your hand, close your eyes and massage the concoction into your face all whilst pretending you're actually lazing on the Côte d’Azur.[/vc_column_text][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29129&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29130&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## **2. AN ICELANDIC GLACIAL GLOW**

Ok, it's not exactly a vacay in Ibiza we're handing you here. But, if we're going to be cold, we'd rather it be because we're in the company of a worldly wonder. What better scenario than amidst the glaciers of Iceland. As soon as this little gem stone landed on our desk all the way, we declared we'd give it our everything. (We having a bit of a wandering eye when it comes to cleansers). The Skyn Glacial Face Wash really packs a punch for a deep clean and we invariably found it easy to stay faithful – especially after harsh days in the wintery elements. definitely the one I’ve been waiting for. It promises to tackle a greasy complexion, clogged pores, break-outs and irritations, grime and oil – *yes, yes, and yes! *Fast forward two weeks after starting this product and we're happy customers. The squeaky clean feeling that they promise is not a drill people, and somehow they manage to retain moisture even after such a decadent cleanse. Better yet, it helps to calm stressed skin and inflammation significantly reduces – not so surprising considering it’s formulated from the glacial waters of Iceland.[/vc_column_text][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## **3. A KOREAN VOLCANIC DASH**

Although the word *gommage* may be French, as soon as this buttery pore-refining mask lathers on the nose and inner cheeks we remember how the innovation of Koreans skincare. Also, the [Mizon Push Out Volcanic Gommage](http://www.mecca.com.au/mizon/push-out-volcanic-gommage/I-027677.html) face mask looks and smells good enough to eat. You leave it for three minutes and then wash it off, and it clears pores right out. It’s magical ingredient is the volcanic ash from Jeju Island off the South of Korea that allows for easy absorption of excess sebum, moisture replenishment, pore and blackhead reduction. Also, in a moment of shameful procrastinating we discovered the region hosts some of the most beautiful beach resorts… in case you’ve run dry on exotic places to dream about at your day job.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29131&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29132&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## **4. A SEOUL SATURDAY**

When we read the brand promise of Saturday Skin of the “clearest, dewiest complexion” we were won over. And their brand motto is “All play. No work” which, as busy women, we're always keen for. But as we all know, talk can be cheap, so we put the product to the test and, damn, do they deliver. The Featherweight Daily Moisturising Cream has a nice, thick consistency which feels especially luxurious in winter. The avocado protein extract formula works like a wonder potion and once used morning and night there's a considerable difference in the softness of the skin. Once again this is a Korean skincare, Seoul specifically, favourite ready to tackle problem skin, and best of all the brand is an advocate for daydreaming and adventure-seeking – need we say more?[/vc_column_text][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## **5. A GRECIAN GETAWAY**

For any heavy duty exfoliation, the Greeks know what’s up. And, in particular, Greek pharmacist and founder of Korres, Georgios Korres, who believed beauty products made up of natural and homeopathic ingredients reap the best benefits for the skin – kudos to you, Georgios. We opt to try their limited edition [Kiwi Gentle Exfoliating Scrub](http://www.mecca.com.au/korres/beauty-shots-kiwi-mild-exfoliating-scrub/I-024266.html?gclid=CMSz19rr3NQCFQolvQodwXsIXw). This exfoliator may not be a trip to the Greek Islands but it sure smells like one with an aromatic blend of orange, apple, kiwi (obviously) and Aloe leaf juice, along with almond oil and provitamin B5. So, although it takes away the dirt and excess oil, it manages not to irritate or excessively strip the skin’s natural balance. Most of all it restores moisture back into the skin and gives an enviable boost of radiance, and there's nothing mythical about that…[/vc_column_text][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29133&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_empty_space][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]*Story by Claudia De Berardinis*.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
