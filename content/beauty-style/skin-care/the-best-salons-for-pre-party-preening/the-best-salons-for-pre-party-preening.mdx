## FOR HAIR

### Barney Martin, Sydney

This quirky space is the brainchild of one of Sydney’s top hair stylists, Barney Martin. Barney has tended to the hair needs of some of the finest in the business, including Kate Moss and Drew Barrymore. Located on the corner of Riley Street Surry Hills, the salon offers killer haircuts, colours, styling and blow dries by a team of professional hair stylists – with something for everyone. Well if it's good enough for Ms Moss… Book your appointment ASAP for the ultimate pre-party style.

### [Wieselmann, South Yarra](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/wieselmann-south-yarra#bkZX7sV5x8D03LRf.97=)

Ring in the new year with a gorgeous new ‘do at Wieselmann Salon. The award-winning, chic hair salon located in South Yarra's cosmopolitan Toorak Road, is renowned for doing the best blondes in Melbourne. Since Wieselmann's establishment 40 years ago, the salon has gone from strength to strength – offering a full range of professional hair services from colouring and cutting to styling and treatments. Leaving you ready to start the new year in style.

## FOR BROWS

### [Amy Jean, salons across Australia](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/amy-jean-eye-couture-sydney)

With her deft handiwork, gorgeous product range and chic signature salons across Australia, [Amy Jean](https://www.beauticate.com/who/amy-jean-eye-stylist) is the queen bee of brows. With 4 salons in the country including: Palazzo Versace Hotel on the Gold Coast, Bambini Trust Building in Elizabeth St Sydney, Fortitude Valley in Brisbane and Paris End in East Melbourne, Jean caters to all your brow needs. With an A-list clientele, including Naomi Campbell and [Delta Goodrem](https://www.beauticate.com/who/delta-goodrem-musician), Jean has been dubbed the ‘Arch Angel’ by Vogue – and with good reason. The [salons](http://www.amyjean.com.au/) offer quick, fuss-free brow sculpting, lifting, and waxing by well-trained staff. Say hello to #browgoals this festive season.

## FOR BODY AND SKIN

### [Stephanie's Vintage Spa, Cleveland](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/stephanies-vintage-spa-cleveland)

![Static1.squarespace 5](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/the-best-salons-for-pre-party-preening/static1.squarespace-5-1.jpg)


All that Christmas shopping (and late night martinis) makes a body tense – release that tension at [Stephanie's Vintage Spa](https://stephaniesvintagespa.com.au/). This spa is the preferred spa experience of our Brisbane Beauticater's – renowned for its unbelievably effective (and soul-changing) spa treatments. From massages to facials, the therapists at Stephanie's offer a little slice of skin and body heaven. Plus everybody who works there is incredibly nice, which makes the whole experience even more mind-blowing.

### Face Plus Medispa, Bondi Junction

The festive season can wreak real havoc on your skin. Enter Face Plus Medispa – one of the country's top boutique medispas for Sydney's socialites and celebrities (there's even a complimentary valet and private entrance to avoid the paps). The practice is the work of high profile cosmetic surgeon Dr William Mooney (best known as Dr Will from Channel 9's Mornings).

## [ME, SKIN AND BODY, SOUTH YARRA](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/me-skin-and-body-south-yarra#3DDcq45jzeMyfGuP.97=)

![Static1.squarespace](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/the-best-salons-for-pre-party-preening/static1.squarespace-30.jpg)


Owned by Brooke Holmes, beauty therapist and skin whisperer to Zoe Foster Blake, [Me Skin and Body](http://www.meskinandbody.com.au/) is responsible for the flawless complexions of some of Melbourne's most elite. The beauty salon is all about the personal experience – all clients have an action plan devised for them by a team of professionals. And the best part? The team make a conscious effort to ensure their clients receive the best skin possible through education – explaining why certain treatments are more suited for an individual based on their skin type and concerns, whilst equipping them with the knowledge to ensure beautiful skin is maintained all year round.Story by Stephanie Russo
