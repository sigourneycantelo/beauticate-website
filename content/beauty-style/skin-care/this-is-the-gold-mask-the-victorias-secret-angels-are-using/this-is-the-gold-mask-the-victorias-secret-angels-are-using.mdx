In very predictable fashion, the internet is buzzing about the Victoria Secret runway show in Paris last night. From the famous [Angels flight](https://www.beauticate.com/news/vs-countdown-angels-gram-their-way-to-paris-for-show) to its French destination to [baby rumours](https://www.beauticate.com/news/baby-news-for-vs-model-revealed-moments-after-show), we've been on a VS information high. But, being the beauty buffs we are, we can't get enough of the flawless complexions of these winged creatures…

If, like us, you've been dying to get the lowdown on the glowy skin of Elsa Hosk, Iza Goulart, Irina Shayk and Alessandra Ambrosio, the real VS secret has been revealed: this removable gold facial sheet by Mimi Luzon.

[Breathtaking @alessandraambrosio preparing for the @victoriassecret show with 24k Pure Gold Mask #vsfashionshow2016 ⭐](https://www.instagram.com/p/BNXjx-yheu5/)

A video posted by Mimi Luzon (@mimiluzon) on Nov 28, 2016 at 12:59pm PST

The mask will set you back $300 and is made with pure gold leaf (hence price tag). According to the product description it contains, "a unique combination of a corrective anti-wrinkle mask with gold leaf, that provides skin with immediate lift and radiance." Each mask contains cell-renewing ingredients that improves "skin's elasticity, texture, and tone" using peptides such as Hexpeptide 8 that reduces wrinkle depth and relaxes facial tension.

Studies have shown that pure gold is a skin miracle-worker, giving complexions extra radiance when applied directly to the skin, as well as having properties that treat redness and fine lines. At this stage though, you might have to be on a supermodel salary to give it a go.
