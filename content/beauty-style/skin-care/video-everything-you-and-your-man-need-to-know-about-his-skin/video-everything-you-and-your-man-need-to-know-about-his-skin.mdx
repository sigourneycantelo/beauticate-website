### With Father's Day on the horizon, it has us thinking about men's skincare. We've come a long way… By that, we mean that men actually use it now. Gone are the days of soap and water, when they remembered. But, compared to women, there is still a relative lack of information about not only what products men should use (spoiler: NOT yours), but also when to apply them and how. Schlepping too much Sorbolene post shower is at best redundant and at worst damaging long term. So, Beauticate founder Sigourney (pulling in her brother, Rory as our road tester!) consulted cosmetic chemist and founder of cosmeceutical range Synergie Skin, Terri Vinson to give us the lowdown on manscaping skincare.
