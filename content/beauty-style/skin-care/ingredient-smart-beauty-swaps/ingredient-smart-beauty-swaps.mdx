## THE TOOTHPASTE EDIT

[**Grants of Australia Whitening + HA Natural Toothpaste**](https://www.grantsofaustralia.com.au/collections/whitening/products/whitening-ha-natural-toothpaste-fluoride-free-110g)

This is the minty-fresh mainstay that changed my oral care game. It’s free from fluoride and sulphates, but don’t mistake it for a basic swap. This formula is powered by Hydroxyapatite (HA) – a naturally occurring mineral that makes up our teeth and bones – known to remineralise enamel, strengthen teeth, and brighten your smile without sensitivity.

Add in baking soda for gentle stain removal, coconut oil to help fight bacteria, and a crisp, clean mint flavour that actually lasts. It's the kind of swap you stick with – not because you should, but because it works.

We’re not gatekeeping good toothpaste. Grants’ **[Blueberry Burst](https://www.grantsofaustralia.com.au/collections/kids/products/new-blueberry-burst-kids-natural-toothpaste-fluoride-free-75g)** and **[Strawberry Surprise](https://www.grantsofaustralia.com.au/collections/kids/products/new-strawberry-surprise-kids-natural-toothpaste-fluoride-free-75g) **Kids Toothpaste has the same clean credentials in a sweeter, kid-friendly formula – no fluoride, no SLS, no drama at bedtime.

				Did you know? Hydroxyapatite is the very same mineral that forms our tooth enamel. Unlike fluoride, it restores and strengthens without irritation - and Grants uses a non-nano, vegan, allergen-free form that ticks all the boxes.

## The Deodorant That Ended the Search

[**MyAura Pink Citrus Natural Deodorant**](https://myaura.au/collections/antiperspirants/products/myaura-pink-citrus-antiperspirant-spray)

A fresh, citrusy scent that doesn’t make you smell like a salad – and it actually holds up on humid days. Palm oil-free, smooth on the skin, and completely unembarrassing to keep in your bag or bathroom drawer.

				Did you know? Aluminium salts in traditional deodorants block sweat ducts - but they’re not the only way to stay fresh. Ingredient-savvy formulas now use magnesium, baking soda or zinc to neutralise odour, not your body’s natural rhythm.

These gentle, skin-supportive options are designed with younger skin in mind – simple, effective, and cool enough to actually get used.

**[Wildhood Deodorant + Hydrating Mist Duo
](https://mywildhood.com.au/products/sunkissed-body-mist-duo-copy)**

For tweens starting to build healthy hygiene habits, this duo is a dream. Aluminium and palm oil-free, the formulas are soft on skin and the branding is fun but not babyish – a great first foray into gentle personal care.

**[Mudie Cow Fragrance](https://mudiecow.com.au/shop/)**

No synthetic overload, no baby-pink glitter bombs. Mudie Cow makes fragrance for tweens that feels thoughtful, elevated, and age-appropriate. Created by a beauty insider and mother who couldn’t find anything that struck the right balance, these Australian-made scents are fragrance-free of the usual irritants (think: essential oils and synthetics) and designed to respect developing senses – and identities.
