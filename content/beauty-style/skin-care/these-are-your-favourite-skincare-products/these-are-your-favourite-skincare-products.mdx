## [WE ASKED](https://www.instagram.com/p/CUXHbbXtb9-/): HOW DO YOU KEEP YOUR LIPS FROM DRYING OUT?

### *“Nourish with a layer of Burts Bees then cover over with Vaseline lip therapy to lock it in overnight!” – @cherrysx07 *

This tip takes inspiration from one of the skincare’s biggest trends, slugging. The less-than-sexy sounding method involves sealing in hydration with a layer of occlusive product, such as petroleum jelly, to prevent moisture from evaporating overnight. We love Burt’s Bees Overnight Intensive Lip Treatment features ceramide to protect delicate lips from environmental irritants.  Apply a layer, then seal that goodness in with Vaseline’s Lip Therapy balm for the softest lips ever come morning.

### *“Lanolips always and forever til death” – @squares.of.beauty *

This Aussie brand’s star ingredient is lanolin, a soothing emollient derived from sheep’s wool that keeps in moisture and softens dry, flaky skin. We’re especially obsessed with Lanolip’s Golden Dry Skin Salve, a delicious blend of the purest grade lanolin, Manuka honey and vitamin E that works wonders on lips – and hands, heels, cuticles, just about anywhere else!

### *“Laneige lip sleeping mask." – *@raeannetabikh

This K-Beauty fave received a LOT of love from you guys thanks to it's ultra-plumping Moisture Wrap™ Technology, which uses beta-glucan to form a hydrating layer that lasts all night long.

## [WE ASKED](https://www.instagram.com/p/Cb2IYPeroPg/): WHAT’S YOUR FAVOURITE FACE MASK TO USE BEFORE A BIG EVENT?

### *“TriDration B!! Only takes 5 mins for juicy skin.” – @tilly.nguyen*

This fragrance-free 'express medi mask' calms skin in just 5 minutes – perfect if you're running late! The cooling gel formula combines multiple methods of hydration to ensure supremely plump, dewy skin: humectants to attract and hold moisture, saccharide isomerate (an ingredient that actually stimulates the skin to make its own natural hyaluronic acid deep in the dermis), and super-effective direct hyaluronic acid, as well as hero ingredient niacinimide to reduce redness.

### *“Biologique Recherche VIP 02 or Vivant! Best in the business!” – @gbazp *

Best in the business indeed! Biologique Recherche may test your French pronunciation skills, but their range has a reputation rivalled by few other skin care brands, and is used by celebrities and their aestheticians alike. The famous Masque Vivant is purifying concoction containing a high concentration of the brand's signature Yeast Extract, producing a unique colour and scent which has been compared to Vegemite. While that may not sound glamorous, this ingredient actively combats the bacteria that causes breakouts, and is rich in B group vitamins to regulate oil production.

Their Masque VIP O2, on the other hand, is a hydrating, brightening treatment for a red carpet-worthy glow. The clever Biologique Recherche Oxygenating Complex draws oxygen into the skin to rev up a dull, tired complexion, while cucumber extract hydrates. Biologique Recherche is an exclusive brand used in select salons, so unfortunately it's a little harder to get your hands on than some products. You can find your closest stockist here, or head to [skin specialist Melanie Grant](https://www.beauticate.com/who/creatives/melanie-grant-skin-specialist/)'s online boutique (you will need to register an account to purchase, as these products are potent!).

### *“KORA Organics Turmeric Brightening & Exfoliating Mask.**” – @emmaleawilliams*

This supermodel approved skin treatment gets skin glowing with tumeric, papaya enzymes, rosehip seeds and peppermint for skin that's exfoliated, refreshed and red-carpet ready.
