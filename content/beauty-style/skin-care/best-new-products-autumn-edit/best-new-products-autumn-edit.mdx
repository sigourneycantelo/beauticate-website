## THE HERB GARDEN COLOGNE:

A fresh season calls for a fresh scent (or three). We are currently misting the Jo Malone Herb Garden Collection in the office – they’re fresh, light and have that perfect crisp sweetness we love about the first sign of autumn air.

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## THE COSSETING TREATMENT CREAM:

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

The ultimate in luxe skincare, La Mer have once again impressed with their latest dream cream. Part moisturiser, part mask, use the cushy, dreamy La Mer Perfecting Treatment twice daily over your face and neck, and revel in soft, dewy skin.

## THE DAB-IT-ON HIGHLIGHT CREAM:

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

If your Autumn skin is feeling a little lack lustre, Luma Illuminating Highlighter in Nude Lamé is the perfect shade of ballerina pink to pat onto your cheekbone, brow bone and cupids bow for a subtle creamy glow.

CHANEL LE GEL COAT

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## THE INSTANT GEL COAT:

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

The cooler months are the perfect excuse for a glossy new manicure. Chanel have answered our prayers in the form of their new Le Gel Top Coat. Activated by natural light, it extends the wear of normal nail polish for up to seven days, so there’s no need to replace last winter’s colours, we’re still loving peachy nudes for a polished look.

## THE PERFECT NUDE TINTED BALM:

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]

Cult classic Lanolips has long been a handbag staple – especially come autumn. Their new tinted balm is the perfect shade of nude for a boost of healthy colour while hydrating chapped lips.
