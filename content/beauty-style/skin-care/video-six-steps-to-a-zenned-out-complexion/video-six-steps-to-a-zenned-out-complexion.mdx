*Hands up who haphazardly slaps moisturiser on each morning before dragging it all off at night with a little Micellar? Me. I’m totally guilty. Until I took a little trip to the Jurlique Farm in SA (watch the vid [here](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/a-journey-into-natural-skincare-sigourneys-edit)) and after enjoying a spoil-worthy facial at their flagship store, I was schooled in the art of the skincare ritual. I teamed up with Jurlique to a make a little video to share these six beautiful serene steps (complete with slow-mo spritzing and soft focus lighting). With scents and textures like these, it’s definitely worth taking some more time to marinate in your beauty routine. Your skin (and your soul) will thank you. *
