Lara Worthington recently shared some of her favourite beauty brands to travel with via Instagram stories, and we were all eyes.

A frequent traveller, the 29-year-old took to social media on Sunday to show off the luxury products she currently uses, including a $180 face mask, a $160 eye cream, and a sea salt spray that retails for just $20.

The travel-sized products were laid out on a marble bench top, and each image was captioned with a little blurb about what she loves about each brand and item –all totalling around $1,000.

![ Image Source: @laraworthington Instagram ](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/lara-worthington-shares-the-ten-products-she-cant-travel-without/img-6.jpg)

 Image Instagram: @laraworthington

Lara also teased the possibility of creating travel-sized products for her own cosmetic line, [The Base ](https://www.thebase.me)(um, yes please!).

'Good products to travel with @oribe I think I need to make some travel size products for The Base,' Lara wrote alongside two mini cans of luxury hair care products.

More products which the model swears by, include Sisley's Black Rose Cream Mask, a La Mer moisturiser, and a Shu Uemura eye lash curler. The blonde beauty also lists a bottle of $43 hair oil from Ouai as a favourite, and can’t go pass a $20 sea salt spray from Wella – helping her achieve that signature effortless undone 'do.

Lara also took the opportunity to give some love to her own range, listing her [Velvet Bronzing Makeup Base ](https://www.thebase.me/collections/cosmetics/products/the-velvet-bronzing-makeup-base)as a go-to for eyeshadow or primer.

Fingers crossed Lara reveals more secrets behind her perfectly imperfect beauty routine…
