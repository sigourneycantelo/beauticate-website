### We asked Victorian Beauticater Lauren Kennedy – (who is also a wine blogger and gets her fair share of the vino-variety of antioxidants) to test a host of protective serums and report back. She canvassed her top performers here. So you can say goodbye to fine lines and wrinkles and hello to a brighter and more radiant complexion. ## BEST FOR A SPLURGE

### Elizabeth Arden PREVAGE Anti-Aging + Intensive Repair Daily Serum, $265

ELIZABETH ARDEN PREVAGE ANTI-AGING + INTENSIVE REPAIR DAILY SERUM

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]“If you’re a city dweller like myself then your skin is constantly exposed to environmental stress and inflammation so we need something super strong. PREVAGE Anti-Aging + Intensive Repair Daily Serum contains the much-hyped super free-radical-fighter Idebenone, along with a host of other protective, high-tech ingredients. It is a whopping $265, but if you feel like a splurge you won’t be disappointed. It screams luxury from the moment of application. The lightweight gel, spreads easily over skin and is gold and shimmery in colour. The shiny silver bottle comes with its own calibrated dropper to allow for precision dispensing, ensuring you don’t waste a skerrick of this lush liquid. As for the results? I was super impressed. After only a few days of lathering this lavish serum morning and night my skin was noticeably smoother, softer, more radiant and brighter.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## PERFECT FOR BATTLING PIGMENTATION

### Aspect Pigment Punch, $121

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27981&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

ASPECT PIGMENT PUNCH

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]“If pigmentation is your peeve Aspect Pigment Punch is your remedy – this antioxidant skin serum promises to banish age spots, brown spots, freckles, hormonal marks, blotchy uneven skin tone and acne scars. I’ve read that blemishes and discolouration, more so than wrinkles, add years to your appearance and until recently, battling pigmentation meant using irritating chemical skin lighteners. Aspect’s hero ingredient Tyrostat eliminates this problem and lightens and brightens skin without burning, irritating or causing trauma – which is perfect, as I suffer from sensitive skin. After a few weeks of use, my sensitive skin was clearer, brighter, more evenly toned and my facial redness calmed down considerably. If you ask me, a serum for approximately $121, which stops pigmentation from rearing its ugly head in the first place is a sage investment.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

COSMEDIX AFFIRM ANTI-OXIDANT FIRMING SERUM

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Cosmedix Affirm Anti-Oxidant Firming Serum, $99

“Every year, as summer approaches, I start looking for a miracle serum that will reverse the years of sun damage I copped as a kid, and defend my skin against further environmental destruction. Cosmedix Affirm Anti-Oxidant Firming Serum has answered my prayers. Powered by peptides and over ten antioxidants this supercharged serum promises to defend against harmful free radicals and help heal sun exposed and dehydrated skin. This lightweight serum soaked into my skin immediately. It definitely boosted my skin’s moisture levels without being greasy and after a few weeks of application my skin felt smoother and firmer. I looked up the ingredients and was impressed – Resveratrol (the antioxidant in my beloved wine!) and Vitamin E provide potent and powerful antioxidant protection while biopeptides help reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. I love that it can also be used on sensitive skin and acne prone skin and paired with other serums to achieve your desired results.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

GO-TO FACE HERO

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]“Former beauty director and author Zoe Foster Blake’s ([read Beauticate’s interview with her here](https://www.beauticate.com/who/zoe-foster-blake-author-and-beauty-entrepreneur)) latest addition to her already stellar Go-To Skincare lineup is the saviour my skin had been waiting for. When I started using this product I questioned whether an oil would make my skin breakout. The answer? Absolutely not – in fact, quite the opposite. Oily skin is, more often than not, dehydrated due to over cleansing and exfoliating which leads to congestion. Face Hero helped my skin function properly to produce the right amount of oil it needed to clear up my blemishes. I also love that it’s certified cruelty free and there are absolutely no nasties like parabens, sulfates, palm oil and petrochemicals, meaning it’s super gentle on your skin and is perfectly safe for pregnant women too (I should know, I used this through my entire pregnancy and while breastfeeding too).”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
