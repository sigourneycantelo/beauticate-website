ST Tropez Self Tan Bronzing Mousse Extra Dark

## The Customisable Tan

Fellow tan-a-holics, raise your bronzed hands if you’ve ever felt personally victimized by blotchy, tangerine tan. It’s been months since my last tanning trauma, and all credit goes to this guy right here. The beauty of this St Tropez Extra Dark Mousse, besides the stellar formula, is that it allows you to choose your depth of dark, leave on for 4 hours for the standard dark tan or sleep in it for 8 hours for an extra-dark beach bronze that lasts for days. A staple in my weekly Thursday night beauty regime, this really truly works. Case in point: I recently returned home from holidays still practically porcelain after weeks spent under the South-East Asian sun. Using just one coat of this bad boy and marinating in it for the full 8 hours while I slept, I managed to fake my way to a substantial amount of compliments regarding how “glowy” I looked post vacation. Keen to avoid a faux pas, I have never admitted my secret until now. No shame about faking this one.

Micellar water was a true innovation in the beauty world, a must-have in any worthy bathroom vanity, and is the go-to for easily, gently and effectively removing dirt, makeup and oil from skin. So it’s a no-brainer that [PANTENE Pro-V Micellar Shampoo](https://shop.coles.com.au/a/a-national/product/pantene-shampoo-micellar-water) and [Conditioner](https://shop.coles.com.au/a/a-national/product/pantene-conditioner-micellar-water-3416027p) would work the same impurity-eliminating magic on your mane also (although keep in mind that unlike regular micellar water, these products must be rinsed out). Not only that, but the range is also pH-balanced, silicone-, paraben- and dye-free – which is très important for maintaining shiny, healthy locks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What pharmacy beauty products are worth buying?**
A: This edit highlights four standout pharmacy picks: St Tropez Self Tan Bronzing Mousse Extra Dark for a customisable faux tan, Pantene Pro-V Micellar Shampoo and Conditioner for gentle yet effective cleansing, Real Techniques Blend & Blur Foundation Brush for seamless base application, and Antipodes Blessing Anti-Pollution Light Face Serum for an affordable antioxidant boost.

**Q: How does St Tropez Extra Dark Mousse work?**
A: The mousse can be left on for four hours for a standard dark tan, or worn overnight for eight hours for a deeper beach bronze. It allows you to choose your depth of colour, and the formula is praised for looking natural rather than orange.

**Q: What makes Pantene Pro-V Micellar Shampoo different from a regular clarifying shampoo?**
A: Unlike a traditional clarifying shampoo, the Pantene Micellar formula removes impurities without stripping the hair's natural protective oils or accelerating colour fade. It is also pH-balanced, and free of silicone, parabens, and dye, making it a gentler option for maintaining healthy, shiny hair.
