## The Customisable Tan

Fellow tan-a-holics, raise your bronzed hands if you’ve ever felt personally victimized by blotchy, tangerine tan. It’s been months since my last tanning trauma, and all credit goes to this guy right here. The beauty of this St Tropez Extra Dark Mousse, besides the stellar formula, is that it allows you to choose your depth of dark— leave on for 4 hours for the standard dark tan or sleep in it for 8 hours for an extra-dark beach bronze that lasts for days. A staple in my weekly Thursday night beauty regime, this really truly works. Case in point: I recently returned home from holidays still practically porcelain after weeks spent under the South-East Asian sun. Using just one coat of this bad boy and marinating in it for the full 8 hours while I slept, I managed to fake my way to a substantial amount of compliments regarding how “glowy” I looked post vacation. Keen to avoid a faux pas, I have never admitted my secret until now. No shame about faking this one.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## The Micellar Water For Your Hair

Think clarifying shampoo, but sans the harsh stripping of your hairs’ natural protective oils (and therefore fading that exxy dye job with lightning speed). Micellar water was a true innovation in the beauty world, a must-have in any worthy bathroom vanity, and is the go-to for easily, gently and effectively removing dirt, makeup and oil from skin. So it’s a no-brainer that [PANTENE Pro-V Micellar Shampoo](https://shop.coles.com.au/a/a-national/product/pantene-shampoo-micellar-water) and [Conditioner](https://shop.coles.com.au/a/a-national/product/pantene-conditioner-micellar-water-3416027p) would work the same impurity-eliminating magic on your mane also (although keep in mind that unlike regular micellar water, these products must be rinsed out).
