## Biossance 100% Squalane oil

If you haven’t heard of squalane oil, you’re not alone, but you *are* missing out. It mimics the lipid squalene (with an “e”), a moisturiser that is naturally produced by our skin cells, but less so with age. What secured this product a permanent #shelfie spot (as well as being a trusty travel buddy) is the fact that this single-ingredient, lightweight oil is a hard multi-tasker – slather on your face, body and/or hair ends for weightless hydration and locked-in moisture.

## Skin Regimen Cleansing Cream

Don’t underestimate the importance of the humble cleanser – it’s a crucial step in any skincare routine, and this non-drying cream is a fab option to do just that, and then some. It detoxifies the visage from nasties that accumulate on skin on the daily, effectively removing makeup, SPF, dust and excess sebum. Loaded with an exclusive mix of organic superfood extracts (think spinach, maqui berry and wild indigo), it’s gentle enough to do a thorough double cleanse with, which rebalances the skin without over stripping it – paramount when prepping for a clean slate on which to lather potent serums and the like.

## Naturally Serious Cold-Pressed Peel Pads

Cardinal skincare sin? Sleeping with your makeup on. Enter these pre-soaked pads, which have proved to be a saviour when kept by the bedside in case of boozy nights out when feeling extra lazy, or in the gym bag to quickly cleanse, post-workout. They are packed with ingredients including colloidal oatmeal (to lock in moisture), antimicrobial probiotics (to restore skin’s natural balance) and six antioxidants including pomegranate and goji berry, to help combat visible signs of ageing caused by pollution and free radical damage.

## Synergie Skin Enviroshield

Looking to top up your pollution protection throughout the day? This nifty spray goes straight over makeup, protecting against the effects of airborne pollutants in the form of microscopic, harmful nanoparticles, which produce ageing effects as destructive as that of UV rays – yikes! With just a spritz (or three), it helps to counter the effects of toxic city air by inhibiting the penetration of hydrocarbons, all whilst setting your makeup with a dewy glow. Talk about clever.
