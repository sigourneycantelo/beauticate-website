    answer: "Yes, coconut oil is safe and effective for removing makeup, including stubborn eye makeup. It's a natural alternative to commercial cleansers that gently dissolves makeup without harsh chemicals.   - question: "Why do you need to warm coconut oil before applying it?"
    answer: "Coconut oil is solid at room temperature, so gently rubbing it between your palms melts it into a liquid that's easier to spread across your face and massage into makeup. This warming step also makes the oil more effective at dissolving makeup without tugging at delicate skin."

**Step 1 –:**Scoop a small handful of coconut oil out of the jar. Usually it will be solid, unless it is a hot day.

![margaret 1](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/remove-make-up-with-coconut-oil/margaret-1.gif)

**Step 2 –:**Turn it into a liquid by rubbing it gently between your palms.

![margaret 2](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/remove-make-up-with-coconut-oil/margaret-2.gif)

**Step 3 –:**Smear it all over a dry face. Massage it into your eyes if you've got thick eye makeup on, "and let it sit for a second so everything dissolves," adds Margaret.

![margaret 3](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/remove-make-up-with-coconut-oil/margaret-3.gif)

**Step 4 –:**Next, remove it – and your makeup. “Just grab a pad and wipe everything off,” she says.

![margaret 4](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/remove-make-up-with-coconut-oil/margaret-4.gif)

**Step 5 –:**Next, Then rinse your face with water. There will be some coconut oil residue, just massage it in for a moisturising treatment.

![margaret 5](/content/beauty-style/skin-care/remove-make-up-with-coconut-oil/margaret-5.gif)
