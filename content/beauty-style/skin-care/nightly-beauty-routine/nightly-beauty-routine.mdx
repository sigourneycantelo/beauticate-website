By the time I get home, I don’t want silence. I want movement. Familiar chaos. Hello, ADHD. I want a clean face, no bra, a glass poured, emails half-checked, and **Bravo** on loud enough that I can hear women yelling while I move around the apartment. Candles lit because – why not? It’s not about switching off – it’s about switching gears.

Especially now, with a kitten called Blueberry Muffin supervising proceedings and a month full of obligations already behind me. These nights aren’t about pretending I don’t love things. They’re about ritual, routine, and using products properly -many I’ve relied on for years, some newer, all genuinely part of my life.

I won’t lie: I love products. I love experiencing the scents, textures, and most of all… the packaging. I am a marketer’s dream.

## The Arrival Ritual: How the Night Actually Starts

I don’t rip my makeup off the second I walk through the door. I like a buffer.

I wind down first. Shoes off. Bag hung. My new [**Steven Khalil x Black Blaze candle**](https://www.blackblaze.com.au/pages/steven-khalil-x-black-blaze-ivory-garden-scented-candle?srsltid=AfmBOoo7CgwWWOIwV_K0YJO9co8-0rrULY6AwV6LoKR6_UA0KkvJq9x_)lit. A glass of wine poured – one of my longest friends has a vineyard in Victoria’s King Valley Wine Region, founded and run by women – and my varietal of choice, the [**Symphonia Arneis 2022**](https://symphoniafinewines.com.au/collections/our-whites/products/symphonia-arneis-2021)**.** I check emails and messages while setting up my watchlist.

This is the decompression window – the slow shift from public-facing to private.

## Only then do I cleanse.

I double cleanse every night. Always have. I’ve tried all the oil cleansers, and the holy grail is the [**MECCA Cosmetica Nourishing Cleansing Oil**](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/mecca-cosmetica/nourishing-cleansing-oil-I-042608/). I work it into dry skin (pro tip from the very talented**James Vivian**: cleanse for at least 60 seconds) before removing it with a [**Retreatment Botanics muslin cloth**](https://www.retreatmentbotanics.com/products/muslin-cloths) soaked in water that’s slightly warmer than warm.

Followed by the [**Rejuvaus Deep Exfoliating AHA Cleanser**](https://au.rejuvaus.com/products/deep-exfoliating-aha-cleanser). I am the least active person you will meet, but this cleanser walked so everything else could run. It includes 15 intelligent acids -including lactic, malic, citric and tartaric acids – plus activated charcoal, prebiotics and a lipid complex. It’s lightweight and leaves my skin feeling clean and nourished, never dry or squeaky.

About ten years ago, I was seeing an incredible facialist who introduced me to [**Sesderma**](https://www.sesderma.com/es_en/about-us.html), a Spanish skincare brand that genuinely changed my life. After my hysterectomy at 30 (cervical cancer survivor over here), my skin went into chaos thanks to no natural hormones and the introduction of multiple medications, including HRT. The [**Azelac Lotion Spray**](https://www.caretobeauty.com/au/sesderma-azelac-lotion-for-face-body-and-hair-100ml/) is a staple in my beauty cupboard and always will be. A few squirts onto a cotton pad and gentle swipes across the face soothe the skin, diminish redness, and help regulate sebum. If you have folliculitis, this is the product.

I finish with [**Jurlique Calendula Toner**](https://bit.ly/3L37CCt), pressed in with my hands. It’s grounding, uncomplicated, and exactly what my skin wants at the end of a long day. If I want extra hydration, I’ll dot the [**Calendula Cream**](https://bit.ly/48TjKzf) onto my cheeks, chin, nose and forehead, then gently massage it in.

This is where the night actually begins.

## Why These Nights Matter

I like luxury. I like products. I explore beauty for a living – and I use it properly.

These nights aren’t about resetting anything or performing calmly. I’m still checking my laptop, still moving around, still engaged. The difference is that I’m doing it surrounded by things I genuinely enjoy.

So light the special candle. Lather in the good body wash. [**Pop on a BonPatch Wearable Sleep Patch**](https://bit.ly/3KSBvFy). Whether you’re a one-step Wonder Woman or an “I don’t have enough space for all my products” girly – enjoy your beauty routine.

This is ritual, not restraint. Luxury, used.
And that’s the point.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What order should you apply skincare at night?**
A: Apply from thinnest to thickest. Cleanser first, then toner or essence, then serums, then eye cream, then moisturiser, then a face oil or balm as the final seal. Active ingredients like retinol go after cleansing but before moisturiser.

**Q: What is double cleansing and why does it matter?**
A: Double cleansing means using an oil-based cleanser first to dissolve makeup and sunscreen, followed by a water-based cleanser to remove residue. The article recommends cleansing for at least 60 seconds with the oil cleanser before rinsing, then following with an AHA or exfoliating cleanser for a thorough result.

**Q: What does Sesderma Azelac Lotion Spray do?**
A: It is a toning lotion that soothes skin, diminishes redness and helps regulate sebum. The article notes it is particularly effective for folliculitis, and has been a staple since hormonal and skin changes following a hysterectomy at 30.

**Q: How should you moisturise your body at night?**
A: The routine described layers two body products - Glossier Body Hero lotion for softness and scent, followed by Rationale Illuminating Body Creme. A weekly self-tan rotation (Sontse Golden Hour Mousse and Eleffect The Tint in Medium) is also worked into Thursday evenings.
