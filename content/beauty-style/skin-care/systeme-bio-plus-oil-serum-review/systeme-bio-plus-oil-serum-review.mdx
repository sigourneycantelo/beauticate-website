## A Note on Skin Types & Sensitivity

As with any [skincare product](https://www.beauticate.com/beauty-style/skin-care/these-beauties-will-help-you-refresh-your-skincare-regime/), particularly those containing active ingredients, results can vary depending on individual skin type and tolerance.

While the majority of our trial team experienced significant improvements in hydration, glow and overall skin quality, a small number of participants with highly reactive or sensitised skin reported irritation during use. This was most notable among those with pre-existing conditions such as rosacea or known sensitivity to [Vitamin C](https://www.beauticate.com/beauty-style/skin-care/going-outside-dont-without-applying-this/).

In one case, a participant chose to discontinue facial application but continued using the serum on the neck, where it was well tolerated and delivered positive hydration results.

This is not uncommon when working with potent actives. Ingredients like Vitamin C can be transformative for many, but are not universally suited to every skin type – particularly those that are compromised or highly reactive.

**As always, introducing any new active product gradually and patch testing prior to use is recommended, especially for sensitive skin.**
