## **1. LAYER UP! (WELL, OBVIOUSLY…)**

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31271&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE FROM: [ADORENESS](http://www.adoreness.com/sweater-weather-fall-essentials/)

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text el_class="no_margin"]This tip seems pretty obvious, and you probably hear it every Winter, but layering is certainly one of our favourite ways to bring summer clothes into the cooler seasons. Get creative- layer your knits over shirts, or shirts over knits. Think outside the box by throwing a denim jacket over the top of your look or turn a sarong into a shawl. There are so many possibilities…[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]**Just add:** a gradual tanner to extend your Summer glow. Our pick is Tan-Luxe’s The Butter Gradual Tanner to keep your skin bronzed and hydrated throughout Winter.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31282&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## **2. LAYER YOUR STATEMENT SLEEVE BLOUSE UNDER A SLIP DRESS**

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text el_class="no_margin"]Statement sleeves have been a key trend lately, with everything from bell sleeves to  80s style balloon sleeves (we bet you didn’t think you'd see those again!), hitting the runways and taking over street style. Make the most of yours by layering it under a summer slip dress with a pair of ankle boots, to create this stylish trans-seasonal look.[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]**Just add:** a hair spray like Redken’s Quick Tease 15 Backcombing Finishing Sprayto add both volume and texture to your hair.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31273&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31478&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

## **3. ADD A BLAZER TO YOUR TEE & JEANS COMBO**

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31272&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: FASHION ME NOW

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text el_class="no_margin"]It’s no secret the power suit came back with a vengeance this season. We're happy to report: It’s time to revive the blazer. Throw this classic on over your t-shirt and jeans for an easy and effortless look. A change of shoes can take this look from day to night, so don't be afraid to take your blazer out after-hours.[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]**Just add:** a scented candle like Tom Ford Oud Wood Candle. The woodsy scent is the perfect way to prepare for the cooler months and keep your wardrobe smelling fresh.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31274&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## **5. WEAR TIGHTS WITH YOUR SUMMER SKIRTS AND DRESSES**

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31277&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: @ROSIEHW

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text el_class="no_margin"]Keep your legs warm while wearing your favourite skirts and dresses by adding some tights or stockings. You can thank Balenciaga for making them cool again. Take your pick of sheer or opaque – we love Rosie Huntington-Whiteley’s look with a nice blouse and jacket layered on top.[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31278&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]**Just add:** An Autumnal inspired lip like this Clinique High Impact Lip Colour in Red-Y To Wear.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## **6. OR WEAR YOUR SUMMER DRESSES OVER PANTS**

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text el_class="no_margin"]Already a popular street style trend last year, Summer dresses over pants are here to stay for the time being. With shorter dresses, we love pairing a wide leg pant for some balance. Whilst a slimmer fit pant suits midi & maxi hem lines. If you’re feeling constricted in a button front dress, go for this look, like Brooke Testoni, and unbutton the bottom half.[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31280&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]**Just add:** A sweet yet soft fragrance such as the Chloé Nomade Eau de Parfum as the perfect scent to match this look.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31279&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]
