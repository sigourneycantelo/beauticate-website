## ALLANAH JANSONS, 24, RECRUITMENT CONSULTANT

### "The most instant thing I noticed was how glowy my makeup appeared."

“I do A LOT of things to help manage my dry, pink/red patchy, sensitive skin mostly from the inside out (my insides probably resemble your local health food shop) however, my skin has never really gotten to that magical place where it doesn’t have *some* sign of sensitivity. A few months ago I cut out coffee, replacing it with matcha (with a spoonful of powdered collagen to boot) and this has probably made the most dramatic difference to my skin but it still didn’t get me quite “there”. Enter Dermalogica’s [Calm Water Gel](https://goo.gl/CJ5UKN)  and Barrier Defense Booster. I was a bit skeptical as the Calm Water Gel contains hyaluronic acid, which usually makes my skin feel so “hydrated” it’s sticky – like smearing lip balm all over my face (no thanks).The barrier defense booster is a serum, containing a myriad of oils and again, I’ve used oils which have generally made my skin break out.But I had only heard amazing things about Dermalogica so I figured I had nothing to lose trialing these products. So, I didn’t wake up the next morning singing Beyonce’s ‘Flawless’ but after a few days I did start to notice that the more dramatic red spots had subsided and after a week I swore my pores had probably halved in size making my skin appear *much* smoother. The most instant thing I noticed was how glowy my makeup appeared. The extremely light weight formulas are seriously impressive – it honestly feels like I have nothing on my skin and the scent is so relaxing (almost aromatherapeutic), using a product so carefully formulated really is a luxury. These have pushed my previous long-time (not cheap either) moisturizers off their pedestal – they’re quite literally two of the most effective products I’ve used – ever.”

## LUCY SEARLE, DIGITAL MARKETER

### "These products really helped me get on top of the never-ending dryness and accelerated the healing process post-treatment tenfold.”

"As an avid fan of monthly peels when the opportunity arose to test the [new duo](https://goo.gl/JZrcFA) by Dermalogica, I knew I was the perfect candidate. The timing couldn’t have been better either, I had just had a strong peel and microdermabrasion treatment, meaning my skin was red, sensitive to touch and I was about to enter the glamorous post peel flaking stage. My skin literally drank up the Barrier Defense Booster. The oil formula, isn’t “oily” as such, in that it leaves no trace or sticky residue, but is absorbed beautifully into the skin leaving it feeling baby’s bottom-soft.I followed up with the [Calm Water Gel moisturiser](https://goo.gl/CJ5UKN), which almost dissolves upon application and not only hydrated my parched skin but also felt like it protected it.I was thrilled to wake up the next morning and discover my skin was dewy, soft and hydrated, a welcome change to how tight and rough it usually feels after a peel and microdermabrasion. They also prep skin for makeup so it seems to glide on effortlessly and give you that glow. Over the coming days, with twice-daily use, I noticed a dramatic improvement in skin suppleness and general hydration. My skin dries out pretty quickly (thanks to the office aircon that never shuts off) and sometimes no matter how much moisturizer I apply, it’s never enough. These products really helped me get on top of the never-ending dryness and accelerated the healing process post-treatment tenfold.”

## MADELINE RILEY, 23, BRAND PARTNERSHIPS COORDINATOR

### "It works well with my very dry skin, and has stacked up very well against my most-loved serums and moisturisers."

“I have very sensitive and intolerant skin. I get a lot of texture and redness due to environmental factors and am extremely dry and dehydrated. I’ve been using the new [Dermalogica Ultracalming Duo](https://goo.gl/JZrcFA) morning and night for one week as a substitute for my usual serums and moisturiser and I’m very impressed. The Barrier Defence Booster has the most relaxing and calming lavender scent, which makes me feel like I’m at the spa every time I apply it. It’s the perfect consistency that doesn’t take too long to absorb into the skin but provides a great level of nourishment and hydration.I was sceptical about the [Calm Water Gel](https://goo.gl/CJ5UKN) at first as I usually use a rich cream moisturiser and I wasn’t sure how it would layer with the oil based booster, but again, I’m very impressed. The gel is lightweight and cooling and while it doesn’t have the lovely scent of the booster, it does leave the skin feeling fresh, calm and hydrated. Since I’ve started using this duo, the redness and irritation I usually experience has dramatically reduced, evening out my skin tone and leaving me with plump, radiant skin all day long. It works well with my very dry skin, and has stacked up very well against my most-loved serums and moisturisers. I’ll definitely continue using this one, especially heading into the colder, windy months when my skin is at its most sensitive."

Bravo Dermalogica, finally a range of skincare for sensitive skin that’s actually sensitive.
