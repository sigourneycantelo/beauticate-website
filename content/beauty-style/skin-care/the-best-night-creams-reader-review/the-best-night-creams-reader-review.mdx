## BEST FOR: THE HYDRATION HUNTER

### Avon Nutra Effects Hydration Gel Cream

“This gel offers hydration without the heaviness of a thick cream. It instantly refreshes and cools my skin, and is formulated with active seed complex and chia seed designed to help fortify the skin’s barrier and keep moisture locked in. The scent is fresh, like clean linen, and my skin absorbs it with no sticky residue. Despite it being made for night, I find it works well as a day cream too due to the lighter consistency.”

## BEST FOR: THE CLASSIC BEAUTY

### Avon Nutra Effects Ageless Multi-Action Cream

“This beautiful Avon night cream is the ultimate nourishment for skin. A thick, cream formula, this multi-action product softens my fine lines while I sleep, and results in gorgeous smooth, firm skin in the morning. There's something so lovely and familiar about the scent that instantly reminds me of hours spent playing with my mum’s beauty products many years ago. This cream is very gentle on my yet works wonders to smooth out fine lines and plumps my dull areas.”

## BEST FOR: THE CITY SLICKER

### A’kin Replenishing Antioxidant Night Cream

“Jam-packed with antioxidants this super hydrating (but not too heavy) moisturiser is just what my skin needs after summer. The all-natural formula contains organic coconut water and vitamin E for nourishment while green tea extracts work to protect my skin against pollution and free radical damage – perfect as my skin is exposed to environmental stressors. The lightweight cream spreads easily over my skin, smells like a day spa and works a treat. After only a week of lathering this on every night, I wake up to noticeably softer, more supple skin.”

## BEST FOR: MATURE SKIN

### Weleda Evening Primrose Age Revitalising Night Cream

“I try to avoid some anti-ageing products as they tend to make my skin feel greasy, so I am interested to see how I fare with this night cream. Like so many Weleda products, the amazing natural fragrance is instantly calming and perfect for my pre-bedtime ritual. The cream is formulated with evening primrose seed oil, Inca nut oil and macadamia nut oil, which strengthens and plumps my skin. I initially find the texture to be slightly greasy, but once I apply it my skin soaks up all the goodness and it doesn’t leave any sticky residue. My skin feels firmer, brighter and incredibly hydrated.”

## BEST FOR: THE LUXE LOVER

### Bobbi Brown Hydrating Intense Night Cream

“If you’re looking for super hydration, then say hello to your new best friend. This luxe moisturiser from Bobbi Brown feels like a little treat for my face at night. The creamy, cushiony texture feels beautiful on my skin without being too heavy or oily (so perfect for all skin types). The rich, restorative cream features an advanced Protein Activator to reduce the appearance of fine lines, Milk Thistle extract to plump up tired skin and Sweet Almond Oil to boost moisture levels.”

## BEST FOR: STRESSED OUT SKIN

### Comfort Zone Renight Cream

“Undernourished, stressed and tired skin types rejoice. This night cream by Comfort Zone is formulated with active natural ingredients to help boost skin hydration including hyaluronic acid for plumping and a potent dose of antioxidants to encourage regenerative support for skin tissue. After a day at the beach in the West Australian sun I find this night cream to be a miracle worker as it replenishes moisture perfectly. My skin feels very soft and even in the morning with all evidence yesterday’s activities erased. It is reasonably priced for a higher end product and one I think will become a staple in my skincare regime.”
