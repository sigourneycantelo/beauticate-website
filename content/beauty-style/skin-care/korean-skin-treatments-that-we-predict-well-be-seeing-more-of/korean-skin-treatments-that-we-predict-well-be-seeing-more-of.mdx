## Lhala Peel

Chemical peels can be intimidating, especially if you have sensitive skin prone to redness or irritation. Enter the Lhala Peel, a fourth-generation chemical peel that utilizes Lipo Hydroxy Acid (LHA). Unlike traditional alpha or beta hydroxy acids, LHA mimics the skin's natural renewal process, gently dissolving dead skin cells without causing visible peeling or downtime.

**Why it’s trending:** The Lhala Peel is perfect for achieving that coveted "glass skin" effect - a smooth, poreless, and translucent complexion. It’s a great alternative for those who want the benefits of a chemical peel but without the risk of redness, flaking, or downtime. The treatment is designed to gently exfoliate and clarify the skin while maintaining its natural moisture barrier, making it suitable for even the most sensitive skin types.

**Potential side effects:** Although gentler than traditional peels, some people may still experience mild tingling or tightness during and after the treatment, particularly if they have very sensitive skin. There may be some very slight dryness or flakiness on the second and third day.

## BB Laser

![](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/korean-skin-treatments-that-we-predict-well-be-seeing-more-of/1.gif)


BB Laser combines the benefits of laser technology with a focus on skin rejuvenation. This treatment utilizes a gentle laser to target pigmentation issues, reduce redness, and smooth out skin texture. The BB Laser stimulates the skin's natural healing process, leading to a more even skin tone and a healthier, brighter complexion.

**Why it’s trending:** The BB Laser went viral for making skin look like it naturally has "bb cream" (a perfecting tinted skin moisturiser). It is the go-to solution for individuals seeking to address skin concerns like pigmentation, acne scars, or dullness. Its versatility allows it to be customized to suit different skin types, offering effective results with minimal downtime, making it ideal for those with busy lifestyles.

**Potential side effects:** Some people may experience mild redness, swelling, or a sensation of warmth in the treated area immediately after the procedure. These side effects are typically short-lived, subsiding within a few hours to a day. In rare cases, temporary skin sensitivity or slight dryness may occur.

## InMode Lifting

InMode Lifting is all about sculpting and contouring your face using the power of radiofrequency (RF) technology. This non-surgical facelift uses RF energy to heat the deeper layers of the skin, triggering collagen production and tightening the skin without needles or scalpels.

**Why it’s trending:** In Asia, where achieving a V-shaped face is highly desired, InMode Lifting offers a way to contour the jawline, reduce the appearance of double chins, and sculpt facial features without surgery. Think of it as a high-tech alternative to traditional facelifts but with natural-looking results.

**Potential side effects:** Some individuals might experience temporary swelling, redness, bumpiness, mild bruising, or a slight burning sensation due to the heat involved. One[:**TikTok user**](https://www.tiktok.com/@jisoyl/video/7425610508218158369) said it looked like she got a "beating". However, these side effects usually completely subside after 4-5 days.

## LDM (Local Dynamic Micro-Ultrasound)

![](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/korean-skin-treatments-that-we-predict-well-be-seeing-more-of/2.gif)


LDM is setting a new standard in skin therapy by using micro-fused ultrasound waves that penetrate deep into the skin's dermis (the layer below the superficial skin surface) to deeply hydrate. This treatment improves skin's moisture retention by enhancing the distribution of water molecules and promoting better blood circulation.

**Why it’s trending:** One of the standout features of LDM is its ability to supercharge the absorption of skincare products. During a session, skin ampoules infused with active ingredients (like hyaluronic acid or peptides) are applied, and the ultrasound waves help increase their penetration. The combination of micro-ultrasound energy and potent serums leads to a glowing, plumped complexion that looks like you’ve just had a luxurious facial - but with longer-lasting effects.

**Potential side effects:** While generally considered safe, some users may experience mild redness or warmth on the skin immediately after the treatment, which typically subsides within a few hours.
