## CLEANSE, CLEANSE, CLEANSE

If there’s one thing we’ve learnt in this beauty game, it’s the importance of washing away the day’s grime each and every night, no exceptions. But, for Claire, it’s equally important to cleanse the face in the morning. “I always use my beautiful Foam Cleanser, and not every day but quite a lot I use it with a Clarisonic,” explains Claire. “I use the cleanser morning and night without fail.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="24382&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_empty_space height="15px"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="24383&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## TONE UP

Toners are a beauty product with a bit of ‘middle child syndrome’, it can get lost in the mix. But, although it maybe doesn’t get as much airtime as cleansing and moisturising, it’s a pretty important part of any skincare regime. Claire agrees. “I use the Cellular Softening and Balancing Lotion which is a toner that works really beautifully with my skin. I use cotton balls for a really nice, even application.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_empty_space height="15px"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## SERIOUS ABOUT SERUM

Serum is vital, dear readers. If you’re ever thinking of upping your skincare regime to the next level, do it with a serum. They absorb into the skin and have arguably the highest efficacy of all at-home skincare products. “The first serum I ever used, and now use all the time, is the Skin Caviar Crystalline Concentre firming serum,” says Claire. “I add it to my daily regime. But if my skin needs a hydration boost, I go for the Cellular Swiss Ice Crystal Serum. I always use that, I add it into my daily regime. I also use the Platinum Rare Cellular Eye Essence serum.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="24384&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_empty_space height="15px"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="24386&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## CREAM FOR COMPLEXION PERFECTION

Opting for a nourishing face cream seems a bit of a no-brainer for perfecting your complexion, but you’ve got to choose the right one for your skin. This can be a bit of trial and error, but worth the effort. “I use the Skin Caviar Absolute Filler, I just started using it,” says Claire. “I did a bit of searching before I found the perfect one, and this is sublime on my skin. I love it.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_empty_space height="15px"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

## MASTER THE MASK

Just as our hair needs a weekly bout of TLC, so too does our skin. Popping a mask on every couple of days will ensure you aren’t constantly chasing your tail to fix your skin’s problem areas – it helps to maintain the hard work your skincare regime is putting in. “Every three days I use the Cellular 3-Minute Peel. It’s my little bit of luxury, my indulgent time,” says Claire. “It’s a bit tingly and you get a little bit of redness so you know it’s really working, but you just wash it off thoroughly and it subsides after ten minutes.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="24387&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_empty_space height="15px"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]
