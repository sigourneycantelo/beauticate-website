In recent years, the beauty ingestible market has exploded. Like most biological processes, scientists have found a way to support our specific health & beauty needs with potent actives that work from the inside, out. Whether it’s healthy lustrous hair, diamond-hard nails, or candle-lit-skin you’re after, there’s a beauty supplement designed to support you on your journey. While some can be cautious to extol the benefits of supplements (preferring instead to focus on a nutrition-rich diet), experts have led promising studies into the science that sits behind these formulations – particularly if your diet is lacking (1).  While this inside-out approach is gaining popularity, the ultimate beauty move is to combine the appropriate ingestibles with a potent outside-in skincare routine. Wellness company, Swisse, recognized the power of combining beauty supplements with skincare and have created a protocol of pairing their premium quality beauty supplements with their high-performance skincare products.

Hydrolysed collagen in particular has been found to be better absorbed by the body (3).The Swisse Beauty Collagen + Hyaluronic Acid Booster contains hydrolysed collagen to help improve skin elasticity and Vitamin C to support collagen production, plus our favourite skincare active – Hyaluronic Acid. Using collagen plus hyaluronic acid topically has long been a way to nourish and support skin health. By adding a pump of the Hyaluronic 2% Vitamin B5 Glow Booster Serum into your mornings and evenings, you’re doubling down on skin-plumping hyaluronic acid, as well as adding soothing and moisturising Vitamin B5, which plays an essential role in protecting the skin’s moisture barrier.
