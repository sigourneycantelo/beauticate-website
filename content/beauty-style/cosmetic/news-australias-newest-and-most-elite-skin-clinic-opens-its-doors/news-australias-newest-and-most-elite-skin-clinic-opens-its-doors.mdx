Aesthetic physician to the A-list Dr. Joseph Hkeik, recently opened the doors to his new high-end clinic in Sydney’s Double Bay. Over ten years in the making, the practice is situated in a designer-finished house that feels more like a palatial home than a clinic.

The chic two-level space was first imagined by Hkeik almost ten years ago, who employed designer Blainey North to realize his vision. It features six treatment rooms, a skin consultation room with a makeup and photography station.

There’s even a meditation zone for clients to chill out post-treatment.With a client list reads like a who’s who of Australian celebrity, Joseph Hkeik is known for his holistic view on aesthetic treatments – and the concept behind his latest clinic showcases that perfectly.Hkeik is known for his light touch when it comes to cosmetic artistry and creating work that is undetectable.

![](/content/beauty-style/cosmetic/news-australias-newest-and-most-elite-skin-clinic-opens-its-doors/download1.jpeg)


“Maintaining normal facial expressiveness is fundamental in my eyes. I believe that less is more – this is at the heart of my ‘art of face’ philosophy. You will walk out of All Saints Skin Clinic feeling and looking more beautiful, natural and radiant,” says Hkeik.

The Double Bay clinic is the third All Saints practice, joining sister properties in Darlinghurst and Parramatta. For more information, visit All Saints Clinic*Story by Sigourney Cantelo*
