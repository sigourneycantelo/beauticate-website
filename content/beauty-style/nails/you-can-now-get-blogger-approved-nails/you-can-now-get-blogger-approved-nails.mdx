Style mavens will already be well acquainted with Sara Donaldson, the creative powerhouse behind fashion blog Harper & Harley.  Fashion and beauty bloggers too often possess an effortless style that seems impossible to emulate – until now.

EN-US

JA

Harper & Harley has teamed up with The Nail Lab to bring us three limited bespoke nail colours, and we’re obsessed (*dries nails and types article one-handed*). Harley is the perfect deep, almost black burgundy, Undone is a flattering shade of neutral beige, and Harper is the perfect sheer ballerina pink to give natural nails a polished finish.

EN-US

JA

Super quick drying, we found three coats gave us opaque coverage. Two left our nails looking ‘done’ and just one of either Harper or Harley (pink or beige) was a more than adequate coverage when we only have 30 seconds before running out the door.

EN-US

JA

The set is $50, with $15 of every one sold donated to MS Research Australia. You can get your hands on them online, or at the The Nail Lab store in Darlinghurst.
