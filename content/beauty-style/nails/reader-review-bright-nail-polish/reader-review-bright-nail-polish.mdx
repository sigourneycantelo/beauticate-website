## BEST FOR: A HIT OF GLAM

### Ciate Cupcake Queen 011

“I’d be lying if I said I didn’t fall in love with each bottle of Ciate nail varnish on first sight. These might just be the most handsome polish bottles on the market. This fuchsia pink contains a very slight oil-slick glimmer to give a certain* je ne sais quoi* to your manicure, and the formulation is rich and smooth.”

## BEST FOR: NO-FUSS APPLICATION

### Dior Bonheur 661

“This hot pink shade from Dior packs a real pigment punch – I find one coat does the trick, and the brand’s trademark wide brush with a nicely rounded edge makes application extra quick.”

## BEST FOR: NASTY-FREE FORMULATION

### Kester Black Coral

“This classic coral shade is perhaps the chicest summer accessory, but best of all this formula contains no DBP, Formaldehyde, Toluene, Formadehyde Resin or Camphor (also known as 5-Free), so it’s kind on your nail beds as well. Two coats are essential.”

## BEST FOR: VERSATILE COLOUR

### OPI Are We There Yet?

“OPI’s slightly more muted coral offering contains a very subtle shimmer, perhaps lending itself to that Antipodean novelty of Christmas in summer. This slightly thinner formulation applies very easily but definitely requires two coats.”

## BEST FOR: NAIL VARNISH NOVICES

### [Essie Blossom Dandy](http://www.essie.com/colors/blues/blossom-dandy.aspx)

“For those a bit nervous at the prospect of adorning their trotters with such vibrant shades, take a cue from Essie’s sweet duck-egg-blue polish. Essie also champions the wider brush and once applied this colour offers a subtle nod to the season without shouting. Apply two coats for best result.”

## BEST FOR: RULE BREAKING SHADE

### [Mecca Cosmetica Valeria](http://mecca.com.au/mecca-cosmetica/artistry-nail-polish/V-018308.html)

“Mecca offers a supremely shiny aqua shade to break up the traditional summer pinks and reds, and this colour is as glossy and sweet as seasonal candies. Application needs two coats.”

## BEST FOR: LONG-LASTING COLOUR

### Jamberry Getaway 125

"This Jamberry shade is not for the light-hearted! A deep turquoise such as this brings a little of the ocean to your fingertips, and best of all is 5-Free! Perhaps the trade-off for such a benefit is that at least two coats (perhaps three) are required to achieve a nice even colour. But what’s so great about Jamberry – no waiting for them to dry. They last ages, and I think this innovation is the best."

### *Quick tip: Don’t forget to always apply a nourishing base coat before colour, and finish off with a high-shine, quick-dry top coat to lock in your favourite shade. Find the time to massage cuticle oil in to the nail beds twice a week, and your digits will look pristine for longer.*

*Review by Katherine Ring*
