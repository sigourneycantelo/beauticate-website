So, in the ever evolving pursuit to extend the life of a mani comes the latest in manicure mastery, Nail Dipping. It's a powder that you pour, sprinkle or dip onto natural nails to create a myriad of finishes: French tip, ombré, glitter, you name it.. there's a powder that can do it.

No more peeling polish, this powder is chip and crack resistant. You don’t even have to smell up the room when doing your nails. The powder is odour free. Even better, it has vitamins and calcium to strengthen your nail beds, and no UV lights required. The reviews will have you switched to powder in a matter of time.
