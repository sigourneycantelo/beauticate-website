SIGOURNEY WEARS WITCHERY LUXE DRAPE PANT, TEXTURED TANK, RESIN SEED NECKLACE AND CYNTHIA SLIDES

### To celebrate the launch of Witchery’s new beauty collection, I chose four low-maintenance looks from the fashion range and worked them back with some easy makeup looks. Here’s how to do effortless style and summer beauty (read: more time for beach).

### 1. Team bronze eyes and nude nails with neutrals

WITCHERY GEL NAIL POLISH IN SAHARA, CRYSTAL TWIST RING IN ROSE GOLD AND GRAND PALETTE

![Holding 0](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/Holding-0-4.jpg)

WITCHERY CRYSTAL TWIST RING IN ROSE GOLD AND SMOKY EYE FROM GRAND PALETTE

WITCHERY GEL NAIL POLISH IN SAHARA, WITCHERY CRYSTAL TWIST RING IN ROSE GOLD AND WITCHERY GRAND PALETTE

The loose-fit drawstring harem pant is such a smart yet relaxed look. Worn with no-fuss nude nails, a silk tank and sporty slides it can translate easily from a day running errands to a stylish choice for a sunset barbecue. To take it into evening simply add a bronze smoky eye and a statement necklace.

After seasons of nail art, the nude manicure is back in the fashion spotlight. I love this matte flesh-toned shade – it works with everything in my wardrobe and is the perfect nude. This rose-gold ring would make a beautiful Christmas gift, too.

![Img 9428](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/IMG_9428-1.jpg)

To create this smoky eye, line the eye with the Wind Up Liner and then follow with the bronze shade in the Grand Palette blending up towards the socket line. Take some of the gold shadow and blend it up to the brow bone.

### 2. Pair prints with a bright poppy pedicure

WITCHERY PRINT TUBE SKIRT, GILIA SILK TANK. HOLLY WEDGE AND NATALIA CROSS BODY BAG

WITCHERY HOLLY WEDGE AND GEL NAIL POLISH IN VIVID YELLOW

WITCHERY ROWENA ZIP POUCHES, SHIMMER LOTION. BRONZING LOTION,FIBRE LASH MASCARA. WIND UP EYE PENCIL GEL AND GEL NAIL POLISH IN VIVID YELLOW

![9429](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/9429.jpeg)

This palm leaf print skirt just screams summer and is perfect paired with a fresh white blouse and a poppy pedicure. It’s a great choice for a summer party or lunch with friends. A tropical print like this begs to be worn with bronzed skin, so I mixed the Bronzing Lotion with a dab of Shimmer Lotion and slathered it on my arms and legs.

Embrace brights and enhance your faux glow with a neon yellow pedicure. Keep toenails short and square and I would stick to toes only for a colour this bright. The yellow looks smoking hot against black shoes and slides.

### 3. Get creative with all white

WITCHERY COLOUR BLOCK KNIT TEE, WILLOW SHORTS, KAT WEDGE SLIDE AND STACEY WALLET.

![9430](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/9430.jpeg)

WITCHERY COLOUR BLOCK KNIT TEE, STACEY WALLET AND EYE PALETTE IN LUMINOUS PINK.

[WITCHERY ](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty?rq=Easy%20Holiday%20Style%20and%20Beauty#)KAT WEDGE SLIDE, EYE PALETTE IN LUMINOUS PINK AND STACEY WALLET.

An all white ensemble is given a graphic lift with sections of black and gold. (The luxe summer wedge is going on my Christmas wish list!) When you’re paring back your fashion you can afford to have a bit more fun with your makeup. I love the idea of adding a flash of coloured eyeliner to the outer edges of your lashes.

To create coloured liner, simply dip an eyeliner brush into water and wet down a bright eyeshadow to turn it into liquid. Layer it on close the lashline, then apply lashings of black mascara.

![Img 9043](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/IMG_9043-1.jpg)

### 4. Metallics love bright lips

WITCHERY STIPPLED FOIL TANK, WILLOW SHORTS, SILVER KELLY THONG, OCRF CRYSTAL BAR RING AND LIPSTICK IN SUNSET.

WITCHERY STIPPLED FOIL TANK, OCRF CRYSTAL BAR RING, CHEEK PALETTE AND LIPSTICK IN SUNSET.

WITCHERY IRIS HARDCASE CLUTCH, CHEEK PALETTE,  OCRF CRYSTAL BAR RING, LIPSTICK IN SUNSET.

![Img](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/how-to-easy-holiday-style-and-beauty/IMG_9044.jpeg)

A silver foil tank is a fresh option for evening or dressed-up daytimes and it looks super modern worn with a bright orange or fuschia lip. A punchy pout is easiest way to dress up your makeup and you can throw just the lipstick in your clutch for instant high-impact touch ups.

The Witchery lipstick is so highly pigmented that you can slick it on straight from the tube, but if you want to get really precise then grab the creamy lip pencil and line the lips first. I lined my lips with Lip Crème Pencil in Sunset then coloured them in before applying the Lipstick in Sunset over the top to really pump up the colour and make it last. Add a pop of peach colour to your cheeks with the Cheek Palette – it has a luminiser so you can put the pop of colour on the apples of the cheeks and the shimmer shade along the tops of your cheekbones.
