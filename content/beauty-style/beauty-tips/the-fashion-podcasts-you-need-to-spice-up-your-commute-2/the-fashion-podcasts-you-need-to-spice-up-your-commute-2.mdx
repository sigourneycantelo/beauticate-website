## [**FASHION: NO FILTER**](https://itunes.apple.com/au/podcast/fashion-no-filter/id1183345770?mt=2)

There’s a high chance that the chic, effortless style of Parisiennes Camille Charrière and [Monica Ainley de la Villardière](https://www.instagram.com/monicaainleydlv/?hl=en) have been gracing your Insta feeds for the past few years (if not, follow them right now – you won’t regret it.) On each episode, they interview some of their most fashionable friends, from Chanel muse [Caroline de Maigret](https://www.instagram.com/carolinedemaigret/?hl=en), the Man Repeller’s Leandra Medine, and model Laura Bailey. They discuss the hard-hitting issues around fashion, like ethically-made clothing, the #MeToo movement and more – while keeping things light enough to not bring down the mood on your morning commute. Our fave part? When the girls share the Instagram accounts they’re loving, so you can add a little *je ne sais quoi *to your feed, too.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31973&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## [**FASHION UNZIPPED**](https://itunes.apple.com/au/podcast/fashion-unzipped/id1321368650?mt=2)

The podcast for all your fashion news needs. This weekly podcast from the UK Telegraph newspaper takes you through all the breaking fashion news from across the world – from red carpet events, to how to make the most of your Zara buys, and the key to buying *the *perfect pair of jeans. It’s fun, it’s informative and it will ensure you’re always up-to-date with the latest fashion news.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## **PARDON MY FRENCH WITH GARANCE DORÉ**

If you’re a fan of Garance Doré’s gorgeous illustrations and her blog, [Atelier Doré](http://www.atelierdore.com/), then we recommend you familiarise yourself with her podcast. She sits down for frank conversations with the likes of Christian Louboutin, makeup artist Violette and former French Vogue editor-in-chief, Joan Juliet Buck, about their lives and careers (plus a super fun quick-fire round at the end). These interviews are interspersed with “pocket” episodes – relaxed chats with her friends about the topics on her mind (think PDA, birth control and dating) that make you feel like you’re just spending time with your girlfriends. Plus, we’re totally obsessed with Garance’s French accent.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31977&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## [**AT HOME WITH&#8230;**](https://itunes.apple.com/au/podcast/at-home-with/id1228706016?mt=2)

Want to hear how Lancôme Creative Director Lisa Eldridge got her career start? Or how Madeleine Shaw first learnt to get that glow? This is the podcast for you. Youtubers and bloggers [The Anna Edit](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCth3krFQiaXe7vV-i79a9zg) and [Lily Pebbles](https://www.youtube.com/subscription_center?add_user=whatihearttoday) interview a myriad of creative people in their homes for a relaxed chat about anything and everything – all while allowing us a nosy into their interior decorating tastes. The first season was released last year, so start catching up now.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_empty_space][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Story by Tess Schlink[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
