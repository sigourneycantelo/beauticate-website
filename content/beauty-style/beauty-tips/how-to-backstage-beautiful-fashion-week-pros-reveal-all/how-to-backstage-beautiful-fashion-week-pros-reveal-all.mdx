### If you were wondering what must-have beauty buys should be on your wish list this season, wonder no longer.   This week during Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week Australia 2014/15, Beauticate was busy backstage speaking with those seriously in-the-know, finding out their top picks.  Here, the ultimate edit of beauty case essentials for the season ahead.

Backstage at CARLA ZAMPATTI

### Nigel Stanislaus, Make Up Director for Maybelline NY

 Nigel Stanislaus Backstage at Carla Zampatti

### This season’s must-have – Maybelline Colour Sensational Nude Lipstick in Honey Beige 728.

“Every girl needs this lipstick. Very natural yet glamourous at the same time, the soft nude tone makes this lipstick incredibly versatile. Not only does it look great on lips, but you can also wear it on the eyes or use it on the cheekbones to give contour. The ultimate beauty all-rounder.”

Backstage at ELLERY

### James Molloy, Make Up Director for MAC Cosmetics

 Backstage at Ellery MAC Cosmetics Crème Eye Liner

### This season’s must-have – MAC Cosmetics Crème Eye Liner in Black

“This super jet-black liner is one of those real old school liquid liners that gives you great precision, and it’s my pick for the coming season. The great thing about it is that when you apply it you have a few seconds of play time to smudge (if you want to), but then it completely sets and won’t budge all night.”

Note: Liner available exclusively at MAC Pro Cosmetics stores.

Backstage at CHRISTOPHER ESBER

### Alan White, Hair Director for GHD

 Backstage at Christopher Esber GHD Eclipse Styler

### This season’s must-have – GHD Eclipse Styler

“If there is one tool you want to invest in this season, it’s the Eclipse.  Built to beautify, it is ideal for achieving natural movement in the hair – a definite emerging trend we’ve seen this week.  The key is to keep the Eclipse moving as you work it through the hair in order to get that really natural finish. It’s the best styler on the market in terms of hair heat protection too, because of the quality of the plates and as it doesn’t go above 180 degrees (the point at where hair starts to break).”

Backstage at DION LEE II

### Jon Pulitano, Hair Director for Redken

 Backstage at Dion Lee II Backstage at Dion Lee II

### This season’s must-have – [Redken Rootful 06 Root Lifting Spray](http://www.redken.com.au/products/at-home/styling/volume/rootful-06)

“Rootful is one of the most versatile hair products going round and something every women needs.  It can be used to texturize hair, add volume, or to create that the real Australian look of ‘beachy’ waves.  Use it on damp hair or dry hair – take your pick.  It’s what I like to call a is a new age volumiser, meaning it doesn’t work on a sticky base but rather a more powdery one, which ultimately gives a better finish.”
