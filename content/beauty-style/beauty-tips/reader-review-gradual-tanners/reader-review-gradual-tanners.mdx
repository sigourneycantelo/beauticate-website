### “With gradual tanners you are mere days away from silky, spring appropriate skin that’s ready to bare,” says Julie. “Having a paler, sensitive Celtic complexion has led to some tanning disasters in the past, I’ve concluded that a progressive, layered approach with formulas that take my skin tone up a few notches each time perform best.” Here are her pick of the best.

## THE WASH & GLOW

### St. Tropez Gradual Tan In Shower

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26383&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]ST. TROPEZ GRADUAL TAN IN SHOWERv[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]“With an in-shower tanning experience St Tropez may just be changing the way we tan forever. Simply wash with your usual shower gel then turn the water off and get to slathering this rich, creamy formula on. Working on the principle that warm, wet skin is more absorbent this innovative formula is designed so the tanning agent, DHA won’t dissolve on contact with wet skin instead allowing the optimum amount of DHA to be absorbed in 3 minutes to kick-start a subtle colour change. The velvety formula lathers slightly as you massage it all over your wet skin, the scent is subtle – a very distant citrus freshness. Next the hard bit: standing still and braving the chilly morning air while I let the formula set to work. I find an electric toothbrush and a thorough teeth clean or a face massage with a cleansing brush usually distracts me. After waiting the full three minutes it is time to rinse. My skin is left feeling soft and comfortable thanks to sweet almond oil negating the need for moisturiser. There is no residual stickiness, no lingering scent and the colour that developed was extremely subtle which I built on throughout the week to a gentle golden-based glow.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
