### “Sometime too hot the eye of heaven shines”

BEAUTICATE LOVES HELENA CHRISTENSON BY XAVI GORDO FOR ELLE SPAIN

When the sun beats down and the pool seduces you in, do waterproof beauty the Helena way.

Charcoal smoky eyes with protected skin that survives a swim? Eyeko and La Roche-Posay deliver.

### “So long as men can breathe and eyes can see”

BEAUTICATE LOVES CLAUDIA SCHIFFER BY ELLEN VON UNWERTH FOR GUESS

### “But thy eternal summer shall not fade”

BEAUTICATE LOVES KATE MOSS BY MERT & MARCUS FOR VOGUE UK

For enduring summer skin, do sunkissed Kate with Stila's Stay All Day matte Bronzer.

Pair with a long-lasting velvet nude lip from Tom Ford. Offset matte summer beauty with a décolletage adorned in silver.
