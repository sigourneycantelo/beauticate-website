To keep with the breezy poolside vibe but still be in the Christmas spirit I’m going to wear this gorgeous red and white Maje dress with flat sandals (as I’ll running around after the kids!!) and a generous spritz of Les Eaux De Chanel Paris-Biarritz Eau De Toilette.
