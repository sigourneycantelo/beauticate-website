Mariah Carey has collaborated with makeup powerhouse MAC and we were lucky enough to try three of the products from the line before they hit stores. Spoiler: it’s everything you expect from one of pop’s biggest divas… glitter, shimmer and a whole lot of sparkle.

The ‘All I Want’ lipstick is a champagne shimmer frost that epitomises the '90s pop princess, and is encased in a silver glitter bullet, of course.

For diva eyes, it's onto the ‘It’s Everything’ eye shadow quad complete with a muted black, lustre grey, a pearly plum and silver shimmer. It’s the ultimate in Saturday night glam.

The Extra Dimension Skin Finish in My Mimi is gold pressed powder (emblazoned with a print of MC's face, no less) that will give you serious shimmer. We’re predicting this will sell out fast for the party season.

The collection goes on sale in free standing MAC stores on Dec 26.
