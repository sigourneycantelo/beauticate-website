Pat McGrath, launched her Dark Star 006 kit and the line is already sold out. The legendary makeup artist created the limited-edition Dark Star 006 which consists of three eye kits: Dark Matter, UltraSuede Brown, and UltraViolet Blue. The eye kits are loaded with intense, sparkly pigments. Kim Kardashian West is the #McGrathMuse and has been loyally promoting the unique new product.

[DarkStar006 available at PatMcGrath.com #patmcgrathlabs006 @patmcgrathreal @makeupbymario @chrisappleton1](https://www.instagram.com/p/BSgsri8F8_Z/)

A post shared by Kim Kardashian West (@kimkardashian) on Apr 5, 2017 at 9:47am PDT

In a press release, Pat McGrath describes that “The Dark Star 006 kit is all about the infinite blackness of outer space.” This perfectly describes the galactic vibes the kit provides. The base kit, Dark Matter, includes the Dark Matter pigment, Astral White pigment, Black Smudgeliner eye kohl, the Cyber Clear eye gloss, the Mercury pigment and a new blending brush.

 Image of Dark Matter kit provided by PatMcgrath.com

The UltraSuede Brown kit includes all the products in the Dark Matter base kit plus the extra eyeshadow pigment, UltraSuede Brown.

The UltraViolet Blue kit also includes all the products from the Dark Matter base kit plus the extra eyeshadow pigment, UltraViolet Blue.
