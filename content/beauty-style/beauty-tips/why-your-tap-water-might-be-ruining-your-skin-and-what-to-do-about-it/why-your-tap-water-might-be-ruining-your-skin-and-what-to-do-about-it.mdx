## What’s Hiding in Your Tap Water?

Most people assume tap water is clean enough—after all, it’s been treated. But that doesn’t mean it’s free of skin-disrupting contaminants.

- Chlorine – Added to disinfect water, but strips skin of natural oils, leading to dryness and irritation.
- Calcium & Magnesium – Found in hard water, these minerals clog pores, trigger breakouts, and worsen rosacea and eczema.
- Heavy Metals (Lead, Copper, Iron) – Can cause oxidative stress, leading to redness, sensitivity, and premature aging.
- Microplastics & Bacteria – Yes, really. These can build up on your skin, clogging pores and dulling your complexion.

My water in my home town of Sydney’s, like many major cities worldwide (including London, Melbourne, and parts of the US), contains trace levels of contaminants that—while deemed ‘safe’ for drinking—aren’t necessarily ideal for your skin.
