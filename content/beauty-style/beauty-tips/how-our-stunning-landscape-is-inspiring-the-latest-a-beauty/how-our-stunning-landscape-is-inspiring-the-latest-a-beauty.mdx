## OCEAN-INSPIRED OFFERINGS

All things marine have been a huge trend in worldwide beauty for the last couple of years, so with world-class waters at our doorstep, it stands to reason that Aussie brands are offering up some of the best sea-inspired products.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43244&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## A NEW TAKE ON SKINCARE SLUGGING

After spending some time living on an island in the pacific, Baché de Mar’s founder decided to harness the powers of sea cucumbers (which boast marine collagen and antioxidants) for skincare, and created her Sea Cucumber Collagen Regeneration Serum which aims to both plump and hydrate.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43617&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]

## THE (ALGAE) BLOOM OF YOUTH

A recent addition to its range is the [3 Step Skin Routine](https://ausnz.vidaglow.com/collections/skincare/products/3-step-skin-routine?variant=15815465500775), consisting of cleanser, vitamin C serum and moisturiser, which are all enriched with red algae marine collagen to improve cell turnover.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43468&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## WAVEMAKERS

It’s not only skincare brands looking to our oceans. Aussie haircare brand Bondi Boost now offers a texture spray featuring Australian sea salt – just spray into damp roots for added lift, and all over for definition of curls and waves. In keeping with the salty theme, Australian perfumers Asriq have released [Bondi](https://www.asriq.com.au/collections/frontpage/products/bondi-body-oil), a body oil fragranced with notes of sea salt and heady warm weather florals that hydrates and protects parched summer skin thanks to its botanical ingredients.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43471&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## FROM BEACH TO BAY TO BUSH

One of the elements that makes our beaches so uniquely beautiful is that they aren't necessarily standalone eco-systems, but instead are often connected to all the wonders of fauna and flora the Aussie bush has to offer.  This fact is not lost on Minenssey, whose Flannel Flower Absolute draws its hydrating effects from its namesake plant, which seeks out sufficient moisture to grow anywhere from coastal regions to dry forests, and can withstand plenty of our harsh sun exposure.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43616&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]

## JUST DESERTS

Ok so yes, the desert is not the coast, but they both contain sand and, more relevant to our purposes, offer up indigenous ingredients that pack a significant skincare punch. Mere Lifestyle, a brand new launch this past December, utilises native botanicals, with its [Cellular Balance Activated Cleansing Gel](https://mereskincare.com/products/cellular-balance-activated-cleansing-gel) incorporating Mere’s Desert Harvest Complex. This contains quandong, desert lime and wattle seed which boast major antioxidant benefits, helping both to protect and brighten. Organic Australian brand Raww also relies on desert fruits as key ingredients in their skincare range. Wildberry Harvest, a complex found in their [Super Hydrate-ME Night Cream](https://www.rawwcosmetics.com/super-hydrate-me-night-cream), is made up of three berries sourced from the outback for their outstanding abilities to hydrate.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="43618&#8243; img_size="full" css_animation="fadeInUp"][vc_column_text]
