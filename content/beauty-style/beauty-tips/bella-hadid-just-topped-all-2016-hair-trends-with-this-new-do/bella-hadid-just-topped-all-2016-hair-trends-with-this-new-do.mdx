Although she pulls off brunette convincingly, it is not her natural colour. Bella Hadid is a beachy blonde. And now, she's returned to her roots, literally. On her latest magazine cover, for PAPER magazine, the the model has ditched her signature shade to debut (much!) lighter tresses.

When PAPER announced their Winter ’16 issue, they Instagrammed their cover image, featuring a platinum Bella, including brows. Along with the new ‘do, the Victoria Secret model is also sporting a neon-green smoky eye, and a black lipstick, filled with rainbow colours and an oil-slick finish that only a Hadid could pull off. She bares a great resemblance to her blonde mother and sister, Yolanda and Gigi Hadid, in the images.
