## NEW PRODS ON THE BLOCK

While some of Yolanda’s faves have stood the test of time, there are some newcomers that have already won their way into her kit. “L’Oréal has a brand new mascara called Paradise that I love,” says Yolanda. “It’s amazing – it gives thick, black, lashes and is perfect in just two coats.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## PERFECT POLISH

While some cheaper varnishes just dish up a range of drab and unappealing shades, Yolanda has found one with killer colours to match its fab price point. “Revlon have amazing nail polishes. ## EYE TO EYE

You don’t need to splash out on an expensive eyeliner to ensure you’re getting a quality product. Yolanda rates this budget buy as highly as some of its pricier peers – if not higher. “The Covergirl Perfect Blend Eye Pencil is a good one – it’s soft and it comes with a smudger, which some budget brands don’t have,” she explains. “It’s just as good as an expensive brand, but cheaper.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row]
