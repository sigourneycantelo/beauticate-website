Lucy Hale has been giving us major hair envy all year round: from honey-painted strands to her trendy lob and bangs, we’ve been fixated over the actress' ever-changing tresses.

Now, the *Pretty Little Liars* star has gone back to her darker roots and opted for a shorter ‘do. Lucy's hairstylist, Kristin Ess, revealed that the dramatic transformation is for a new upcoming role – no word yet though on what the new gig is.

[? I go back to blaaaaack. Thank you @kristin_ess for taking me back to my true self ??](https://www.instagram.com/p/BNlDIGWB2p1/)

A photo posted by Lucy Hale (@lucyhale) on Dec 3, 2016 at 6:43pm PST

As you've probably noticed, the actress is a big fan of switching things up when it comes to her cut and colour – and never sticks with the same style for too long. So we're predicting that a return to her lighter self will be on the cards soon.
