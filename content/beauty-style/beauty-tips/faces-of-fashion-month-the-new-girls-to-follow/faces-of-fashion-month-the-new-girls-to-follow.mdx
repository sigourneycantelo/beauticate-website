Along with the upcoming styles for Autumn, fashion month exposed a select amount of new millennial models who are becoming extreme fixations in the social media realm. While many designers showcased new faces this season, no show had as much millennial power as Dolce and Gabbana’s. The Fall 2017 show at Milan Fashion Week featured the latest influential bloggers and celebrity royalty along with professional models. It was a spectacle of the latest “It” girls congregated on one, glamorous runway. With their uniquely striking looks and many of them hailing from Hollywood nobility, it is as if it was their destiny to represent this new age of millennial models.

[@dolcegabbana #DGMILLENNIALS ??](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ_Jo_TgDod/)

A post shared by Sailor Brinkley Cook (@sailorbrinkleycook) on Feb 26, 2017 at 11:34am PST

If Sailor Brinkley Cook's classic good looks seem familiar it's because she is an almost spitting image of her mother, Christie Brinkley. The eighteen-year-old has already been featured in sports illustrated and is making a name for herself in the fashion world. Sailor also isn't shy to stand up to Instagram body shamers. After receiving backlash for posting a Sports Illustrated Photo to her account, she proudly stated, "I don't need to be a size 0 to believe in myself." With her strong voice and influential media presence, Sailor's goal is to empower young women to embrace and love every part of their body.

[Thank you @dolcegabbana for making my dreams come true today ??❤ I love being a part of the #DGFamily #MFW #Milan](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ-10SnAxyc/)

A post shared by Corinne Foxx (@corinnefoxx) on Feb 26, 2017 at 8:41am PST

Last year, Corinne Foxx was featured as Miss Golden Globe 2016 where she proudly shared the same stage where her father, Jamie Foxx, has been honored. Though she is Hollywood royalty, Corinne Foxx is determined to make her own name for herself in film and fashion.

[Oh.Em.Gee!!! Thanks so much for letting me walk on the show @dolcegabbana ? Photo by @fashiontomax #DGRinascimento #DGmillennials #DGFW18 #MFW](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ-x1CQjPzx/)

A post shared by Aimee Song (@songofstyle) on Feb 26, 2017 at 8:06am PST

Aimee Song has already reached a considerable amount of success during her time as a fashion and interior design blogger. Her style is effortless, chic, and unique. Thanks to her many fashion connections, her Instagram often features front row peeks at the latest fashion shows.

[thank you Domenico & Stefano. it was a dream & such an honor to have walked in the @dolcegabbana show today ?](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ-tHVIjAq2/)

A post shared by madison beer (@madisonbeer) on Feb 26, 2017 at 7:25am PST

Madison Beer found fame after Justin Bieber tweeted a link to her cover of "At Last" featured on her YouTube channel. Since then, Beer has released singles such as "Valentine" and "Unbreakable." Her social media reflects her "cool girl" style.

[thank you so much @dolcegabbana for having me in your beautiful show and making this dream come true! I was blown away by this collection xo #dolcegabbana #mfw](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQ-3HjzhHIh/)

A post shared by Delilah Belle (@delilahbelle) on Feb 26, 2017 at 8:53am PST

Delilah Belle is the daughter of actor Harry Hamlin and Lisa Rinna. The model was discovered through her Instagram account by Elite Modeling Agency. Belle made her true fashion debut after walking in the Tommy X Gigi Show last Septemeber. Since then she has been compared to such as Gigi Hadid and Hailey Baldwin and speculated to be the next top model.

Their perfectly curated Instagram feeds have attracted an ample amount of followers proving that these millennial style icons are unique influencers to watch.
