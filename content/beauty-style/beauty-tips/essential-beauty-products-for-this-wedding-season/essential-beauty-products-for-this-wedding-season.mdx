## WHAT TO BUY AT MECCA:

**Bare skincare basics**

Any special occasion beauty refresh should begin with a little spring skin cleaning. In the weeks leading up to the big event, commit to regular exfoliation.

To ensure you don’t irritate your precious dermis, skip traditional scrubs and opt for gentle AHA or lactic acids instead.[ Dr Dennis Gross Universal Daily Pee](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/dr-dennis-gross/alpha-beta-universal-peel-daily-V-052381/)l makes it super simple.

The AHA/BHA-infused wipes are the ideal formulation for daily use, so you don’t waste time thinking about whether you’re exfoliating too much or not enough.

![9a8f9992439a7e71f9d22bc2cadacf1f](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/essential-beauty-products-for-this-wedding-season/9a8f9992439a7e71f9d22bc2cadacf1f.jpg)


Simply swipe on the peel pad each night and marvel as it sloughs off dull and dirty skin cells.

The second wipe neutralises the acid to prevent any irritation and ensures the skin is a receptive canvas for further actives

The next step after prep is always hydration. To ensure your skin is swimming in moisture, minus the grease or heaviness, add a hyaluronic acid serum to your daily routine.

It’s the most unassuming of the active world and slips neatly into any skincare routine without upsetting other products.

The [Mecca Cosmetica Hyaluronic Acid Hydration serum](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/mecca-cosmetica/hyaluronic-acid-hydration-serum-I-050460/) is an accessibly priced option that ticks the hydration box quite nicely with 1.06% hyaluronic acid and hyaluronate along with niacinamide and aloe vera, it’s also soothing and brightening.

**Make glow a priority**

For a high-wattage gleam that emanates from within, consider the transformative power of LED light therapy.

The [Dr Gross Spectralite Faceware Pro](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/dr-dennis-gross/drx-spectralite-faceware-pro-I-034280/) offers dual settings-red light to invigorate collagen production and blue light to combat breakout-inducing bacteria: essential when you’re wedding-bound.

## AESOP’S MOST WANTED

On the ground floor amid the busy thoroughfare of beauty and style is [The Strand’](https://www.strandarcade.com.au/stores/aesop)s most photographed and aesthetically pleasing store: Aesop.

Their minimalist packaging and deliciously botanical-scented skincare products are beauty essentials, perfect for the betrothed and their guests alike.

It’s also a mark of beauty that the products are unisex and genderless, making them ideal gifts for bridesmaids and kitchen teas.

![Screen shot 2023 09 14 at 11.29.48 pm](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/essential-beauty-products-for-this-wedding-season/Screen-Shot-2023-09-14-at-11.29.48-pm.jpg)


Our picks? Aesop’s latest fragrance, Ouranon, sparkles with beauty notes of citrus Petitgrain and warm, spicy aromatics of chamomile and frankincense over an earthy base of myrrh, patchouli, and Tonka.

In a word: delicious. Or for something embodying the essence of beauty in a different way, try the beautiful  Kagerou Aromatique Incense with the chic Bronze Incense Holder designed for Aesop by Vogel Studio.

## WHAT TO SMELL AT CREED

A few doors down from Aesop in the beauty quarter is [Creed](https://www.strandarcade.com.au/stores/creed).

![Thestrandarcade 12](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/essential-beauty-products-for-this-wedding-season/TheStrandArcade-12-1.jpg)


A true heritage brand. Founded in England in 1760, the House of Creed has a storied history of enhancing beauty, supplying the English Court with custom-tailored clothing, scented leather gloves, and commissioned fragrances.

It's well worth stopping in and immersing yourself in the beauty of their iconic scents – make sure to experience Virgin Island Water, Fleurissimo (Grace Kelly wore it on her wedding day), and the cult men’s scent Aventus.

For a beauty refresh this spring, explore a new take on the classic Aventus Cologne –it’s a sophisticated yet fresh blend that beautifies with notes of mandarin, pink peppercorn, and vetiver, and a smooth leathery balsamic base of styrax, birch, musk, and Tonka.

The latest Creed scent for her, Spring Flower, is a beauty must-try, mesmerizing with notes of jasmine and white flowers over a sensual ambergris and musk base, it perfectly encapsulates the essence of blooming romance and beauty.

## STOCK UP ON SANTA MARIA NOVELLA

Finally, you can’t go to The Strand without visiting the Officina Profumo-Farmaceutica di Santa Maria Novella.

This beauty institution was founded in 1221 by Dominican friars and is considered the oldest pharmacy in the world.

Their products are timeless and beautiful, and they will gain serious credibility from any beauty aficionado.

The vintage packaging also looks beautiful atop one's bathroom counter.

Our picks? The bath and body ranges are the ultimate in romance. Perfectly in keeping with our Spring theme, add the Rosa Gardenia Bath Gel and Fluid Cream to your collection.

These luxurious picks evoke the freshness of a garden in full bloom, enveloping you in a sensory experience that complements the beauty of the season.
