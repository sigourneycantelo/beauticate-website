***Gifts That Feel Considered, Even If You’re Buying Them This Week***

Curated by the Beauticate team, this is a calm edit of last-minute Christmas gifts chosen for their quality, thoughtfulness and ease. Nothing frantic. Nothing obvious. Just pieces that feel considered, even if they’re bought at the last minute.

Because good taste isn’t about timing – it’s about intention.

## [Sigourney's edit | ](https://www.beauticate.com/about/)

For me, the best gifts are the ones that create a feeling the moment you unwrap them. [**Goldfield & Banks’ Silky Woods**](https://www.goldfieldandbanks.com/products/silky-woods) is exactly that – rich, enveloping and unmistakably Australian, with a depth that feels luxurious without ever being overpowering. I love pairing it with [**Maison Louis Marie’s Fleur de Vanille Hair & Body Mist**](https://bit.ly/3Lea9tw), a softer, comforting scent that works beautifully for everyday wear and quiet moments at home. And for a little extra glow, [**Gisou’s Glow All The Way Set**](https://www.mecca.com/en-au/gisou/glow-all-the-way-set-I-078914/) delivers exactly what it promises – luminous hair and skin that feel polished, joyful and effortlessly put together.

***Quietly luxurious, beautifully considered.***

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are some last-minute Christmas gift ideas that feel considered?**
A: The Beauticate team recommends fragrance (Goldfield & Banks Silky Woods, Bleu de Chanel), skincare (Reome Active Recovery Broth, Kate Somerville Goat Milk Stressed Skin Saviors), and beauty sets (Charlotte Tilbury Hollywood Superstar Glow Kit, Gisou Glow All The Way Set). The principle across all picks is quality and intention over novelty.

**Q: What Christmas gifts does Sigourney recommend from the guide?**
A: Sigourney recommends Goldfield & Banks Silky Woods EDP, Maison Louis Marie Fleur de Vanille Hair and Body Mist, and the Gisou Glow All The Way Set. Her selection leans towards fragrance and luminosity - gifts that create a feeling the moment you unwrap them.

**Q: What is a good Christmas gift for someone who loves home fragrance?**
A: Jayde's pick is Saarde's Saint-Raphael Room Spray, which she describes as turning any space into a calm, welcoming place. Good ambience never goes out of style, and a room spray is a thoughtful, unexpected alternative to another candle.
