faqs:
  - question: "What are the best anti-aging serums for mature skin?"
    answer: "Vitamin C serums like SkinCeuticals C E Ferulic are considered gold standard for anti-aging, working to brighten and protect against environmental damage. Hydrating serums with B vitamins and brightening formulations help plump the skin and reduce the appearance of fine lines. The key is layering complementary serums that address your specific concerns, whether that's hydration, luminosity, or firmness."
  - question: "How often should I use anti-aging serums in my routine?"
    answer: "Most anti-aging serums work best when incorporated into both your morning and evening skincare routines. Start with once daily if you have sensitive skin, then gradually increase to twice daily as your skin builds tolerance. Consistency is more important than frequency-using quality serums daily will deliver better results than sporadic application of multiple products."
  - question: "Can you use multiple serums together in an anti-aging routine?"
    answer: "Yes, layering serums is an effective strategy when done correctly-the key is applying them from lightest to heaviest texture. A hydrating serum followed by a brightening serum allows each product to penetrate and work synergistically. However, it's important to introduce new serums gradually and patch test first to ensure your skin tolerates the combination without irritation."
  - question: "What ingredients should I look for in anti-aging skincare?"
    answer: "Vitamin C, B vitamins, ferulic acid, and hyaluronic acid are powerhouse anti-aging ingredients that address fine lines, brightness, and hydration. These actives work to boost collagen production, protect against free radical damage, and maintain skin plumpness. Look for stabilised formulations and professional-grade products that deliver these ingredients in effective concentrations."
