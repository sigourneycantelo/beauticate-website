And one way she achieves this is by applying a classic red lip colour straight from her own beauty line. For special occasions, Olivia's beloved bronzed smokey eye is bolder and punctuated by smouldering dark plum eye shadow – which you can recreate with her[ Regalia eyeshadow palette](https://oliviapalermo.com/products/eyeshadow-palette-in-regalia). To achieve Olivia's signature glossy locks, [HH Simonsen's moisturising shampoo](https://www.i-glamour.com/Product/700405/hh-simonsen-moisture-shampoo) and [conditioner](https://www.i-glamour.com/Product/700406/hh-simonsen-moisture-conditioner) are a must. They are the holy-grail duo to salvage damaged hair and banish frizziness/flyaways – without weighing your hair down. But how do we create her go-to romantic curls? The [Silver Bullet Duette Hair Straightener Duo](https://silverbullethair.com.au/duette-hair-straightener-duo/) is perfect for producing soft, natural looking curls that always look shiny and bodacious. The pack contains both a full-size ceramic straightener and a travel-size version, which is handy because different parts of your hair will often require different sized tools. In a big to amp up the festive spirit, I will sashay around in this [pussy-bow silk blouse from Balmain](https://bit.ly/3VsNqdn) – it embodies Olivia's  sophistication to a tee and looks regal enough to belong in a royal portrait! To polish up the look, I will slip on this crossover triple band ring from Pandora – the perfect dainty touch to a vibrant outfit.

And finally, Olivia is actually a self-confessed homebody. So I'm going to enjoy a delicious home-cooked meal, cuddle with my loved ones, watch a cheery Christmas movie and look chic whilst doing it. Who says you can't be extra whilst lounging around at home? ## *MIKAELA LEZCANO DE ORLÉANS, BEAUTY ASSISTANT – JANE BIRKIN *

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="60221&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Jane Birkin to me represents the ultimate cool girl. Her effortless yet chic style has inspired my looks for this summer. Birkin embodies the less is more approach to fashion and beauty, which is precisely what I need for a holiday in the heat. How can I achieve this? Easy! With an uncomplicated skincare routine that deeply hydrates and protects my skin from the sun, embracing my natural hair texture and packing clothing staples that can be used in different ways. My main skincare staple this summer is the[ Tropicology Avocado Face Oil](https://www.tropicology.com.au/products/pure-avocado-face-oil), the natural collagen-stimulating formula containing over 11 vitamins that is ideal for hydrating and reducing signs of ageing which are my main concerns when spending extended periods of time in the sun. Dry hair is another worry of mine, salt water and the sun can damage the hair even causing breakage, to avoid this I would recommend using the Kérastase Elixir Ultime, it makes your hair feel silky smooth without making it oily. Jane’s iconic wicker basket represents the importance of a good handbag, therefore this summer I have chosen the [Piper Miami Tote Bag](https://www.myer.com.au/p/piper-miami-tote-bag-in-coral?istCompanyId=84873db0-394f-434b-8958-29526fe5f03c&istFeedId=3dd6959f-3482-45a5-8a47-313fef9bbe16&istItemId=rtwilqplx&istBid=t&gclid=Cj0KCQiA1ZGcBhCoARIsAGQ0kkrX45ZsMbYx2IvCuyH3Q6ZcHL-_SoytKhFp0hUR027PdHPEyCGRum4aAtKLEALw_wcB&gclsrc=aw.ds) in Coral which will always stand out and elevate my beach looks. Sunnies are a must for your Christmas holidays and I cannot recommend Luv Lou enough, their classic and feminine eyewear is perfect for that effortless look. Clothes-wise I will be channelling my inner 70’s Jane by opting for [wide-legged flowing pants](https://alemais.com/collections/collection-08-resort-23/products/soleil-pant) and quality [basics](https://venroy.com.au/products/womens-knitted-ribbed-tube-top-off-white).
