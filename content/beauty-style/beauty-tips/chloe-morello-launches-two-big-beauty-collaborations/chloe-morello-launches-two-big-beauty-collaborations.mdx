In exciting beauty news, YouTube sensation Chloe Morello has joined forces with two high-profile beauty brands to bring you an epic collection of her makeup must-haves this party season.

Chloe's latest collaboration with [Ciaté London](http://us.ciatelondon.com/) – [Chloe Morello Beauty Haul](http://us.ciatelondon.com/products/chloe-morello-beauty-haul) includes a 15 pan eyeshadow palette that features an array of shades chosen by Chloe herself. A purchase of the palette, encased in a navy travel pouch, includes a [Wonderwand Mascara](http://us.ciatelondon.com/products/wonderwand-mascara) and a mini-Liquid Velvet in the shade of Pin Up.

The beauty maven has also partnered with [Revlon](http://www.revlon.com/) to launch the Revlon X Chloe Morello Contour Kit. The kit contains four skin-toned powders that range from a yellow-based beige (Banana) to a softer illuminator (Glow). Complementing these shades are a shimmery tan (Bronze) and a dark matte brown. It's contouring without the fuss.

Chloe is in a line of celebs, bloggers and vloggers who have done a cosmetics collab this year. Australian pop princess [Samantha Jade](https://www.beauticate.com/who/samantha-jade-singer) launched a makeup range with Models Prefer earlier this month. Meanwhile, beauty vlogger, [Lauren Curtis](https://www.beauticate.com/who/lauren-curtis-beauty-vlogger) continues to work with David Jones on their spectacular Beauty Tribe Event for the second year in a row.
