## Help At Hand

If your job requires sitting at a computer typing the day away (ditto), it’s important to keep your hands in good nick and to thank them for all the hard work they do. And especially if your workplace blasts the air conditioning or heating 24/7, you’ve probably been a victim to dry and cracked skin – not ideal. A nourishing hand cream like The Body Shop's Hemp Hand Protector is just the ticket you need.

## Great Hair Day

Scünci Dent-Free Spirals

Sometimes getting the job done requires pulling your hair out of your face and locking into focus mode. But if you’re like us and would rather die than ruin a great hair day with a standard hair tie, a fab alternative are Scünci Dent-Free Spirals – they don't leave those pesky dents in your strands, meaning the 'do you worked hard to achieve looks as good as when you first styled it.
