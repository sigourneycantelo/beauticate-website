When thinking of class and timeless style, it's hard to go past Jackie Kennedy? Natalie Portman perfectly embodied Jackie’s elegance in this compelling film, Jackie, about one of the most renowned First Ladies in history. From her full brows to her pink lipsticks, Natalie sported Dior products in her latest film, which is no surprise as Natalie is a famous face of Dior. Her make-up artist for the film, Miwoo Kim, used Dior Powder Brow Pencil in “Noir” to create Jackie’s signature eyebrow look. Kim also used shades of pink from Dior’s Rouge lipstick line like “Premiere,” “Rose Baiser,” and “Osee”.

Best Actress in a Leading Role winter Emma Stone shines in her role in La La Land, one of the most buzzed about movies of the year. In this film, Emma rocked a bold lip and beautiful, glowing skin. Emma’s bold red lip is such a classic look, and definitely one that radiates confidence. This statement lip doesn’t require much support, it looked just right with Emma’s minimal face and eye make-up in the film.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What makeup did Natalie Portman wear in the film Jackie?**
A: Portman's makeup artist Miwoo Kim used Dior products throughout the film, including the Dior Powder Brow Pencil in "Noir" for Jackie Kennedy's signature brows, and pink shades from Dior's Rouge lipstick line such as "Premiere," "Rose Baiser," and "Osee."

**Q: What was Emma Stone's beauty look in La La Land?**
A: Emma Stone wore a bold red lip paired with minimal eye makeup and glowing skin in La La Land. The classic red lip was the statement piece, requiring little else to complement it.

**Q: What lip colour did Ruth Negga wear in the film Loving?**
A: Ruth Negga wore a plum-coloured lip stain in Loving that complemented her skin tone and was noted as a great shade for the autumn and winter seasons.
