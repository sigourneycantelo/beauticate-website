### 4. The smudgy eye

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27271&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]When Alex rocked her way on to our screens, her punk-princess edge offered a refreshing point of difference. Although a little heavy handed for day-to-day wear, a little smudge and smolder made her blue eyes positively pop.

Opt for a dark, highly pigmented eye pencil like M.A.C Kohl Power Eye Liner in Feline and line the lower lids before smudging back with your fingertip as desired. Hay presto! Say hello to your inner rock goddess.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27273&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 5. The timeless pixie cut

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27275&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]While we may not have been rooting for Anna and Seth (Team Summer until death) we can appreciate a classic pixie cut when we see one. Anna’s Audrey-esque quirk and versatile style had us all seriously contemplating the chop.

The secret to versatile pixie cut lies in its texture. Invest in a light clay-based texturizer like [Redken Rough Clay 2.0 Texturizer](http://www.redken.com.au/products/at-home/styling/texture/rough-clay-20), and use sparingly to add dimension to your look. Short hair can still mean endless styling possibilities.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27276&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
