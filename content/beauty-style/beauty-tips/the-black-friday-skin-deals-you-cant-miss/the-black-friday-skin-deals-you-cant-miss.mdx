## Why the Micro-Infusion System Is a Game-Changer

The other genius product in this bundle is the Qure Micro-Infusion System. This  DIY dermal stamp system doesn’t just apply serums topically—it ensures they penetrate deeply and effectively. You screw your serum onto a single use head that is covered in ultra-fine, gold-plated needles and then literally stamp your skin all over. This creates micro-channels in the skin to allow potent serums to be absorbed much deeper than traditional application methods. The process is a little prickly but not painful, and since  it creates a little trauma to the skin which also boosts the skin’s natural healing process and boosts collagen production. Stamping is preferable to dermarolling because it doesn’t drag the skin, and since the heads are single-used and disposable, it’s a safe and hygienic way. I felt good knowing that it has the tick of approval of some of the most high profile dermatologists including Dr. Shah and Dr. Maxfield. It’s honestly my favourite quick fix before big events or when my skin has been under stress because even after a single treatment at night, the next morning I look like I’ve had 8 hours sleep even when I’ve been burning the candle. [/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image="64421&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]
