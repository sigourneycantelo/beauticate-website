## THE DATE NIGHT SCENT

### B. Balenciaga Skin

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="36847&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]A slightly more sensual version of the original, B. Balenciaga Skin is a woody floral that is as elegant as it is seductive. With luring notes of peony and lily of the valley, it dries down, revealing just enough spice to fit the wintery bill.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## THE WEEKEND WONDER

### [Daisy Dream Blush by Marc Jacobs](http://www.myer.com.au/shop/mystore/fragrances/marc-jacobs-daisy-dream-blush-edt-limited-edition)

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="36848&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

Another fabulously fresh edition to the Daisy family comes [Daisy Dream Blush](http://www.myer.com.au/shop/mystore/fragrances/marc-jacobs-daisy-dream-blush-edt-limited-edition). True to the daisy repertoire, this airy floral is a lush mix of violet, rose and lily of the valley. And the sheer pink bottle awash with daisies is divine. Well, if you’re onto a good thing…

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## THE HEAD TURNER

### Bamboo by Gucci

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="36849&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

Gucci scents are best known to pack a powerful but sophisticated punch, and Bamboo is no exception. A pick for the refined lady who enjoys life in the luxe lane, this scent marries soft floral notes with a woody base of sandalwood – modern and chic, we’re predicting cult status.

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## THE WORKDAY CLASSIC

### Cotton by Marc Jacobs

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="36850&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

Sometimes simple is best, as is the case with Marc Jacobs Cotton. Light, fresh and clean, Cotton is for the minimalists among us (you know the type, impeccably ironed shirts and an organised handbag – they apparently DO exist). Perfect for adding an air of sophistication to an otherwise dreary day in the office…

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## THE VINTAGE GLAMOUR

### Narciso Rodriguez Poudree *Eau De Parfum*

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="36851&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center"][/vc_column][vc_column width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text]

Powdery, musky and with a top note of white floral, Poudree is another incarnation of Narciso's penchant for creating quintessential girly scents. Best paired with your most fabulous cream trench, tousled curls and a pastel mani (high tea optional).
