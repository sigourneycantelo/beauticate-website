Victoria Secret model Irina Shayk is reportedly expecting her first child with actor boyfriend, Bradley Cooper. Just hours after she strutted down the VS catwalk in Paris, the Russian-born beauty's baby news broke on the internet.

Although the private couple, who have been dating since early 2015, have yet to release an official statement, rumours she is in her second trimester (making her at least 13 weeks pregnant) have been fuelled by a retrospective look at her attire on the runway – lots of jackets and cover-ups for a notoriously skin baring show.

We'll hold out for an official nod from the couple's rep before we start face-blending predictions of what will be a ridiculously good-looking offspring… But, if it is true, we have a nifty little list of [essentials](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/the-best-baby-products-sigourneys-edit?rq=Essential) she might want to cast her eye over…
