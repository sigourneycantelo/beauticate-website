### 1. Jo Malone London Christmas Cracker

In my fantasy, my table is dressed with these chic Jo Malone London crackers. What decadence! The cute little gift box contains three of most celebration-worthy Jo scents – Lime Basil & Mandarin Cologne (which is fresh, unisex and perfect for summer), Blackberry & Bay Body & Hand Wash (a mouth watering combo) and Nectarine Blossom & Honey Body Crème which is so pretty, floral and fragrant I often wear it instead of perfume when it’s hot.

JO MALONE CHRISTMAS CRACKER

### 2. Alice + Olivia Cotton Poplin Mini Dress

A simple white dress is perfection for Christmas day – it’s cool, fresh and functional, without being overdressed. (Just be careful with the cranberry sauce) I love this embroidered shift – white cotton is given a lift with a little fabric detailing and I love all the folky interpretations of white this season. Look for dresses with lace, embroidery, crochet or broderie anglaise to make them feel that little bit more festive.

![Wish](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/7-christmas-essentials-sigourneys-edit/wish.jpeg)


### 3. Tom Ford Lip Colour in Cherry Lush

In my opinion, nothing says ‘festive’ quite like a red lip. It’s the ultimate nod to glamour and sophistication and the easiest way to instantly feel dressed up. I also think that donning a red lip for a party or dinner is a sign of respect. It shows you’ve made an effort and believe that your host is worthy of you putting your finest face forward. Tom Ford lipsticks are seriously glamorous bullets to pull out of your clutch.

TOM FORD LIP COLOUR IN CHERRY LUSH

JO MALONE PINE & EUCALYPTUS HOME CANDLE

### 4. Jo Malone London Pine & Eucalyptus Home Candle

Christmas candles are a wonderful way to subtly fill your home with festive spirit. This candle is so incredible – it emanates the fresh scent of pine needles and menthol-fresh eucalypts even when it’s not lit.

### 5. Ferragamo Custom Varinas

When I’m off beauty-duty (and on baby duty) I live in flats, so I was so excited when I was told I could choose my own colour combo of the classic Ferragamo Varinas. The grosgrain ribbon finishes it beautifully and I chose a pastel pink pair with a rose gold bow, but this patent red pair would make a striking statement with an all white ensemble on Christmas day.

![Malone](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/7-christmas-essentials-sigourneys-edit/malone.jpeg)


FERRAGAMO VARINAS

MY RED LIPPY HAT BY SARAH CURTIS PANAMA

### 6. My Red Lippy hat by Sarah Curtis Panama

Hats are probably one of the most important parts of your beauty routine as they protect you even more than sunscreen can. I love the story behind these hats – Sarah Curtis discovered she had melanoma when she was pregnant with her second son. When the hunt for a stylish hat with a big enough brim proved fruitless she launched her own panamas with a 12-14cm brim that promises to keep pesky UV rays at bay.

### 7.  MAC Keepsakes Smoky Face Palette

With three smokey eye essential shades, a cream colour base and powder along with two celebratory lip shades this is the go-anywhere do-anything palette. Love the pearly white finish and cameo feature on the front.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why does Sigourney recommend a red lip for Christmas?**
A: She considers a red lip the ultimate sign of festive glamour and a gesture of respect toward your host, showing you have made a real effort.

**Q: What scents are in the Jo Malone Christmas Cracker?**
A: The gift box contains Lime Basil and Mandarin Cologne, Blackberry and Bay Body and Hand Wash, and Nectarine Blossom and Honey Body Crème.

**Q: What makes the Sarah Curtis Panama hat special?**
A: The hats were designed after founder Sarah Curtis was diagnosed with melanoma during pregnancy. They feature a 12-14cm brim specifically sized to block UV rays more effectively than sunscreen alone.

**Q: When is Jo Malone London offering complimentary calligraphy on gifts?**
A: Complimentary calligraphy is available at the David Jones boutiques in Sydney and Melbourne from Thursday 18 to Saturday 20 December 2014, between 1pm and 4pm.
