The Victoria's Secret Angels might be renowned for their iconic bouncy hairstyles, but sometimes an Angel just has to change it up. One week post-show, veteran VS model Alessandra Ambrosio has revealed new bangs and edgy cut while in the Bahamas.

The cut follows suit to what is tipped to be [the cut of 2017](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/the-one-hairstyle-everyone-wants-right-now#zv0AkZxW7aMXLYLl.97) – slightly shagged, but maintained, and bangs.
