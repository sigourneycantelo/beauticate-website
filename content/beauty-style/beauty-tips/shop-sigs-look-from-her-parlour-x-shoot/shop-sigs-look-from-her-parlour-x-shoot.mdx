## THE MAKEUP

On the makeup she created for Sig, Jess Diez explained: “I decided to go with a soft, glam look, akin to makeup styles from the 1970s. I feel in general that we are moving away from the ’no makeup’ look, with clients expecting a natural healthy glow, but with makeup that can be seen,” shares Jess. “Because I wanted the skin to glow, I applied foundation only where it was absolutely necessary, blending it out with a stippling brush to achieve a dewy, fresh finish.” Want to recreate?
