## OF A FEATHER

Sticking to neutral hues can make your interiors a little soulless – in which case, adding some texture is your best bet for adding interest while keeping things white and bright. If your decorating palette ranges from snow white to cream and no further, may we suggest this feathered candle holder from Zara Home: whimsical and delicate, it’s hard to tell it’s a high street bargain.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
