For those considering mastering mosh-makeup with a little more glam grunge than grime, beauty notes best be taken. Whether you’re front and centre at the Outkast or rugged up hillside for Vance Joy, before you pack the poncho, consider channeling the unsung heroes that have been bouncing from musical set to backstage virtually their entire natural lives. Even before the Kate Mosses and Sienna Millers of the world first braided their hair or slipped into a pair of Hunters. After all, there is something to be said for donning a last name that’s synonymous with rock Royalty – and a rock God’s daughter is literally born to beautify the art of festival going. Come rain, mud, puddle or shine.

DAISY LOWE

### *Daisy Lowe*

**Who:** Daughter of It-Girl Pearl Lowe and Gavin Rossdale.

**What:** Flyaway fringe. Try John Frieda Frizz Ease 100% Shine Glossing Mist.

**How: **Never fear fringed friends, a few spritz of this about a forearms length away from a blow-dried and styled fringe will keep pesky flyaways at bay all day long.

JOHN FRIEDA FRIZZ EASE 100% SHINE GLOSSING MIST

LIV TYLER

### *Liv Tyler*

**Who:** Daughter of Bebe Buell and Steven Tyler.

**What:** Blushed cheeks. Try Becca Beach Tint.

**How:** The ultimate nineties dream girl, Liv Tyler knew the power of a flushed, pink cheek to perk your look right up. If you’re feeling a little flat and would rather look more darling than dawn of the dead in your festival photos, touches of cream blush on the cheeks and lips will bring you back to life and freshen up your look.

COCO SUMNER

### *Coco Sumner*

**Who:** Daughter of Trudie Styler and Sting.

**What:** Ombre lined eyes. Try Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour in Amethyst.

**How:** If you’re after a subtle ombre eye that’s sleeker and slicker than most, look no further. The bluntness of the stick liner means easy reapplication on site, where good mirrors are few and far between. Smudge in the outer corners of the bottom lash line for grunge a la’ Coco.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do you keep a fringe under control at a festival?**
A: A few spritzes of a glossing mist like John Frieda Frizz Ease 100% Shine Glossing Mist, applied about a forearm's length from a blow-dried fringe, will keep flyaways at bay all day.

**Q: What is the best lip product for a festival that won't smudge?**
A: A highly pigmented lip stain is the most reliable option. Double dabbing in the centre of the lips creates a gradient effect, and the stain holds colour without the constant maintenance of a traditional lipstick.

**Q: How should you handle hat hair at a festival?**
A: Prep your hair the night before with a volumising shampoo to add texture, and carry a comb to prevent matting under the brim. A little texture peeking out from the hat pulls the whole look together.

**Q: What is the easiest way to achieve a bronze glow without streaking?**
A: Apply a self-tan towelette formulated for both face and body the night before the festival. Doing it in advance avoids any unwanted residue or streaking mid-set.
