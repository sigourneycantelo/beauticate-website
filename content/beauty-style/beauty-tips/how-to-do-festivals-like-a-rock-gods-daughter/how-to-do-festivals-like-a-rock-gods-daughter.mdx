[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]For those considering mastering mosh-makeup with a little more glam grunge than grime, beauty notes best be taken. Whether you’re front and centre at the Outkast or rugged up hillside for Vance Joy, before you pack the poncho, consider channeling the unsung heroes that have been bouncing from musical set to backstage virtually their entire natural lives. Even before the Kate Mosses and Sienna Millers of the world first braided their hair or slipped into a pair of Hunters. After all, there is something to be said for donning a last name that’s synonymous with rock Royalty – and a rock God’s daughter is literally born to beautify the art of festival going. Come rain, mud, puddle or shine.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29286&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### *Daisy Lowe*

**Who:** Daughter of It-Girl Pearl Lowe and Gavin Rossdale.

**What:** Flyaway fringe. Try John Frieda Frizz Ease 100% Shine Glossing Mist.

**How: **Never fear fringed friends, a few spritz of this about a forearms length away from a blow-dried and styled fringe will keep pesky flyaways at bay all day long.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29285&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### *Liv Tyler*

**Who:** Daughter of Bebe Buell and Steven Tyler.

**What:** Blushed cheeks. Try Becca Beach Tint.

**How:** The ultimate nineties dream girl, Liv Tyler knew the power of a flushed, pink cheek to perk your look right up. If you’re feeling a little flat and would rather look more darling than dawn of the dead in your festival photos, touches of cream blush on the cheeks and lips will bring you back to life and freshen up your look.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29283&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### *Atlanta de Cadenet*

**Who:** Daughter of Amanda de Cadenet and Duran Duran bass guitarist John Taylor.

**What:** Frizz free bed hair. Try Nicky Clarke Instant Calmer Sleek Soften Shine Balm.

**How:** Not only does this smell like a dream, its super light in consistency and won’t encourage any excess greasing. If you want to keep your hair in check all festival long, stick to lighter products and behold the bohemian.

### *Coco Sumner*

**Who:** Daughter of Trudie Styler and Sting.

**What:** Ombre lined eyes. Try Laura Mercier Caviar Stick Eye Colour in Amethyst.

**How:** If you’re after a subtle ombre eye that’s sleeker and slicker than most, look no further. The bluntness of the stick liner means easy reapplication on site, where good mirrors are few and far between. Smudge in the outer corners of the bottom lash line for grunge a la’ Coco.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29279&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### *Pixie Geldof*

**Who:** Daughter of Paula Yates and Bob Geldof.

**What:** A dominant lip. Try [Nars Lipstick in Plum.](http://www.meccacosmetica.com.au/shop/Nars/Lipstick/)

**How:** Va-va-voom is right. Speak volumes that rival the speakers at the main stage with a dominant lip, minus the smudgy drama. Pixie suits this colour perfectly, with her gamine sense of style and adorable hairpin. Pat your lips dry with pigment after application to avoid colour bleeding into the lip line.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29277&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
