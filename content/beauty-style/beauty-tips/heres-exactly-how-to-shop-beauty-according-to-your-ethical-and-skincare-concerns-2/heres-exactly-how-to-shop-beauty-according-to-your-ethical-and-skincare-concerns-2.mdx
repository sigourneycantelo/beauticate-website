### Savvy Shoppers Are Happy Shoppers

Our take? The more informed we are before we spend those big beauty dollars, the better, so kudos to these sites that help us shop smarter. With more choice available than ever before, being able to easily edit your options by concern is a time and sanity saver. As for their next steps, we’d love to see retailers address the last two major issues in online beauty shopping – the environment (providing info on sustainably resourced ingredients, packaging and shipping options), and finally disclosing full ingredients lists. When we know exactly what goes into our products, in every sense, then we can put our money towards supporting what matters to us.[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]*Story by Zoe Briggs.*[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row]
