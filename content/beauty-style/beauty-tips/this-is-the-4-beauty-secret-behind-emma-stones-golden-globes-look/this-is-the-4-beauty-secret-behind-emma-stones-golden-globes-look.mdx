Emma Stone glowed her way through this week's Golden Globes. Exhibit A:

But it's the 'grammed pic of the actress from make-up artist Rachel Goodwin just hours before the show that everyone's talking about.

Goodwin took to her social media account to share Emma Stone wearing a lip mask just before the red carpet with the caption: "Some say beauty is pain, others say it just ridiculous! And so prepping for the #goldenglobes with this hilarious human being! #koreanskincaresilliness."

 

[Some say beauty is pain, others say it just ridiculous! And so is prepping for the #goldenglobes with this hilarious human being! #koreanskincaresilliness](https://www.instagram.com/p/BPBML8Wlw5e/)

A photo posted by Rachel Goodwin (@rachel_goodwin) on Jan 8, 2017 at 1:32pm PST

The mask is Tony Moly's Kiss Kiss Lovely Lip Patch that promises to help plump and firm your lips in just 20 minutes – what a time to be alive. Stone's perfectly hydrated lips were complimented by a natural shade Nars lipstick on the red carpet.

While the trend for sheet masks has taken off in recent years, Stone has opened up a Pandora's (beauty) box with these lip patches. But, she wasn't the only celebrity to use Korean beauty products while prepping for the Globes. Game of Thrones actress Maisie Williams and Drew Barrymore both used sheet masks before the ceremony.
