The Oscars' red carpet is always a spectacle of the latest trends in beauty. This year, stars shined in a range of timeless looks and modern beauty accents that partnered with their fashion-forward gowns. Surprisingly, the most popular beauty trends were those that us little guys can actually pull them off.

1. Side parted hair: An ample amount of A-listers swept their locks to the side. An intentional side part is an easy way to spice up your look. Once hair is parted to the side, it draws attention to the central facial features like the eyes and lips bringing a subtle sensuality to any look. Emma Roberts, Karlie Kloss and Emma Stone are perfect examples of why this trend is so attractive.

2. Natural-Looking Lips: Accentuating natural lip colour is a sly way of accomplishing the effortless beauty look this season. Natural looking lips are quick and easy to attain and help to highlight the eyes.

3. Iridescent Eyes: Hailee Steinfeld, Taraji P. Henson and Olivia Culpo eyes sparkled with bright colors. Whether the shimmering eye shadow featured silver, purple or warm golden brown tones, the iridescent makeup emphasised the women’s greatest tool for expression – their eyes.

5. Wearing The Right Colour: Figuring out the undertones in your skin is important before deciding what to wear. Whether they have cool, warm or neutral undertones, many stars dressed in colours that complimented their complexions. This allowed their skin to glow rather than looking washed out. Chrissy Teigen’s warm undertones were illuminated by her Zuhair Murad gown.
