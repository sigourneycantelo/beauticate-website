7:15am: Manage a manic morning with a mask

![Thisworks](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/10-products-to-help-you-master-multitasking/thisworks.jpg)

Start the day with a ThisWorks Stress Check Face Mask which shields skin from internal and external stressors. Apply and leave for 10 minutes while making breakfast, rising off once your belly is full and your skin is feeling refreshed.

## 7:35am: Leave the house with protection

The dual action handiwork of the broad-spectrum, moisturising sunscreen will save you time and money while the refreshing aroma of spearmint, lemon and lavender aids in adding to the illusion of a good nights sleep.

## 8:20am: Tame your mane with dry shampoo

![Img](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/10-products-to-help-you-master-multitasking/img-73.jpg)

Reach for Badlands R+Co Dry Shampoo when even a top knot won’t save you. Perfect for quick application before ducking out the door, Dry Shampoo Paste gives you a matte, textured finish that will hold through your day’s duties.

## 8:55am: City-living skin saver

It’s not just the sun we have to protect ourselves from, environmental factors in city settings and even inside office buildings [can cause major damage on our skin](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/move-over-spf-your-skin-now-needs-environmental-protection#jXSiJ4kU2qV7psCB.97).

## 4pm: Nail Your Conference calls

![Img](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/10-products-to-help-you-master-multitasking/img-76.jpg)

## 6:50pm: Indulge in some Downtime

The ideal end to a perfectly pampered yet productive day? [Perricone MD’s Refreshing Shower Mask](http://mecca.com.au/perricone-md/preempt-series-refreshing-shower-mask/I-026267.html?cgpath=skincare-treatment-masks#start=1) is designed to stay in place while in the shower. Activated by the shower’s heat and steam, it protects against heat stress and other environmental aggressors.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are the best multitasking beauty products for a busy routine?**
A: This article recommends products that serve double duty across the day, including a stress-relief face mask, a moisturising SPF, dry shampoo paste, an anti-pollutant setting spray, a mineral finishing powder with sunscreen, a nail treatment oil, a shower mask, and a brightening eye cream.

**Q: What is Synergie Skin Enviroshield and how do you use it?**
A: Enviroshield is an anti-pollutant spray that protects skin from airborne pollutants and infrared damage. It also doubles as a makeup setting spray, giving a dewy glow, so it fits neatly into any workday routine.

**Q: Can a dry shampoo paste work on fine or thin hair?**
A: The article recommends R+Co Badlands Dry Shampoo Paste for quick application before leaving the house. It delivers a matte, textured finish and hold that works through a full day.

**Q: What is a good multitasking product for the afternoon?**
A: DermaQuest's On-the-Go Finishing Powder has a built-in brush applicator and contains Zinc Oxide, providing a shine-free finish while also acting as a sunscreen to protect against premature ageing.
