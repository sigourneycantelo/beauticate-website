## FOR THE BEAUTY NOVICE:

### Benefit Benetint

A cult favourite since its inception in the ‘70s, this natural looking stain can be applied to lips and cheeks for an easy, barely there flush, which is flattering on everyone and won't be too much for a makeup newby.

### [LUMA On The Glow Highlighter Stick](http://rstyle.me/n/cvkym6b5kw7)

Highlighters can be a bit overwhelming for those newly enrolling in Beauty Lovers 101, but this glow stick is fool-proof, and is a build-up application (so no accidental glitter stripe across the cheekbone). It's also a multi-duty product – can be used as a subtle shimmer eyeshadow and even a lip tint.

### Kora Organics The Gift Of Kindness Pack

This nifty little pack contains the brand's Body Exfoliator, Luxurious Rosehip Body Oil, Daily Hand Cream and a KORA Organics Bag. The quality, organic body products serve as an amazing introductory experience to the silky smooth skin beauty prods give us. They'll be stocking up their beauty bounty in no time…

## FOR THE BEAUTY ADVENTURER

### Bobbi Brown Crushed Lip Colour

Brand new this Christmas from the brand that gave us the natural look, this lip colour is super hydrating and protecting thanks to the added dose of Vitamins C and E, leaving lips ever so soft. Plus, there’s a great pigment pay-off – the colour holds to a natural stain until you’re ready to reapply. Choose ‘Baby’ for a subtly enhanced look, or go all out with vibrant ‘Watermelon’ for someone who loves to make a statement.

### Glow Recipe Watermelon Glow Sleeping Mask

Packed with hydrating superstar hyaluronic acid and exfoliating salicylic acid, the science behind this mask is good. But honestly, it’s the pretty pale pink jelly texture and Instagram-worthy glass jar that seals the deal. Not sold? Well, earlier this year it boasted a 5000 person waitlist, so the proof is in the popularity.

### Tom Ford Beauty Sheer Cheek Duo

This cheeky blush and highlighter duo is the epitome of accessible designer wear, for your face. The brand synonymous with sleek and sexy delivers again with the on-trend peach and rosy tones. Your loved one will definitely be mentally listing this as their favourite gift…

Glamorous and practical.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is a good beauty gift for someone who is new to makeup?**
A: The article recommends Benefit Benetint, a cult lip and cheek stain that gives a natural flush flattering on everyone, and the LUMA On The Glow Highlighter Stick, which is buildable and fool-proof. Kora Organics The Gift of Kindness Pack is also suggested as a great introduction to quality body products.

**Q: What is the best beauty gift for someone who already has everything?**
A: The article suggests the Chanel La Creme Main hand cream for its iconic egg-shaped packaging, the Victoria Beckham x Estee Lauder Skin Perfecting Powder for its universal shade and show-stopping compact, and Butter London Patent Shine nail lacquer in Trout Pout for a fun summer pedi pick.

**Q: What beauty gifts work well as stocking stuffers?**
A: The article highlights the Omorovicza Queen of Hungary Mist, a refreshing thermal water mist, and Tom Ford Lip Colour in Cherry Lush as instant festive picks. The Lancome Absolue Precious Cells Rose Lotion is also recommended for adding glow before the serum step of a routine.
