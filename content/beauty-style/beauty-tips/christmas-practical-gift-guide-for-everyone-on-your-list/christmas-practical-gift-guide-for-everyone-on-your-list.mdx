## FOR THE BEAUTY NOVICE:

### Benefit Benetint

A cult favourite since its inception in the ‘70s, this natural looking stain can be applied to lips and cheeks for an easy, barely there flush, which is flattering on everyone and won't be too much for a makeup newby.

### [LUMA On The Glow Highlighter Stick](http://rstyle.me/n/cvkym6b5kw7)

Highlighters can be a bit overwhelming for those newly enrolling in Beauty Lovers 101, but this glow stick is fool-proof, and is a build-up application (so no accidental glitter stripe across the cheekbone). It's also a multi-duty product – can be used as a subtle shimmer eyeshadow and even a lip tint.

### Kora Organics The Gift Of Kindness Pack

This nifty little pack contains the brand's Body Exfoliator, Luxurious Rosehip Body Oil, Daily Hand Cream and a KORA Organics Bag. The quality, organic body products serve as an amazing introductory experience to the silky smooth skin beauty prods give us. ## FOR THE BEAUTY ADVENTURER

### Bobbi Brown Crushed Lip Colour

Brand new this Christmas from the brand that gave us the natural look, this lip colour is super hydrating and protecting thanks to the added dose of Vitamins C and E, leaving lips ever so soft. Plus, there’s a great pigment pay-off – the colour holds to a natural stain until you’re ready to reapply. Choose ‘Baby’ for a subtly enhanced look, or go all out with vibrant ‘Watermelon’ for someone who loves to make a statement.

### Glow Recipe Watermelon Glow Sleeping Mask

Packed with hydrating superstar hyaluronic acid and exfoliating salicylic acid, the science behind this mask is good. But honestly, it’s the pretty pale pink jelly texture and Instagram-worthy glass jar that seals the deal. Not sold? Well, earlier this year it boasted a 5000 person waitlist, so the proof is in the popularity.

### Tom Ford Beauty Sheer Cheek Duo

This cheeky blush and highlighter duo is the epitome of accessible designer wear, for your face. The brand synonymous with sleek and sexy delivers again with the on-trend peach and rosy tones. ### Hourglass Ambient Lighting Edit Palette Volume 3

Housed in a gorgeous rose gold case, this palette showcases six of Hourglass’ immensely flattering, glow-giving powders, including two new blush shades. Just perfect for your loved one's upcoming New Year celebrations.

### Butter London Patent Shine Nail Lacquer in ‘Trout Pout’

A pioneer of the ‘3 free’ non-toxic polish trend, Butter London’s polishes have great staying power while being both nail and environmentally friendly. Choose ‘Trout Pout’, a bright salmon, for the perfect summer pedi.

### Victoria Beckham x Estee Lauder Skin Perfecting Powder

This powder comes in one truly universal shade – there’s no white cast, no chalkiness, just a subtly mattifying effect and blurring of imperfections. ## TO SLIP INTO YOUR SANTA STOCKING…

### [Omorovicza Queen of Hungary Mist](https://www.mecca.com.au/omorovicza/queen-of-hungary-mist/I-020154.html)

Containing what one might call ‘spa water’ (a mix of Bungarian thermal water with orange blossom, rose and sage waters), this mist refreshes and hydrates with a scent that is light and uplifting. Perfect to use morning and night, as well as any time during the day when your mood – or your makeup – needs a refresh

### Lancome Absolue Precious Cells Rose Lotion

This luxury essence is best used before the serum stage of your routine and is a gel-to-lotion formula for plumping and giving a glow finish. Oh, and did we mention there’s actual rose petals suspended in it? Now you know you need it…

### Tom Ford Lip Colour in Cherry Lush

From the oh-so-chic signature black and gold packaging to the creamy, highly pigmented formula, this is the ultimate ‘I have ten seconds and need to feel festive’ trick for the season.
