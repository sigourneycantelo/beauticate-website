## WHEN IN DOUBT, POUT IT OUT…

You can never go wrong with a good lipstick. If you’re not confident about coloured clothing (or even if you are) a bold lippy is always a good choice. Pick a vibrant shade, like plum, to  compliment any outfit and paint a selfie-worthy pout.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do you wear colour in winter without it looking out of place?**
A: The key is to let one colourful piece be the statement and pair it back with neutrals. A ruby coat works best when the rest of the outfit is understated, while a bold lip or bright nail can add colour without committing to a full colourful outfit.

**Q: What colour was trending for winter knits at the time of this article?**
A: Yellow was identified as the new millennial pink - a sunny shade that brightens any winter day and works well as a knit layered over basics.

**Q: What is the easiest way to add winter colour if you are not confident?**
A: A bold lipstick is the simplest way to introduce colour - a vibrant shade like plum complements most outfits and needs no outfit overhaul. A coloured nail is another low-commitment option.
