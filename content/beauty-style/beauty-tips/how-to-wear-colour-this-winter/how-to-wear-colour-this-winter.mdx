## WRAP UP IN A COAT…

Rug up in a ruby-toned coat. Pair it back with neutral colours to make it the statement piece in your look. With so many great shades around, you’ll bound to find a coat in the right colour for you.

## BAG IT UP…

Who said bags have to be black? Next time you're shopping, take a walk on the wild side and select a brighter bag. With so many options to choose from, we'll take one in every colour.

## KEEP WARM IN A KNIT…

Yellow is the new millennial pink. Stay on trend with a yellow knit that will brighten any winter day.

## STEP UP YOUR SHOE GAME…

Step out of your comfort zone and have fun in a bright pair of shoes. Avoid making your whole outfit about your feet – pick your shoes and style the rest of the look accordingly.

## GET A MANI…

If colourful clothes are too out of your comfort zone, opt for a coloured nail. Cool weather tends to dry out hands in nails, which makes it especially important to keep those hands preened.

BARRY M GELLY NAIL PAINT – 17 BLUE GRAPE

OPI INFINITE NAIL POLISH – INDIGNANTLY INDIGO

YVES SAINT LAURENT LA LAQUE COUTURE – BLEU MAJORELLE

## WHEN IN DOUBT, POUT IT OUT…

You can never go wrong with a good lipstick. If you’re not confident about coloured clothing (or even if you are) a bold lippy is always a good choice. Pick a vibrant shade, like plum, to  compliment any outfit and paint a selfie-worthy pout.
