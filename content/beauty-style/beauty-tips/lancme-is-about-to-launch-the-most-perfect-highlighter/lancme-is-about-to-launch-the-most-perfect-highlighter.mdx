The so Frenchy so chic beauty brains at Lancôme are about to launch a highlighter so perfect it’s sending the Internet into overdrive. Keeping with the iconic Lancôme Rose, the compact is made up of three-dimensional pink rose petals that are dusted with a soft gold highlight powder. The idea being, you’re able to run your kabuki brush over the delicate petals and pick up the shimmer pigments for a subtle highlight, giving you the perfect glowy finish.

The Rosé collection also includes a host of other Parisian products including a pastel eyeshadow palette and a silver eye brightening pencil designed by  makeup artist Lisa Eldridge. The highlighter launches at Lancôme counters and website this Sunday, 5th February, and will be sold at selected David Jones and Myer stores.
