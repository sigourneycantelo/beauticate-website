The turn of a new year often comes with a ravaged complexion, less than fresh highlights and the dehydration that comes from one too many flutes of Möet. But, it's also about starting afresh, and getting excited about new things. In our case, getting excited about the beauty looks that kicked off the year we can get inspo from. To break up your first day back at work, Here’s a look at some of our favourite NYE looks, ready to screenshot for a go-to beauty hit to get you through the first day back at work…
