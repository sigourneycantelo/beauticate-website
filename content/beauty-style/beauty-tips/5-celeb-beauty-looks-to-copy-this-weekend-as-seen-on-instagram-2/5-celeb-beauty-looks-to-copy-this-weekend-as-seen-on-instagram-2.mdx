## MEGHAN MARKLE

Forget contouring, baking, and any

other laborious makeup application –

the new Duchess of Sussex arrived at

her fairytale wedding at Windsor

Castle with a makeup look that’s one

for the books.Everyone’s current girl-crush kept things understated and fresh with a slightly

smoked-out eye and nude lip, but it was her complexion that had everyone

talking. A light coverage, dewy foundation was artfully applied by makeup artist

refreshingly real way. A perfect case for accentuating – not masking – one's

natural beauty.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What makeup did Meghan Markle wear at her royal wedding?**
A: Meghan Markle's wedding makeup was created by makeup artist Daniel Martin. The look featured a slightly smoked-out eye, a nude lip and a light-coverage, dewy foundation that allowed her freckles to show through. The approach was deliberately understated, celebrating natural beauty rather than masking it.

**Q: How do you get Adriana Lima's bold lip look?**
A: The look, created by makeup artist Patrick Ta, features a blindingly bold almost-neon lip paired with neutral eyes and manicured brows. YSL Tatouage Couture Liquid Matte Lip Stain in Rose Ink is recommended as the product to recreate it.

**Q: What was Kristen Stewart's Cannes beauty look?**
A: At Cannes, Kristen Stewart wore a stark white winged liner rather than her usual black, inspired by her Chanel couture outfit. Makeup artist Beau Nelson created the look, which also featured pearl details on the eyes and a casual topknot, keeping the overall effect edgy rather than overly feminine.
