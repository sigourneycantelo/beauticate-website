## A French Vision Meets Australian Beauty

After moving to Australia, he noticed luxury Australian perfume didn’t yet exist in department stores. This realisation sparked his mission to create a fragrance house that would honour both nature and artistry.

[![Goldfield & Banks Australian luxury perfume bottles](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/goldfield-and-banks-interview-dimitri-weber/website-images-1-scaled-re75t89tg44ihtrv32foxlbd4w3fgs8g64w4c5ble8.png)](https://open.spotify.com/episode/64XoGd3fiUnvMKhuIE2iIf?si=6059740d03d14aaa)

## Building a Fragrance House Like No Other - Insights from the Goldfield & Banks Founder

Founded in 2016, [**Goldfield & Banks**](https://www.beauticate.com/sigourneys-edit/goldfield-and-banks-silky-woods-cairns/) captures the essence of Australia’s landscapes - from the warmth of the desert to the lushness of coastal rainforests. Dimitri’s dedication to quality and storytelling has made the brand a global name while remaining deeply connected to its origins.

## Native Botanicals and Storytelling at the Heart of Goldfield & Banks

In this [**Goldfield & Banks interview**](https://podcasts.apple.com/au/podcast/beautiful-inside-by-beauticate/id1754804721?i=1000721029164), Dimitri explains why ingredients like boronia, wattleseed, and finger lime are more than just notes in a perfume - they are touchstones of memory and emotion. By combining these botanicals with French perfumery expertise, he creates scents designed to transport and inspire.

![Native botanicals in Goldfield & Banks fragrances](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/goldfield-and-banks-interview-dimitri-weber/website-images-2-scaled-re75ugg8dbsznhzox3j5os61201oik3u07jxw5i39s.png)

## Lessons in Creativity and Self-Discovery - Dimitri Weber Interview

Dimitri reflects on the personal growth that comes with building a global fragrance house. He talks about the importance of stillness, the balance between work and creativity, and how staying true to a brand’s story can lead to timeless success.
