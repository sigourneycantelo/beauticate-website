The Critics' Choice Awards were held in La La Land last night and, as usual, the fashion and beauty stakes were high. Here are our three top looks we’ve added to our beauty to-try list.

![Img](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/the-critics-choice-awards-looks-were-coveting-right-now/img-13.jpg)

##

EN-US
JA

Bryce Dallas’ Strawberry Blonde Bangs and Pony:

EN-US
JA

Bryce Dallas look simple and chic in neutral peachy makeup and a thick banded pony tail. What set the simple look apart was her strawberry blonde bangs – the eyelid grazing style is amazing if you’re committed to blow drying daily. We’re also obsessing over the Topshop dress she wore on the red carpet.

Lily Collins Vampy Eye & Lip Combo:

EN-US
JA

Lily did away with the eye *or* lip beauty rule and looked amazing for doing so. She paired a gothic smoky eye with a deep berry pout, but matte finishes and creamy skin kept it from looking OTT. This is our pick for a glam NYE bash.

Sarah Hylands Bobby Pin Art:

EN-US
JA

If you have less than 30 seconds to do your hair, take a note out of Sarah Hyland’s beauty book and use bobby pins to trick up your style. This has been pretty huge in 2016 and we’re hoping it sticks around well into 2017. It can dress up and plain pony or bun- just take a few and line them up, or play around with criss-crossing them over.
