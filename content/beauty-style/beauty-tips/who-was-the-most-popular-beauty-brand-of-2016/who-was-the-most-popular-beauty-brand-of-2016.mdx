With celeb-driven products flooding the market, it's nice to see a cult brand take out the title of Most Popular Brand of 2016: MAC. According to the Best of Polyvore: 2016 trend report, the makeup company remains the most searched among the social commerce site’s community of users. Nude lipsticks in particular were a major drawcard for MAC, taking the spot as the most searched product on the site.

Runner-up was [NARS](https://www.narscosmetics.com/), whose cult-favourite blush in [Orgasm ](http://mecca.com.au/nars/blush-orgasm/I-000763.html?gclid=CKDf-eW98tACFYeVvAodCmQDPw)ranks high in searches. In third place was Urban Decay, with their coveted[ Naked Palette](http://mecca.com.au/urban-decay/naked-eyeshadow-palette/I-021109.html), followed by [Bath & Body Works Velvet Sugar Fragrance Mist](http://www.bathandbodyworks.com/product/index.jsp?productId=23473036) at number four, and Clinique in the fifth slot.

Newcomer to the industry, Kylie Jenner, scored a No.1 on one in Polyvore’s lists – but, of fashion icon. So maybe still a little way to go for the aspiring makeup mogul.
