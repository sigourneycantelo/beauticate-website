## For Groomed Brows: A Wax and Tint at Delphine Sorel

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]Formerly working at renowned salon [Kristin Fisher Eyebrows](https://www.beauticate.com/who/creatives/kristen-fisher-eyebrow-stylist/), French-born Delphine opened her own space earlier this year in Sydney’s CBD. Located in the elegant Dymocks Building, Delphine offers a suite of beautifying treatments for natural-looking brows and lashes, all executed with typically Gallic refinement and chic. I went for her eyebrow styling and tint, and my brows were full, fluffy, and tinted just enough – not so much as too look weird with my fair hair, but enough to make me feel groomed just by simply brushing up my arches.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## For Chic and Low-Key Hair: A Cut and Blowdry at RAW Anthony Nader

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]“I want you to look like you haven’t been to see me,” says Anthony Nader, the iconic hairstylist behind [cult salon RAW in Sydney’s Surry Hills](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/my-hair-done/raw-anthony-nader-surry-hills/). In other words, a salon visit that leaves you looking like you, only better; a refreshing attitude if you’re not one for overly preened and stiff pageant queen blowdries. The industrial cool, pared-back space could be situated in any number of big cities that Nader’s illustrious career has taken him to, complete with a ‘relaxation room’ for hair washing modelled off a first class aeroplane cabin (for the luxurious experience “without the jetlag”). Expect an off-duty model blowout, and low-maintenance haircut that looks good with minimum fuss – perfect for feeling polished on a Zoom call if you’ve just rolled out of bed.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## For A Pampering Manicure: The Ultraceuticals Anti-Ageing Manicure at Jocelyn Petroni

[/vc_column_text][vc_column_text]Not unlike a facial but for your hands, this beautiful treatment uses a customised cocktail of exfoliating lactic acid and actives from Ultraceuticals to rejuvenate and beautify. This is then followed with an exquisite manicure using [Jocelyn Petroni’s](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/beauty-maintenance/jocelyn-petroni-woollahra/) elegant selection of Chanel nail polishes.
