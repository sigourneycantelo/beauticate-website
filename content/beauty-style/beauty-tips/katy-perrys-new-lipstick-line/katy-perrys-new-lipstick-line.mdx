From red carpet to music videos to everyday life, Katy Perry has never been one to shy away from a bold beauty statement. So when we heard she was releasing a makeup collab with CoverGirl (for the second time) we were eager to see the direction they went in. The full range consists of eyeliners, pearly shadow highlighters and lipstick. But it is the latter, a line of demi-matte shades, that really caught our attention.

Not only do they have a smooth, creamy texture, the colours range from mermaid-y pastels, perfect pinks, purple and then, black. Yep, she's covered the whole gamut of lip lovers.

[According to @Refinery29, Katy Kat Matte in Crimson Cat is “the red lipstick everyone should be wearing.”?? Click link in bio to shop the shade that won the #BeautyInnovator Award!](https://www.instagram.com/p/BQWO_H2g03z/)

Whether, you prefer a more subtle tone, like Sphynx, are a traditional pink-lip enthusiast who would enjoy Magenta Mix, or someone who likes to take a trip to the dark side with Perry Panther, Katy’s collection seems to have it all.

Her shade of Cosmo Kitty is surprisingly versatile; wear it during the day to add some pop to your outfit, or wear it for a night out and be the coolest girl in the room. To top it all off, Katy is also releasing her Katy Kat Eye Mascara in three different shades: Black, Very Black, and Perry Blue. We can’t wait for Katy to spice up our make-up habits.
