A brilliant white smile paired with your go-to lippie is an instant and easy confidence booster. However, whether your poison is pinot, piccolo or avoiding professional cleans, the ‘bright white’ component of our smile can be difficult to uphold. While habits wreak havoc on our pearly not-so-whites, DIY teeth whitening has become a lot more effective and enamel safe in recent years. Meaning, multi-taskers can achieve a dream smile without having to sacrifice precious time in the dentist chair.  Gold Coast Beauticater, and self-proclaimed coffee connoisseur, Gracie Clough tried and tested the best teeth whitening products guaranteed to bust the stains and cater to your individual lifestyle.

### Advance Whitening Teeth Whitening Kit

“I see these handheld light teeth-whitening kits on Instagram, and am not sure of their efficacy. But, this gives a seriously ‘gram-ready smile. I use the mess-free kit for 20 minutes a day in two 10-minute intervals (but for those stubborn stains the kit can be used twice a day). I hold the tray to my mouth during the processing time, and find I have a good chance to catch up on my favourite beauty YouTube channels whilst whitening. On the fifth day of use, I notice whiter teeth – just as the kit promises.”

### Oral-B 3D White Whitestrips

“The Oral-B 3D White range is foolproof and ideal for the super stubborn stains that build up over the years. These enamel safe hydrogen peroxide base strips sit easily on my teeth and are so comfortable to wear I forget I have them on my teeth. I pop the strips in both morning and night for 30-minute intervals and notice my teeth appear instantly brighter after each application. The strips have a pleasant minty taste and my mouth feels fresh. However, some gel is stays on my teeth after application so I need to brush straight after use.”
