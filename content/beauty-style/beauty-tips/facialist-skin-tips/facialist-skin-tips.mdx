## THE BEST WAY TO TRANSFORM YOUR SKIN FAST IS…

…getting advice from a skin therapist and using products that are tailored to your personal needs and concerns. Having the right products can be game changing.

## TO GET RID OF A PIMPLE …

…don’t squeeze. Although I know you won’t listen, so this is how to do it properly. Clean your hands and your face. Wrap tissues around your fingers (preferably nails short). Apply pressure from the sides and underneath. Imagine you are trying to squeeze a cork out. Try to get behind and push forward. Then use a toner to sanitise afterwards. The mistake is people squeeze from the top and graze their skin. If you have a blind pimple, try using an anti-inflammatory gel to reduce swelling.

## THE BIGGEST MISTAKE MOST WOMEN MAKE WITH THEIR SKIN IS…

…over exfoliating and being too rough with granular scrubs. Exfoliating is only one element of having a balanced skin treatment regime. For optimal skin health, the art is achieving the right balance between removal and replenishing. It’s about using a replenishing mask in proportion to your exfoliation.

## THE PRODUCT I HAVE RECENTLY DISCOVERED THAT NO ONE KNOWS ABOUT IS….

…Blinc Amplified Mascara – it’s a tubular mascara that thickens and lengthens lashes and stays put all day. They say it's 'life proof'. I sit behind a steamer all day long and it doesn’t budge, I've never had that before. Being tubular, the pigment doesn’t drop creating 'black snow' under the eyes. It comes off with water and pressure easily – however, pressure or water alone won’t move it.
