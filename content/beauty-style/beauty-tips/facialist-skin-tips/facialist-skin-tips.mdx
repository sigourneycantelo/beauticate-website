## THE BEST WAY TO TRANSFORM YOUR SKIN FAST IS…

…getting advice from a skin therapist and using products that are tailored to your personal needs and concerns. Having the right products can be game changing.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

## TO GET RID OF A PIMPLE …

…don’t squeeze. Although I know you won’t listen, so this is how to do it properly. Clean your hands and your face. Wrap tissues around your fingers (preferably nails short). Apply pressure from the sides and underneath. Imagine you are trying to squeeze a cork out. Try to get behind and push forward. Then use a toner to sanitise afterwards. The mistake is people squeeze from the top and graze their skin. If you have a blind pimple, try using an anti-inflammatory gel to reduce swelling.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="32154&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
