## For Head-To-Toe Solutions

![Screen shot 2019 06 28 at 4.11.18 pm](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/three-new-sydney-beauty-destinations-we-love/Screen-Shot-2019-06-28-at-4.11.18-pm.png)

As its name suggests, [Combined Clinics Australia](https://www.combinedclinics.com.au) houses a collective of beauty and health professionals – all in one very chic space, designed by Graham Simmonds of Gray & Co. It's headed by chiropractor Dr. Cody Hanish – we recommend you check out his Instagram, by the way, where he adjusts his patients – it makes for very satisfying and addictive viewing. There's also light therapy facials, kinesiology, waxing and yoga classes available. Schedule in a whole day of treatments here and emerge feeling totally refreshed.

## For An Urban Getaway

![56521628 314212432587327 5036153083231286872 n](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/three-new-sydney-beauty-destinations-we-love/56521628_314212432587327_5036153083231286872_n.jpg)

Located in Sydney's bustling Eastern Suburbs is the lush and luxurious [Sol Spa.](https://www.thebotanicavaucluse.com.au/sol-spa) Once settled into the gorgeous space (think pretty blush chaise-longues, cosy fur rugs and zen water features), it's easy to imagine you're miles away from the city's chaos – the dense, leafy garden surrounding the spa certainly helps in this respect. Sol Spa offers a suite of dreamy treatments, from organic facials to full body massages and Ayurvedic rituals. Our advice? Book in at the gorgeous [Botanica restaurant](https://www.thebotanicavaucluse.com.au/botanica) after your treatment for even more indulgence, post-spa.

## For Holistic Help

![43433608 699664470409534 2771397576399980748 n](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/three-new-sydney-beauty-destinations-we-love/43433608_699664470409534_2771397576399980748_n-1.jpg)

To Wonderland Spa champions "a beauty from the inside, out" philosophy – and they very much practice what they preach with their portfolio of wellness therapies on offer. Providing naturopathy, colonic hydrotherapy and energy healing (as well as beautiful facials and massages), the focus here is on improving internal health for glowing results on the outside.
