## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why does the neck and décolletage age faster than the face?**
A: The skin on your neck and chest is thinner than facial skin, has fewer oil glands and receives just as much (if not more) sun exposure. It is also prone to "tech neck" creasing from constant screen scrolling, and most people apply far less skincare there than on their face.

**Q: How does red light LED therapy help the neck?**
A: Red light (633nm) and near-infrared (830nm) wavelengths penetrate the skin to stimulate fibroblasts - the cells responsible for producing collagen and elastin. This strengthens skin structure, improves firmness and reduces fine lines without downtime or irritation.
