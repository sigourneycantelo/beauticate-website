### Following on from the relaunch of Sigourney’s [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCfuyyVnNfbiwovULXTRQiVA), we decided to give you a little peek behind the scenes here at Beauticate HQ. In and out of the office, a lot of time and hard work goes into curating content for the site.  While it’s not always blooms, baked goods and playing with beauty products, they certainly do keep us sane under the weight of impending deadlines and a diary full of meetings. Of course, all work and no play does not a creative office make, so we’ve also thrown in a little out-of-business-hours shenanigans (if there is such a thing when you run your own business). Please enjoy an insight into our office, the latest deliveries we’re lusting over and how Sigourney juggles business, with her babies.
