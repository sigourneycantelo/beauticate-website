It’s that time of year again… The Victoria Secret runway show (the only runway on Earth that is more about the Who’s Who on the catwalk than the front row) is just days away and the Angels are out in force gearing up for the lingerie spectacular. This year, the show will be in Paris – the first French VS show – and include veterans Lily Aldridge, Adriana Lima and Alessandra Ambrosio, new wings Bella Hadid, Brooke Perry and Grace Elizabeth and the 'It Girl' tribe of Gigi Hadid, Kendall Jenner and Karlie Kloss. The svelte army of Angels have Insta’d their way from terminal to terminal (a flight that has become a show in itself), capturing every BTS moment…
