We’re constantly on the lookout for innovative self-tanning products. [We love the stuff.](https://www.beauticate.com/how-to/video-my-tanning-routine-sigourneys-edit#cVOOWD1IS73UHpAB.97) Foams, gels, sprays, lotions, in-shower application; You name it, we’ve used it. So when we heard that tanning water was actually a thing we were jumping out of our skin with excitement.

Enter the James Read H20 Tan Mist. This recently released cooling mist for the face allows you to control the depth of your tan – used daily until the desired shade of colour is reached. According to the product release, it is recommended that the mist is reapplied 2-3 times a week. Packed with all sorts of delicious stuff, such as rose water and skin-saving vitamins and minerals – your complexion will be left feeling more hydrated, instantly restored (and of course deliciously glowy), with a few simple sprays. The product also promises to work as a makeup primer, setter and refresher. Hardworking, nice.

When it comes to developing innovative, unique tanning products, Mr Read has this down pat. The tanning brand also recently introduced the Sleep Mask Tan Face – an overnight tan that imparts a subtle golden glow to the face while you sleep. Whoever said fake tanning is a chore, obviously hadn't heard of [James Read](http://mecca.com.au/james-read-tan/). Genius.
