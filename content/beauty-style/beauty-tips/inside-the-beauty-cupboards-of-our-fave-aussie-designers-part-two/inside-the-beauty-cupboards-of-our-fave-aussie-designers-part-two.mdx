### "I’m a very simple girl…

…I find something that I love then I use it.  I use Aesop body wash, hand wash and hand moisturiser. I also use [Cetaphil](http://bit.ly/2g9cZjz). It’s super basic I get allergies from perfumes [in beauty products]. I’m also a total MAC girl, MAC everything."

PHOTOGRAPHY BY [ALICE MAHRAN](http://alicemahran.com/)

### "I don’t do a sexy red lip…

I love a lip, but a dark, purple, plum. I’ve done pink, but not the really bright orange-y red shades. Because I don’t wear much jewellery, sometimes it just gives me that colour and definition, I think it’s just fun. I don’t have clothes on that are super sexy, [I prefer] kind of a minimal, organic [style] and I just find a strong lip gives a nice hot edge. I love [Linda Rodin](https://www.beauticate.com/who/linda-rodin-stylist-and-entrepreneur) lipsticks – they’re beautiful. They’re very bright though. There’s kind of a thing with undone hair and lip that’s not, ‘I just came off the beach’."

### "I got a lesson from MAC and everything they said not to do was what I was doing!

[They said] that’s not how you do contouring! I was like, “Really?” They said what I was doing was a big big no-no: black eyeliner under your eyes, I always do that. I love a brow gel and mascara – they’re my basics, I don’t really do eye shadows. The This Works Skin Deep Dry Leg Oil is really good for legs; [when] your legs are a bit gross after winter, you just put it on and it’s goodbye scaly skin."

### "I’m obsessed with fragrance.

I love Tom Ford fragrance. Dan and I try and share because some of the fragrances are really good for boys and girls. I love the Santal and Dan gets the normal Tom Ford Noir. We also share the [Byredo](http://mecca.com.au/byredo/), and I like the Neroli and Rose by Le Labo. I love candles, too – I have them burning constantly. I love this Byredo Burning Rose I got recently."

### "Being a mum, I'm so time poor – for me it's not about having a full skincare ritual…

…but I do love luxurious creams. I love La Mer Moisturizing Cream. I think it's better to use the best of what's out there, giving yourself that little bit of luxury, than using products that aren't as efficient. Or that don't make you feel great. I was on a plane recently and I had a little La Mer tub, it felt so luxurious on a long trip. I'm really fanatical about taking my makeup off. My grandmother was into that sort of stuff, she taught me the importance of this. But I'm more girly girl than her when it comes to really loving the products. I'm a big fan of Aesop body washes and balms, too."

### "I genuinely love makeup. I think more is more.

… I love the products, I love the process of putting it on. I love learning how to do contouring and just have that kind of makeup most days. It's fun for me. The first product I ever bought was a hideous foundation… I felt so grown up buying it, but it was entirely the wrong shade for me. Awful. But, now I have a much better idea about what suits me. I use MAC Waterproof foundation, and am always doing my contouring with a highlighter, and have all the different kinds of makeup brushes. My favourite lipstick is Velvet Teddy by MAC, it's a velvety pink – I actually have a top selling right now in a similar colour."

![Bianca2](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/inside-the-beauty-cupboards-of-our-fave-aussie-designers-part-two/Bianca2-1.jpg)


### "My beauty routine is very nighttime based.

I just have more time and my skin responds better. PM is my revive opportunity! At night – I use the [Garner Micellar Water](http://bit.ly/29YzRQ7) to remove make up. And then I can’t go past [James Vivian](http://jamesviviandt.com/), they sell amazing things!. I use the SkinMedica Lytera Brightening Complex, Results Rx A Solution Vitamin A and Triple AHA Serum. I also am obsessed with Aspect Super Moisturising Complex (S.M.C). In the morning I just moisturise and make up, very straight forward. I always carry Kora Rosehip oil, Aesop Deodorant and organic hand cream during travel so I can feel human and 'fresh' even on the longest of flights."

### "My favourite product is the [Stila 12 Colour Eyeshadow Palette](http://mecca.com.au/stila/eyes-are-the-window-shadow-palette/V-018937.html)…

I add a smoky eye for PM and it’s the best. As for the day I’m definitely a minimalist. I always start with [Stila Illuminating Foundation](http://mecca.com.au/stila/illuminating-foundation-60-watt/I-002839.html?gclid=Cj0KEQjwt-G8BRDktsvwpPTn1PkBEiQA-MRsBR_O732WKOxSpteRQVnLE2cKcROTFMHznAKQFP4Ifc8aAsjO8P8HAQ), it’s light and the perfect base for my skin. [Nars Laguna Bronzer](http://mecca.com.au/nars/bronzing-powder/V-000778.html#q=nars%2Blaguna%2Bbronzer&start=1) is next for an all over glow and then I add Nars Shimmer Stick to my cheeks. I also always have my Chanel lipstick compact handy. I prefer it over one lipstick given I often have to move from day to night- adding a fresh lip colour is my go too. I can't be without Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Lipbalm as well. A spritz of [Serge Lutens – L’Eau de Paille](http://mecca.com.au/serge-lutens/leau-de-paille/V-023912.html?cgpath=brands-serge#start=1) and I’m ready to go."

### "Day to day I like to keep it fresh and simple

I truly believe that beauty starts from within. A beautiful soul always shines brighter than anything else. If ones soul is nourished, it brings perspective to outward beauty and what matters. I start with a clean face using [Dermalogica Special Cleansing Gel](http://www.dermalogica.com.au/au/html/products/special-cleansing-gel-3.html?type=function&cat=cleansers)followed by a layer of my Chantecaille Flower Harmonizing Cream to hydrate my face, particularly in the dry winter months. I’ll then apply my ever-faithful MAC Face and Body Foundation. It’s quite sheer and light on the skin, perfect for everyday wear."

### "Filling in my eyebrows seems to change everything

I use MAC Eyebrow Pencil in brown, then for my eyes, I lightly apply my MAC Black Pencil Eyelineron the top lid followed by a MAC Pencil Brush to soften it. I use my black volumising mascara on my lashes. If I have an event or occasion, I’ll tend to put the focus on my eyes. I wish I was in the habit of using an eyelash curler but it feels like one step too many in my day-to-day routine. To finish, my MAC Crème Blush on the cheekbones makes all the difference. My husband thinks I look my best without any makeup at all – I’m not so sure I agree!"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What foundation do Australian fashion designers use?**
A: The designers featured here use a range of bases, with MAC Face and Body Foundation and Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk appearing multiple times. Stila Illuminating Foundation and Nars Sheer Glow are also favourites, reflecting a preference for light, skin-like coverage over heavy formulas.

**Q: What is the one makeup product Aussie designers can't live without?**
A: Highlighter and bronzer come up repeatedly across the interviews. NARS Laguna Bronzer appears in multiple bags, while individual designers also cite YSL Touche Eclat, Charlotte Tilbury Filmstar Bronze and Glow, and Hourglass Ambient Lighting as essentials for achieving a healthy, luminous look.

**Q: What skincare do Australian fashion designers swear by?**
A: La Mer Moisturising Cream, Kiehl's Ultra Facial Cream and Perricone MD are the most frequently mentioned premium skincare choices. Several designers also rely on Aesop body washes and balms, and Garnier Micellar Water for makeup removal.
