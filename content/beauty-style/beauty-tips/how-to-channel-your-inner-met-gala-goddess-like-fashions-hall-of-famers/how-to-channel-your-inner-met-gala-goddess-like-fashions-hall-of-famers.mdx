### With this year’s theme a glittering and graceful nod to The Orient, the Met’s scarlet stairs boasted the best in fresh, floral and oriental inspired beauty. This year’s round up was certainly not short of supreme standouts; but we think the following red-hot hair and beauty homages deserve a little extra kudos.

### 1. Rihanna’s flushed cheek

RIHANNA

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]Hello, yellow! Rihanna’s flushed cheek is the perfect finish to such a bold and becoming look. The fresh and feminine directive perfectly counteracts with that chic sixties flicked hair. Opt for a warmer blush tone with gold reflects, like [Urban Decay Naked Flushed Palette](http://mecca.com.au/urban-decay/naked-flushed/V-021061.html#start=1) to really build on your natural blush tones.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26871&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 2. Kendall Jenner’s sleek center part

[/vc_column_text][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26866&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text]

KENDALL JENNER

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]So simple yet oh-so-stylish. A Calvin Klein-clad Kendall Jenner did what Kendall does best and kept her look youthful and fresh. With very little fuss, this center-part style is a winner in our books. Apply a taming masque like Shu Uemura’s Shusu Sleek Smoothing Treatment the night before, pre-blow dry, to avoid flyaways and frizz come straightening iron time.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26874&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 3. Kate Hudson’s headpiece hair

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26879&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

KATE HUDSON

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]There are a lot of wonderful things going on here, namely that fabulously ‘fashion’ head of hair. Tousled and effortlessly pulled together, Hudson takes home supreme hair award, hands down. If you’re going to don a decadent headpiece, consider a freer, messier up-style for an undone appeal. Apply a little dry texturizer like [Bumble and Bumble Dry Spun texturizer](http://mecca.com.au/bumble-and-bumble/thickening-dry-spun-finish-150ml/I-016382.html#start=1) to the roots before styling for a little added grit.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_single_image image="26883&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

[BUMBLE AND BUMBLE DRY SPUN TEXTURIZER](http://mecca.com.au/bumble-and-bumble/thickening-dry-spun-finish-150ml/I-016382.html#start=1)

AMAL CLOONEY

SARAH JESSICA PARKER

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/3&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]If we can take a brief moment to divert the eye from the headpiece to trump all others, get a gander at those baby blues.
