Arizona Muse has walked down the isle in Marylebone, London on Sunday, marrying long-term osteopath partner, Boniface Verney-Carron, in what is the most perfect execution of paired-back bridal beauty.

As their engagement was revealed earlier this year, the pair celebrated an intimate ceremony this weekend at Hardy’s wine bar and eatery, surrounded by close family and friends.

Arizona took to Instagram looking classic and understated, posing in the streets of London next to her new hubby. Arizona embodied all things grace, while keeping her beauty look minimal. The twenty-eight year-old looked stunning with her wavy, chin-length locks and turned heads in her Temperley gown and Chopard Jewels. Arizona publicly thanked designer, Alice Temperley, for creating her one of a kind exquisite gown which featured a deep V-neck and front slit, highlighted and held together by a string of delicate buttons. The sleeveless silk gown was elegantly paired with a statement choker necklace from Chopard.

[A post shared by Arizona Muse (@arizona_muse)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BVgyYDJHl1y/) on Jun 19, 2017 at 12:11am PDT

Muse is a major industry influencer who has fronted campaigns for David Yurman, Yves St. Laurent, Louis Vuitton and Fendi. The icon has also appeared on a number of international Vogue covers and editorial pieces and has been in a multi-million-dollar beauty contract with Estee Lauder since 2012. Definitely looking forward to see what's next for Arizona!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Who did Arizona Muse marry?**
A: Arizona Muse married her long-term partner, osteopath Boniface Verney-Carron, in an intimate ceremony at Hardy's wine bar in Marylebone, London in June 2017.

**Q: What did Arizona Muse wear to her wedding?**
A: She wore a bespoke silk gown by British designer Alice Temperley, featuring a deep V-neck, front slit and delicate buttons. She paired it with a statement choker necklace from Chopard.

**Q: What beauty brands has Arizona Muse worked with?**
A: Arizona Muse has fronted campaigns for David Yurman, Yves Saint Laurent, Louis Vuitton and Fendi, and has held a multi-million-dollar beauty contract with Estee Lauder since 2012.
