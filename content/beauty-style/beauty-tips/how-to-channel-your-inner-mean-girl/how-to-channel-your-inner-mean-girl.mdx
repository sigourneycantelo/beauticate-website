### But before you go and slather your face in peppermint foot cream or take to your favorite tank top with a pair of scissors, take heed of our six favorite beauty mantras straight out of the Burn Book.

### On Wednesdays we wear pink

ALTHOUGH RUTHLESS, REGINA DID KNOW HOW TO WEAR HER PINKS…AT LEAST ON WEDNESDAYS

Not only did Karen have like, a fifth sense, she knew the importance of keeping her beauty routine fresh and current. Hump Day tends to drag, so shake it up at least once a week with a bold lip colour that’s bound to put a spring back in your step. Clinique Chubby Sticksboast endless shades of pink, so take your pick!

### Her hair is full of secrets that’s why it’s so big

"RIGHT?!" SO SAYS REGINA

Every mean girl knows volume is all but essential. Nobody is going to take you seriously with hat hair or drab locks, so make a beeline for [Swell Advanced Volumizing Masque](http://www.feelunique.com/p/Swell-Advanced-Volumizing-Masque-150ml) to ensure you’re always bouncy.  It’ll add hydration without the harsh chemicals and is a sure fire way to achieve the drama you actually want in life.

### My nail beds suck

IF KAREN DOESN'T PLAY DUMB WITH HER NAILS, NEITHER SHOULD YOU.

What good is a mani-pedi with underachieving nail beds? When the going gets tough and it’s time to bring the claws out, you should only ever do so with an enviable buff and shine. Get back to basics and start with your cuticles, [Eve Lom Cuticle Cream](http://mecca.com.au/eve-lom/cuticle-cream/I-007977.html) will ensure you’re always ahead of the pack, not clawing your way to the top.

### You can only wear your hair in a ponytail once a week

PULL IT BACK AND SHOW OFF YOUR FACE, JUST LIKE CADY DOES HERE

While a ponytail has earned its keep to maintain in steady rotation, mixing up your usual fallback hairstyle is a great way to avoid getting stuck in a style rut. So you usually rock a ponytail Monday to Friday? Try a braid or low-slung chignon. Or treat yourself to a mid-week blow wave to breathe new life into your workweek.

POOR GRETCHEN, SHE CLEARLY NEVER GOT THE MEMO

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What beauty tip does the Mean Girls "On Wednesdays we wear pink" rule inspire?**
A: Mix up your midweek look with a bold pink lip colour. Clinique Chubby Sticks come in a range of pink shades that add an instant lift without much effort.

**Q: How do you add volume to hair in the Mean Girls style?**
A: The article recommends Swell Advanced Volumizing Masque, which adds hydration and volume without harsh chemicals.

**Q: What fragrance advice does the article give, inspired by Mean Girls?**
A: Choose a fragrance that whispers rather than shouts. Visit a beauty bar for a guided scent session to find an eau de parfum that suits your skin type.

**Q: What hair care investment does the article recommend for lustrous locks?**
A: A quality hair oil between salon visits, specifically Kerastase Elixir Ultime Oleo Complex, which has a formula suited to every hair type.
