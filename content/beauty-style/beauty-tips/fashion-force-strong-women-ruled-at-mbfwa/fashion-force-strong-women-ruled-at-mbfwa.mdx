SAM HARRIS BACKSTAGE AT ASHKAR LINE

**Ashkar Line**

James Nicholson for [Kevin Murphy](http://kevinmurphy.com.au/): “There’s a slightly wet, slicked-back look at the front, but I spent a lot of time blowing out the back with a rolling brush. The wet-look at the front and the blow-dry at the back is a play on textures.”

Putting O&M Sukuroi creamaround the edge and on the ends to make it look a little bit oilier.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27280&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Tough tresses

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]**Jayson Brunsdon**

Richard Kavanaugh for [Redken](http://www.redken.com.au/professionals/why-redken): “We start with putting a really strong texture base into the hair and then we’re cinching the hair back so that it creates a really narrow, but androgynous silhouette. The clothes are very feminine so when the girls walk there’s this kind of toughness that anchors them as well.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27288&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### Textured talons

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]**Alice McCall**
