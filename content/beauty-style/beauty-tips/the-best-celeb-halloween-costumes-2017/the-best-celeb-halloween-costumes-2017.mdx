## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Who won Halloween 2017 according to Beauticate?**
A: Heidi Klum won Halloween 2017, as she does most years. She dressed as a Thriller-era werewolf, a costume so elaborate it required multiple Instagram posts to document the full transformation.

**Q: What did Gigi Hadid dress as for Halloween 2017?**
A: Gigi Hadid dressed as Felicia Hardy, also known as The Black Cat, the Marvel character who is Spiderman's love interest. Her partner Zayn dressed as Spiderman to complete the couple costume.

**Q: What Halloween costume did Sarah Michelle Gellar wear in 2017?**
A: Sarah Michelle Gellar and Freddie Prinze Jr recreated the characters Andie and Duckie from the 1986 film Pretty in Pink, posting a photo alongside the original stars Molly Ringwald and Jon Cryer.
