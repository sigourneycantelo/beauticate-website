### 1. For that Bronzed Glow

If cold winter days have you feeling like you’ve lost your summer glow, try popping on a little James Read Sleep Mask Tan for Face before you get into bed. Combining the beautifying effects of a facial (thanks to cucumber, aloe vera and red algae extracts) with a hint of self-tan, it promises to deliver a healthy overnight glow.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27593&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

### 3. The Night Cream

They’ve been around since as far as we can remember, but overnight creams are still a skincare staple of beauty-savvy women world over. And rightly so, as they help to repair and restore skin, leaving it feeling soft and rejuvenated in the morning. A current favourite of the Beauticate team is Rationale’s DNA Reactivating Night Cream. Rich in vitamin A, it works to increase hydration levels in the skin while helping to fade pigmentation and improve tone and texture, to give you a more luminous complexion.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="27600&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

If waking up with baby soft, kissable lips sounds like your kinda thing (Sleeping Beauty, we’re looking at you!), make a beeline for your nearest Mecca Cosmetica store to snap up their new Lip De-Luscious Night Lip Treatment. ### 7. The Body Oil

A deeply nourishing body oil is the ultimate skin savior come wintertime. Former British Beauty Director, Kathy Phillips, no doubt had this in mind when she created her This Works Deep Sleep Night Oil; a calming lavender-scented body oil with chamomile that moisturizes and soothes dry skin. It’s perfect for applying pre-bedtime because it’s fast absorbing, which means no greasy residue to rub off onto your favourite PJ’s.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="29361&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
