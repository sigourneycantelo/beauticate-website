## THE ONE HAIR-REVITALISING TRICK YOU MAY HAVE NEVER HEARD OF IS&#8230;

&#8230;before you go to bed, tie your damp hair in a twisted top knot, and when you get up in the morning unleash your hair and you've got beautiful textured movement.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]
