## THE ONE HAIR-REVITALISING TRICK YOU MAY HAVE NEVER HEARD OF IS&#8230;

&#8230;before you go to bed, tie your damp hair in a twisted top knot, and when you get up in the morning unleash your hair and you've got beautiful textured movement.

## THE ONE HAIR RULE I LIKE TO BREAK IS&#8230;

&#8230;don't wash your hair too often. From my experience the natural oils condition the hair amazingly. That being said nobody likes greasy hair – so it's a fine line.
