### Why I Became Obsessed With Better Sleep

It’s no secret that I’ve become a bit of a sleep obsessive lately. After reading [*Why We Sleep* by Matthew Walker,](https://www.kingliving.com.au/shop/bedroom) I swapped my bright white bulbs for amber ones, cut down on alcohol and caffeine, and started to actually *think* about how I sleep. But after a whirlwind overseas trip and a serious case of jetlag, I fell back into bad habits. Enter: The King Living Sleep Collection, my new bedtime obsession.

### The Byron Sleep Retreat That Changed My Routine

Last year, I was lucky enough to join The [King Living Sleep Retreat ](https://www.instagram.com/p/C-rpzm4y2fu/)at the breathtaking Bunker Byron house. It was the kind of getaway sleep dreams are made of: a night away in Byron Bay with a curated mattress menu, personalised sleep consultations with Dr. Carmel Harrington, and the chance to trial King Living’s ultra-luxe bedding. (Cue: B-roll of impossibly chic interiors and fabulous food by Three Blue Ducks.)

															*

Dr. Harrington shared some pearls of wisdom during the retreat, like how to reset your sleep routine; the importance of a consistent bedtime routine and how light exposure impacts melatonin levels. These tips couldn’t have come at a better time-my own routine had been sidelined by long-haul flights and frantic nights working in bed (it’s just SO much more comfortable than the desk).

When the team offered me a chance to trial one of their [bed and mattress combos](https://www.kingliving.com.au/shop/bedroom), I decided it was time to reset my sleep game.

### The Nightly Routine That Helps Me Sleep Better

Week 1: Getting Back on Track

After a week of trialling my [King Living bed](https://www.kingliving.com.au/shop/bedroom) and jetlag protocol, I’ve already noticed a difference. Those first few nights were a little challenging-I tossed and turned, but my husband, Damien, barely noticed thanks to the mattress’s partner-disturbance technology.

As I shared in part two of my [Youtube Sleep Series Video](https://youtu.be/6MUpW9ZkRPw). We know that the ideal temperature for sleep is 18.3 degrees C so the aim is to keep as cool as possible. We’ve been using the King Living CoolFit cover on our mattress, with its Japanese gel-infused fibres that draw heat away-an absolute lifesaver in Sydney’s summer heat. (Definitely a contender for the Best Mattress for Hot Sleepers Australia Award.)

The cover pairs beautifully with the latest fabric innovation which are even silkier than bamboo sheets. [Mynt](https://myntlab.com/?srsltid=AfmBOoosbqINA4dkdyxeKH2F5AjsqGtVNDHfybzTGhSXps4gxYE3w0_V) are a blend of pine, eucalyptus and mint and they’re super cool, slinky and antibacterial.I find aircon too hotel-like but a ceiling fan makes the temperature just right*.

### Tips From Sleep Experts You’ll Actually Want to Try

To complement the epic setup, I followed advice from [sleep expert Olivia Arrazello](https://oliviaarezzolo.com.au/), who shared her pre-bed protocol:

- One hour before bed: reduce light exposure and wear blue light glasses. (I like Baxter Blue)
- Take a warm bath with [Salts & Co Pure Magnesium Flakes Bath Salts](https://amzn.to/4hkNPJn)  (already a nightly ritual for me).
- Try supplements like magnesium glycinate and Reishi mushrooms (I like Life Cykel drops, so much easier to take than powder).
- Read for 20 minutes with low light (a paperwhite [Kindle](https://amzn.to/3XOiE1x) on a low or no light setting is okay).
- Charge your phone in another room-a game-changer for avoiding late-night doom scrolling.

The result? I’ve felt calmer, more refreshed, and ready to tackle my day-even when jetlag threatened to derail me.

**Week 2: Fine-Tuning My Sleep Routine**

By week two, I was fully immersed in my [King Living Sleep](https://www.kingliving.com.au/shop/bedroom) experience. I swapped the CoolFit cover for the AirFit cover, which is breathable and temperature-regulating, and flipped the mattress to its softer side-perfect for side sleeping.

And then on Thursday, we actually had a cool snap, so I was able to try it out The WarmFit cover, complete with an integrated electric blanket (yes, you can wash it!). It’s indulgent and practical, so I don’t have to choose between warmth and my beloved mattress topper. I used it just to heat the bed before I jumped in and can confirm it was super cosy. With my old electric blanket you could feel the coils through the sheets and I always felt a bit “princess and the pea” about it. With this one, the heating element is undetectable and I love that I can just throw it in the machine.

### Week-by-Week: What Changed After Two Weeks of Better Sleep

Transforming my sleep routine has been about more than just upgrading my bed-it’s been about creating a space and habits that genuinely support rest and recovery. The customisable mattress let me fine-tune comfort to suit my sleep needs, while cooling covers and breathable bedding helped me stay comfortable even during hot summer nights. Adding small but impactful rituals-like wearing blue light-blocking glasses, magnesium baths, charging my phone outside the bedroom, and winding down with meditation-has made all the difference.
