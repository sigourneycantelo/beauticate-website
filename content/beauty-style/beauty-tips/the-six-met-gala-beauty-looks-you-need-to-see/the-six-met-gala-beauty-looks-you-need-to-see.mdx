New York’s night of nights is upon us, where all of the glossiest celebrities don their finest couture for fashion’s biggest party, The Met Gala. We’ve been pouring over social media for the last hour ‘ohh-ing’ and ‘ahh-ing’ over each and every glamorous shot, and admiring the handiwork of some of the worlds finest hair and makeup artists. This is our curation of some of the most shocking, amazing and simply unbelievable beauty looks to grace the red carpet.
