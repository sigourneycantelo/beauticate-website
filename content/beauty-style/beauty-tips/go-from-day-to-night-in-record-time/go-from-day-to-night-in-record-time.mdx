### PR maven turned beauty entrepreneur Kirsten Carriol has been to her fair share of functions over the years. The petite blonde spent ten years throwing fabulous beauty events and still makes a regular appearance on Sydney’s social scene – despite being a mum to two boys and running a thriving business. How? She takes her day look into the evening with minimal fuss. Here, in her walk-in wardrobe, she shows us how to freshen your foundation, amp up your eyes and create a chic cocktail bun in less time than it takes for the cab to arrive.
