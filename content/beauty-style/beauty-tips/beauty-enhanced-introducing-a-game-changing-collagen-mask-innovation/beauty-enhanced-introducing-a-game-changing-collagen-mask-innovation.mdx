## WHAT IS RECOMBINANT COLLAGEN?

Infused with 'recombinant' collagen for enhanced beauty, it’s the latest in pioneering collagen technology sourced from a leading lab in Spain, trademarked as ‘Col-Frag remasteredTM'.

It’s won awards for its innovation in the beauty industry and has clinically approved efficacy.

By comparison with other collagens that are sourced from animals (fish, pigs, and cows), recombinant collagen is bio-engineered, plant-based, and non-GMO for beauty-conscious consumers.

It shows excellent efficacy in wrinkle reduction, can help with skin barrier regeneration, and can help with skin collagen renewal, offering lifting and plumping beauty benefits.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="61946&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## INSIDE THE INNOVATION

Col-Frag remastered™ won the “Most Innovative Raw Material” at the European BSB Innovation Award 2022. 

The way they do it is fascinating for the beauty industry. In a biofactory, scientists insert a fragment of synthetic human type I collagen, often used in beauty products, into the cytoplasm of the plant (that’s a gel-like fluid that fills the plant cell).

As it grows, the plant then begins to produce the grafted protein naturally, using its own protein synthesis mechanisms, which is revolutionary in beauty biotechnology.

This protein is then harvested, extracted, and purified to obtain this bio-engineered plant-based type 1 collagen fragment, which is highly pure and contains no potentially irritating toxic substances, making it ideal for beauty applications.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="61938&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]
