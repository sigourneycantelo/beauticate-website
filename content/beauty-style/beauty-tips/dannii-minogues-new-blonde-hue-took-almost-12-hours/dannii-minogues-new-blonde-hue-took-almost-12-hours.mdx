A change so drastic though isn’t without its challenges… When we caught up with Dannii yesterday, she revealed the entire process took almost 11 hours to complete – for context, binge watching The Crown season 1 would take you 10 hours. That's some dedication to one's own crown.

Normally, to transition from medium brown to blonde would be done over a series of weeks (excepting you, Kardashian/Jenners), however the L’Oreal Professionnel team lightened Dannii’s colour in one day – starting at 10am and working right through to around 9pm. Dannii explained that her hair stayed so strong using L’Oreal Professionnel's new in-salon service, SmartBond. Perfect for colour addicts, it repairs damage inflicted on hair due to excessive colour and bleaching.
