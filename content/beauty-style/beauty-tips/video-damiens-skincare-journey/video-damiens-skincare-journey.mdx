### While we try to be diligent with our beauty rituals, it’s easy to forget that the guys in our lives are also feeling the dermal effects of the elements and ageing.  So after my husband Damien had a particularly nuts few months of traveling and working late I thought it was high time he had a little time out for himself. I booked him in to male-centric medispa Sydney's [Face of Man](https://www.faceofman.com.au) for an Ultraceuticals Vita-Brasion Facial – a deeply exfoliating paramedical treatment infused with potent anti-ageing and antioxidants Vitamin A and C. He was also prescribed a skincare regime to continue at home (so he can finally stop using my products now!). Ever the good sport, here he shares his best tips for embarking on a male-skincare routine…
