The one-time Disney teen star, Zendaya – now 20-year-old [Covergirl ](http://www.covergirl.com.au/)ambassador and all round boss lady – has launched her own app, which will feature live streams, shopping access to her clothing line Daya by Zendaya, and beauty DIYs – for a small fee.

The app works similar to the Kardashian apps – the initial download is free, however the majority of content will cost $3 a month. Fingers crossed she reveals the secrets and products responsible for that flawless skin…

The app is available from iTunes store now.
