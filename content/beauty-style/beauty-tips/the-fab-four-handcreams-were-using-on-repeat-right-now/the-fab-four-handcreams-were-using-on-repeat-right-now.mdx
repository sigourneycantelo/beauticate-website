## Autumnal Bliss

When we say this stuff smells good, we mean it. Queensland pear with warm vanilla notes and a hint of clove will have you feeling cosy, even out in the cold. Ecoya’s French Pear Fragranced Hand Cream is perfect to throw in your handbag for silky smoothness all day. This stuff does pack a bit of a punch scent-wise, so if you are a longtime lover of all things Ecoya, we definitely recommend picking one of these up. Pair with a tube of Ecoya Lotus Flower Hand Sanitiser for luxury hand hygiene on the go.

## Unconditional Love

It only takes a small amount of [Ella Baché Hand and Nail Treatment Cream](https://bit.ly/3f0GeBc) to have hands feeling like new. With antioxidant fruit oils, lanolin, and squalene, this rich, nourishing cream will leave your skin protected all day. Its non-greasy formula also has vitamin E and keratin to keep cuticles conditioned and nails strong. If you’re a fan of fuss-free products, this will be perfect for you as it absorbs quickly into the skin and has a soft, subtle fragrance.

## Beach Babe

If you haven’t had the pleasure of smelling something from the delicious Palm Beach Collection, we urge you to grab one of their Hydrating Hand Creams immediately. They’re packed with shea and coco butter for the most supple skin, and are proudly Aussie made too. We are loving the [Coconut and Lime](https://bit.ly/3h0222c) scent with zesty citrus and dreamy, creamy coconut notes. If you need a slice of summer all year round, this one’s for you. Pair with the Palm Beach Collection Coconut and Lime Hand and Body Wash for pure tropical bliss.

## Damage Control

If you’re looking for something more sensible, Biologique Recherche Emulsion Rénovatrice Mains is the extra defence you need. Designed to restore dehydrated, damaged hands, this light, non-sticky formula provides relief for lipid-deficient skin. This faithful favourite will soothe and condition, whether you are in need of some autumnal aid or preparing to battle the winter elements.
