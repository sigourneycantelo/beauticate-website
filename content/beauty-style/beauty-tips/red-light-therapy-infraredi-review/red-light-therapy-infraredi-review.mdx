## **LED Masks vs. Light Panels: What’s the Difference?**

So, you’ve probably heard about **LED masks**-they’re the talk of Beauty Town. But in the world of red light therapy, **light panels** are the mothership. All LED masks use red light therapy, but not all red light therapy is done via masks. Panels (like my Infraredi Pro Max) deliver **higher irradiance** and **broader coverage**, while LED masks are gentler and more targeted-ideal for daily facial treatments.

At first, red light therapy feels like witchcraft. But it’s grounded in **serious scientific research**-and it’s evolving fast.

Here’s what the studies say:

### **Skin Health & Collagen Production**

Red light stimulates fibroblasts-the cells that produce collagen and elastin. Translation: firmer, smoother skin.**

### Pain, Inflammation & Muscle Recovery**

Wavelengths in the **660–850nm** range reduce joint pain, accelerate muscle repair, and boost blood flow.**

Red light therapy can help regulate melatonin and boost serotonin.**
