A uniquely coloured gradient lip accentuates the natural radiance of the model’s glowing complexion. Glossy cheekbones reflect the light, without looking too glittery.Models reflect light from every angle, due to glitter and rhinestones placed on the skin. Hair is a structural and strong up-do, with a slightly dishevelled look.Rhinestone pieces, in the shape of warrior markings, are placed on natural skin, and paired with soft, volumised waves that incorporate ties as elastics.A veil of matte, glowy skin (trust us, it’s a thing) and earthy tones, like terracotta-orange, mustard, and tobacco, on the eyes, lips and cheeks.Thick and graphic eyeliner is paired with a retro-feminine, pulled back, hairdo.Long, wispy braids, and naturally wavy hair are loosely styled with a hat or head scarf. Neutral tones and rosy makeup portrays an ‘earthy western’ feel.Story by Molly Gay
