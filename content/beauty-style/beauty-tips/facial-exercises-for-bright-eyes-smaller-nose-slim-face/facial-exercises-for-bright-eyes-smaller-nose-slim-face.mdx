## How long should each facial massage step take?

The recommended duration for **each individual step** within the massages below is 30 seconds. For example: Complete step 1 in 30 seconds, then proceed to step 2 for 30 seconds and so on.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Facial Massage for Smaller Nose

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1) Use your knuckles to massage both sides of your nostrils, in a circular motion.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62918&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]2) Place one knuckle on each side of your nostrils.

Massage upwards in a zigzag motion until you reach the lower eyelids. Then massage downwards in a zigzag motion, until you reach the nasal base.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62523&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]3) Completely cover both sides of your nose (use four fingers for each side). Then slide your fingers up and down vertically.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62929&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]4) Use four fingers tips from your left hand to press against the entire right area of your nose. Hold this position for the duration of this instruction.

Place four fingers from your other hand on the edges of the right side of your nose and proceed to massage towards your temple in an upward, gliding motion.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62978&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]5) Press your nose together by deeply inhaling – think of it as squeezing your nose together by holding your breath in.

Then exhale, and repeat the "inhale-exhale" motion.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62892&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]6) Use your finger tips to press the sides of your nose together.

At the same time, smile widely and then purse your lips together in an "air kiss" pose.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62930&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]7)  Use your knuckles to pinch your nose tip inwards.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62895&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]8) Start from the sides of your nostrils and use your knuckles to slide upwards until you reach the inner corner of your eyebrows.

Then glide your knuckles alongside the area slightly beneath the eyebrows and also just above the eyebrows.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62966&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_separator][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Facial massage to reduce double chin and puffiness

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1) Open you mouth widely and smile. Then press your lips together in an air-kiss expression.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62982&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]2) Open your mouth widely and smile, so that your mouth is shaped like an upside down triangle. Then proceed to press your lips together whilst you close your eyes.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62983&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]3) Use your tongue to circle around your teeth in an anti-clockwise motion and then clockwise manner.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62984&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]4) Massage your jaw area in a circular motion, whilst your mouth remains open.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62985&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_separator][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Facial massage for brighter eyes

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1) Massage your eyebrows in a “pinching” motion from the inner corner of your eyebrows to the outer.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62971&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]2) Use two fingers to massage your temple area in an upward circular motion.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62973&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]3) Place your palms on the upper third of your head and lift your palm upwards, as though you are pulling your skin back.

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62975&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]4) Use two fingers to lift the outer corner of the eyebrows upwards in a vibrating motion.

Then proceed to lift the middle of your eyebrows in the same upwards, vibrating manner.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62976&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_separator][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Facial massage for 11 lines

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1) Hold a knuckle on the inner corner of one eyebrow.

Simultaneously, place a knuckle on the skin just above the inner corner of the other eyebrow. Then, use that knuckle to push and pull the skin in a horizontal manner, until you reach the outer corner of the eyebrows.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62943&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]2) Press your index finger on the glabella (i.e. region between your eyebrows) horizontally and hold this position. Then, place a knuckle slightly beneath the index finger and proceed to massage upwards in a vibrating motion, until you reach the glabella.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62944&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_separator][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

## Facial massage for plumper lips

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1)  Slightly tilt your head and hold one finger on the centre of your lower lip.

2) Use your tongue to push the lower lips outwards — beginning from one corner of the lips to another. Simultaneously, use one finger to massage the lip line, so it is in sync with your tongue movements (e.g. same direction and placement). After completing this massage on your lower lip, apply the same instructions to your upper lip.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62928&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

3) Repeat steps 1-2, three times.

## Facial massage to lift eyebrows

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]1) Place your hands underneath the outer eyebrow.

2) Pinch the skin just below your outer eyebrow and continue in an upward, diagonal manner until you reach the upper temple/scalp area.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="62968&#8243; img_size="full"][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_separator][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]
