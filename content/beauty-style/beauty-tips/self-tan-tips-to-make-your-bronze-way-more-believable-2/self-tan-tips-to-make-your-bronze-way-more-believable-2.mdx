### 1. Cheyenne: Easy Does It

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41165&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM @CHEYENNETOZZI

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]If you’re new to tanning or prefer a subtler option, gradual tanners are ideal. These moisturisers with a hint of DHA (the tanning ingredient) are almost impossible to stuff up as they allow you to build up to a desired colour. [Cheyenne Tozzi ](http://www.beauticate.com/who/who-cheyenne-tozzi)loved [Jergens Natural Glow Daily Moisturiser](http://bit.ly/22vQHbk) so much that she became the face of their brand. [Lisa Messenger](http://www.beauticate.com/who/lisa-messenger-entrepreneur-and-author-1) and [Ashley Hart](http://www.beauticate.com/who/ashley-hart-model-and-yogi) are also fans of the slowly-slowly approach, opting for St Tropez Gradual Tanner.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

### 2. Elle: Enlist The Help of a Professional

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41166&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM@CHLOELECAREUX

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]For all those summer parties, a spray tan can be one of the best decisions you’ll make. [Elle Ferguson](https://www.beauticate.com/who/influencers/elle-ferguson-elle-effect/) is so well-known for her flawless tan that she launched her very own line of self-tanning products, so now we can all emulate Elle's signature glow. It's reassuring to know that even faux tanning obsessives still rely on the experts – Elle says, "I go to [Tan Temple](https://www.beauticate.com/the-go-tos/beauty-maintenance/tan-temple-bondi/) with my own mousse and get them to put it through their spray gun."[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

### 3. Rosie: Preparation is Key

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41167&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM @ROSIEHW

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]If you’ve decided to take on the role of tanning yourself, preparing your skin is paramount. Make sure skin is exfoliated prior, and you’ve done any hair removal at least a day before. A great tip we grabbed from bronzed beauty [Rosie Huntington-Whiteley](http://www.beauticate.com/who/rosie-huntington-whiteley-model) is to “put a little bit of moisturiser on the elbows and knees and around the knuckle areas so the tan doesn’t grab or stain.” She swears by ModelCo Tan in a Can.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

### 4. Lara: Love Your Glove

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41168&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM@LARAWORTHINGTON

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]In the form of a tanning mitt, that is. Perennially bronze-limbed [Lara Worthington](http://www.beauticate.com/who/lara-bingle) knows that using a mitt to apply your tan can make the task a lot less daunting: “I start my tanning from head to toe and I always use a mitt, otherwise it just gets everywhere.” Use a velour glove that allows you to see exactly where the product is going and erase streaks as you go, without turning your palms orange.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

### 5. Erika: Contour Your Body

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41169&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM@ERIKAHEYNATZ

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]In the same vein of using darker shades to contour your face, you can also use them when fake-tanning. [Erika Heynatz](http://www.beauticate.com/who/erika-heynatz-actor) loves this trick – and says she had it done with tanning guru [Katrina Brown](http://www.beauticate.com/who/katrina-brown-tanning-guru). “I had a great contoured tan once before a photoshoot at The Bronzing Boutique – it shaved off a few kilos and gave me a free six pack.”[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]

### 6. Match Your Makeup

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image="41170&#8243; img_size="full"][vc_column_text]

IMAGE: INSTAGRAM@LOZCURTIS

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]One crucial step many forego when they have fake tanned is to match their makeup. Firstly, you’ll need to ensure your foundation matches your new colour. [Bahar Etminan](http://www.beauticate.com/who/bahar-etminan-author-and-editor) loves to apply [JBronze Illuminator](https://www.priceline.com.au/brand/jbronze/jbronze-shimmer-instant-illuminiser-50-ml) to bring some life to her face when she has fake tan on. Beauty vlogger extraordinaire [Lauren Curtis](http://www.beauticate.com/who/lauren-curtis-beauty-vlogger) is also a well-documented fake tan devotee (she uses Loving Tan Dark Mousse). She says that different shades of cosmetics can either enhance or wash out your skin tone. “Lipstick in a nude salmon pink looks great against tanned skin,” she says. Make the most of your burnished glow by introducing pastel and fluro colours by way of a punchy pout or statement mani.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]
