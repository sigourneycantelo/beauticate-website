### As Mercedes Benz Fashion Week Australia reaches a frenzied crescendo and social media feeds go into monotonous-meltdown, we thought it reasonable to go back to the very beginning: the inspiration behind a look. The muse.

![ We love Brigitte Bardot photographed behind the scenes of her film 'Il Disprezzo' ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-11.jpg)

 We love Brigitte Bardot photographed behind the scenes of her film 'Il Disprezzo'

We asked six Australian designers which beauty icons they look to – both on and off the runway. From eclectic influencers like David Bowie to the debonair Brigitte Bardot, these style pioneers knew the foundations of striking beauty and continue to influence us today.

*Story by Sigourney.*

### Kym Ellery, Ellery

![ Designer: Kym Ellery ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-12.jpg)

 Designer: Kym Ellery

“My all time favourite beauty icon is David Bowie.  The transformations throughout his various persona’s is nothing short of genius.”

![ Beauty Icon: David Bowie ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-13.jpg)

 Beauty Icon: David Bowie

### Alexandra Smart, Ginger & Smart

![ Designer: Alexander Smart ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-14.jpg)

 Designer: Alexander Smart

“Carine Roitfeld epitomises ageless beauty and confidence. She expresses herself creatively through her magazine and work with luxury brands but keeps a very sexy, androgynous look for herself.”

![ Beauty Icon:Carine Roitfeld ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-15.jpg)

 Beauty Icon:Carine Roitfeld

### Genevieve Smart, Ginger & Smart

![ Designer: Genevieve Smart ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-16.jpg)

 Designer: Genevieve Smart

“Lou Doillon. So French- done but undone. Love her total insouciance.”

![ Beauty Icon: Lou Doillon ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-17.jpg)

 Beauty Icon: Lou Doillon

### Liberty Watson, Watson x Watson

![ Designer: Liberty Watson ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-18.jpg)

 Designer: Liberty Watson

“My beauty icon is Brigitte Bardot. I love her Blonde Bombshell look, luscious locks and effortlessly sexy, defined eyes. The heavy lash gives her a flirtatious fun feel which is the same look we are going for with our models on our MBFWA.”

![ Beauty Icon: Brigitte Bardot ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-19.jpg)

 Beauty Icon: Brigitte Bardot

### Somer Watson, Watson x Watson

![ Designer: Somer Watson ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-20.jpg)

 Designer: Somer Watson

“I've always been inspired by a natural-model-off-duty-look, no one does it better than the likes of Daria, or Freja Beha Erichsen.”

![ Beauty Icon: Freja Beha Erichsen ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-21.jpg)

 Beauty Icon: Freja Beha Erichsen

### Jennifer McCloy, Jennifer Kate

![ Designer: Jennifer McCloy ](/content/beauty-style/beauty-tips/fashion-designers-on-their-beauty-muses/img-22.jpg)

 Designer: Jennifer McCloy
