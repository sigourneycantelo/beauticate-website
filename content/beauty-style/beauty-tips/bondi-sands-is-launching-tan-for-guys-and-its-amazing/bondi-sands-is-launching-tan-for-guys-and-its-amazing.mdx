## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is the Bondi Sands Everyday Gradual Tanning Foam For Men?**
A: It is a gradual self-tanning foam designed specifically for men, formulated to build colour slowly for a natural, not-from-a-bottle result.

**Q: How does the men's Bondi Sands tanning foam smell?**
A: Unlike many self-tanners, it smells like a luxe men's cologne rather than coconut, making it more appealing to a male audience.

**Q: Is the Bondi Sands men's tan easy to apply?**
A: Yes. The foam format is straightforward to use and builds colour gradually, making it difficult to over-apply or streak.
