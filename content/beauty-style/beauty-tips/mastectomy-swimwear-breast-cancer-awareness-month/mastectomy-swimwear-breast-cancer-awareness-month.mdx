## DIVE IN

It takes an Aussie brand to get an

item as quintessentially Australian as

a pair of swimmers and to revamp it

for women who have undergone

mastectomies – and that’s exactly

what Jets have done. Available in a

range of ultra chic colours and

silhouettes, these gorgeous one-

pieces offer extra support and

internal pockets for prostheses to be

worn, without being in the least bit

obvious. And for all online orders in

October, 5% of sales go to the

McGrath Foundation. Top marks,

Jets.

## HEAVY METAL

Over the past few years, a lot of us

have been thinking about changing

out our conventional deodorants for

more natural options – especially

given aluminium in anti-perspirants

has been linked to a higher risk of

breast cancer. So what better time to

make the switch than this month,

with Black Chicken offering a

bumper-sized version of their cult

Axilla Deodorant Paste? It’s free from

aluminium (as well as a slew of other

nasties) and $2 from every sale goes

to fund breast cancer research. Win,

win.

## BATHING BEAUTY

We’re always in awe of the work that

[Pink Hope](https://pinkhope.org.au/) do around educating

women about their risk of developing

breast and ovarian cancer. So we

were excited to see them team up

with Salt By Hendrix to release [a dreamy spa set](https://pinkhope.org.au/product/salt-by-hendrix-spa-set/), boasting a gorgeous

bath soak, lip tint and clay mask.

Profits from the sale go to fund Pink

Hope’s invaluable initiatives, so you

can indulge while supporting a

worthy cause too.

[SALT BY HENDRIX SPA SET](https://pinkhope.org.au/product/salt-by-hendrix-spa-set/)

SAND AND SKY EXFOLIATOR KIT

## TICKLED PINK

Cult clay mask brand Sand and Sky

 have brought out a limited edition kit

 this month, with 30% of profits

supporting [Look Good Feel Better](https://lgfb.org.au/) –

and yep, it’s in their signature

millennial pink. Comprised of their

Flash Perfection Exfoliating

Treatment and a super cute (and

cheeky) silky robe, it’s the perfect

combo for an all-pink pamper sesh.

## OUR LIPS ARE SEALED

We’re pretty smitten with this Lulu

Guinness x ghd offering, and the fact

that $20 from each sale goes to the

[National Breast Cancer Foundation](https://nbcf.org.au/) means it’s a sure thing we’re adding

this to our wish lists this October.

Adorned with the quirky British

designer’s iconic lips design, the gold

styler boasts dual-heat technology, to

protect hair from damage and leave

strands looking smooth and sleek.

What’s not to love?

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Does Jets swimwear make mastectomy swimsuits?**
A: Yes, Australian brand Jets has a range of swimwear designed for women who have undergone mastectomies. The one-pieces come in a variety of colours and silhouettes, with extra support and discreet internal pockets for prostheses.

**Q: Does aluminium in deodorant increase breast cancer risk?**
A: Aluminium in anti-perspirant deodorants has been linked to a higher risk of breast cancer, which is why many women are switching to natural alternatives. Black Chicken's Axilla Deodorant Paste is aluminium-free and, during October, donates $2 from every sale to breast cancer research.

**Q: What breast cancer charities are supported by beauty brands in October?**
A: Several brands run Breast Cancer Awareness Month initiatives. Highlighted here are Jets (donating 5% of October online sales to the McGrath Foundation), Black Chicken (donating $2 per Axilla Paste sale to breast cancer research), Salt By Hendrix in partnership with Pink Hope, and ghd's Lulu Guinness styler ($20 from each sale going to the National Breast Cancer Foundation).
