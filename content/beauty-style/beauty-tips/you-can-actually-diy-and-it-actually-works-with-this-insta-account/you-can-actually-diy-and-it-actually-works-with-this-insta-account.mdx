If you haven’t heard of Farah Dhukai, you should get acquainted, asap. Her YouTube channel and Instagram account @farahdhukai has a combined 6.5m strong following, and for good reason. Moving away from the DIYs made for people who have seven hours prep time and won't leave the house un-strobed, Farah (although a fan of the strobe contour herself) gives the advice we all want. From teeth whitening to amazing face masks, her quick and to the point DIY beauty tutorials and hacks are cheap, natural and totally doable.

One of her most popular vids? She refers to it as “life changing” and “acne murdering” face mask, and her millions of fans are praising the easy two ingredient mask (Calcium Bentonite Clay and Apple Cider Vinegar).

[A post shared by Farah D (@farahdhukai)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BRCh9zKAoZZ/) on Feb 27, 2017 at 7:05pm PST

One of our personal faves is the tea facial. Using your choice of tea (black, green, chamomile) and agar agar powder mixed and cooled on your face gives your skin an instant refresh.

[A post shared by Farah D (@farahdhukai)](https://www.instagram.com/p/BFFQfAKI0RI/) on May 6, 2016 at 3:13pm PDT

And if hair is your concern, she has that (literally) covered with a split end remedy using only papaya, honey, almond oil, and a plastic bag.
