T’was the night before Christmas and all through the house,

not a creature was stirring, not even a mouse.

The gifts lay en masse all under the tree. Adorned in trinkets and bows, wrapped so beautifully.

Only morning will tell what does lie inside,

To ensure that it’s perfect, our gift to you is this guide.

From glamazons to trend setters, or the flirty, frivolous type,

Sport luxe lovers to natural beauties- no two are alike.

Christmas is a time to enjoy food, friends and some bubbles

The last thing you desire are gift-shopping troubles.

The hard work is done, so please take a seat and don’t fuss,

Merry Christmas you beauties, love from all of us.
