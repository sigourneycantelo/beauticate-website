## How to Choose the Right Father's Day Fragrance

Choosing a fragrance doesn't have to feel overwhelming. Think about his style: does he prefer bold statements, fresh simplicity, or refined elegance?

Fresh notes (citrus, aquatic) = light, approachable, modern. Woody, amber, or leather notes = richer, warm, and ideal for evenings or special occasions.

## For the Dad Who Owns the Room

![Versace Eros Najim Parfum](/content/beauty-style/fragrance/fathers-day-fragrance/fragrance-4.jpg)

**Versace Eros Najim Parfum**

Notes of bright mandarin and saffron open into oud, caramel, and cedarwood, finishing with incense, patchouli, and tonka bean. Why he'll love it: Confident and magnetic, this fragrance makes a statement without saying a word. Perfect for the dad who commands attention.

## For the Modern Gentleman

![Burberry Hero Parfum Intense](/content/beauty-style/fragrance/fathers-day-fragrance/fragrance-1.jpg)

**Burberry Hero Parfum Intense**

Spicy black pepper and smoky cedar oils grounded by warm leather undertones. Why he'll love it: Strong yet refined, this scent balances tradition and modernity. Perfect for dads who embody quiet strength and effortless style.

## For the Storyteller

![Goldfield & Banks Tales of Amber](/content/beauty-style/fragrance/fathers-day-fragrance/fragrance-6.jpg)

**Goldfield & Banks Tales of Amber**

Moss and orange blossom melt into orris, cinnamon, labdanum, agarwood, and ambergris. Why he'll love it: Rich, warming, and complex, this fragrance is for the dad who appreciates craftsmanship and loves a scent with a story.

## For the Effortlessly Cool Dad

![Coach for Men Eau de Parfum](/content/beauty-style/fragrance/fathers-day-fragrance/fragrance-3.jpg)

**Coach for Men Eau de Parfum**

Crisp citrus and peppery spice with warm leather and aromatic woods. Why he'll love it: Smooth and versatile, this is the everyday go-to for dads who transition easily from work to weekend.

## For the Classic & Timeless Dad

![Issey Miyake L'Eau d'Issey Pour Homme](/content/beauty-style/fragrance/fathers-day-fragrance/fragrance-7.jpg)

**Issey Miyake L'Eau d'Issey Pour Homme**

Clean aquatic brightness with hints of spice and wood. Why he'll love it: Light yet sophisticated, this iconic fragrance is perfect for the dad who favours subtle polish over bold declarations.

## The Gift That Becomes a Memory

The best Father's Day fragrance gift isn't just about how it smells — it's about how it feels. From bold and powerful to light and timeless, these five scents offer something for every kind of dad.
