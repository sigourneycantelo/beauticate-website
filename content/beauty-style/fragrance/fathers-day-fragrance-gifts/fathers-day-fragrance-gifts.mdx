## How to Choose the Right Father’s Day Fragrance

Choosing a fragrance doesn’t have to feel overwhelming. Think about his style: does he prefer bold statements, fresh simplicity, or refined elegance?

**Fresh notes** (citrus, aquatic) = light, approachable, modern.
**Woody, amber, or leather notes** = richer, warm, and ideal for evenings or special occasions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I choose a Father's Day fragrance if I don't know his preferences?**
A: Consider his personality and lifestyle - does he prefer bold statements or understated elegance? Fresh citrus and aquatic notes suit everyday dads who like light, modern scents, while woody, amber and leather notes work better for dads who enjoy richness and complexity. If in doubt, versatile options like Coach for Men or Issey Miyake L'Eau d'Issey Pour Homme suit most men.

**Q: What is the difference between Eau de Parfum and Eau de Toilette?**
A: Eau de Parfum has a higher fragrance concentration (15 to 20 per cent) and typically lasts six to eight hours, making it ideal for special occasions and evenings. Eau de Toilette is lighter (5 to 15 per cent) and lasts three to five hours, suiting everyday wear. For a Father's Day gift that makes a lasting impression, Eau de Parfum offers more presence.

**Q: Which fragrance from this edit suits a dad who loves classic, timeless scents?**
A: Issey Miyake L'Eau d'Issey Pour Homme is the classic choice - clean aquatic brightness with subtle spice and wood that has remained iconic for decades. Burberry Hero Parfum Intense is another strong option, balancing tradition and modernity through refined leather and cedar.
