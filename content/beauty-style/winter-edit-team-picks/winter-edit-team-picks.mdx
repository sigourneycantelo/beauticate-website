Winter always brings a shift — in what we reach for, what we wear, and how we look after ourselves. We asked the Beauticate team to share what they're actually buying and using right now.

## Sigourney Cantelo, Publisher

"Winter always makes me gravitate towards pieces that feel a little more structured and tactile."

Sigourney is in a strong Goldfield & Banks Purple Suede moment — smoky, woody, a little bit sexy — and has been wearing it consistently through the season. When her skin becomes dry, she relies on Mukti Marigold Cream for nourishment and texture. Sharp blazers and woven leather shoes complete the picture.

**What she's buying:** Goldfield & Banks Purple Suede EDP · Henne Bowie Twill Blazer · Mukti Marigold Hydrating Cream · Love Isabelle Carla Hoops · Alias Mae Shay Mules

---

## Kate Waterhouse, Style Editor

Chocolate brown tones are at the centre of Kate's winter wardrobe — sophisticated without being stark. She's embracing cosy elevated staples: layered neutrals, timeless coats. Her skincare shifts toward nourishing formulas and her interiors lean into richer, earthy hues like terracotta and sienna.

**What she's buying:** Forever New Felled Scarf Coat · Henne Carr Top · En Gold Clay No.3 Sienna · Ultraceuticals Ultra DNA³ Complex Recovery Night Cream · Maison Balzac Saint T Room Spray

---

## Rae Morris, Makeup Editor

"In the cooler months, I naturally gravitate towards pieces and rituals that feel warm, functional and effortless."

Rae prefers room sprays over heavy fragrances for creating a cosy home environment, and keeps SPF non-negotiable year-round even in winter. Her July Juliette Suede Work Tote has become the bag she reaches for every day.

**What she's buying:** July Juliette Suede Work Tote · Frédéric Malle Jurassic Flower Room Spray · Rae Morris Invisible Mattifier · Goldfield & Banks Silky Woods EDP

---

## Monique McMahon, Hair Editor

"This is the season where I lean into softness and care, both in beauty and in how I look after myself."

Scalp care has become Monique's winter priority, with Christophe Robin Fortifying Scalp Serum used daily. She's swapped creams for rich body oils and increased hydration from within. Her routine becomes more ritualistic and intentional — less, but better.

**What she's buying:** Christophe Robin Fortifying Scalp Serum · JS Health Vanilla Protein Powder · Goldfield & Banks Silky Woods EDP

---

## Dr Amy Chahal, Aesthetic Expert

Intentional layering defines Amy's winter approach. The Rationale No.3 Tinted Serum SPF50 provides a polished, no-makeup-makeup finish, while suede sneakers in deeper tones offer elevated ease. She's also elevating her home with thoughtful details — glassware, fragrance, the small things that make daily rituals feel considered.

**What she's buying:** Sézane Gaspard Cardigan · Rationale No.3 Tinted Serum SPF50 · Alohas Suede Sneakers · Lumira Cuban Tobacco Parfum · Maison Balzac Bordeaux Wine Glasses

---

## Camilla Thompson, Wellness Editor

"Winter always makes me slow things down. Softer mornings, warmer layers, more time at home."

Camilla is prioritising energy support and sleep optimisation as natural light decreases. Red light therapy from Bon Charge has become a post-sauna ritual anchor, and creatine is her supplement recommendation for focus and energy this season.

**What she's buying:** Assembly Label Laurie Cashmere Knit in Merlot · Cultiver Linen Duvet Cover · Bon Charge Red Light Therapy Device · Eir Women Fuel Creatine · Subtle Energies Aura Protection Mist

---

## Jacqueline Allwill, Nutrition Expert

Jacqueline's picks centre on warmth and restoration. Her Nook infrared sauna has become a cornerstone of her winter wellness routine, and The Beauty Chef Collagen Boost is her daily supplement — particularly important when skin is under stress from cold and heating systems.

**What she's buying:** Vrg Girl Lennon Bomber Jacket · Nook Core 1 Person Sauna · The Beauty Chef Collagen Boost · Basics by B Liquid Glow Drops

---

## Kerrie Gentle, Makeup Expert

"A funnel neck coat is my go-to when I want to feel instantly put together. Clean, sculptural, and effortless."

Kerrie's focus is on winter exfoliation — the Sodashi Enzyme Face Polish to refine skin before makeup application — and a more ritualistic cleansing routine anchored by the Elemis Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm. Makeup shifts toward softer, warmer neutral tones.

**What she's buying:** Elemis Pro-Collagen Cleansing Balm · Sodashi Enzyme Face Polish · Basics By B Face Palette · Maison Balzac Le Bois Candle · Cable Melbourne Brushed Cashwool V Jumper
