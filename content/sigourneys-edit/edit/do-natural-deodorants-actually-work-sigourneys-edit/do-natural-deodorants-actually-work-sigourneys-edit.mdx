SIGOURNEY AND LULU

[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]“Sweat is odorous and sterile,” explains [MooGoo](https://moogoo.com.au/) founder Craig Jones who formulated the brand’s popular [Fresh Cream Deodorant](https://moogoo.com.au/deodorant/fresh-cream-deodorant.html). “Deodorants let you perspire (unlike antiperspirants), as we naturally should, but control the bacteria that causes the odour using an alkaline formula,” he says. “Unlike antiperspirants with Aluminium, you will perspire when using natural deodorants. Depending on how well the formula controls the odour-causing bacteria determines their effectiveness.” Craig says that when he couldn’t find a natural deodorant that worked or that “didn’t smell like a Tea Tree forest”, he set about creating his own.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][/vc_row_inner][vc_row_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]He wanted to create a roll-on product that was easy to use (many natural deodorants come in the less convenient paste form) and wasn’t riddled with essential oils  “Essential oils may smell wonderful at first, but after a few hours tea tree starts to smell quite bad in our opinion.  A lot of people experience irritation when they use essential oils,” he adds. The finished Fresh Cream Deodorant was so pure that it’s actually edible. And while Craig doesn’t suggest sucking on the rollerball like a lollypop, he says it’s important because the skin under the arms needs to be treated with the utmost of care. “A lot of people don’t realise that the skin in this area is actually the thinnest on the body. If you don’t like to put chemicals on your face, why would you blast such a delicate area with them?” he asks.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column_inner][vc_column_inner width="1/2&#8243;][vc_single_image image="31933&#8243; img_size="full" alignment="center" css_animation="fadeIn"][vc_column_text css_animation="fadeIn"]

When it comes to exercise and stressful situations I still reach for the heavy duty antiperspirants, but I wish I didn’t have to. To decrease my chemical load however I do use natural deodorants on at-home days and times when a bit of wetness under the arms isn’t going bother me.
