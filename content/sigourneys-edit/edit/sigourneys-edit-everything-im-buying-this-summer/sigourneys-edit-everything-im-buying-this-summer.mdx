## **Dresses + Lipsticks**

Traditionally earthy shades have been associated with winter wear, but the burnt orange and ochre hues of the season have had a summer reimagining with this fun Shona Joy number (how pretty are those balloon sleeves?). Pair with our latest obsession, Lanolips's Golden Lip Water: a hydrating, sparkling, and oh-so-festive gloss.

A timeless white midi dress is a clever investment that will see you through many a summer, serving as a blank slate that you can wear in a myriad of ways. Opt for a nude eye and lip combo for day-to-day wear, or add a good dose of glamour with Nars' iconic [Red Square Lip Pencil.](https://www.mecca.com.au/nars/velvet-matte-lip-pencil-red-square/I-000493.html?gclid=Cj0KCQiA5bz-BRD-ARIsABjT4niktkaDLhdeLJgPCyOkmklGDIwVRlFTB9cUokigDXDWYKALPhGureQaAgWjEALw_wcB&gclsrc=aw.ds)

And for festive summer soirées, this heavenly Palm Noosa dress (and a slick of pretty pink lipstick, like MAC's Powder Kiss in A Little Tamed) is just the ticket for sipping and swirling the night away.

## Swimwear + Sunscreens

It stands to reason that Australians make some of the most beautiful swimwear in the world; here are a few offerings we're currently lusting after. Ephemera's Balconette style is such a flattering, feminine cut, and is fitted to your bra – not dress – size: a clever innovation that makes for a better fit. Bondi Born's Colette one-piece offers strong 1990s supermodel vibes, which we are completely here for. And if you're after a two-piece, this high-cut, belted bottoms – paired with its matching bandeau top – is floral perfection.

No summer story can be exhaustive without sun protection, and thankfully a bounty of really good SPFs have hit our shelves this year.

## Activewear + Athleisure Beauty

Don't want to take your handbag when doing the Bondi to Bronte? Not a problem: this compact bum bag can take care of your phone and keys, so you can focus on *that* view. Pop on [a chic halterback sports bra](https://bit.ly/3gvMBwr) and bike shorts, and you'll be ready for whatever the path ahead throws at you.

A smudgeproof mascara is an eternal summer essential, never more so than when exercising. Happily, Covergirl's Outlast Active Mascara was designed and fulfils that very purpose. And then when you get home, nix any residual soreness with this arnica-spiked muscle gel. Then a session with the Theragun will have you feeling ache-free and ready to lace up again in no time.

## **Lingerie + Perfume**

For everyday wear, [Calvin Klein really are a go-to](https://bit.ly/3greUMA): they're comfy yet cool (a tougher combo to nail than you might think). And on those lowkey days where you still want to feel like you've made an effort, this hair mist from Jo Malone is the perfect compromise between informality yet still feeling groomed.

If you're after a chic pair of pyjamas for unwrapping presents on Christmas morning, you can't go past these feathered beauties for a serious dose of chic. While Marilyn Monroe is famously supposed to have wore only Chanel No. 5 to bed, you might prefer their Coco Mademoiselle L'Eau Privée, a light and intimate scent designed specifically for night wear.

For Christmas day, [the Christina bra](https://bit.ly/3lXS00o) from Heidi Klum is both glamorous and festive; pair with a scent inspired by a gin cocktail for the perfect combo for celebrating.

## Sandals and Pedis

Birkenstocks are enjoying a happy time in the fashion spotlight at the moment, and while their Proenza Schouler collaboration was quickly snapped up earlier this year, you can still nab [their classic Arizona style in silver](https://bit.ly/3mYLvMn) which has a similar look. Red nail polish is basically a neutral in our books; we're pairing our Birkenstocks with this chic Essie hue with an appropriately festive name.

K. Jacques's sandals are handmade in St Tropez and count Inès de la Fressange and Kate Moss as fans; if you're looking to invest in a pair that you'll love and wear for years to come, their Loki style are timeless. A cheerful pink like Sally Hansen's Peachy Breeze would be the perfect summery pedi to match.

[Nine West's Gianna slides](https://bit.ly/2VVCbwM) have the feel of a particular beloved pair of designer sandals, but without the heavy price tag. And if you can't quit the neutrals even in the height of summer, Smith & Cult's Doe My Dear is the perfect perennial taupe that looks oh-so-chic year round.
