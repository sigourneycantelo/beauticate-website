## Protect Your Skin with Cold Weather Beauty Rituals

### Science-backed luxury:

Augustinus Bader The Rich Cream**

**This is the big guns of winter skin. A powerhouse moisturiser for winter-weary skin. Powered by TFC8®, it helps regenerate, deeply hydrate, and restore radiance overnight. I layer it over oil after a day in the cold and wake up with skin that looks like it’s had a week at a spa.

### Daily mineral SPF:

Synergie UberZinc SPF 15

When I’m not on the slopes or planning to be outside a lot in winter I opt for a non-greasy mineral SPF moisturiser. Synergie Uberzinc protects, calms, and layers beautifully under makeup. For actual skiing, I prefer a high-SPF chemical sunscreen like La Roche-Posay Anthelios Ultra-Light Fluid SPF 50+, topped up with a zinc stick.

### High-altitude hero:

[**La Roche-Posay Anthelios Invisible Fluid SPF 50+**](https://bit.ly/44Fuejp)

For high-altitude conditions (or high-stakes city days), [this cult French pharmacy SPF](https://bit.ly/44Fuejp) is a no-brainer. The ultra-light, almost watery texture glides on like silk and sets invisibly - no ghostly cast, no greasy film, no clogged pores. It’s my go-to base layer on the slopes, because it actually stays put (even under a helmet or through a snowball fight), and layers beautifully under makeup or a tinted zinc stick for extra coverage. Dermatologist-developed and fragrance-free, it’s gentle enough for sensitive skin, yet powerful enough to shield against UVA/UVB and environmental aggressors. Ski trip or school run - it’s your daily dose of smart sun defence.
