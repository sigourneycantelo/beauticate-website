## What's Lurking in Our Water?

It's easy to assume that our tap water is pristine, but even in Australia, contaminants can sneak in. Studies have found that per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) — often dubbed "forever chemicals" — have been detected in drinking water across the country, including in parts of Sydney and regional areas.

Additionally, lead and copper contamination has been identified in household drinking water in New South Wales, primarily due to aging infrastructure. Exposure to these metals can have adverse health effects, particularly in children and pregnant women.

While these contaminants are more concerning when ingested, they can also affect your skin and hair when present in shower water, leading to dryness, irritation, and other issues.
