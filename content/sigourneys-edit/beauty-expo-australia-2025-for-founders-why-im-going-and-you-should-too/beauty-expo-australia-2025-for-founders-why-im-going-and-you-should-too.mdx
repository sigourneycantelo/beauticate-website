## What Makes It So Special?

Whether you're a salon owner, a brand founder, an RN, or just someone who lives and breathes beauty — the Beauty Expo event is a game changer.

It's not just the access to new products (though the aisles are packed with incredible innovations). It's the energy, the people, the conversations. You'll leave feeling inspired, connected, and ready to take your business or career to the next level.

> "Some of my favourite beauty discoveries have happened at Beauty Expo — it's where you really see the next wave before it hits mainstream."

## What I'm Excited About This Year

The lineup is shaping up to be amazing. Some of the Aussie brands I'm looking forward to seeing include:

- Derma Aesthetics
- Intraceuticals
- Calmerceuticals

It's such a rare chance to explore what's emerging in skincare, devices, injectables, and professional treatments — all in one place. I love seeing how brands present themselves, tell their stories, and connect with their community.

Plus, the education sessions are always a highlight for me. Hearing directly from founders and industry experts about how they've built their businesses (and survived the tough stuff) is both inspiring and incredibly real.
